Invalid photosIf the photos fail to meet compliance in TICA, the IMU may be contacted in order to assess if the photo can still be used for the issue of a smartcard.

If the photos fails to meet the compliance standards, and the IMU has not approved them for use, the action required will depend on whether the customer has valid digital images stored and the transaction being processed.

If the customer has valid images stored with sufficient life to cover the period remaining on the driver licence, then a smartcard can be issued.

If the customer does not have valid images stored with sufficient life they may submit a new image in an electronic format (for example JPEG) by email.

The image must have a resolution of 600dpi or higher. An assessment must be made to ensure the person in the electronic image is the same as the person in the physical photograph originally provided. Where any doubt exists it should be referred to the IMU. The electronic image must meet compliance in TICA.
