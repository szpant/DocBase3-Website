When to Register (3003)


What is it?Registration allows vehicles to access Queensland roads or road related areas provided all requirements are met.
Req'ments
  • Vehicle must be a registerable vehicle
Rules
Definitions
Definitions
    Road
    Road
      TO(RUMS) Act 1995 Schedule 4 defines a road as:
      • Includes a busway under the Transport Infrastructure Act 1994
        and
      • Includes an area that is:
        • Open to or used by the public and is developed for
          or
        • Has one of its uses as, the driving or riding of motor vehicles whether for payment of a fee or otherwise
          or
        • Is dedicated to public use as a road

      Does not include an area declared under a regulation not to be a road.

    Road Related Area
    Road Related Area
      An area that is part of a road and:
      • Divides a part of the road from another part of the road and is
      • Formed by lines marked on the road or is constructed on the road (eg traffic island)
      • Is a footpath or nature strip
      • Is open to the public
      • Designed for use by cyclists or animals
    Vehicle
    Vehicle
      TO(RUMS) Act 1995 Schedule 4 defines a vehicle as:
      • Any type of transport that moves on wheels and a hovercraft but
      • Does not include a train or tram
Exempt from Registration
Exempt from Registration
    A vehicle is not required to be registered if the vehicle operator has one of the following:
    • A vehicle is being driven or towed to where it is to be inspected provided the operator carries a completed Application for Registration and CTP insurance for a registration term of 3,6 or 12 months (whichever is applicable for the vehicle) TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration)Regulations s15or s16
    • A vehicle operating under:
      • An unregistered vehicle permit under TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration) Regulations S60
      • An authority to use unregistered vehicle under TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration)Regulations s107
      • A permit to use intercepted unregistered vehicle under TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration)Regulations s108
      • A vehicle used under the authority of a dealer plate under TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration)Regulations s62
      • Vehicle is exempt from registration under TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration) Regulations s4
      • Vehicle is temporarily in Qld under TO(RUM-Vehicle Registration) Regulations s105
Vehicles Not Considered Temporarily in Qld
Vehicles Not Considered Temporarily in Qld
    A vehicle is not deemed to be used temporarily in Qld if:
    • It has a permanent Qld garaging address
    • Has had a Qld garage address for more than 14 days
    • It is over 4.5t GVM and operated in Qld for more than 3 continuous months
