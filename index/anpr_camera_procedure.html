ANPR Camera Procedure (7622)

Applies to
Applies to
    This procedure will outline how to operate an ANPR Camera.
1
Powering up the camera
Powering up the camera
    1.Turn the power isolation switch to 'ON'. The red indicator light in the power isolation switch will come on.
    2.Press the 'Power' button located on the ANPR Shutdown unit. The 'Power' button will show blue to indicate power to the unit.
    3.When the 'Power' button is blue and the 'Hold' light is green the unit is now on and recording.
    4.With the ANPR camera power up process completed, view the monitor to check the camera is operating correctly.
    5. Once the display has been checked complete the ANPR Compliance Vehicle Log sheet.
2
Transfer data
Transfer data
    There is a designated parking spot at each nominated TMR compliance office were data from the ANPR camera can be uploaded.

    To upload the data press the 'Power' button once, the blue 'Power' button will go off.

    The green 'Hold' light will stay on until the data upload is complete. During the data upload the display will show the files being transferred, once the upload is complete the display will show the shutdown procedure.

    If the unit is out of range of the Wi-Fi Hotspot, the green light will go off after about 5 minutes and the data will be stored on the camera.

    When the display shows 'No Sync' the power isolation switch can be turned off.
3
Turning off the camera without data transfer
Turning off the camera without data transfer
    1.Press the ‘Power’ button once, the blue power button will go off.
    2.After the system shuts down turn the power isolation switch to the 'Off' position.

    Note:Turning the ignition key or switching the Nautec system off will only turn off the ANPR monitor
