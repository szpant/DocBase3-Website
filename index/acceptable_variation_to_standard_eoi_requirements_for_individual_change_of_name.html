Acceptable Variation to Standard EOI Requirements for Individual Change of Name (4737)

N1. Customer under 18 & can't locate parent to change name
N1. Customer under 18 & can't locate parent to change name
    A customer who is a new applicant and is under 18 years of age and is unable to locate their parent/s to consent to a name change.
    Rationale
    Rationale
      In some instances an applicant may be using an assumed family name (eg. mother remarried and uses stepfather's family name). If the applicant is under 18 years of age they need the signed consent of both parents to apply for a BDM Name Change Certificate. This may be unobtainable if the applicant cannot locate one of their parents.
    Evidence of Identity Required
    Evidence of Identity Required
      • Cat A in birth name
      • Cat B documents in assumed name
      • F2031 (Customer Identity Declaration)
    Documents to be recorded in TICA
    Documents to be recorded in TICA
      • Cat A (Birth name recorded as per Birth Certificate then Change name using CORR)
      • Cat B's
      • CIDF (F2031)
      • Residency

N3. Wants to use previous name listed on TICA
N3. Wants to use previous name listed on TICA
    Applies to
    Applies to
      A customer who:
      • Is in TICA with a Driver Licence
      and
      • Wishes to revert to a previously used married or birth name listed in TICA
    Variation Requirements
    • Standard EOI
    • The name required must be:
      • The birth name or a previous married/civil partnership/relationship name (TICA Code = MARR) that is listed in TICA
      • and
      • The name is not one recorded prior to a name change for any of the following reasons:
        • Deed Poll (DEED)
        • Change of Name Certificate (CHNM)
          Rationale
          Rationale
            As Deed Poll and Change of Name Certificate legally change the name by cancelling the validity of any previous names (birth or married) any change back to a previously recorded name must be supported by a Change of Name Certificate issued by the Registrar of Births, Deaths & Marriages.
    Examples
    Examples
      Name Change Document not required
      Name Change Document not required
        1.This person started with a family name of Smith, changed to Jones by marriage, then to Kelly by marriage and to Bond by marriage.

        These name changes would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

        TypeName Reason
        CURRBond, Dianne Michelle
        HISTKelly, Dianne Michelle MARR
        HISTJones, Dianne, MichelleMARR
        HISTSmith, Dianne, MichelleMARR

        This person may revert to the use of the previous married names of Kelly or Jones or the maiden name of Smith, without the production of a name change document as:
        • the link is shown in their Name History on TICA
        • and there have been no changes after any of them by Deed Poll, Name Change Certificate.

        Once this person has reverted back to their birth or married name, any further name changes must be supported by a Change of Name Certificate issued by the Registrar of Births, Deaths & Marriages.

        2.This person started with a family name of Smith and changed to Jones by Statutory Declaration or Declaration, but did not get their name changed legally by Deed Poll or Change of Name Certificate.

        This name change would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

        TypeName Reason
        CURRJones, Dianne Michelle
        HISTSmith, Dianne, MichelleSTAT
        This person may revert to the use of the previous maiden or legal name of Smith without the production of a name change document, provided they can produce the standard EOI documents in their maiden/legal name, as:
        • the link is shown in their Name History on TICA
        • and there have been no changes by Deed Poll or Name Change Certificate

        Once this person has reverted back to their previous maiden or legal name, any further name changes must be supported by a Change of Name Certificate issued by the Registrar of Births, Deaths & Marriages.

        3.This person had an assumed family name recorded on TICA when under 18 years of age (see N1 above), but has now turned 18 and wishes to revert to their birth/legal name as recorded on their birth certificate.

        This name change would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

        TypeName Reason
        CURRJones, Dianne Michelle
        HISTSmith, Dianne, MichelleCORR
        This person may revert to the use of their birth/legal name of Smith without the production of a name change document, provided they can produce the standard EOI documents in their birth/legal name, as:
        • the link is shown in their Name History on TICA
        • and there have been no changes by Deed Poll or Name Change Certificate

        Once this person has reverted back to their previous birth/legal name, any further name changes must be supported by a Change of Name Certificate issued by the Registrar of Births, Deaths & Marriages.
      Name Change Certificate required
      Name Change Certificate required

        This name change would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

    N4. Replacement / re-issue / renew & retaining name & link in TICA
    N4. Replacement / re-issue / renew & retaining name & link in TICA
      Applies to
      Applies to
        A customer who:
        • Is applying:
          • for a Replacement Licence,
          • for a licence to be re-issued or
          • to renew a licence
        and
        • Is retaining the name currently shown in TICA and on the Driver Licence
        and
        • There is a link in TICA matching the names on the EOI documents provided

      Variation Requirements
      • Standard EOI
      • There is a link in TICA between the names on the EOI documents presented
      Examples
      Examples
        Example 1
        Example 1
          This person started with a family name of Smith and changed to Jones by marriage.

          These name changes would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

          TypeName Reason
          CURR Jones, Dianne, Michelle
          HISTSmith, Dianne, MichelleMARR

          EOI Documents produced for replacement licence:

          Birth Certificate = Dianne Michelle Smith
          Category B x2 = Dianne Michelle Jones

          The replacement/re-issued/renewed licence may be issued in the name Dianne Michelle Jones as there is a link between the EOI produced on TICA.

        Example 2
        Example 2
          This person started with a family name of Smith, changed to Jones by marriage, then to Carter by a 2nd marriage.

          These name changes would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

          TypeName Reason
          CURR Carter, Dianne, Michelle
          HISTJones, Dianne, MichelleMARR
          HISTSmith, Dianne, MichelleMARR

          EOI Documents produced for replacement licence:

          Birth Certificate = Dianne Michelle Smith
          Category B x2 = Dianne Michelle Carter

          The replacement/re-issued/renewed licence may be issued in the name Dianne Michelle Carter as there is a link between the EOI produced on TICA.

        Example 3
        Example 3
          This person started with a birth name of Smith and used an assumed name of Jones (exemption from name change approved as under 18 and unable to locate parents to change name)

          These name changes would appear in the Name History on TICA as follows:

          TypeName Reason
          CURRJones, Dianne, Michelle
          HISTSmith, Dianne, MichelleCORR

          EOI Documents produced for replacement licence:

          Birth Certificate = Dianne Michelle Smith
          Category B x2 = Dianne Michelle Jones

          The replacement/re-issued/renewed licence may be issued in the assumed name Dianne Michelle Jones as there is a link between the EOI produced on TICA.

      N5. Replacement / re-issue / renew & retaining name & NO link in TICA
      N5. Replacement / re-issue / renew & retaining name & NO link in TICA
        Applies to
        Applies to
          A customer who:
          • Is applying:
            • for a Replacement Licence
            • for a licence to be re-issued
            • to renew a licence
          and
          • Is retaining the name currently shown in TICA and on the Driver Licence
          and
          • There is no link in TICA matching the names on the EOI documents provided

          Variation Requirements
          • Standard EOI
          • If only the assumed name / non birth name is recorded in the Name History on TICA, the licence may be issued if:
            • A link between the names can be found on a previous driver licence application form
            Example
            Example
              Birth Certificate number produced matches that recorded on previous driver licence application form or a copy of a name change document is filed with the application

          Example
          Example
            This person started with a family name of Smith and changed to Jones by marriage. The person did not get a driver licence until after the marriage.

            The Name History on TICA would appear as follows:

            TypeName Reason
            CURRJones, Dianne, Michelle
            EOI Documents produced for replacement licence:

            Birth Certificate no. 1234 = Dianne Michelle Smith
            Category B x2 = Dianne Michelle Jones

            The replacement/re-issued/renewed licence may be issued in the name Dianne Michelle Jones as long as a TMR document can be retrieved providing a link between the names Smith and Jones. eg. The birth certificate number 1234 has been recorded on a previous driver licence application.

      N6. Is applying for a Queensland product in a new name
      N6. Is applying for a Queensland product in a new name
        Applies to
        Applies to
          A customer who:
          • Is applying for a Queensland product in a new name
          and
          • Is recorded in TICA under an old name

          Variation Requirements
          • Standard EOI
            • A link between the two names can be established in NEVDIS or another document issued by BDM.
            Example
            Example
              Customer previously held Queensland licence in maiden name. Customer then transferred interstate and married.

              The Name History on TICA would appear as follows:
              TypeName Reason
              CURRJones, Dianne, Michelle

              EOI document produced for licence transfer:
              Interstate licence in Smith, Dianne, Michelle
              Category B x 2 = Dianne Michelle Smith

              The TICA record should be updated and the Queensland product issued in the married name provided that the NEVDIS record displays both names or a document from BDM has been provided. This includes a child's BDM Certificate which displays both names.
