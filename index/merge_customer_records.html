Merge Customer Records (851)

Merge Customer Records
What it isMerging is the combining of duplicated customer records on TICA into a single customer record.

Customers may have duplicated records created on TICA due to:
  • Operator creating a second customer record as their customer search was too broad or incomplete and did not identify the customer's existing record
  • Customer re-identifies themselves using a legal name different to the one currently recorded on TICA
  • Moving of data between systems
What it doesDuring the merge transaction one CRNs must be nominated as the preferred or 'primary' CRN. The other CRN defaults as the 'secondary' CRN.

After a merge all information is recorded against the 'primary' CRN. This has the following effects:
  • The following from both CRNs will be combined and recorded against the primary CRN:
    • Customer name and address histories
    • All current licences, authorities, vehicle registrations, qualifications and products (inc. smartcards)
    • Licence, authority and product histories, including traffic histories and infringements
    • Any customer account balances
    • Finance records e.g. receipts, payment requests
    • Any EOI document records
    • Any digital photos and signatures
  • Any smartcards originally issued under the secondary CRN have their respective digital certificates queued for deactivation
  • Secondary CRN ceases to exist, if secondary CRN is entered for display the primary CRN will automatically display
Merge RulesThe following rules are to be followed when considering the merging of two customer records

Rules for Merging
Rules for Merging
    Records are only to be merged where this is a high level of certainty that they are the same customer. If there is any doubt the records should not be merged. A merge transaction cannot be reversed.

    Selecting the Primary CRN
    Selecting the Primary CRN
      The primary CRN should be the one with:
      • The most historical details
      • A current driver licence
    Customer Details
    Customer Details
      Records with different names (eg spelling differences, not all names recorded) can be merged. There is no requirement to update the incorrect name prior to the merge.
    Active products on CRN's
    Active products on CRN's
      • All active products (eg 18+ cards, learner permits) on the secondary CRN must be surrendered and all Smartcard's returned prior to the merge.
      • If the merge will create the need to replace a product eg APA was on the secondary CRN and a Driver licence on the primary) card the customer must be present and return the product prior to the merge.
    Merging multiple CRN's
    Merging multiple CRN's
      • Only two CRN's can be merged at a time. In all merges the primary CRN should remain and all records merged individually to that CRN.
    CRN with digital images
    CRN with digital images
      IMU must be consulted prior to merging where:
      • Both CRN's have digital images
      • A CRN has a digital image with a status of PENDING or an image under review (IMGR) or image declined (IMGD) restriction
    CRNS with Qualifications
    CRNS with Qualifications
      Customer records with the following qualification types are not to be merged:
      AIS (Approved Inspection Station)
      • EDIC (Service Access Client)
      • INSU (CTP Insurer)
      • NDEA (Motor Dealer - New Vehicles Only)
      • SILS (SILAS Organisation)
    Secondary CRN's with a Planned Payment Scheme (PPS)
    Secondary CRN's with a Planned Payment Scheme (PPS)
      Where the secondary CRN has a active PPS account a card will need to be reissued to the primary CRN. A new card can be requested through the Electronic Business Unit
    PrepL CRN's
    PrepL CRN's
      The merging of CRN's is not to be undertaken until after the customer has completed the PrepL course. PrepL merge scenarios must be followed as outlined in Driver Licence > 5. Testing > PrepL > Common PrepL scenarios that require operator action > Merging CRNs
Merge RequirementsDocuments
Before merging two customer records the following documents are required to be completed/obtained:
1.Completed Merge Request Form (Individual/Organisation)
2.Screen print of address histories for both customer records
3.Screen print of infringement listings for both customer records
4.Screen print of Customer Account Statement if customer participates in Planned Payment Screen
5.Email approval from IMU for merge to proceed if both customer records have digital photos and signatures recorded
