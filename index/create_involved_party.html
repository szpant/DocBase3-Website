        Other Staff
          Systems Security considers and grants access to people other than Transport Service Delivery, Customer Service Direct and Compliance Staff, such as Road Safety and System Management (RSSM), Finance and Main Roads.

          Access can be requested by completing;
          • F3457 Create/Modify Novell & Lotus Notes User Application
          or/and
          • F3277 Access to On-Line TRAILS Profile Request

          Access the On-Line TRAILS Profile Request (F3277) and email it to Systems Security
          After F3277 is sent, mark that you have sent it to Systems Security for your profile, then email the remaining paperwork to the Service Centre (IMD's IT Helpdesk) or your department's respective IT Helpdesk.
