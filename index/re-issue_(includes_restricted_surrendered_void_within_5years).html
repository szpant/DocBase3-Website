Re-issue (includes restricted/surrendered/void within 5years) (3313)

Applies to
Applies to
    Re-Issue:
    • Prov/Prob/Open licence after a period of inactivity due to:
      • Cancellation
      • Disqualification
      • Dishonoured Cheque
      • Voluntary Surrender (eg. travel)
    • Restricted licence after a period of inactivity due to:
      • Cancellation
      • Disqualification
      • Dishonoured Cheque
      • Voluntary Surrender
    and
    • Issue a Restricted licence (after DD offence)

Conduct 1:1 Image Match (if required)
Conduct 1:1 Image Match (if required)
    Conduct 1:1 Image Match (TICA)

    ~~~ Related TICA Steps ~~~
    Capturing facial image
    Capturing facial image
      If TICA prompts to capture the customer's facial image, direct the customer to the seat for the default camera and request them to:

      • Sit on the seat provided and to stay within the markings
        Note: If customer uses a mobility aid, retract or remove the chair and have the customer position themselves within the designated markings of the photo area
      • Look straight ahead at the camera (a frontal image of their face is required)
      • Remove any head attire or sunglasses (unless there are valid religious, cultural or medical reasons for not doing so, then refer to Manager)

      The customer must remain in your line of vision during the entire transaction.
      Once the facial image is successfully captured, request the customer to return to your counter.
    1:1 image match fails
    1:1 image match fails
      If the 1:1 image match fails contact the Identity Management Unit for an assessment of the image

      If IMU advises that the image match is unacceptable:
      • IMU will advise what action is to be taken
        Refer to:
        Smartcard Facial Image Recognition

      End of procedure

      If IMU advises that the image match is acceptable:
      • Continue with procedure

Conduct Prerequisite Testing (if required)
Conduct Prerequisite Testing (if required)
    Written
    Written Road Rules Test
    Written Road Rules Test
      • Check customer has a paid written test fee
      • Check customer has not already done a written test in the same day
      • Prepare the Answer Sheet:
        • Have customer complete applicant details section
        • Record CRN and sign answer sheet (Operator)
        • Show customer where to sit & what to do when finished
      • Advise Customer:
        • They cannot talk to anyone other than a CSO/interpreter during the test
        • They cannot refer to any other materials to do the test
        • Cheating will result in a void test and failed result
    'I' Candidacy
    Alcohol Ignition Interlock Program (AIIP)
    Alcohol Ignition Interlock Program (AIIP)
      • Verify if the customer is required to comply with Qld AIIP Program ('I' candidate). (Using the 'Verify I Candidacy' button under 'Additional Requirements')
      • If customer is an 'I' candidate, advise the customer that:
        • Their licence will be endorsed with an 'I' licence condition code
        • There is an additional fee payable upon re-licensing
        • They must comply with the requirement of the AIIP
        • Provide them with an AIIP brochure
        • Continue with process

    Transport Licence History Detail (Formless centres only)
    Transport Licence History Detail (Formless centres only)
      If the transaction was processed at a Formless centre a Transport Licence History Detail must be generated.

      Refer: Obtain Details Report (TICA)

      The operator must explain the declaration to the customer and ensure they sign and date in the designated space. Key information should be highlighted such as:

      • licence type and class
      • effective and expiry date
      • transaction details
    Verify Receipt details
    Verify Receipt details

      • If any corrections are required, amend the relevant information and reprint the Driver Licence Receipt

Give/Advise customer
Give/Advise customer
    Give customer
    • Customer portion of Medical Certificate (where applicable)
    • Their original EOI/residency documents
    • Driver Licence Receipt
    • Copy of the Driver Licence Classes, Codes and Conditions fact sheet (form S4815)
    • Financial Receipt (if applicable)

    Advise customer
    • That the Driver Licence Receipt authorises them to drive until the smartcard is received, or for up to 28 days, whichever is sooner.
    • The Driver Licence will be posted by standard mail in an unmarked envelope to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address

    If Licence has an LNDR Restriction

    • If licence has been endorsed with an 'I' licence condition code they must comply with the Alcohol Ignition Interlock Program (AIIP) requirements.
