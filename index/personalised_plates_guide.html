Personalised Plates Guide (3092)


What are They?Personalised plates can display an alpha and/or numeric combination:
  • Chosen by the customer
  • A standard number that is personalised
Why Personalise a Plate Number
Why Personalise a Plate Number
    Personalised plates may be ordered by any one wishing to:
    • Attach to a registered vehicle
    • Give as a gift
    • Own the number and leave vacant
    • Retain for attaching to a vehicle in the future

Transfer Fee Exemptions
Transfer Fee Exemptions
    Transfer fees are exempt if the plates are transferred to:
    • A member of the immediate family of the disposer and the transfer is wholly by way of a gift. (Immediate family is specified as being the spouse or parent or child of the person in whose name the personalised plates are currently recorded, or the spouse or parent or child jointly with that person (the word spouse may refer to either married or de- facto relationship).
    • A beneficiary, administrator or executor of a deceased estate (includes transfer to the Estate of <deceased>).
    • The recipient of a gift (TICA exemption reason - Gift Code SPEC). If personalised plates are purchased as a gift (for example, as a birthday present) the initial transfer from the purchaser to the gift recipient is exempt from transfer fee.
      • When full payment is received by PPQ for plates purchased as a gift, PPQ email a "Fee Exempt" letter to the purchaser. The purchaser is advised to give the letter to the gift recipient with the number plates. The gift recipient is to present this letter to TMR when transferring the number plates to their name. If the gift recipient does not present the letter at the time of transfer the CSC operator may access the PPQ website or contact PPQ directly via the PPQ contact number to confirm purchase was a gift and transfer transaction can be processed with fee exempted.
      If the disposer lodges the Seller's Copy of the Personalised Number Plates Transfer Application (F2963) they aren't required to pay the transfer fee. A debt will be raised in the acquirer's account and a Customer Account Statement will be automatically sent.
    Closed Organisations
    Closed Organisations
      For an organisation with a closed status on TICA, an exemption can only be applied if:
      • The new plate owner provides a written statement that the personalised plate/s have not been on-sold; and
      • The plates are being retained by:
        • A shareholder of the organisation
        • Legal entity (second registered operator) responsible for the business or trust; or
        • An immediate family member (defined above)

      TICA Exemption Reason - SPEC

      Note:If the plates have been on-sold (including on-sold to one of the above) by the organisation, the personalised plate transfer fee is applicable.

Rights of the Plate Owner
Rights of the Plate Owner
    The owner of the plate number can:
    • Transfer the plate with the vehicle to another owner
    • Retain the plate as a vacant plate if registration is cancelled
    • Transfer/sell vacant plate to another customer
    • Retain ownership of the number but attached it to another's person's vehicle
    • Surrender the plate if not currently being used
    • Change the plate type, colours and theme

    Note:The following applies where a CONF Restriction is recorded by the Queensland Police Service (QPS):
    Vehicle is not being transferred along with the plates
    Vehicle is not being transferred along with the plates
      If the vehicle is not being transferred along with the plates (ie only the plates are being transferred), this transaction cannot be processed during the confiscation period as TICA will not allow the plates to be removed from the vehicle while the CONF Restriction is applied.
    Transfer of plates that are attached to a vehicle in TICA
    Transfer of plates that are attached to a vehicle in TICA
      If the plates are not attached to a vehicle in TICA, this transaction may be processed during the confiscation period.
    For more information on the notice, advise customer to contact QPS directly by visiting the Motor Vehicle Impoundment page on the QPS website at
    https://www.police.qld.gov.au/programs/roadSafety/vehimpound/

Corrections
Corrections
    Prior to doing any corrections or changes to the ordered plate you must always contact the Product Management Unit (PMU) who will determine if the plate manufacturing process can be stopped.

    Note:Once the plate shows as ordered on TICA TMR has already paid for the manufacturing fee.

    Customer Error
    Customer Error
      Customer:Plates not dispatched
      Changes their mind or misunderstood the choice they have madeEither:
      • Reprocess plate order free of charge (at Managers discretion)
        or
      • Charge customer the manufacturing fee and then reprocess order
      Plates dispatched
      Either:
      • Customer accepts plates as ordered
        or
      • Surrender and cancel received plates and process a new order at full cost
      Customer:Plates not dispatched
      Changes their mind an no longer wants the platesEither:
      • Refund the whole plate fee (at Managers discretion)
        or
      • Charge the manufacturing fee and refund the difference
      Plates dispatched
      Either:
      • Customer can accept the plates
        or
      • Plates are surrendered and cancelled with no refund

    Operator Error
    Operator Error
      If it is proved that the operator made an error when ordering the plates, the error will be corrected at no further cost to the customer.
Lay-by of Personalised Plates and the purchase of Gift Vouchers
Lay-by of Personalised Plates and the purchase of Gift Vouchers
    Lay-by:
    A lay-by system is available through PPQ for the purchase of:
    • Personalised plates
    • Prestige plates
    Customers should be advised:
    • To contact PPQ to register for the system
    • All payments are made through PPQ
    Prestige Plates:
    • Lay-by Terms: 6 months
    • Minimum deposit: $500
    • Minimum repayment per fortnight/month: $200
    Personalised/EURO/Theme Plates:
    • Lay-by Terms: 3 months
    • Minimum deposit: $100
    • Minimum repayment per fortnight/month: $50

    Gift Vouchers:
    • must be purchased through PPQ (no longer available at CSCs)
    • direct customer to PPQ 136 356 or ppq.com.au

Vacant Personalised Plates
Vacant Personalised Plates
    These are plates not currently attached to a vehicle.

    Before attaching to a vehicle customer must:
    • Lodge application for attachment
    • Surrender currently attached standard plates
      or
    • Lodge application to have currently attached personalised/customised plates removed

      Note:Vacant personalised plates cannot be attached to a vehicle currently under a Number Plate Confiscation Notice issued by the Queensland Police Service (QPS). For more information on the notice, advise customer to contact QPS directly by visiting the Motor Vehicle Impoundment page on the QPS website at:
      https://www.police.qld.gov.au/programs/roadSafety
