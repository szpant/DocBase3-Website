Process an IA Renewal application (4551)

Applies to
Applies to
    Processing an Industry Application for renewal of a:
    • Dangerous Goods Driver
    • Driver/Rider Trainer
    • Pilot/Escort Vehicle Driver
    • Tow Truck Driver/Assistant
    and
    • Traffic Controller
1
Check Transaction Requirements & Eligibility
Check Transaction Requirements & Eligibility
    Dangerous Goods Driver Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Driver Rider Trainer Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Pilot Escort Driver Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Tow Truck Driver/Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Traffic Controller Transaction Requirements Checklist
      If eligibility or requirements are not met, refer below to Refuse to Consider Application
2
Check documents and EOI
Check documents and EOI
    Item
    Details
    Application Form
    • Check that the application form/s has been completed correctly
    • Ask the customer to sign & date the application form in your presence
      • If the application form is not completed, signed or dated, either:
      • Have the customer complete it, otherwise refer below to Refuse to Consider Application
    • Complete Office Use Section of application form
    • Date stamp original application form

      Note: Do not stamp or mark any original EOI/qualification/competency documents
    • Check that the customer has supplied their contact details (Telephone/Fax and/or Email)
    EOI Documents
    • Compare signatures on EOI and application form
    • Check EOI document authenticity (if required).
      Refer to: Evidence of Identity > Recording and Verifying Documents > Manually Verifying Authenticity of documents
    Competency Documents
    • Check documents meet requirements (transaction checklist)
    • Photocopy or scan (if required)
    Medical
    • Check Medical Certificate (if required)
3
Conduct 1:1 Image Match (if required)
Conduct 1:1 Image Match (if required)
    Conduct 1:1 Image Match
    Related TICA Steps
    Capturing facial image
    Capturing facial image
      If TICA prompts to capture the customer's facial image, direct the customer to
      the seat for the default camera and request them to:
      • Sit on the seat provided and to stay within the markings
        Note: If customer uses a mobility aid, retract or remove the chair and have the customer position themselves within the designated markings of the photo area
      • Look straight ahead at the camera (a frontal image of their face is required)
      • Remove any head attire or sunglasses (unless there are valid religious, cultural or medical reasons for not doing so, then refer to Manager)
        .
        The customer must remain in your line of vision during the entire transaction.
        .
      • Once the facial image is successfully captured, request the customer to return to your counter.
    1:1 image match fails
    1:1 image match fails
      If the 1:1 image match fails contact the Identity Management Unit for an assessment of the image

      If IMU advises that the image match is unacceptable:
      • IMU will advise what action is to be taken
        Refer to:         Smartcard facial image recognition
        End of procedure
      .
      If IMU advises that the image match is acceptable:
        Continue with procedure
4
Refuse to Consider Application (if required)
Refuse to Consider Application (if required)
    Prepare Refuse to Consider notice (if required). Refer to : Product policy guide > Transactions Details > Refuse to Consider > Written Notices
    Give customer
    • Their original EOI / other documents presented
    • Application form (Keep the application form if 1:1 image match is performed)
    • Notice requesting further documents or information relevant to the application (if applicable) refer above
    Advise customer
    • That the application cannot be considered at this stage
    • The reason the application cannot be considered
    • The application may be considered when the required eligibility is met, or requested documents provided
    File Documents (if applicable)

    End of procedure

Record EOI Documents
Record EOI Documents
    Record/Update/Exempt/Invalidate EOI, Name Change, Residency Doc or Exemption
    Note: All newly presented documents must be recorded, even if in excess of minimum EOI requirements.
6
Conduct searches and check eligibility documentation
Conduct searches and check eligibility documentation
    1.Use the Transaction checklist to determine competency and eligibility requirements
    2.Confirm that the licence requirements are met (TICA)
    3.Perform onscreen assessment of traffic history. Print only when the history is unsuitable or questionable or to be refused based on the the applicants traffic history.
    Print Traffic History(TICA)
    4.Confirm if criminal history has been finalised
    Finalise Criminal History Request (TICA)
    If customer has text advising that criminal history exists contact Industry Licensing Policy to obtain history. Assess on screen. Print only when the history is questionable or to be refused based on the the applicants criminal history history.
7
Assess application; approve or refuse application
Assess application; approve or refuse application
    To assess the search results, use the criteria in the:
    • Dangerous Goods Driver Licence Guide > Eligibility Requirements > Assessing Traffic & Criminal Histories
    • Driver Trainer Guide -> Eligibility -> Eligibility Requirements > Assessing Traffic & Criminal Histories
    • Pilot / Escort Licence Guide > Eligibility Requirements > Assessing Traffic & Criminal Histories
    • Tow Truck Driver/Assistant Certificate Guide -> Eligibility Requirements -> Assessing Traffic & Criminal Histories
    • Traffic Controller Guide -> Eligibility Requirements > Assessing Traffic & Criminal Histories
    • Determine if the application is to be approved or refused using the suitability assessment, eligibility and ability to meet competencies.
    • Record the decision to approve or refuse by completing 'Office Use Only' section on application form.

Record Refuse Result (if required) (TICA)
Record Refuse Result (if required) (TICA)
    Record Refuse Decision (TICA)
    Record Refuse Decision (TICA)
      • Record the decision to approve or refuse in TICA
        Refuse Application (TICA)
    Advise Applicant of Decision if Refused
    Advise Applicant of Decision if Refused
      Applicants must be advised within 14 days of the date of the decision.
      • Prepare refusal letter and send to applicant
        Note: Refer to Individual Policy documents (ie: Dangerous Goods Licence Guide, etc) for refusal letters and guidelines
      Additonal actions required for Dangerous Goods Licences
      Additonal actions required for Dangerous Goods Licences
          • Include a copy of 'Reviews and Appeals Information Bulletin'
          • Record text message on TICA outlining details
          • Notify DGU (Dangerous Goods Unit) by email when refusal processed
            (address:dgu@tmr.qld.gov.au) advising:
            • CRN
            • Applicant's full name
            • Applicant's date of birth
            • Refusal date & eligible reapply date
          Refund the appropriate fees
      • Include with filing documentation:
        • A copy of the refusal letter in the filing documentation;
        • Relevant documentation that supported the decision to refuse
          (e.g. Traffic printout and/or Criminal history documents).
      • Retain filing documentation for 28 days to allow time for the appeal process to expire
      • File as per office procedure
      • Once the review period has been exhausted, forward a copy of the refusal letter and the application for filing to Iron Mountain.
