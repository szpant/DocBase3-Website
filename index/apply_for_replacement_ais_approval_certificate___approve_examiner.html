Apply for Replacement AIS Approval Certificate / Approve Examiner (1345)

Applies to
Applies to
    The replacement of the AIS Approval Notice / Approved Examiner Accreditation Document.
1
Before You Start
Before You Start
    Check that the customer has:
    • Approved Inspection Station Scheme Change Replacement Application form (F4638) for a replacement Approval Notice
      or
    • Approved Examiner's Application form (F2339) for replacement approved examiner accreditation document

      Note:There are no fees for this transaction

2
Check Documents and EOI
Check Documents and EOI
    • Ensure the applications forms are completed and signed by the applicant.
    • Ensure EOI requirements are met.

    Refer: QT Shop > 1. Customer Products > AIS Guide > AIS EOI Requirements

3
Finalise Transaction
Finalise Transaction
    • Date stamp the application form
    • Lodge application by scanning all sides of the application form and supporting documents and then email the scanned documents from the CSC mailbox to the AIS Administrator
4
Contacts
Contacts
    AIS Administrator

5
Finalise Documents
