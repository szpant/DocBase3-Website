Learner Licence Logbook
Learner Licence Logbook
    A Learner Licence Logbook is available in a hard copy form or via the learner logbook app. When a customer is issued with a learner licence they should be encouraged to use the learner licence app and only be issued with a hard copy logbook if requested.

    If a customer initially opts to use the app and then changes their mind they can be issued with a logbook free of charge.

    Note:If a customer loses their logbook they will have to pay a replacement fee.

    Hardcopy Learner Logbook:
    • May be purchased at any time
    • Is not limited in the number that may be purchased
      Note:There are no refunds if the customer no longer requires the Logbook

    Hardcopy logbooks are available from:
    • TMR Customer Services Centres
    • QGAP Offices
    • QPS licence issuing agencies
    • TMR Internet Site
    The learner logbook app can be downloaded from Google Play or the App Store.

    State Boat Harbour and Buoy Mooring fees
      What is a State Boat Harbour
      What is a State Boat Harbour
        There are 11 government owned state boat harbours in Queensland.

        State Boat Harbours are designated harbours managed by MSQ. State Boat Harbours have moorings that are available for lease.

        For further details of state boat harbours, refer to the below link:
        http://www.msq.qld.gov.au/Waterways/Recreational-boating-infrastructure/State-boat-harbours.aspx

        As State Boat Harbour Mooring Fees are issued through SAP they must be receipted as an Accounts Receivable Invoice.

      What is a buoy mooring
      What is a buoy mooring
        Customers may apply to MSQ for approval to install a buoy mooring. A buoy mooring is attached to the seabed and consists of a device with a system of ropes, cables or chains attached for the purpose of mooring a ship. Buoy moorings are located outside of State Boat Harbour areas. Buoy mooring holders pay an annual fee.

    GST & Non Customer Products
      GST applies to all saleable items.

      QGAPS and Tax Invoices for Miscellaneous Non-Customer Products
      TMR will not issue tax invoices for other businesses done at QGAPS as:
      • TICA is not programmed to distinguish between GST applicable or exempt products of other Departments
      • It is the other Department’s responsibility to forward a tax invoice, upon request, to the customer

      For further information refer to: Admin > 3. Money > Administration Money Guide
