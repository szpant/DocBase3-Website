HVRAS Training Package (4660)

What it isThe HVRAS Training Package is delivered to train applicants on how to inspect and measure motor vehicles more than 4.5 tonnes GVM and trailers more than 750kg ATM prior to registration to ensure that they comply with:
  • dimensional limits
  • approved load sharing axle systems
  • safe tyre limits
  • vehicle identifiers (engine number, VIN, chassis number, modification plates)

Note:HVRAS accredited persons, cannot perform inspections for imported vehicles/trailers that have never been registered in Australia before.

The training packages include methods of measuring vehicles, dimensional limitations and load limitations.

Further information is available in the HVRAS Information Sheet which can be found on the TMR website at: www.tmr.qld.gov.au/hvras

Note:A HVRAS accreditation document will not be issued until payment is verified.
Fees
Standard Training Fees
Standard Training Fees
    A standardised training fee applies to HVRAS courses. This fee includes the trainer's salary to develop, deliver and maintain the course, administration costs such as travel, course material and catering. The course fee is $235, which may take up to 2 days.
Regional Training Officer Requirements
Regional Training Officer Requirements
Regional Training Officer Requirements
    Regional Training Officers must:
    • deliver training and assess the trainee's level of competence
    • determine if the trainee has passed or failed the training course
    • If the applicant is unsuccessful advise them they will need to complete the HVRAS course and pay the fee again if they want to be accredited in the future.

    Collate and forward to Training Coordinator (TC):
    • Copy of receipt for payment of training fee (Accredited person only)
    • Completed Competency Checklist
    • completed Heavy Vehicle/Trailer Assessment form
    • completed Vehicle/Trailer Details form
    • completed Vehicle/Trailer Inspections sheets

    Post the completed Application (F4331) of competent trainees to HVRAS Administrator
