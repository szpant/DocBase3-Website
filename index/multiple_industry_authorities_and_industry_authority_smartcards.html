IA period/term alignment
Authority term alignment
Authority term alignment
    Industry licensing types (i.e. Dangerous Goods Driver, Traffic Controller, Pilot/Escort Driver, Tow Truck Driver/Assistant and Driver/Rider Trainer) do not have the flexibility to allow a shorter or longer term than the standard accreditation period (i.e. 1 to 3 years, depending on the product).

    However, driver authorisations may be aligned to the same expiry date (day, month and year) of an existing Industry Authority.

    Refer to: Passenger Transport Document Database > Part C DA Policy & Procedure > 1. DA Policy > 1.26 Period of Issue
Specific Smartcard - Industry Licensing
Approval
Approval of a Multiple Industry Authority
Approval of a Multiple Industry Authority
    Approval of each product
    Approval of each product
      Customers are required to lodge the application and meet all prerequisites relevant to the new authority including all prescribed fees, (if applicable).

      The approval of each product will be determined on the product applied for. Refer to the relevant product guides.

      Note:A smartcard fee will only be charged on the first application lodged and will be used when an approval occurs. When a second application is lodged and the system already has at least one other lodged application, where a smartcard fee is receipted, it does not ask for another smartcard fee. Each smartcard produced for an IA that attracts a fee requires a smartcard fee.

    System messages when approving a Multiple IA
    System messages when approving a Multiple IA
      When approving an Industry Authority, if there is an outstanding lodged application for another industry authority type, the following message will be displayed on TICA:
      "Customer has outstanding applications for smartcard being issued.
      Do you want to continue?"

      Check the outstanding application/s and see if all requirements have been met and if possible approve the remaining application/s.

      If all applications are not approved on the same day, contact the customer and advise them of the following:
      • They may be required to pay for another smartcard fee, and
      • They will need to return to TMR to record another PIN for the new smartcard, OR
      • The operator will have to exempt the PIN capture (via prerequisite) for the new smartcard.

      The customer is required to advise whether they need the approved IA straight away or if they may prefer to come back to the centre and complete the above requirements before the outstanding application/s can be approved.

    Approval of a Multiple IA when one product is free
    Approval of a Multiple IA when one product is free
      When processing an IA application approval where multiple IA applications have been lodged, and one of the products applied for is free of charge, one of the following will apply:
      • If approving both or all products at once, the approval can be processed in any order.
      • If only approving the product that is free of charge, process this application as per normal. When approving this IA, the system will request a smartcard fee. Therefore a manual adjustment will be required for the outstanding smartcard fee (refer below).
      • If only approving the product/s that required a fee, process approval as per normal procedure. (The industry authority application that does not require a smartcard fee can be processed without the system requiring any adjustment, if it is the only outstanding application.)

      Adjustment of smartcard fee
      Adjustment of smartcard fee

    Adding a new authority to an existing Smartcard Industry Authority
    Adding a new authority to an existing Smartcard Industry Authority
      Customers adding a new authority to an existing Smartcard Industry Authority are required to lodge the application and meet all prerequisites relevant to the new authority including all prescribed fees.

      The customer will retain the existing Industry Authority smartcard until the additional authority has been approved and the new smartcard has been received in the mail.

    Destruction of superseded Smartcard
    Destruction of superseded Smartcard
      The customer will be instructed to destroy the superseded Smartcard Industry Authority and/or Interim Industry Authority once they receive their new smartcard in the mail.

    Adding a new authority to an existing laminate IA
    Adding a new authority to an existing laminate IA
      When a customer with a laminated authority attends a Smartcard CSC or QGAP Office to apply for another authority, the customer is required to lodge the application and meet all prerequisites relevant to the new authority including all prescribed fees.

      Until the approval of the new authority,the customer retains the laminated industry authority. Once approved, the Smartcard Industry Authority will be mailed out displaying all approved Industry Authorities.

    Destruction of laminated IA
    Destruction of laminated IA
      The customer will be instructed to destroy the superseded laminated industry authority and/or Interim Industry Authority once they receive their new smartcard in the mail.
Renewing multiple IA's
Renewing multiple authorities
Renewing multiple authorities
    The Smartcard Multiple Industry Authority displays the expiry date of each individual authority. When an authority has been renewed and approved while the customer is at the counter when all prerequisites have been met:

    Without changes
    Without changes
      • The current smartcard can be retained by the customer and is to be destroyed by them when the new smartcard is received in the mail;
      • The Interim Industry Authority may be issued to the customer

    With changes
    With changes
      • The current smartcard may need to be retained by the CSO;
        Refer to:         Smartcard > Return
      • The Interim Industry Authority will be issued to the customer to allow them to continue to operate a vehicle/s for each renewed industry class.
Voluntary Surrender Authority
Voluntary Surrender Authority
Voluntary Surrender Authority
    If a customer has a Smartcard displaying multiple authorities and they choose to surrender an authority type during the currency but retain the other/s existing authority/s, the existing smartcard must be surrendered and returned and a new replacement smartcard will need to be issued.

    Refer to: The Smartcard Guide > Replacement > When to Replace- Explanative > 10. Current product exists - includes MLI removal on DL (PROD)
