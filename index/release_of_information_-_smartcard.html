Release of a Digital Photograph
What it is
What it is
What it is
    A request for the release of a copy of a stored digital photo from the following records:
    • Driver licence
    • Industry Authority
    • Marine Licence
    • Photo Identification Card
    • Non-TMR smartcard
Who can request
Requests for a digital photograph
Requests for a digital photograph
    Applications for the request of the cardholder's own digitial photograph
    Applications for the request of the cardholder's own digitial photograph
      An application for the request of a release of a copy of a digital photo can only be made by the person who:
      • Requests their own digital photo; and
      • Has satisfactorily established their connection to the requested digital photo, by EOI document and 1:1 image verification; and
      • Applies in the approved form (Release of Digital Photo Application (F4669))

    Applications for the request of another person's digital photograph
    Applications for the request of another person's digital photograph
      Police Officer
      Police Officer
        A police officer can apply for a copy of a digital photo if they:
        • Are enforcing an offence under Photo ID Act; TT Act; TO(MS) Act;TO(PT) Act; TO(RUM) Act
        • Are enforcing an offence for dangerous operation of a vehicle Criminal Code Act. s328A
        • Are enforcing, investigating or prosecuting under criminal law.

        Any police request for a copy of a digital photo are to be directed to the Identity Management Unit.

        Note:Police requests for a copy of the digital photo must not be accepted at Customer Service Centres.

      Power of Attorney holder
      Power of Attorney holder
        A person can apply for a copy of a digital photo of another person if they hold one of the following:
        • A general power of attorney authorising the attorney nominated in the form to sign documents on behalf of the person whose digital photo is sought
        • An enduring power of attorney nominated in the form to sign documents on behalf of the person whose digital photo is sought.
          Power of Attorney

          Note:A power of attorney about the card holder’s financial matters is not acceptable.

      Court Subpoena server
      Court Subpoena server
        The chief executive may be required, by subpoena, to produce a copy of the digital photo to a court.

        Any request for a copy of a digital photo by subpoena must be directed to the IMU.

      Interstate Driver Licensing Authorities
      Interstate Driver Licensing Authorities
        An interstate driver licensing authority which grants or issues driver licences may apply for a copy of a digital photo which was used to issue a driver licence.

        Any request for a copy of a digital photo by an interstate licensing authority must be directed to the Identity Management Unit.

        Note:Driver Licensing Authorities requests for a copy of a digital photo must not be accepted at Customer Service Centres.

      TMR Transport Inspector
      TMR Transport Inspector
        A TMR transport inspector may request an digital photograph when performing duties under the TT Act; TO(PT) Act; TO(RUM) Act and NHVL
        Refer: Request for Customer Image

    Document Requirements for a copy of a digital photograph
    Document Requirements for a copy of a digital photograph
      By an Individual
      By an Individual
        An application for the release of a digital photograph must be submitted in writing, using Release of Digital Photo Application (F4669) and include the following:
        • Name;
        • Address;
        • CRN (if known and available);
        • Confirmation the request is for a digital photo;
        • Verification of the date or time period for which the digital photo is requested; and the
        • Applicant's signature and date of the application
      By a Power of Attorney
      By a Power of Attorney
        • Release of Digital Photo Application (F4669) signed by the applicant
        • Power of Attorney document, Refer: Individual > Power of Attorney
        • Standard EOI documents required of the Power of Attorney
        • Power of Attorney signature Refer: Individual > Power of Attorney

    Processing the request for a copy of a digital photograph
    Processing the request for a copy of a digital photograph
      The operator needs to:
      • Check application (Release of Digital Photo Application (F4669)); and
      • Verify the identity of the person requesting the digital photo (Check EOI); AND
      • Take digital photo for a 1:1 facial image verification (Cardholder's own photo requests only)
        Note:This step is not taken where the application is lodged by the holder of a power of attorney
      • Verify postal address on the application against the postal address held on TICA.
      • Complete the section Office Use Only on Release of Digital Photo Application (F4669)
      • Scan and email a copy of the completed application to IMU
    Releasing the digital photo
    Releasing the digital photo
      IMU will:
      • Print a copy of the digital photo; and
      • Send the copy of the digital photo to the customer’s nominated postal address.

      Other Request for Release of images
      Other Request for Release of images
        Digitised signatures may be released by IMU to the following persons when a current Letter of Authority is given:
        • Police
        • Court subpoena server
        • Interstate driver licensing authorities

        The Letter of Authority must state:
        • The particulars of the relevant record
        • The applicant's name, position and serial number (if applicable)
        • The purpose of the request
