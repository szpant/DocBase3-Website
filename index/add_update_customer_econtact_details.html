Add/Update Customer eContact Details (5105)

1.From the TICA Homepage select Individual Customer hub
The Individual Customer Hub will display
Note:There is a link in TICA on the Update Customer Addresses Page (under Other Addresses) that flows directly to update eContact details
2.Display customer by using Name Search, CRN or Customer Account Number
Display Successful message displays

If customer not displayed, follow current TICA process to create individual customer
3.Access the Customer Online Account Management web page by either:
  • Click the eContact Details hyperlink located at the bottom of the screen
  • Using the navigation bar and selecting Online Account Management then
The Customer Online Account Management web page will open in a new window and any currently recorded eContact details will display
4.Click the Edit button or Update eContact Details hyperlink.
5.Type the customer's email address in the Email Address field. Re-type the email address in the Email address confirmation field.

Note:It is essential that the confirmation email address is retyped and not cut and pasted.
6.Enter the customer's mobile or SMS number in the Mobile or SMS number field (if applicable).
7.Verbally confirm the details with the customer.
8.Press the Update pushbutton
Customer eContact details updated successfully message displays
9.Advise the customer they will receive an email to their nominated email address confirming their registration.
10.Discuss with the customer the benefits of enrolling in e-notices and e-reminders and enrol if customer agrees or advise them how they can manage their own preferences through their TMR Online Account.
