Amend Driver Licence Condition/Class (not medical) (3317)


Applies to
Applies to
    The following actions taken during the currency/renewal of a driver licence:
    • Adding, Expiring or Updating a condition
    • Downgrading a class
    and
    • Removing a learner licence
    when the licensee is at the CSC or Agency

    Examples
    Examples
      • As a result of a voluntary surrender of a licence class
      • The result of show cause action

Check documents and EOI
    Item
    Details
    Application Form
    • Check that the application form/s has been completed correctly
    • Ensure EOI documents presented match those listed by the customer on the application form
    • Ask the customer to sign and date the application form in your presence
      • If the application form is not completed, signed or dated, have the customer complete it, otherwise refer below to Refuse to Consider Application
    • Complete Office Use Section of application form
    • Date stamp original application form

      Note: Do not stamp or mark any original EOI/qualification/competency documents
    • Record details of any assistance provided to the customer in completing the form
    • Check that the customer's personal details and description on the application form match TICA. Investigate any discrepancies and update if required.

Conduct 1:1 Image Match (if required) (smartcard office only)
    Conduct 1:1 Image Match (TICA)

    ~~~ Related TICA Steps ~~~
    Capturing facial image
    Capturing facial image
      If TICA prompts to capture the customer's facial image, direct the customer to the seat for the default camera and request them to:
      • Sit on the seat provided and to stay within the markings

        Note:If customer uses a mobility aid, retract or remove the chair and have the customer position themselves within the designated markings of the photo area
      • Look straight ahead at the camera (a frontal image of their face is required)
      • Remove any head attire or sunglasses (unless there are valid religious, cultural or medical reasons for not doing so, then refer to Manager)

      The customer must remain in your line of vision during the entire transaction.

      Once the facial image is successfully captured, request the customer to return to your counter.
    1:1 image match fails
    1:1 image match fails
      If the 1:1 image match fails contact the Identity Management Unit for an assessment of the image

      If IMU advises that the image match is unacceptable:
      • IMU will advise what action is to be taken
      Refer to:
      Smartcard Facial Image Recognition

      End of procedure

      If IMU advises that the image match is acceptable:
      • Continue with procedure
