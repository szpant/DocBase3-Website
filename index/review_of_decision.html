Review of Decision (3034)


What is it?A review of Decision is a customers request to review a decision made on behalf of the Chief Executive.
Requirements
  • Must be a reviewable decision
    Decisions that Can be Reviewed
    Decisions that Can be Reviewed
      • Not to register a vehicle (excluding statutory write-offs)
      • Not to renew the registration of a vehicle (excluding statutory write-offs)
      • Not to record the transfer of registration of a vehicle
      • Refusal to issue an unregistered vehicle permit
      • Refusal to issue an overseas vehicle permit for a vehicle
      • Refusal to issue and "Authorisation" under TO(RUMS - Vehicle Registration) Regulation 2010 section 107.

  • Application must be made in writing
ReviewReview of Decision Process
1. TMR Issues refusal Letter
1. TMR Issues refusal Letter
    A customer who has been refused may request written notification of the refusal.

    The letter from TMR must contain:
    • Reasons for refusal
    • Details of the review process
2. Customer forward written request for Review
2. Customer forward written request for Review
    Letter from customer must:
    • Be forwarded with 28 days of the issue of the TMR Refusal letter
    • Contain the grounds for requesting the review
    • Be sent to the relevant delegate position for an internal review. Please see the following webpage link: https://inside.tmr.qld.gov.au/corp/legislation/Delegations%20database/Forms/AllItems.aspx

      Refer: Inside TMR home page > Policies and Procedures > Delegations > Legislation Services > Delegations > Delegations Database
    3. Review is Undertaken
4. Notification of Result
4. Notification of Result
    Customer is sent written notification of the result

    Review Approved
    If the review is approved the requested transaction is undertaken.

    Review Declined
    The applicant may appeal to the Queensland Civil and Administrative Tribunal (QCAT).
