Photo Identification Card Voluntary surrender
Voluntary surrenderThe holder of Photo Identification (Photo ID) Card may voluntary surrender their Card for any the following reasons:
  • No longer wishes to hold a Photo ID Card (VOLS); or
  • Expired: or
  • Deceased

How to surrender
The holder:
  • Surrenders the Photo ID Card; and
  • Gives a written notice of the surrender
    Notes:If the person does not have the physical card to return they need to declare, on their written notice stating the card was:
    • Destroyed
    • Lost
    • Stolen.
    A confirmation slip of the Photo ID card surrender must be given to the holder or the authorised representative.

    Notes:The CSO must not issue a Confirmation Slip to a person who is not the holder of the Photo ID Card or an authorised representative.
