        National Heavy Vehicle Regulator's Code of Practice for the Approval of Heavy Vehicle Modifications
          The National Heavy Vehicle Regulator's Code of Practice for the Approval of Heavy Vehicle Modifications is made up of two (2) parts:

          1. National Code of Practice - Heavy Vehicle Modifications (commonly called VSB 6)
          2. State specific codes that apply only in Queensland

          Refer to the National Heavy Vehicle Inspection Manual - Vehicle Modifications -Appendix B for all relevant codes.
