Luxury Light Vehicle Duty Increase (7319)

Project NameLuxury Light Vehicle Duty Increase
When1 July 2018
BackgroundThe current Government made an election commitment to increase the rate of vehicle registration duty for luxury light vehicles valued at $100,000.01 or more. The election commitment was to increase the rate of vehicle registration duty by $2 per $100 of dutiable value for all light vehicles valued $100,000.01 or more.
Who will be impacted and how?Customers who register or transfer a light luxury vehicle with a dutiable value of $100,000.01 or more from 1 July 2018 will be impacted. For example, the rate of duty for a 4-cylinder vehicle with a dutiable value of $120,000 will be $5 per $100 which equates to 6,000 in total duty payable.
Further informationCustomer complaints and enquiries about the new rates of duty for luxury light vehicles can be directed to ccc@treasury.qld.gov.au or 1300 300 734.
