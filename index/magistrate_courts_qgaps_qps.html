TMR On-line/Off-line Agent Types
TMR On-line/Off-line Agent Types
    TMR agents can be categorised into two types:

    On-line
    Collect appropriate fees and process Registration applications directly to TICA

    Off-line
    Collect appropriate fees and Registration applications and forward to TMR for processing
TMR Agent Functions
TMR Agent Functions
    TMR agents may perform the following functions:
    • Issue permits/plates/VINs
    • Inspect motor vehicles/motor cycles/trailers
    • Collect & receipt registration fees
    • Obtain & collate registration documentation
TMR’s Role
TMR’s Role
    Account monitoring of Dealers and Agents
    Account monitoring of Dealers and Agents
      CSC’s manage their respective off-line dealers and agents accounts.

      Customer assistance can be provided in monitoring accounts by:
      1. Printing account statements:
        • On completion of each new schedule
        • At the end of each week
      2. Forwarding to customers by fax or arranging collection

    Off-line Dealer Transaction Discrepancies
    Off-line Dealer Transaction Discrepancies
      Underpayment of schedule:
      • The dealer or agent should be contacted and any anomalies or discrepancies should be addressed
      • Text recorded in the dealer, agents or customer's account (if required)
      Matters which are unable to be resolved to the satisfaction for all parties are to be referred to:
      • Customer Account Manager, Finance Branch
      Note: This unit will perform bi-annual reviews of the management of these accounts and report to the Director (Finance Branch).

    Account Statements
    Account Statements
      On-line dealers
      On-line dealers
        Dealer & Agency Interface System (DAIS) dealers have the capability to request their account statements (maximum 50 transactions at one time) using their own network.

        The DAIS Support Unit will address account issues when raised by respective dealers only on request.

        Matters which are unable to be resolved should be referred to:
        • Customer Account Manager, Finance Branch

      Off-line dealers & agents
      Off-line dealers & agents
        Statements are sent at the end of each month to customers who are in debt to TMR:
        • List all registration transactions processed on the customer account
        • Reflect debits and credits, during the past month

        Note: Pro-rata charges less than $20.00 are waived by TMR, and will not appear on the statement.


    Number plates
    Number plates
      Issue of Plates
      Issue of Plates
        Off-line dealers and agents obtain Registration number plates from their nominated CSC.

        CSCs should ensure:
        • Before new plates are issued that applications for previously issued plates are submitted and up to date
        • Plate orders are restricted to one month's supply, with a minimum of 1 box

      Processing Plates
      Processing Plates
        Where dealer's registration documents are brought to CSC for processing, it is not necessary to move the stock from location to location:
        • Process the transaction under the dealer processing mode
        • TICA will then check both locations for the plate
        • Operator will receive a "yes" message back from FiMs

        Thursday Island Court House
        Thursday Island Court House
        This is a DJAG led QGAP which processes TMR licensing and registration transactions online. However, when processing registration transactions from the outer islands, the following applies:
        • Customer emails completed application/documents to the TI courthouse
        • Documents are printed for review and a quote completed for exact cost of registration
        • If the customer has provided any payment card details these must be redacted in black permanent marker
        • Customer is contacted via phone to confirm fees
        • Customer provides credit card details over the phone to DJAG staff
        • Credit card details are entered directly into the EFTPOS device (they are not to be written down)
        • DJAG staff contact 1300 number (Atherton CSC) who process registration transaction through QMTHURSDAY 1800 processing
        • Monies are remitted through SSQ into Acc#600091161 for reconciliation by Atherton CSC
        • DJAG staff check paperwork to ensure all payment card details have been redacted prior to forwarding to Ahterton CSC
        • Paperwork is sent to Atherton CSC for checking
        • Atherton forward paperwork to Iron Mountain
