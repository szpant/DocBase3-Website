Customer Flow Management and Reporting (CFMR) - Phase 2 (7408)

Project NameCustomer Flow Management and Reporting (CFMR) - Phase 2
WhenLate 2018
BackgroundSince the early 1990s, Customer Services Branch (CSB) has been using a queue management system supplied by Q-Matic Australia in many of its Customer Service Centres (CSCs) across the state. The Q-Matic system (including kiosks) has been unsupported since 21 December 2017, exposing CSB to the risk of unrepairable system faults throughout the network.

The CFMR project has seen the previous Q-Matic system replaced with Q-Flow, a centrally managed and supported solution that meets TMR's current and future needs. All CSCs received Q-Flow software that allows data collection and reporting on the number of customers served and Back of House processing time, and 40 CSCs with higher customer volumes also received hardware (ticket kiosks and display monitors).

Approval has been received to roll-out Phase 2 of the project which is to implement the Q-Flow software to TMR led QGAPs and RSO sites.
Who will be impacted and how?All TMR led Queensland Government Agency Programs (QGAPs) and Regional Service Outlet (RSO) sites across the state will be impacted by the implementation of Phase 2.

To support all staff during this change, the project team will be communicating with QGAP and RSO site Managers to provide up-to-date information.

All QGAP and RSO site staff will receive training for the new Q-Flow system through an interactive course, and printable training materials will be available in DocBase.

The implementation of the new system will allow visibility and reporting on customer visit information including service time and transaction type, and allow the capture of Back of House processing times for customer facing staff.

31 TMR led QGAPs and RSO sites began using Q-Flow software from Monday 17th December 2018.
Technical Support / TroubleshootingWith the implementation of Q-Flow at all CSCs, the ongoing support has been handed over to the Business Systems team.

The support process will be a phased approach to allow for handover, while system upgrades are completed. All Q-Flow support requests are to be emailed to Business Systems.

The final phase of the support process will be communicated and recorded in DocBase when it is implemented.
