Sale of Modification Books & Plates (724)


Where to applyApproved Persons (AP) can make an application to purchase Certificates of Modification (modification books) and/or Modification Plates (plates) from CSC's. Motorcycle and S10 Livestock Loading plates are stored at Mount Isa CSC, APs can apply for these plates at any CSC and they will be mailed to them by registered post.

Only APs located interstate are able to purchase modification books and/or plates by mail from the Carseldine CSC. All other APs must purchase these items from their local CSC.

Modification books and plates can only be issued to the AP nominated on the Application to Purchase Books of Certificates of Modification and/or Modification Plates (F1856) form. The latest version of the application form F1856 must be used by the AP as the form outlines current accreditation requirements.

This information is also used by the AP scheme administrator to allocate the purchased stock to that AP.

Modification books and plates are the responsibility of the individual AP, not the APs employer.
Purchase of Modification Books and PlatesThe purchase of Certificate of Modification and/or Modification Plates must be receipted against the Approved Person's CRN and not the company/business that they own or work for.

Note:Unused and undamaged modification certificate books that are receipted incorrectly, for example to a business instead of an approved person, may be resold by the customer service centre. The operator would need to raise a system BIID to the Business Systems area requesting the modification book number be removed from TICA. The modification book should not be resold until confirmation is received from Business Systems that this has been done.

Purchase of Modification Books and/or Plates by an Approved Person (AP) in a CSC
Purchase of Modification Books and/or Plates by an Approved Person (AP) in a CSC
    When purchasing modification books and/or plates, an AP must produce the following:
    • a completed application form (F1856);
    • the required fee;
    • current AP Accreditation document;
      and
    • evidence of identity – refer to the Evidence of Identity (EOI) Requirements for individuals and organisations (F4362) form.
Purchase of Modification Books and Plates by a person other than the AP in a CSC
Purchase of Modification Books and Plates by a person other than the AP in a CSC
    An AP may nominate another person to purchase the modification books and/or plates, provided that the following is produced:
    • a completed application form (F1856) including the Authorisation to Act on my behalf section;
    • the required fee;
    • current AP Accreditation document;
      and
    • evidence of identity for the person who has been authorised to act on behalf of – refer to the EOI Requirements for individuals and organisations (F4362) form.
Purchase of Motorcycle or S10 Livestock Loading modification plates
Purchase of Motorcycle or S10 Livestock Loading modification plates
    Motorcycle (silver) and S10 Livestock Loading (maroon) plates are held at Mount Isa CSC.

    If an AP applies for these plates they must follow the usual application process Refer above: Purchase of Modification Books and/or Plates by an Approved Person in a CSC.

    The CSC operator should then:
    • Receipt payment
    • Advise the customer that the plates will be posted to them at their listed postal address, they may take up to 3 weeks to arrive.
    • Update the relevant spreadsheet listed under "Our Tools" on the Inside CSB SharePoint page. The operator should complete the below sections:
    • Number of plates required
    • Customer name
    • CRN
    • Postal Address
    • User ID
    • CSC/Agency
    • Receipt number
    • Scan and email the completed application to the AP Administrator

    Mount Isa CSC will check the spreadsheet weekly and then mail the plates by registered post to the AP. Enquiries relating to the issue of the plates can be sent to mount_isa_csc@tmr.qld.gov.au.
Purchase of Modification Books and/or Plates for an interstate Approved Person (AP)
Purchase of Modification Books and/or Plates for an interstate Approved Person (AP)
    Interstate APs may purchase modification books and/or plates by mail.

    The AP must send all of the following items to the Carseldine CSC:
    • a completed application form (F1856);
    • a cheque or money order for the required fee;
    • a certified copy of their current AP Accreditation document; and
    • a certified copy of evidence of identity documentation that meets the EOI requirements for individuals and organisations on form F4362.
Application Forms
Application Forms
    Refer: Fees and Forms > 2. Forms > Forms > Customer Products (CPRO) > Approved Inspection Station (AIS)

    The Office Use Only section of the application form must be completed in full including the receipt/invoice number by the issuing officer and;
    • scanned, emailed or posted to the AP Administrator .

    Every Wednesday a 'Modification Books and Plate Sales' report is generated and emailed to CSCs that have sold Modification Books or Plates in the last week. This report should be checked to ensure that each application form and any supporting documentation has been scanned and emailed to AP@tmr.qld.gov.au.
