Vehicle Descriptions (333)

VehicleDescription
Agricultural MachineA machine with its own automotive power built to perform agricultural tasks, and being used for agricultural purposes. eg Tractors, harvesters used for agriculture
Articulated BusA bus with 2 or more rigid sections with access between for passengers & the sections are connected so as to allow rotary movement between the sections.
CarA motor vehicle (other than a motorcycle) that is <= 4.5t GVM and is built to carry <= 12 adults, including the driver.
BusA motor vehicle that is built to carry more than 12 adults, including the driver.
B-doubleA vehicle combination consisting of a prime mover towing 2 semitrailers, with 1 semitrailer supported at the front by, and connected to, the other semitrailer.
Note:A road train is not a B-double.
Learner Approved MotorcycleA production motorcycle that is fitted with an electric motor, or has an internal combustion engine with an engine capacity of not more than 660mL; and
  • has a power to weight ratio of not more than 150kW per tonne; and
  • that has not been modified other than for an allowable modification; and
  • that is stated to be a learner approved motorcycle in a list kept by the chief executive and published on the department’s website.
MopedA motorcycle with engine capacity <=50ml & has a manufacturer's top rated speed <= 50 kmh
Note:A power-assisted cycle is not a moped.
MotorcycleA 2-wheeled motor vehicle, with/without a sidecar attached, and a 3-wheeled motor vehicle that is ridden in the same way as a 2-wheeled motor vehicle.
Note:A 4-wheel motor vehicle ("quad bike") is not a motorcycle for licensing purposes, except for the helmet requirements and age-related passenger restrictions.
Motor VehicleA vehicle propelled by a motor that forms part of the vehicle, and includes a trailer attached to the vehicle.
Prime moverA motor vehicle built to tow a semitrailer.
Road trainA vehicle combination consisting of a motor vehicle towing 2 or more trailers.
Note:A B-double is not a Road Train.
SemitrailerA trailer built to be supported at the front by, and connected to, a prime mover; and supported at the back by its own wheels.
Specially Constructed Vehicle
  • An agricultural machine (including tractor) within the meaning of
    TO(RUM-VR)Reg 2010
  • A crane, hoist or load shifting equipment for which either of the following are held:
    • a WHS certificate issued before 1 January 2012; or
    • a certificate of competency for that type of equipment issued by a Registered Training Organisation (RTO).
  • Any other motor vehicle that is not constructed to carry passengers or a load, except things used in performing the vehicle’s function

Note:A motor vehicle with a chassis that is substantially the same as a truck chassis is not a specially constructed vehicle.

Examples
Examples
    • Road scraper
    • Road roller
    • Road grader
    • Front end loaders
    • Backhoes
    • Bulldozers
    • Earthmoving equipment
TractorA motor vehicle used for towing purposes, other than a motor vehicle designed to carry passengers or goods (other than its own fuel or water).
Note:A tow truck is not a tractor
TrailerA vehicle that is to be towed, or is towed, by a motor vehicle.
Note:Does not include a motor vehicle being towed
TruckA motor vehicle with a GVM > 4.5t, other than a bus, tractor or tram.
