Off Line Processing System (OLPS) (1974)

What can be ReceiptedThe products that can be receipted are:
  • Drivers Licence renewal
  • Registration issue an renewal
  • Miscellaneous Customer Products
  • Miscellaneous non-Customer Products
Req'ments
Information Required
Information Required
    The customers CRN must be presented when processing.

    Customers must have all required documentation for the transaction.
Rules
Banking
Banking
    Banking is done in two steps:
    • After reconciliation all cash is banked in the day of receipt after reconciliation as an Operator Over in TRAILS
    • When OLPS is uploaded to TRAILS overnight the next banking day results in a corresponding Operator Under in TRAILS for the cash.

    Cheques are reported with that day’s operator receipts
Processing of Transactions
Processing of Transactions
    Registration renewals are processed directly.

    For non-customer products a single CRN is used. This acts as a Sundries Misc Account and is monitored by the Customer Account Management Group.

    Money received in this account must be processed in TRAILS by the receipting office as soon as possible.
