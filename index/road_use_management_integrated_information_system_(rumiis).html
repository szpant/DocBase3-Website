Road Use Management Integrated Information System (RUMIIS) (6028)


Road Use Management Integrated Information System (RUMIIS)
What it isThe following instruction outlines the database administration and edit functions for Operator Accreditation & Authorisation (OA&A) and Compliance regional officers.
Accessing Customer RecordsPlease be aware that the confidentiality of customer records is paramount at all times. The TMR policy Access to Customer Records provides clear direction to all employees not to take action to access a customer record unless the access is authorised. Refer: Admin > Security > Access to Customer Records
RUMIIS Access
RUMIIS Access
RUMIIS Access
    Editor access to RUMIIS is via the QDOT email groups. To access RUMMIIS officers must be a member of one of the following email groups:
    Compliance (SEQ South)
    Compliance (SEQ North)
    Compliance (Central)
    Compliance (Northern)
    Compliance (Southern)
    Regions are responsible for maintaining these groups.

Make a Transport Inspector an Auditor in RUMIIS
Make a Transport Inspector an Auditor in RUMIIS
    To enter Audit results a Transport Inspector must be recorded as a Transport Inspector in RUMIIS. Refer to How To: Make a Transport Inspector an Auditor in RUMIIS.

RUMIIS actions available to Compliance Officers
RUMIIS actions available to Compliance Officers
    Compliance Officer can perform the following functions in RUMIIS:
    • Make the following changes to an existing RUMIIS records (excluding accreditation details) that are detected during AIS audits including
    • change AIS postal address (not physical address);
    • change AIS telephone, fax number or email address;
    • change accredited person's residential address;
    • change accredited person's telephone number;
    • change AIS equipment (exception: AIS mobile unit deletions or
    • additions);
    • record the results of audits and complaint investigations, including details of PINs, CARs and warnings;
    • results of show cause actions, show cause reviews, show cause appeals and any stay applications;
    • delete nominees and approved examiners from an AIS;
    • record complaint investigations.
