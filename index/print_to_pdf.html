Print to PDF (7633)

Project NamePrint to PDF - Financial receipts and GST tax invoices
When1 February 2021
BackgroundFrom the 1st of February, all TMR financial receipts and GST tax invoices will be converted to a PDF document rather than automatically printing from the Kyocera printer.

This change has been implemented to reduce the amount of paper receipts that are printed by the department

For payments recorded against a customer (including organisations) the PDF document will be stored in the Customer Online Account Management (COAM) Screen, operators can access the COAM screen to email or print the document if requested.

If a non-customer product is receipted against a customer it will also display in their COAM screen. If a non-customer product isn't receipted against a customer operators can choose to email the document directly to the customer or preview the document to print it.
Who will be impacted and how?
Customers
Customers
    Customers will now have the options on how they access their receipts.

    Individual customer can choose to:
    • access their receipt by logging into the TMR Online Services account and selecting "Online receipts and documents"
    • have their receipt emailed to them
    • have their receipt printed and handed to them at a CSC or agency.

    Organisation customers can choose to:
    • have their receipt emailed to them
    • have their receipt printed and handed to them at a CSC or agency.
Operators
Operators
    Operators should now advise individual customers that a copy of their receipt will be stored in their online history for the next 24 months and that they can access it through the online services. If the customer is an organisation or an individual requesting a copy of their receipt operators should offer to email it in the first instance, otherwise the receipt can be printed and handed to the customer.
Further information
Customer Products
Customer Products
    The payment screen in TICA has been updated with a direct link to the customer's COAM screen. After a product has been receipted and the "View Document" menu item is selected the customer's COAM screen will display.


    If the customer has their email address recorded in their eContact page it will be automatically be populated. Operators can select the checkbox to email the document. Alternatively, operators can select the document to preview and print it.
Non Customer Products
Non Customer Products
    If a non-customer product isn't receipted against a customer the receipt can be emailed or printed by selecting the "View Document" menu item and then choosing to email or preview the PDF.


Reprinting receipts
Reprinting receipts
    Receipts that are reprinted through the "Payment Items Hub" will still be generated through the Kyocera printer.
