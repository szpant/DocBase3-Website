Displaying of Plates (3082)

Attachment Plates must be securely attached:
  • One on the front
  • One on rear
  • If only one plate required it must be attached to the rear of the vehicle
  • In an upright position
  • No more than 1.3m from the ground
  • Where it will be legible from 20m away in an arc of 45 degrees from the surface of the plate above or to either side of the vehicle

Must be attached to the nominated vehicle within 1 day of:
  • Receiving the plate;
    and
  • Having the attachment recorded in TICA.
Plate Covers/Frames To assist camera detection and legibility plate covers* must be:
  • Clear, clean and untinted
  • Flat on entire plate surface
  • Non-reflective as per TO(RUM - Vehicle Registration) Reg 10 s30

*A plate cover includes a frame and must:
  • allow for a clear space around the plate details (number and registering State or territory);
    and
  • Not sit flush to any plate details as this can cause a misread by camera detection devices.
AlterationsDrilling holes in a number plate is only permitted:
  • to enable it to be attached to a vehicle;
  • if the holes do not interfere with the appearance of any characters on the plate;
  • if plates are not altered in any way so as to damage or deface the plate.

Drilling holes in a plate to attach a reversing camera is not permitted.
