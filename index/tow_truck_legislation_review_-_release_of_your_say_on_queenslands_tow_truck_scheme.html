Tow Truck Legislation Review - Release of Your say on Queensland's Tow Truck Scheme (7485)

Project NameTow Truck Legislation Review - Release of Your say on Queensland's Tow Truck Scheme
WhenCommencing 28 October 2019
BackgroundThe Tow Truck Act 1973and Tow Truck Regulation 2009will be reviewed, in conjunction with a review of the private property towing reforms which were introduced in April 2018.

A discussion paper seeking feedback on the impact and effectiveness of the private property towing reforms, as well as how the tow truck legislation meets current and future needs for Queensland motorists, the tow truck industry, interested parties and other members of the community will be published on the Queensland Government’s “Get Involved” website.

The discussion paper and accompanying online survey will be available for a five week period from 28 October 2019 to 1 December 2019.
Who will be impacted and how?TMR’s website and Facebook page will advertise the Your say on Queensland’s Tow Truck Scheme Discussion Paper(Discussion Paper).The Discussion Paper will be published on the government’s “Get Involved” website from 28 October 2019 to 1 December 2019.

CSC staff
CSC staff
    CSC staff may be asked questions about the discussion paper. Q&A's have been developed to assist in responding to questions that may arise from the Discussion Paper.
Call Centre staff
Call Centre staff
    Call Centre Staff may be impacted by increased enquiries from the public and the tow truck industry.
Further informationSpecific tow truck complaints should continue to be referred to TMR’s Tow Truck Hotline for further investigation. Further information on the discussion paper will be available on the TMR website www.tmr.qld.gov.au/business-industry/Accreditations/Tow-truck-licensing-scheme from 28 October 2019, or stakeholders may wish to contact tow.trucks@tmr.qld.gov.au for any other tow truck matters.
