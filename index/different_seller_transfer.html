Different Seller Transfer (292)

Applies to
Applies to
    Customers who present documentation for the transfer of a registered vehicle where the disposers details do not match the record.

    Where possible all transactions to be processed in sequence.


    The department has an obligation to record the current registered operator of the vehicle and therefore, where possible, the transfer should be effected when all other transfer requirements have been met.
