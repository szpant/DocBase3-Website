Service Delivery Knowledge Base (SDKB) Project - DocBase and BIID Replacement (7546)

WhenTo be advised
BackgroundIt has been identified that DocBase, Business Issues and Improvement Database (BIID) and PTDoc (primarily used by TransLink) should be replaced with a single contemporary knowledge base solution (now known as TIPPS).
WhyThere are several reasons, including:
  • merging multiple systems into one
  • age of, and support for Lotus Notes
  • finding a solution that enables better customer service delivery.

The new solution (TIPPS) will have better search, enhanced functionality (such as adding articles to favourites and subscribing to them) and improved support. TIPPS also provides information owners with a real-time process to create, edit, approve and publish documents to be used by frontline staff.
What's happeningTIPPS will replace three business-critical Lotus Notes databases - DocBase, BIID and PTDoc – with one knowledge base system used in the delivery of our products and services to our customers.

TIPPS will launch on Monday, 23 August 2021.

From Monday, 23 August you can access TIPPS by visiting https://knowledgebase.tmr.qld.gov.au. Alternatively, you can find TIPPS on our intranet under Systems & Resources > Business tools and systems > TIPPS.

For those who still have not completed training, you should complete the online training course/s available through Accelerate (search 'TIPPS'). Once you've completed the online course, you can familiarise yourself with TIPPS in the practice environment.

Important Note:There will be test data in the TIPPS training environment​, and you shouldn't use this information in your role.​

DocBase and PTDoc will remain available to use until close of business Friday, 20 August 2021. From this date onwards, you will only be able to access TIPPS.
DocBase and PTDoc will be archived and eventually decommissioned.

All TIPPS support requests, including access requests, enhancements and incidents, should be requested/reported through Service Centre Online.

All the data contained in DocBase and PTDoc will be moved to TIPPS. You will still find the same information you are used to, but it will be easier to find and presented in a more user-friendly manner.

For those that create knowledge base articles, we have stopped any updates or new content from COB Monday, 09 August until TIPPS goes live on Monday, 23 August 2021. The change freeze is to ensure the transferred data from DocBase and PTDoc is correct.
Who will be impacted, and how
  • Customer-facing staff that consult DocBase daily to access policy and procedural information.
  • Other CSB and TMR staff that consult DocBase and access the Business Issues and Improvement Database (BIID) to raise updates to DocBase.
  • Road Safety Officers that access the Road Safety policy and procedures.
  • Compliance staff that access DocBase for daily messages, policy and procedural information.
  • TransLink staff that access DocBase and PTDoc for policy and procedural information.

During delivery, the project team will work hard to minimise any disruption to business operations and impacts on customer service.
