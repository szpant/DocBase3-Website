Perform Card Authorisation (117)

A card authorisation confirms that a Visa or MasterCard credit card is valid, has not been reported lost or stolen, and has sufficient funds available to cover the transacted amount.

A Card Authorisation is not able to be done for credit cards other than Visa or MasterCard or debit cards.

To perform a credit card authorisation:
  • Get your sites merchant number
  • Phone 132636 (service available 24 hrs a day, 7 days per week)
  • When prompted to enter the last 9 digits of your site’s 16 digit merchant number (eg. 5353109494123456)
  • When prompted enter the credit card number being confirmed

Note:Credit card details must not be written down when provided verbally, and must be blacked out when provided in writing.

If the CBA advises the card has been reported stolen or lost:
  • Do not accept the card payment and request an alternative method of payment
