One-Stop Shop (OSS) (5148)


What it isThe One-Stop Shop (OSS) service outlet has been developed to allow customers access to the full service catalogue of Queensland Government Agencies.
Locations(OSS) service outlets are currently located at:
  • Beaudesert
  • Gatton

Queensland Government Digital customer centres (self-service kiosks) are located at:
  • Plainland
  • Laidley

A mobile customer centre offering additional Government services also operates out of Jimboomba for one week every month.

Further information is available at http://www.qld.gov.au/dsitia/initiatives/one-stop-shop/
Participating Queensland Government AgenciesAgencies for which services and referral will be provided:
  • Department of Transport and Main Roads
  • Department of Justice and Attorney General
  • Smart Service Queensland and QGAP services
  • Department of Housing & Public Works
  • Office of Fair Trading
  • Office of Liquor and Gaming Regulations
  • Office of State Revenue
  • Public Trustee
  • State Penalties Enforcement Registry
  • QLD Ombudsman
  • Department of Natural Resources and Mines
  • Department of Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry
  • Department of Communities, Child Safety and Disability Services
  • Queensland Police Service

Other agencies will be accessible via their online services on www.qld.gov.au or 13QGOV.
Services deliveredThe services delivered at a OSS are:
  • Counter based services for transactions
  • Specialist Appointment services
  • Supported self-service transactions
  • Customer referral services
Service Types
Service Types
Service Types
    Service TypeDescription
    Counter TransactionAny of the following transactions where the customer:
    • makes a payment
    • shows evidence of identity
    • lodges a form or documentation
    • is provided with specific information or a product at the end of the transaction
    Self-service TransactionA transaction that can be completed online or over the phone by a customer in the self-service zone (support available if required).
    Specialist Appointment serviceA service that is only available on a set schedule via pre-booked appointment due to availability of trained resources.
    ReferralIf a service or information is not available at the location where the customer presents, they are provided with the information to get started (eg. brochure, forms, pre-requisites, online information, contact details/address, opening hours)
    Information requestWhen a customer requests information about an agency and /or it services, they are provided with:
    • information via supported self-service
    • brochures/forms
