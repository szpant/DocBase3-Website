Prior Driving Experience Application (3155)

What it isA person may apply for credit of prior driving experience by itself or in combination with a learner logbook.

Note:It is not to be used for a lost, stolen or damaged learner logbook.
Who may apply
Eligibility criteria
Eligibility criteria
    An application for recognition of prior driving experience may be made by:
    • A customer who is required to complete 100 hrs supervised on road driving experience
    and
    • They have one of the following:
      • Held a Queensland learner licence granted before 01/07/07
      • Holds or has held an interstate class C learner licence
      • Holds or has held a NZ class C learner licence
      • Holds or has held a Foreign class C learner licence from a recognised country, listed under the -
        Austroads Approved Countries - Recognised Country only
How much credit can be applied for
Interstate/NZ learner licence held
  • Up to 100 hours supervised driving experience in Australia or NZ while the holder of a class C interstate or NZ learner licence, including 10 hrs driving experience at night.
  • Time with a driver trainer at a ratio of 1:3 up to a maximum of 10 hrs
Foreign learner licence from a Austroads Recognised Country held
  • Up to 50 hrs supervised driving experience (including 10 hours driving experience at night) done in a recognised country, listed under the -
    Austroads Approved Countries - Recognised Country only
  • Time with a driver trainer at a ratio of 1:3 up to a maximum of 10 hrs
How to Apply
Application requirements
Application requirements
    Documents
    • A completed Prior Driving Experience Application F4450; and
    Note: More than 1 supervisor
    Note: More than 1 supervisor
      If driving experience has been gained with more than 1 supervisor then multiple Prior Driving Experience Applications (F4450) should be completed.

      If a general logbook or a document that records information similar to the Queensland learner logbook has been presented as evidence and all supervisors have signed each entry, it is not necessary for all supervisors to sign the Statutory Declaration or separate Prior Driving Experience Applications (F4450). A single Prior Driving Experience Application (F4450) must still be submitted with each application.
    • Supporting evidence of prior driving experience; and
    Example documents
    Example documents
      • Receipts from a driving school/driver trainer
      • A signed statement on letterhead from a driving school/driver trainer
      • Any logbook or document that records similar information to a Queensland learner logbook
        (i.e. dates, vehicle registration numbers, distances travelled, driving time in minutes, names of supervisors)
      • A letter issued by VicRoads confirming the number of supervised driving hours. The following details are specific to the VicRoads verification letter:
        • It is the customers responsibility to obtain a copy of the verification letter.
        • The letter is always emailed to the customer in PDF format. It is not supplied as a hardcopy.
        • The customer may request VicRoads send a copy of the PDF letter to Learner Logbook CPU (LLBCPU)
        • Immediate confirmation may be obtained if the customer or an authorised TMR representative calls the VIC Licensing Authority VicRoads Public Enquiries number.

        Learners Verification Letter - Sample - October 2020

        Note:It is the customer's responsibility to obtain a copy of this document.
    • A completed learner logbook (if applicable)
    EOI Req'd
    • None
