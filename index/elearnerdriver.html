PrepL (6054)

Project NamePrepL
When20 November 2018
BackgroundTMR is changing the way in which customers apply for a learner licence for a car. A new online service, PrepL, will be implemented so that customers no longer need to study Your Keys to Driving (YKTD) and then complete the written road rules test in a Licence Issuing Centre.

The online service will allow TMR customers to learn the Queensland road rules and licensing information as well as complete an online assessment to apply for and be issued with their C class learner licence. Assessment will be used to satisfy the department of the applicant’s understanding and application of the content.

PrepL will be an interactive service and will also focus on influencing safe pre-learner driver behaviours and attitudes to drive positive road safety outcomes.

YKTD, or a similar publication will still be available to customers who wish to refer to the road rules for other reasons.
Who will be impacted and how?The target audience is primarily young people aged between 15 – 24 years old. However, the online service will be accessible to all Queenslanders.

As the online service will not be suited to all customers, some exceptions will be available.

CSC’s will see a reduction in the number of customers completing their tests in a CSC.
Transition PeriodThere will be a transition period of at least 3 months from the implementation date in which customers will have the choice to either complete the written test or PrepL. The duration of the transition period will be reviewed during this time.
Further informationRefer to the Questions and Answers below and if you require further information, contact prepl@tmr.qld.gov.au
TrainingTraining for PrepL is now live in Accelerate and should be completed prior to the go live date on the 20 November 2018. You will also need to read the latest Project Update and FAQs.

The training will include a refresh of the overview of what PrepL is, common scenarios you will see, processes/transactions that are changing and answers to common questions customers may ask.

Training for this initiative is mandatory for all CSC staff and completion will be recorded in Accelerate.
