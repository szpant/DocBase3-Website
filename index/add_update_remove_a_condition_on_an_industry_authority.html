    Conditions Imposed by Policy Team

      Note:The customer must carry the original conditional letter and a 'Y' condition code will be displayed on the face of the Smartcard.

    Conditions imposed by CSC or QGAP Manager
      Customer will be advised by letter of a conditional approval by the CSC or QGAP Manager.

      Note:A 'N' condition code will be displayed on the face of the Smartcard and the customer does not need to carry a copy of the conditional letter.
