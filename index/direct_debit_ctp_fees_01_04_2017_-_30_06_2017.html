Direct Debit CTP Fees 01/04/2017 - 30/06/2017 (7119)

Note:Transport and Main Roads (TMR) collects Compulsory Third Party insurance premiums on behalf of licensed Insurers. It is not TMR's role to provide information on Insurers' premiums. Customers must contact the Insurers for any further information regarding these rates.

A 3 month payment option is only available for vehicles on Direct Debit Renewal with a Purpose of Use of:
  • Private
  • Commercial
  • Driver Training or Tuition
  • Produce Carrying (Farming)
  • Limousine
  • Limousine Special Purpose
  • Used for Non Profit
  • Taxi
  • Taxi Peak Demand
