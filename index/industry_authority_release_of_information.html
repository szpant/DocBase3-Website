Industry Authority Release of Information (3358)


Release of Industry Authority Information
What it isInformation from the following can be requested and released from the register:
  • Industry Authority card details includes address or full name history (includes aliases)
  • Industry Authority history

Note:A release of information does not include the Industry Authority cardholder's digital photo and digitised signature.
Request must be made by IndividualApplications for release of card details/industry authority history information can only be made by a person:
  • Requesting their own information
  • Who has been authorised by the Industry Authority cardholder to obtain their information
  • Who has a proven legal authority to obtain the information eg. Police officer
Representing
When representing an Industry Authority cardholder
When representing an Industry Authority cardholder
    If an Industry Authority cardholder is unable to present the application themselves, they can have another person apply on their behalf.

    This also applies to legal entities or organisations.

    A representative of the entity or organisation is required to transact the business.

    When applying for information about another person's industry authority record, the Industry Authority cardholder's written authorisation is required.

    Note:There are further EOI documents, authorities and signatures required for persons who are transacting the business on the card holder's behalf.

    Applications for release of personal/industry authority details/industry authority history information can only be made by a person:
    • Who has been authorised by the Industry Authority cardholder to obtain their information
    • Who holds a power of attorney for the Industry Authority cardholder > See Power of Attorney

    The Release of Information Application F2121 provides an area for the Industry Authority cardholder’s written authorisation.
    • The Industry Authority cardholder must be contacted to verify the release of the information unless the applicant holds a power of attorney for the Industry Authority cardholder.

      Refer to: Power of Attorney

    Note:The Industry Authority cardholder's written authorisation is not required, if requested by a person with proven legal authority.

    Refer to: Transaction Requirements below for full requirements.
Requests
Requests from persons with proven legal authority
Issue
Issuing the Document - Industry Authority Card information
Issuing the Document - Industry Authority Card information
    The customer is provided with an Industry Authority History or Industry Authority Details report. Reports can be obtained from TICA.

    Note:Screen prints from DocBase and TICA/TRAILS must not be given to customers, external agencies or any other third party.

    Information Released
    Information Released
      • Licence holders name and address
      • Date of birth
      • Industry Authority Number
      • Authority type
      • Authority status
      • Effective and expiry dates of the authority
      • Any conditions attached to the licence that relate to the cardholder performing the role. If the authority has a 'Y' condition code, any information recorded for the specific industry authority that relate to the cardholder performing the role.

      Note: Any information relating to conditional approval information (eg text indicating that a conditional accreditation has been approved due to traffic/criminal history concerns) is NOT to be included.

T.R.CTransaction Requirements Checklist
Release Copy of Documents (Industry Authority Application Forms)
What it isUnder industry authority legislation, individuals are entitled to copies of documents that were given at the time of an application for an Industry Authority card.

They are entitled to copies of documents:
  • That they have provided to TMR; and
  • Which contain their personal information.

This applies to the Industry Authority Application form and accompanying documents (photocopied or scanned) eg EOI documents.
  • Photocopied documents are requested from Iron Mountain.
  • Scanned documents are requested from IMU.

Note:QPS officers may request copies of documents for enforcement purposes free of charge.
