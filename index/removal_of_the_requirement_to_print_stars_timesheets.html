Removal of the Requirement to Print STARS Timesheets (6102)

Project NameRemoval of the requirement to print STARS timesheets
WhenA trial is currently underway for staff in Northern Region, and business units located at Level 3 B Block, Carseldine GOP.

This initiative will be rolled out to the whole of CSB on the 3rd October 2016, for fortnight ending 14th October.
BackgroundAs a result of improvement opportunities identified as part of the CI Fundamentals training in May-June 2016, CSB is progressing an initiative to remove the requirement to print timesheets and leave confirmations each fortnight, and introduce a fully electronic submission, reconciliation and approval process for CSB salary staff (excluding Compliance Officers).

A trial of this process is currently underway in Northern Region and Level 3 B Block, Carseldine GOP, with very positive feedback received about the time and resource savings being realised for both areas.

As a result of the initiative’s success and benefits in both areas, this initiative will be rolled out to all salary staff in CSB (excluding Compliance Officers).

Work is also underway to identify and streamline timesheet processes for Compliance Officers and casual staff members, and more information will be provided as it becomes available.
Who will be impacted and how
How will this impact individual team members?
How will this impact individual team members?
    From 3rd October 2016 (for fortnight ending 14th October), at the end of each fortnight, once you have checked and submitted your timesheet in STARS (within 3 working days of fortnight end), you will no longer need to print and sign your timesheet and give it to your supervisor. Simply submit it in the system and move to the next fortnight’s timesheet.

    When you submit your timesheet in the system, your supervisor will be able to see that it’s been submitted and is ready for reconciliation and approval. If your supervisor sees you have not submitted your timesheet in the system, they will remind you.

    Additionally, if you have applied for leave during the timesheet period, you will not need to print your leave approval confirmation, as this will be checked electronically by your supervisor.
How will this impact STAR Supervisors?
How will this impact STAR Supervisors?
    From 3rd October 2016 (for fortnight ending 14th October), at the end of each fortnight, you will no longer need to reconcile STARS timesheets and leave off printed documents, and there will be no paper documentation to sign and store. All existing reconciliation and approval activities will just be done electronically, by checking the timesheet in STARS, opening and comparing ESS and STARS and approving the timesheet in STARS.

    In lieu of printed timesheets, a recurring calendar invite will be set up and sent to all STARS supervisors in CSB, as a reminder to reconcile and approve timesheets each fortnight. Please ACCEPT the invite to ensure the reminder is added to your calendar.

    To reconcile leave against the timesheet electronically, a procedure has been developed for you to set up and use a team leave calendar function of ESS. You can then open your team member’s STARS timesheet and this calendar and reconcile leave on the screen.

    Electronic STARS Procedures

    STARS captures and stores all information relating to users who have accessed and/or modified records in the system, including who submits timesheets. Where special circumstances exist and you are required to submit a timesheet on behalf of one of your team members, you must print the timesheet and both you and the employee must sign it to prove the action taken was endorsed.
Further informationThe current STARS user guides for general users, supervisors, and administrators will be updated and published on the STARS SharePoint site before the roll out date.
