Sell a Work Diary (1225)

Applies to
Applies to
    The sale of National Driver Work Diary (work diary) to current driver licence holders. It includes the sale of a:
    • Work diary for the first time issue
    • Replacement of a previously issued work diary (including lost, stolen or destroyed)
    • Replacement of a seized work diary
1
Does not apply to
Does not apply to
    If the work diary has been left behind.
    Refer: QT Shop > 1. Customer Products > Work Diaries > Replacement > Replacement National Work Diaries > If the National Driver Work Diary has been left behind
2
Before you start
Before you start
    Check that the customer has:
    • A current driver licence
    • A previous work diary (if applicable and available to produce)
    • The correct payment method
3
Check prior work diary and driver licence
Check prior work diary and driver licence
    • Request the old work diary
    • If the old work diary:
      Is produced
      Is produced
        • Obtain a new work diary from storage
          Note:If no diaries are available, the driver can use supplementary sheets for up to 7 days.
        • Check if all of the pages are completed:
          • If completed, refer to Step 4.
            If it has unused pages
            If it has unused pages
              • Cancel the old work diary in TICA
              • Cancel each page by one of the following options:
                • Stamp each unused page with a ‘cancelled’ stamp
                • Cancel with two diagonal lines across each unused page
                • Write ‘CANCELLED’ on each unused original page
      Is lost, stolen or destroyed
      Is lost, stolen or destroyed
        • Cancel the old work diary in TICA
        • Obtain a new work diary from storage
          Note:If there are no diaries available, they will need to complete the Lost/Stolen/Destroyed Notification form.
          Lost/Stolen/Destroyed Notification
        • Advise the driver that if the old work diary is located, they must follow the instructions on the Information Sheet for Return of Heavy Vehicle Driver's National Driver Work Diary given at Step 7.
4
Prepare new work diary
Prepare new work diary
    Driver responsibilities
    The applicant must:
    • Fill in the application form in the front of the work diary

    TMR Officer responsibilities
    The TMR officer must:
    • Check the signature against the applicants current driver licence
    • Detach the completed application form
5
Manage Accountable Number Books (TICA)
6
Make Payment (TICA)

Note:Once payment has been made, write the receipt number on the top right hand corner of application form.
7
Give customer
Give customer
    • New work diary (and previous if supplied)
    • Driver licence
    • Payment receipt

    Additionally, if the work diary was lost, stolen or destroyed, give them the work diary information sheet.
    Work Diary - Information Sheet 2013

    Note:Once the information sheet is given to the customer, make a note on the right hand corner of the application that the information sheet was provided.
