Withdraw Authority to Drive in Qld (Medical) - Non Qld Licence (2828)

IMPORTANT: Recording of Medical Condition Notifications

All actions regarding medical reporting by a licence holder are required to be recorded including:
  • All medical certificates presented to TMR
  • Notices issued and
  • Action/s taken

Note:Medical certificates for non QLD licence holders are only recorded in the MCR Hub

Applies to
Applies to
    Withdrawing the authority to drive in Qld on medical grounds for the holder of a non Qld driver licence.
