Contest - The Offence (2761)

What it isAn alleged offender may wish to contest an Infringement Notice because they believe it was unjustly issued.

This process applies to withdrawing the Infringement Notice before it is registered with SPER.
How to Contest NoticeThe alleged offender may apply in writing to the relevant Notice issuing authority, outlining the reason they believe the Infringement Notice has been unjustly issued.

All court elections are sent to the Prosecution Unit
