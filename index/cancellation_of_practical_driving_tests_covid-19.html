Cancellation of Practical Driving Tests COVID-19 (7533)

What it isFollowing Queensland Heath advice, all practical driving tests will be cancelled from Monday 2 August - Saturday 7 August, in the following local government areas:
  • Noosa
  • Sunshine Coast
  • Ipswich
  • Logan
  • Redlands
  • Moreton Bay
  • Brisbane
  • Gold Coast
  • Scenic Rim
  • Lockyer Valley
  • Somerset

Customers have been contacted by email and SMS about their cancelled test. They will be sent further advice about how to rebook a prioritised test.

Tests were previously cancelled from 28 March 2020. Heavy vehicle practical driving tests were reinstated from 25 May 2020 and light vehicle tests were reinstated from 15 June 2020.

This change in service delivery is due to the high-risk nature of this activity when considering COVID-19 and the guidelines for social distancing and hygiene practices as recommended by Queensland Health.
COVID 19 processCustomers who have a scheduled PDT were notified by email, phone or SMS and had an option to either receive a refund or retain the funds in their customer account to use for a future booking.

The CSB Senior Leadership Team, along with local teams, worked collaboratively with driving examiners to arrange opportunities for redeployment into other work areas and to assist in the interim period.
How customers apply for a refundThe test booking fee will remain in the customer's TMR customer account unless the customer requests to receive a refund.

Customers can request a refund of their test booking fee by responding directly to the email they receive or by sending an email to services@tmr.qld.gov.au

Booking Fee Paid Online
Booking Fee Paid Online
    If the customer paid their booking fee online, the fee will be returned to the credit card the booking was paid from.
Booking Fee Paid at a Customer Service Centre (CSC)
Booking Fee Paid at a Customer Service Centre (CSC)
    If the customer paid their booking fee at a CSC, the fee will be refunded by cheque. The refund cheque may take a few weeks to come through and will be sent to the customer's recorded residential/postal address.

Note:Refunds are unable to be sent to a nominated bank account or credit/debit card.
