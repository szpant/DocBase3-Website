ROI Written Requests (Ship) (3071.1)

Accidents
Accidents
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information, for a ship involved in an incident. It includes hit and run incidents.

    Under no circumstances should a search be conducted to trace witnesses or possible witnesses to an incident.
    Who Can ApplyIndividuals/Organisations who are:
    • Involved in the incident
    or
    • Representing those involved in incident
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Registration Records Request (F3522) and either:
      SituationDocuments
      IncidentEither
      • Request on letterhead detailing the incident for a Solicitor/Lawyer
        or
      • Statutory declaration witnessed by a JP describing the incident plus property damage.
        The statutory declaration must include the ship details (Registration number and Make/description of ship).
        and either a:
        • Repair quote for property damage or
        • Copy of any available witness statements
        The witness statement must include the full name and address of the witness.
    TICA ReasonAccident
    Cost: Fee is charged
Registered Operator
Registered Operator
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information of a registered operators registration or for the transfer date to/from their name.
    Who Can Apply
    • Registered Operator
    • Representative of the Registered Operator
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Ship Registration Records Request (F3522) and:
      If Request made by:Documents Required
      Deceased Registered Operators RepresentativeEither
      • Letter from solicitor/public trustee stating that they are either:
        • The administrator/executor of a deceased estate
          or
        • Acting for the administrator/executor of a deceased estate

      If not a solicitor/public trustee
      • Letter stating the applicant is the administrator/executor of a deceased estate
        and
      • Acceptable Proof of Death document.
    TICA ReasonExecutive Discretion
    Cost: Fee is charged
Local Authoritites
Local Authoritites
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information from a Local Authority.
    Who Can Apply
    • Local Governments (i.e. City/Shire Councils)
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Registration Records Request (F3522) and one of the following:
      • Request on letterhead stating information is required for matters of:
        • enforcing ship noise offences under the Environmental Protection Act, s440z
          and a
        • Statement that they are the person delegated to request the information
        or
      • Copy of formal approval from the TMR's Chief Executive allowing access to the information
    TICA ReasonLocal Authority
    Cost: Fee is charged
Statutory Authority
Statutory Authority

    Who Can Apply
    • Statutory Authorities (ie Airports, Hospitals)
    Requirements
    Requirements

    TICA ReasonStatutory Authority
    Cost: Fee is charged
Government Department
Government Department
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information from a government department.
    Who Can Apply
    • Government departments
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Registration Records Request (F3522) and one of the following:
      Requested by:Requirements
      Government Department
      • Request on letterhead quoting the section from the legislation allowing access to the information
        and
      • Statement that they are the person delegated under that legislation to request information
        or
      • Copy of formal approval from TMR's Chief Executive allowing access to the information
      Qld Police Service (QPS)
      • Request on letterhead or from QPS email address (i.e. ...@police.qld.gov.au). The email must have the appropriate signature block that clearly identifies the person requesting the information.
      • Copies of documents will be sent via the post.
    TICA ReasonGovernment
    Cost: Fee is charged to all Departments except QPS

Family Court
Family Court
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information relevant to family court matters.
    Who Can Apply
    • Solicitors/Lawyers
    • Search requests for Family court matters from individuals must be referred to the Department of Transport and Main Roads' Freedom of Information Unit for processing.
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Registration Records Request (F3522)
      and:
      Requested by:Requirements
      If Starting Court Proceedings
      • Solicitors/Lawyers letter stating litigation is about to be commenced and that the person's address is needed from the register as the last known address is incorrect and the address is needed to serve/file court documents
        or
      • Copy of proposed claim to be lodged to the court fully completed except for the defendant's address
        or
      • Court order made against the person whose information is being requested
      If Finalising Court Proceeding
      • Request on letterhead detailing the court matter
        or
      • Court/legal document detailing the court matter
    TICA Reason
    • Legal (Starting Court Proceedings)
    • Court (Finalising Court Proceedings - certified details)
    Cost: Fee is charged
Bankruptcy
Bankruptcy
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information, relevant to bankruptcy proceedings.
    Who Can Apply
    • Insolvency Entity
    • Receiver
    • Trust
    • Solicitor/Lawyer
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Registration Records Request (F3522)
      and either:
      SituationDocuments
      If starting court proceedings
      • Request on letterhead exercising powers under the Bankruptcy Act 1966 (CWTH)or Corporations Law from either a:
        • Insolvency Entity
        • Receiver
        • Trustee
        • Solicitor/Lawyer
      or
      • Copy of the proposed claim taken by the insolvency entity exercising powers under the
        • Bankruptcy Act 1966 (CWTH)
          or
        • Provisions of the Corporations Law relating to external administration (fully completed except for the defendants address)
      If finalising court proceedings
      • Request on letterhead exercising powers under the Bankruptcy Act 1966 (CWTH)or Corporations Law from either:
        • Insolvency Entity
        • Receiver
        • Trustee
        • Solicitor
    TICA Reason
    • Legal (if starting court proceedings
    • Court (if finalising court proceedings - certified details)
    Cost: Fee is charged
Fraud
Fraud
    What is it?Request for an ROI statement showing current or historical information, relevant to fraud investigations. This excludes the recovery of money for the ship eg. a person transfers a registration out of the name of the registered operator/s without their consent.
    Who Can Apply
    • Individuals
    • Solicitors/Lawyer
    Requirements
    Requirements
    Requirements
      Search of Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Registration Records Request (F3522) and either:
      Requested by:Requirements
      Starting Court ProceedingsEither a:
      • Copy of the proposed claim to be lodged to the court (fully completed except for the defendants address),
      and a,
      • Statutory declaration from the previous registered operator making the claim and stating:
        • The fraud alleged
        • Litigation is about to be commenced
        • The persons address is needed from the register as there is no accurate existing address
        • The claim will be lodged in court when the address is found
      or
      • Court order made against the person whose information is being requested
      Finalising Court Proceedings
      • Solicitors/Lawyers letter detailing the court matter
      or
      • Court/legal document detailing the court matter
    TICA Reason
    • Legal (Starting Court Proceedings)
    • Court (Finalising Court Proceedings - certified details)
    Cost: Fee is charged
