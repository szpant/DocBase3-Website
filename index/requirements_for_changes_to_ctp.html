    • Change of class letter issued by the insurer. (not required for changes at transfer or changes with payment of renewal)
    • Change of class can proceed without a change of class letter in the six week period before the expiry date of the current registration period, when next renewal has been raised.
    • A change of class (and any associated changes to the POU) made during the currency of the registration are effective from the date indicated on the insurers change of class letter.

    Note:
    The change of class letter does not have to be the original copy - it can be a photocopy, fax or email copy.

    Note:
    Any change e.g. private use to a taxi, or 1 seat to 2 seat bike modification that involves a change of class to a higher class cannot be processed without a change of class letter (unless the change is at transfer or renewal).
