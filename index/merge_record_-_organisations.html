Merge Record - Organisations (1666)

Applies to
Applies to
    Customer records for an Organisation that have been identified as requiring combining.
    • Change name or record alias
    • Update physical attributes (height, gender, eye colour, hair colour, complexion)
    • Correct an error (eg. date of birth)

Identify customer with multiple CRNs
    • Customers with multiple CRN can be identified by:
    • Searching for a customer (TRAILS) and finding a listing of customer with the same or similar names with identical personal details
    • Displaying a registration (TRAILS) and finding the customers CRN is different from the one on the licence presented
    • Displaying the registration and finding the customer is restricted
