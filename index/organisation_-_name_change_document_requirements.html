Organisation - Name Change Document Requirements (1459)


Applicants for a change of name need to complete the relevant section of form (F3526)
Change of Customer Details and provide the following supporting documents:
OrganisationDocuments Required (one of the following)
Company
  • Verify the company name has changed on the ASIC website http://www.asic.gov.au/. A copy of the website results must be filed with the application form. OR
  • The original Certification of Registration on Change of Name issued by ASIC. The Certificate must have the words ‘change of name’.
Body Corporate
  • Confirmation Statement issued by Titles Operations (Department of Natural Resources)
Government & Semi Govt
  • Written advice on letterhead, and
  • Sighting evidence from a government listing (phone book, Internet, publication).
Trust
  • Original written advice from the trustee stating the new name. The Trustee must remain the same.
  • EOI for the Trustee
Registered Business
  • Verify the business name has changed on the ASIC website http://www.asic.gov.au/. A copy of the website results must be filed with the application form; or
  • Original Business Name Registration Certificate issued by ASIC; or
  • Original Certificate of Registration on Name Change

The existing registered operator, who must be the proprietor of the business is to be retained.
From an Incorporated
Association or Organisations created using Letters Patent to a Ltd Company		Provide all of the following:
  • Original Office of Fair Trading
    • Notice of Authority to Transfer Incorporation Associations Incorporation Act 1981' and
    • Authority to Transfer letter - both issued by the Office of Fair Trading;
  • Original Certificate of Registration as a company (new name) issued by ASIC.
