Checklist of Transfer Documents by Vehicle (3078)

Customers may be entitled to exemptions and concessions. Please refer to the relevant document for
the entitlements, rules and requirements.

For Missing Disposer/Acquirer Signature Requirements for Transferring Vehicles, please Refer:Vehicle Rego > 08. Transfer >Transfer > Signature Requirements > Missing Disposer/Acquirer Signatures
Light Vehicles
Light Vehicles

    Disposer
    • Vehicle Registration Transfer Application (F3520) - Part B (Notice of Disposal)
    • Standard number plates attached prior to transfer is PPQ attached at time of sale
    • Disposers copy of the Safety Certificate
    • Declaration is required when vehicle/vessel is being transferred from joint to a single registered operator and only one of the registered operators has signed. Declaration must state acquirer has a legal right to transfer the registration into their name.

    Aquirer
    • Vehicle Registration Transfer Application (F3520) - Part A (Transfer Application)
    • Personalised Number Plates Transfer Application (F2963) If applicable
      Refer: Vehicle Rego > 11.Plates > Personalised Plates Guide > Plate Transactions > Personalised Plate Transaction
    • Original Safety Certificate (unless exempt)
    • Original/Certified Copy of Deed of Trust if being transferred in or out of Trust name
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Concession documents (if applicable)
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
    • Standard EOI
    • Declaration is required when vehicle/vessel is being transferred from joint to a single registered operator and only one of the registered operators has signed. Declaration must state acquirer has a legal right to transfer the registration into thier name.

    For Specific additional documents regarding Court Settlements, De-facto, Trust, Paying Agent, Org/Bus Name, swapping registered operators and Repossession/Redemption, Refer to: Vehicle Rego > 08. Transfer >Transfer > Transaction Requirements Checklist
Heavy Vehicles
Heavy Vehicles

    Disposer
    • Vehicle Registration Transfer Application (F3520) - Part B (Notice of Disposal)
    • COI
    Note:The National Heavy Vehicle Accreditation Scheme (NHVAS) is administered by the National Heavy Vehicle Regulator.

    The COI is exempt on transfer when a current NHVAS maintenance module (ATMT) is recorded on the vehicle.

    Check the vehicle is currently in the NHVAS maintenance module in TICA.

    If the NHVAS maintenance module is not current in TICA, real time verification of heavy vehicles in NHVAS can be found at: NHVAS AVM Search

    If the NHVAS maintenance module is current in the NHVR portal AVM search, email or phone NHVAS to have it recorded on the vehicle.

    Definition of a NHVAS nominated vehicle.
    Any vehicle over 4.5 tonnes GVM or ATM that is nominated for operation by a NHVAS accredited operator for participation in the NHVAS.

    Important:
    Please refer to the following link for processing NHVAS to NHVAS Refer: Vehicle Rego > Transfer > Transfer > Rules > Transfer of vehicle on alternative compliance

    However, if a vehicle is currently recorded in a maintenance module scheme member's accredited fleet and is not remaining in NHVAS, the disposer MUST obtain a COI prior to transfer if it is sold as a registered vehicle.


    Aquirer
    • Vehicle Registration Transfer Application (F3520) - Part A (Transfer Application)
    • Personalised Number Plates Transfer Application (F2963) If applicable
    • Refer: Vehicle Rego > 11.Plates > Personalised Plates Guide > Plate Transactions > Personalised Plate Transaction
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Certificate of Inspection (unless exempt)
    • Original/Certified Copy of Deed of Trust if being transferred in or out of Trust name
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
    • Concession documents (if applicable)
    • Proof of tare weight (Refer: 05. Vehicle Requirements > Weights/Measurements > Proof of Tare Weight)
    • Change of Vehicle Particulars Notification (F3524) (if applicable)
    • Standard EOI
