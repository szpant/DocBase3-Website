RMDL/PWCL Conditions (3365)

Conditions of using an RMDL/PWCL
Licence Conditions
Conditions
Conditions
    A Recreational Marine Driver Licence or Personal Watercraft Licence can be granted, subject to reasonable and relevant conditions.

    Conditions may include:
    • Wearing corrective lenses
    • Medical fitness related conditions
    • Other conditions.
Display of imposing, adding and removing conditions
Display of imposing, adding and removing conditions
    Imposing Conditions
    Imposing Conditions
      Wearing corrective lenses
      Wearing corrective lenses
        If an applicant indicates on the Recreational Marine Driver/Personal Watercraft Licence Application(F2334) that they require corrective lenses to safely operate their ship the condition will be recorded on their licence record.

        If an applicant indicates a different answer to what is recorded on TICA, that is if they indicate that they do not require corrective lenses whereas previously it was recorded that they did, it will be removed from their licence.

        If a marine licence applicant holds a DL and is required to wear corrective lenses for their DL the corrective lenses condition will automatically be recorded for their recreational marine licences.
      Medical Fitness
      Medical Fitness
        To be granted a marine licence an applicant must be medically fit to safely operate a ship.

        If a marine licence applicant has a medical condition which may affect the safe operation of a ship Maritime Safety Queensland may impose conditions or restrictions to the granting of the licence.

        The licence holder must operate a ship in accordance with the conditions imposed.
      Other Conditions
      Other Conditions
        A court order may impose operating restrictions on a marine licence.

        Refer: Restricted Marine Licence Guide
      How conditions will be displayed
      How conditions will be displayed
        Marine Licence Indicator Receipt (MLIR)
        Marine Licence Indicator Receipt (MLIR)
          The MLIR will display a 'Y' in the conditions field if conditions apply or 'N' if no conditions apply. If conditions apply, full details of the condition will be displayed.

        Smartcard MLI
        Smartcard MLI
          The Smartcard MLI will display a 'Y' in the conditions field on the face of the smartcard if conditions apply or 'N' if no conditions apply.

          Note:The wearing of corrective lenses is classed as a condition.

          Refer to The Smartcard Guide -> Smartcard Specifications -> Front of the card & Back of the card

        DL Smartcard with MLI
        DL Smartcard with MLI
          The DL Smartcard with a MLI will display a 'Y' in the conditions field on the rear of the smartcard if conditions apply or 'N' if no conditions apply.

          Refer to The Smartcard Guide -> Smartcard Specifications -> Front of the card & Back of the card

        Smartcard Chip
        Smartcard Chip
          The smartcard chip will store the code 'S' if a corrective lenses condition applies and 'TEXT' if other conditions apply.

          Refer to The Smartcard Guide -> The Smartcard Chip
      Adding or Removing Conditions
      Adding or Removing Conditions
        When conditions, are displayed on the Smartcard MLI or the Drivers Licence Smartcard with MLI, a replacement smartcard will be issued fee free.
        • Replacement application Form F3006 for a DL Smartcard with MLI issued in a non-formless centre.
        • Replacement application Form F4817 for a Smartcard MLI.

        Change corrective lenses condition
        Change corrective lenses condition
          Smartcard MLI
          Smartcard MLI
            ADD
            New Smartcard Required
            New Smartcard Required
              If the marine licence is currently unconditional, that is the Smartcard MLI shows an 'N' in the conditions field, adding an 'S' condition will require a replacement Smartcard MLI.

              To add a 'corrective lenses' condition to a currently unconditional licence, the customer must complete the Replacement Marine Licence Indicator form F4817.

            New Smartcard Not Required
            New Smartcard Not Required
              If the marine licence is currently conditional, that is the Smartcard MLI show a 'Y' in the conditions field, adding an 'S' condition will not require a replacement Smartcard MLI.

            Remove
            To remove an 'S' condition the marine licence holder must apply to MSQ (contact details below).

            On receipt of a letter from MSQ authorising the removal of any corrective lenses condition and the return of the Smartcard MLI, the "S" condition is to be removed in TICA and a replacement Smartcard MLI application is to be processed (fee free).

            Note:There is no eyesight testing done for marine licences by the CSO.

          Drivers licence with MLI
          Drivers licence with MLI
            An 'S' condition (corrective lenses) in TICA applies to both the Driver licence (DL) and marine licence. A customer who has a DL with MLI must use the DL process to add or remove an 'S' condition.

        Change 'TEXT' condition
        Change 'TEXT' condition
          At customer request
          At customer request
            To change a TEXT condition related to a restricted marine licence the customer must apply to the court for a variation of the restrictions.

            To change other TEXT conditions the customer must apply to MSQ (contact details below).

          At request of administering agency
          At request of administering agency
            The Administering Agency may amend a TEXT condition through a show cause process.


        Record Conditions (if required)
        Record Conditions (if required)
          If MSQ have provided a licence conditions letter to give to the customer, record the text condition "Must at all times operate in accordance with the conditions detailed in the letter from the administering agency".
