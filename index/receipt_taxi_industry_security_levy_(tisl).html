    What is a Taxi Industry Security Levy (TISL)
    What is a Taxi Industry Security Levy (TISL)
      The Taxi Industry Security Levy is collected from owners of taxi service licences that are held in areas where secure taxi ranks operate. Invoicing of this levy occurs in August of each year.

    Note:All documentation must be forwarded to the relevant PT Office within 2 - 3 days of receipting payment.
