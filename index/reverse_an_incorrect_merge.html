Reverse an Incorrect Merge (3237)

Applies to
Applies to
    Reversing the incorrect merging of two customer records.
1
Before you start
Before you start
    You will need to identify all of the products against the CRN, and ascertain which product belongs to which customer. The customer may be able to assist you with this. If not, refer to the steps below.
    • Driver Licence
    • Correspondence
    • Vehicle Registration
    • Card 18+
    • Infringements / Breaches
    • Work Diaries
    • Marine Licence
    • Industry Authority
    • Customer Details (check for involved party qual)Adult Proof of Age Card
2
Create New CRN
Create New CRN
    Create a new CRN on TICA for one of the customers
    Create Individual Customer (TICA)
3
Separate Customer Information
Separate Customer Information
    Customer Details
    Customer Details
      • Determine which EOI/EOR documents, customer qualifications, address information belongs to each customer. Most of this information will be available on documents retrieved for licensing and registration products below.
      Remove new CRN customers details from existing record
      Add Customer Qualifications (TRAILS)
      Record/Update/Exempt/Invalidate EOI, Name Change, Residency Doc or Exemption
      • Any updates to the customer such as name and address changes need to be forwarded as a system BIID
      • Add Information to proforma (Refer to: Step 3)

    Driver Licensing Products
    Driver Licensing Products
      • Retrieve the driver licensing documents from Iron Mountain to ascertain which licence history belongs to which customer
      • Add Information to proforma (Refer to: Step 3)
      • Replace licence (if applicable)

        Replace Smartcard
        Conduct 1:1 Image Match
        Related TICA Steps
        Capturing facial image
        Capturing facial image
          If TICA prompts to capture the customer's facial image, direct the customer to
          the seat for the default camera and request them to:
          • Sit on the seat provided and to stay within the markings
            Note: If customer uses a mobility aid, retract or remove the chair and have the customer position themselves within the designated markings of the photo area
          • Look straight ahead at the camera (a frontal image of their face is required)
          • Remove any head attire or sunglasses (unless there are valid religious, cultural or medical reasons for not doing so, then refer to Manager)
            .
            The customer must remain in your line of vision during the entire transaction.
            .
          • Once the facial image is successfully captured, request the customer to return to your counter.
        1:1 image match fails
        1:1 image match fails
          If the 1:1 image match fails contact the Identity Management Unit for an assessment of the image

          If IMU advises that the image match is unacceptable:
          • IMU will advise what action is to be taken
            Refer to:             Smartcard facial image recognition

            End of procedure
          .
          If IMU advises that the image match is acceptable:
            Continue with procedure

    Registration Products
    Registration Products
      • Retrieve the vehicle registration documents through Iron Mountain to ascertain which vehicle belongs to which customer
      • Process a dummy transfer and exempt all of the requirements on TRAILS.
      • Add text message explaining reason for transaction
        Add Text Message (TRAILS)
        (TICA) Add (use text type option)
        (TICA) Edit (use text type option)
        (TICA) Delete

    Infringements
    Infringements
      • Attempt identification of infringement owner by comparing known customer details with the offender details e.g. vehicle details
      • Add Infringement details to proforma below.
        Infringement notices are only retained on-site at offence processing for 9-12 months and then forwarded to Iron Mountain. Infringement notices are retained at Iron Mountain for a period of 7 years.
        Infringement Request
      • Contact the Offence Processing Unit to retrieve the infringements against the CRN and identify who they belong to
      • Request Transfer of Infringement Notice.
        Transfer of Infringement Notices Request
        Note: The Offence Processing Unit is unable to transfer tickets that have been forwarded to SPER (status of FINSPER, VIPSPER) old archived tickets and camera detected offence. These infringements will need to be transferred by contacting Service Centre Online and should be added to the BIID
      Note: A new CRN must be created before requesting these amendments.

    Breaches
    Breaches
      • Retrieve filing for breach
      • Contact HelpDirect (by email) to request transfer of breach/s to the correct person
      Note: A new CRN must be created before requesting these amendments.

    Correspondence
    Correspondence
      • Identify which correspondence belongs to each CRN.
      • Add Information to proforma (Refer: Step 3)
    18+ Cards
    18+ Cards
      • Invalidate the incorrect 18+ Card on TICA
        Invalidate 18+ Card
      • Issue an APA Card against the new CRN (if applicable)
        Issue APA Card (TICA)
      • Do an adjustment to credit the customers account for the fee (if applicable)
        Create Adjustment (TICA)
      Note: There should be no fee charged to the customer.

    APA Cards
    APA Cards
      • Invalidate the incorrect APA Card
        Invalidate Incorrect APA Card (TICA)
      • Issue another APA Card against the new CRN (if applicable)
        Issue APA Card (TICA)
      • Do an adjustment to credit the customers account for the fee (if applicable)
        Create Adjustment (TICA)
      Note: There should be no fee charged to the customer.

    Work diaries
    Work diaries
      The Work Diary will show a status of cancelled on TRAILS due to the merge being processed (system problem).
      • To correct this you need to display the cancelled Work Diary on CPRO
      • Using the Update action put the reason PAPI in the action / reason field and enter. The status of the Log Book will then show as current.
      • If the work diary is on the incorrect CRN, add information to proforma (Refer to: Step 3)

    Marine licences
    Marine licences
      • Contact Help Direct (by email) to transfer the Marine Licence to the correct CRN.

    Industry Authority
    Industry Authority
      • Unmatch the Payment
        If unmatching a smartcard payment that has two lines of payment information, the Card fee MUST be unmatched first.

        Smartcard only

        If there is a smartcard with a status of Active or Production Requested:
        • Contact the IMU to request the unmatching of the payment and the invalidation and deletion of any images required.
      • Contact Help Direct (by email) to invalidate the accreditation
      • Re-issue the accreditation under the new CRN
      • Use the invoice action for payment of the accreditation

    Other Customer Details
