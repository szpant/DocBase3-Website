Excess Dimensions Permit (3101)

What is it?An excess dimension exemption permit may be considered to allow access to the road network for a specific type of vehicle that exceeds regulation:
  • Width
  • Height
  • Length
  • Rear overhang
  • Forward projection
and is not covered by an appropriate Notice or Guide.
Regulated Dimensions
Regulated Dimensions
    DimensionRegulation Limits
    Width<= 2.5m
    HeightNo greater than:
    4.4m - Double decker bus
    4.6m - Trailer carrying cattle, horses, pigs or sheep
    4.6m - Double Decker car carrier (top deck loaded)
    4.3m - Any other vehicle
    LengthNo greater than:
    12.5 - Rigid vehicle
    18.0 - Articulated Bus
    Combination LengthNo greater that 19m
    Refer -> Registerable Vehicles -> Heavy Vehicles ->Sample Graphics
    Rear
    Overhang    		The rear overhang of a vehicle must not be more than the lessor of 3.7m and -
    Semi/Dog Trailers
    • 60% of the distance between the articulation point and the rear over hang line
    Other Trailers
    • 60% of the distance between the front of the body or load carrying area and the rear overhang line
    Vehicles
    • 60% of the distance between the centre of the front axle and the rear overhang line
Guidelines
RequirementsThe National Heavy Vehicle Regulator (NHVR) administers the new National Heavy Vehicle Law that provides for granting an exemption to the Heavy Vehicle (Mass, Dimension and Loading) National Regulation.
Where to Apply
For Permit
For Permit
    Excess Dimensions by load:
    Excess Dimensions by load:
      Applications for all Class 1 Over-size and Over-mass (OSOM) and Special Purpose Vehicles (SPV) undertaking trips within Queensland are made to TMR.

      Applications with supporting documentation should be emailed to:
      QLDAccess_HVROPO@tmr.qld.gov.au

      Note:For trips that required Police Escorts you will also need to:
      • Complete a Queensland Police Service 'Application for Special Services form' and ensure the Permit Application Number is recorded in the top right hand corner.
      • Email the Application for Special Services to: HeavyVehicleRoadOperationsProgramOffice@police.qld.gov.au
        For Police Escorts phone 1300 105 647

      All other applications, including cross-border applications and cross-border OSOM/SPV applications apply to NHVR. Applications should be submitted to the NHVR using the forms available on the NHVR website.

      To contact the NHVR call 1300 MYNHVR (1300 696 487) or Fax (07) 3309 8777 or Email - info@nhvr.gov.au


    Excess Dimensions by Construction:
    Excess Dimensions by Construction:
      Where the dimensions are exceeded by construction, the customer must apply to the NHVR for a permit/document allowing the vehicle to be registered and driven within Australia.
