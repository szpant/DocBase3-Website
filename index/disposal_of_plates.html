Disposal of Plates (3088)

DisposalSims Group Australia Holdings Limited ("Sims") has been contracted to manage collection and disposal of obsolete (surrendered /cancelled) plates in the majority of the Department's obsolete plate storage locations, i.e, CSCs, QGAPs, Motor Dealers, Motor Auctions and other agencies.

Sims have provided selected collection sites with lockable wheelie bins (and in special cases, a skip) the bins are the property of Sims.

Sites with plate disposal bins will be serviced as detailed in the Plates collection spreadsheet. Clusters are serviced as a run, any services outside of this schedule will incur additional costs for CSB.

Sites that do not have bins should manage plates as specified in the Plates collections spreadsheet. If no collection method is specified the plates should be forwarded to Iron Mountain with filing.

Plates Collection

Note:All sites where the collection of plates is covered by Sims Metal Management MUST NOT use other methods of disposal.

TMR disposal arrangements meet Government environmental sustainability, asset security and workplace health and safety objectives.
Requesting additional servicesAll requests for Plate Collections must be made directly to the metal recycling contractor using the following template:

Plate Bin Collection Request - template

Note:To minimise the cost to CSB, collections should only be requested when the bins are full.
Ownership and SecurityAll plates remain the property of the Department and must be returned to the Department.

Obsolete number plates must be kept secure until collected or despatched as per one of the disposal arrangements.

Number plates surrendered in CSCs should be regularly collected from the counters throughout the day and placed in lockable plate storage bins.

Plate storage bins must always be in a controlled access environment (designated staff area with controlled access points)

Lockable plate storage bins within the location's premises can be kept unlocked during business hours, and then locked at close of business each day.

Lockable plate storage bins outside the location's premises must be locked at all times and held in a secure area.

Keys for lockable storage bin locks are to be assigned to a responsible officer within the location and kept secure at all times. The locations access register must be maintained as per current policies and procedures.

This policy applies to all locations in all circumstances.
RecyclingAll plates are to be recycled.

There will be no need to deface or cut up plates if one of the approved disposal arrangements are in place.
