Account Management (229)

What it isAccount management is the management of debits and credits that appear in a customer’s TMR account.

Customers who are approved by Finance may have an account for ease of transacting business. Having an account allows the customer to debit/credit their account for multiple transactions.
Benefits of an AccountBenefits of having an account include:
  • Ability to record several transactions on one statement
  • Creation of a 28 day payment period
  • Ability to use Bpay to pay an account
  • Ability to issue one refund cheque instead of many
  • Ability to reapply credit gained from a cancellation
Payment of an AccountFull payment is due within 28 days from issue date of the account statement.

Within this period, customers are still able to do business with TMR.
Account DebitsA debit is created in an account when the customer owes TMR money and is shown as a positive amount. Some transactions will automatically creates a debit, these include:
  • Vehicle Transfers
  • Replacement plates
  • Pro-rata of fees
  • Manual Adjustments
  • Reversal of transaction where payment has not been honoured
  • For example, dishonoured cheque, credit card refusal

An Account Statement will not be generated until the debt is at least $20.

Refer: Admin > 3. Money > Account Management Guide > Account Details > Account Statements
Account CreditsA credit is created when the department owes the customer money (shown as a negative amount). This will occur in the following circumstances:
  • Overpayments
  • Cancellation with a refund due
  • Pro-rata of fees
  • Manual Adjustment

If the money is not to be used for a specific transaction in the future and is more than $10 the amount is to be refunded.

Any refund amount payable for the unused portion of a deceased customer's driver licence will be issued upon request regardless of the refund amount. For all other requests, amounts $10.00 or less will not be refunded unless requested by the customer.
