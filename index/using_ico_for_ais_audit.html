Using ICO for AIS Audit (7619)

Applies to
Applies to
    This procedure outlines how to conduct checks in ICO for AIS Audit or Investigations.
1
Login to ICO using your TMR email address and the password you created.
2
Select Auditor from the menu on the left.
3
Enter the AIS number or AIS name into the search bar.
4
Records matching the search criteria will be displayed.
5
Select the relevant AIS in the AIS Name column (the name will be underlined in blue).
6
Confirm the AIS details under the AIS details heading.
7
Select the Examiners heading to display the approved examiners (AE) associated with the AIS who are enrolled in ICO.

AE’s associated with the AIS in RUMIIS will not be displayed if they are not enrolled in ICO.
8
The Approval Holder can choose to exclude an AE from using ICO even though they are still associated with the AIS in RUMIIS.

AE’s who have been excluded from ICO are listed under the Inactive Examiners heading.

Examiners who are active in ICO are displayed under the Active Examiners heading.
9
Select the Inspections heading to display all of the inspection records for the AIS.
10
You can search the records by entering any of the details recorded into the search bar.

FOR EXAMPLE, the inspection number, plate number and so on.
11
You can also sort the records by clicking on the heading of each column to sort the data in alphabetical or numerical order.
12
You can also select Advanced Search to search the records using a VIN, Chassis number, plate number or inspection number.
13
To view the details of the inspection record, including any photos/files attached to ICO, click on the relevant item in the Results column.
14
To view the PDF inspection certificate that was issued, click on the blue button to the right of the relevant inspection record with SC (Safety Certificate), COI (Certificate of Inspection) or Report (Inspection Report).
15
To email a copy of the inspection certificate, click on the envelope symbol beside the blue button and enter your email address.
