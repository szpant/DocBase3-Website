Replacing Licences (2925)

Replacement licences
When to Replace
Note:An operator may renew, rather than replace, a damaged, destroyed, lost or stolen driver licence if it is within 12 months of the expiry date.

When to replace smartcard
When to replace smartcard
    Smartcard Replacement

    Note:If a customer's licence is lost, stolen, damaged or destroyed, they may be able to apply for a replacement online.

    Refer: Online Services > 1. Online Services > TMR Online Services Guide > TMR Online Services List > Licensing > Replace driver licence
Driver Licence Receipt
Suspended Driver LicenceA replacement driver licence cannot be issued to a person who is serving a period of licence suspension. (TO(RUM) Act. s127)

Grant of a marine licence whilst driver licence suspended
Grant of a marine licence whilst driver licence suspended
    If a person is granted a Marine Licence under TO(MS) Act, and the person's Qld driver licence is suspended, the person will be issued a Marine Licence Indicator Receipt. The person will need to apply for a replacement driver licence (free of charge) after the licence suspension has ended.

    Note:The smartcard will not be automatically sent to the person if they do not return to apply for a replacement licence with the MLI code.
Return and non-return of Superseded Driver LicenceFor information about the return and non-return of Superseded Driver Licence, please Refer: Smartcard > 1. Smartcard > The Smartcard Guide > Smartcard Return
Replaced licence located
Replaced licence located
Replaced licence located
    If a licence holder applies for a replacement licence because the licence is lost or stolen and the original licence is located after the issue of the replacement licence, the licence holder is not required to return the licence.

    The licence holder must cut up the superseded licence. If the licence is returned to TMR, the licence must be destroyed.

    Note:This information is contained in the Driver Licence Smartcard Delivery Notice form F4625.

    Refer: Smartcard > 1. Smartcard > The Smartcard Guide > Destroying Returned smartcard
