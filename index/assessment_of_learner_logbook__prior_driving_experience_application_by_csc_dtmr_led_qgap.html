Assessment of Learner Logbook/ Prior Driving Experience Application by CSC/DTMR led QGAP (3157)

Customer Service Centres (CSCs), and Department of Transport and Main Roads-led QGAPs, may only accept a Queensland learner logbook and/or a prior driving experience application for lodgement and assessment in exceptional circumstances.


Resubmitting after returned to customer as "Incomplete" by LLBCPU
Resubmitting after returned to customer as "Incomplete" by LLBCPU
    The customer is resubmitting a learner logbook and/or prior driving experience application previously assessed by the Learner Logbook CPU (LLBCPU) and deemed "Incomplete" due to:

    Incomplete reasons, Refer: Driver Licence > 6. Learner Licences > Learner Logbook Guide > Action taken if Incomplete or Inaccurate > Logbook and/or Prior Driving Experience Application Incomplete or Inaccurate (Action Taken)
    and
      • Provides the Notice of assessment issued by the LLBCPU detailing why the documentation was assessed as incomplete (unless all details clearly entered on TICA)
      • Has a scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test
      • Resubmits the completed documentation during normal business hours; and
      • Resubmits the completed documentation at least two hours prior to the scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test
Customer submits a learner logbook for a scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test
Customer submits a learner logbook for a scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test
    When a customer presents a learner logbook for an assessment at a CSC or DTMR led QGAP to undertake a scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test on or within 12 business days of their booking date.

    The customer must:
    • submit the documentation during normal business hours; and
    • submit the documentation at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled practical driving test if within 14 days following 17th birthday.

    Learner logbooks and prior driving experience applications should not be mailed internally from a licensing issuing centre to the LLBCPU in any circumstance.

    Customers who do not meet the above criteria are to be advised to lodge their completed documentation using the normal channels:
    DocumentationWhere to Lodge
    Learner Logbook only
    or
    Learner Logbook + prior driving experience application
    • Australia Post
    • Online using the learner logbook app
    Prior driving experience application onlyBy mail to the Learner Logbook CPU


Where appropriate, the Call Centre/SBS will advise customers to submit the learner logbook and/or prior driving experience application at least 24 hours prior to a scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test.

The CSC/QGAP Office will assess the learner logbook and/or prior driving experience application and record the result prior to the applicant commencing their Q-SAFE practical driving test.

Assessment checklists
Assessment checklists
    The main assessment form can be located under Assessment Logbook and/or Prior Driving Experience Application. Refer: Driver Licence > 6. Learner Licences > Learner Logbook Guide >Assessing the Logbook and/or Application > Assess the Logbook and/or prior driving experience application

IMPORTANT NOTE: The applicant should be advised that they must make prior arrangements for assessment of the learner logbook with the Manager of the appropriate CSC if the test is to be undertaken within 12 business days of their scheduled Q-SAFE practical driving test.

TMR mobile licensing units may also accept a learner logbook and/or prior driving experience application for lodgement and assessment when operating in remote communities (particularly far north Queensland) and there is a need to assess a customer's documentation before taking them out for a Q-SAFE practical driving test on the same day.
