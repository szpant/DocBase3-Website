Pilot and Escort Vehicle Driver Accreditation (7525)

Project NamePilot and Escort vehicle driver accreditation
WhenJuly 2020
BackgroundIndustry Accreditation and Licensing (LTSR-CSSR) is contacting all Queensland Pilot and Escort vehicle drivers to advise them about changes to Victoria's accreditation scheme.
Victoria will require Queensland-accredited Escort vehicle drivers to provide evidence of having completed the traffic control competency TLIF3060 - Control Traffic as a Pilot Vehicle Operator within the last three years prior to July 2020 to continue operating in Victoria.

Escort vehicle drivers will need to provide a copy of a Statement of Attainment, Completion certificate, CPVD Renewal or similar document to the Department of Transport (DoT) in Victoria (formerly VicRoads) by email certifiedpilots@roads.vic.gov.au or by post.

This change does not affect Queensland-accredited Pilot vehicle drivers unless they also hold accreditation as an Escort vehicle driver and wish to operate in Victoria.
Who will be impacted and how?Escort vehicle driver accreditation holders – Escort vehicle driver accreditation holders will need to provide documentation to evidence training has been completed within the last three years to Victoria by July 2020.

Customer Service Centre (CSC)
Customer Service Centre (CSC)
    Customer Service Centre Officers may receive enquiries in person from current accreditation holders about the changes to Victoria’s requirements or recognition in Victoria. Enquiries should be referred to the Department of Transport (DoT) in Victoria (formerly Vic Roads). Pilot and Escort vehicle driver information is available on TMR's website. Q&A's have been developed to assist in responding to any enquiries.

Customer Contact Centre (CCC)

Enquiries should be referred to the Department of Transport (DoT) in Victoria. Pilot and Escort vehicle driver information is available on TMR's website.

Q&A's have been developed to assist in responding to any enquiries.
Further informationThe correspondence is in a Q&A format and will be emailed/posted to all accreditation holders in February. The Pilot and Escort vehicle driver information on the TMR website ('Queensland- accredited pilot/escort vehicle drivers wishing to operate interstate') will also be updated.
