Sale of Safety Certificate/Certificate of Inspection (SC/COI) Books (4656)


Where to Apply
Where to apply
Where to apply
    Customers can apply for Safety Certificate/Certificate Of Inspection books through:
    CSCs
    CSCs
      • Requests in person
    Mail
    Mail
      • The customer must send:
        • AIS EOI documents Refer: Accreditation > Approved Inspection Station > Approved Inspection Station (AIS) Guide > EOI Requirements > AIS EOI Requirements
        • The correct amount for payment (this includes the cost of the books and the postage and handling fee)
        • A copy of the current AIS Approval
        • The signed and completed reorder now form which can be found in the inspection certificate books.
Application/Sale of inspection books
Inspection Books
Inspection Books
    There are 5 categories of inspection certificate books.
    • LV - Cars or Light Commercial Vehicles (up to and including 4.5 tonne RGVM)
    • MC - Motorcycle
    • LT- Caravans and Trailers (over 0.75 tonne ATM up to and including 3.5 tonne ATM)
    • HT - Caravans and Trailers (over 3.5 tonne ATM to and 10.0 tonne ATM)
    • HV - Vehicles (over 4.5 tonne up to and including 16.0 tonne RGVM)

    Note:There are no inspection certificate books for CL inspections. AIS's can only issue certificates of inspection for CL by using Inspection Certificates Online (ICO).

    Ensure the correct category of book is given to the customer - the inspection categories for the AIS are displayed on the AIS Approval.

    CSO's are required to:
    CSO's are required to:
      • check re-order form has been completed and only categories held are being applied for and signed by the proprietor or nominee
      • check that the proprietor/nominee is recorded on RUMIIS as being current
      • ensure that books are receipted to the CRN for the AIS not the nominee/proprietors CRN.
      • ensure that receipt/invoice information is included on the office use only section of the application

      Refer: QT Shop > 1. Customer Products > The How To: Customer Products > Customer Product Procedures > Sell Work Diary, Modification Plates, SC/COI Book > Sell Safety Certificate/Certificate of Inspection (SC/COI) Book
Lodging application
Lodging the application:
Lodging the application:
    • Complete the office use section including the receipt or invoice number
    • Place documents and all attachments with the operator's daily filing sheet for that day to be sent to Iron Mountain.
