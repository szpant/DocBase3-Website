Information about delivering training for Class MC for licence outcome
Information about delivering training for Class MC for licence outcome
    TLIC4006 is the competency unit required for people wishing to be licensed for this class of vehicle. This unit is delivered through TMR approved registered training organisations.

    Note:To deliver TLIC4006 through a registered training organisation, the accredited driver trainer must be appropriately qualified as an assessor by having completed either:
    • TAE40116 – Certificate IV in Training and Assessment, OR
    • TAE40110 Certificate IV qualification in Training and Assessment with one from (a) and one from (b) below:
          a) either TAELLN411 or TAELLN401A - Adress adult language, literacy and numeracy skills
          b) TAEASS502, TAEASS502A or TAEASS502B - Design and develop assessment tools
