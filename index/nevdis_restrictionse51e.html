Stolen Process
    If a vehicle is recorded as stolen in NEVDIS CSC is to:
    • Note vehicle details and restriction type
    • Contact Help Direct
    • Advise customer to take a seat (do not advise that QPS are being contacted)
    • Retain paperwork

    Help Direct will:
    • Contact QPS to determine the status of the vehicle

    If vehicle remains stolen on the QPS system:
    • Transaction cannot be processed
    • CSC to contact local QPS station to have Officers come and investigate

    If vehicle restriction no longer applies:
    • Help Direct will remove the TICA restriction
    • QPS will have the NEVDIS restriction removed
    • CSC can process transaction when restrictions removed

    If applicants vehicle has the same identifiers as the vehicle listed as stolen but is not the vehicle listed as stolen a surrogate identifier is to be issued.

    For the process to issue a surrogate identifier, please refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > The How To: Vehicle Requirements > Vehicle Requirements Procedures > Issue a Surrogate Vehicle Identifier
