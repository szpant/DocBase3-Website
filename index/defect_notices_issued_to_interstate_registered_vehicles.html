Defect Notices issued to interstate registered vehicles (7644)


Applies to
Applies to
    Issuing Defect Notice to an interstate registered vehicle.
1
    Issue the Defect Notice to the driver of the defective vehicle.
2
    In the QCIS intercept screen enter Defect Notice number and severity under 'Compliance Action'.
3
    DMS the duplicate copy of the Defect Notice.
    The recommended DMS Container name is:
    'Vehicle Management - Vehicle Inspection (Programmed and On-Road) - Interstate Defect Notices'.
4
    The recommended DMS File Subject naming convention is Defect Notice Number, Vehicle registration number and QCIS intercept ID
    (i.e. D67984 9 - 123ABC - QCIS ID 123456)
5
    Forward a copy of the Defect Notice using the template below, to the relevant interstate authority:

    Interstate Defect Letter Template
