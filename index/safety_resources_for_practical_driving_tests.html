Safety Resources for Practical Driving Tests (7562)

Safety measuresDuring the COVID-19 pandemic a number of additional safety measures have been implemented to ensure the health and safety of CSC staff and customers.
Presenting for the testIf a customer presents for a test but is refused because they don’t pass the screening tests or the vehicle cleanliness guidelines, they won’t forfeit their booking fee. It can be refunded to their TMR customer account to be used for a future booking. They should not be re-prioritised unless there is a serious emergent need.  
 
Similarly, if a customer is unable to attend their test booking because they are feeling unwell on the day of their test they will not forfeit their booking fee. These customers can be re-prioritised for a future booking.  
Applicant DeclarationEach driving test applicant must complete a declaration prior to taking their test.

Manager/DE Requirements
Manager/DE Requirements
    Managers are to ensure that all Driving Examiners are aware that they need to notify their manager immediately if they;
    • Are experiencing or displaying any COVID-19 symptoms (fever, cough, sore throat, fatigue and/or shortness of breath).
    • Have been in contact with a confirmed case of COVID-19.
    • Driving Examiners are not to conduct any tests upon experiencing either of the above.

    Additionally the Toolbox Talk - Driving Examinations should be delivered monthly to all staff undertaking driver assessments.
    Refer below: Safety Resources - Novel Coronavirus Toolbox Talk - Driving Examinations
Additional persons in a vehicle Additional safety measures have been developed to allow additional persons in a vehicle for training purposes or where the applicant requires a support person during their test.
