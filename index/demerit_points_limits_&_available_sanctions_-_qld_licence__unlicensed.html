*Licence may be one granted outside Qld that corresponds to a Qld Learner, P/P1/P2 Type or Open licence.

Note:An F4007 - Suspension of Authority to Drive may not be generated and sent to a non-Qld learner or provisional licence holder. Customers should be advised to keep their residential address up to date for possible correspondence. They should also be advised to transfer their licence to Qld if they are able to meet permanent resident requirements.
^Once the sanction has commenced a person cannot obtain a Qld licence until the requisite suspension period ends. Refer: Demerit Point Limits & Assoc Sanctions - Interstate / Foreign

If the person holds a valid non-Queensland licence, the sanction may be withdrawn. Refer: Demerit Point Sanctions - Withdrawing an Unlicensed Person (DEFA) Sanction
#If during the 1 year period any of the demerit points were accumulated while the person held an open licence the person will not be sanctioned.
Example:
On 1 April 2017, Joe Citizen accumulated 2 demerit points while on an open licence
On 1 September 2017 he is issued a probationary licence following a disqualification.
On 20 January 2018 he accumulated 2 more demerit points on his probationary licence

While he has accumulated 4 in 1 year, as 2 points were accumulated when he had an open licence he will not be sanctioned.
