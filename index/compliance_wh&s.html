Q Fever
Q Fever

    Q Fever, also called Query Fever is caused by a bacteria commonly found in cattle, sheep and goats and wild animals such as kangaroos, wallabies and birds. Humans typically get Q Fever through contact with placental tissue of fluid, or urine of infected animals.

    TMR provides Q Fever vaccinations for Transport Inspectors. Transport Inspectors may at anytime during their employment as a Transport Inspector request the vaccination. Having the vaccination is not compulsory.

    For further information on Managing work-related infectious and parasitic disease refer:
    Procedure – Managing Work-related Infectious and Parasitic Disease
