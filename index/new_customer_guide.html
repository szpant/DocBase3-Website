New Customer Guide (1637)


What is a CustomerA customer is any individual/organisation that does business with the Department of Transport and Main Roads and has allocated a:
  • Customer reference number
Customers are created as entities in the register (TICA) separate to any products. Products such as registration and licences are then linked to the customer.
What is a Customer Reference Number (CRN)A CRN is a unique identifier of a customer. It is allocated when a customer is created in TICA. If the customer has a Qld Driver Licence, their CRN is also their Driver Licence number.
Recording Customer DetailsWhen customers are created their individual details should be recorded, this should include their identifiers, address, date of birth, place of birth and eContact details.

Place of birth
Place of birth
    The new customer application and TICA require a city/town of birth to be provided. If the city/town differs from the place listed on the birth certificate, use other sources (if required) to verify that the place is within the town/city specified on the form.

Note:If the country is Australia (AUS), all place of birth fields are mandatory
(the Australian State must be entered as an abbreviation e.g. QLD).

If the country is New Zealand (NZL), the Country and Town/City fields are mandatory.

If the country is other than Australia or New Zealand, the Country and Town/City are to be recorded, however only the Country field is mandatory.

Documents Required
Non-formless centres
Non-formless centres
    New customers may need to complete New Customer Application Form (F3503).

    As Evidence of Identity (EOI) is not required to create a customer on TICA, the New Customer Application form (F3503) is only used in conjunction with other product application forms when:
    • A customer is conducting business for the first time with TMR
    • The product requires EOI
    The application form for the relevant product will indicate when a New Customer Application (F3503) is required to be completed.

    Example when F3503 required
    Example when F3503 required
      A new customer registering a vehicle requires a completed:
      • New Customer Application form (F3503)
      and
      • Vehicle Registration Application form (F3518)

      The Vehicle Registration Application form (F3518) indicates when a New Customer Application form (F3503) is required
    Example when F3503 NOT required
    Example when F3503 NOT required
      A new customer applying for a learner licence only requires a completed Driver Licence Application/Renewal form (F3000).

      A New Customer Application form (F3503) is not required as all the relevant customer information is included in the Driver Licence Application/Renewal form (F3000)

Formless centres
Formless centres
    If a new customer presents at a formless centre to complete a transaction, application forms for individual applicants may not be required. Refer to the Document Requirements for the relevant transaction.

    Application forms for new Organisation customers are required.

    If the customer is not required to complete an application form then mandatory questions must be asked. Refer: Fees and Forms > Formless > Formless Customer Applications > Mandatory Questions
    CRN CardThe CRN Card has been developed for TMR staff to provide to new customers as a reference tool when they make future contact with the department.

    The CRN Card is a business card that contains space on the front for operators to write the customer's CRN, and useful contact information for TMR on the back. Please refer below for a sample of the card.

    CRN Card Sample

    Where a new customer would like to keep a written reference of their CRN, staff can write the customer's CRN on the CRN Card and provide it to the customer.


    Note:Please reiterate to the customer that the CRN Card is not an evidence of identity document and cannot be used as such.

    For information about how to order stock of the CRN Card, Refer: Admin > 4. Stock Control > Stock Control Guide > Ordering Stock > Forms and Stationery > CRN Card
