Passenger Transport Audit Entity (7436)

The Passenger Transport Audit Entity (PT Audit) has been introduced to capture PT Audits conducted by Compliance.

Recording of PT Audits allows for the capture of time spent on conducting audits.

All staff have the ability to create this entry type via CID.
1
Select Data Entry
Select Data Entry
2
Select PT Audit (Network) from the Form Menu
Select PT Audit (Network) from the Form Menu
    Note: Selecting PT Audit (Network) will allow you to link other entities.

3
The PT Audit (Network) Form will be displayed
The PT Audit (Network) Form will be displayed

What the PT Audit entity links to in CID
1
Disseminated Intel Product
Disseminated Intel Product
    • Disseminated intel Product may only be linked by Heavy Vehicle Analysis
2
Case
Case
    • A Case may be linked as a trigger or an outcome
3
Complaint
Complaint
    • A Complaint may be linked as a trigger for the Audit
4
Crash
Crash
    • Crash entity may only be created by heavy Vehicle analysis, this link may be requested as a trigger for an Audit.
5
Event
Event
    • Events can only be created by Heavy Vehicle Analysis, however once created it may be linked as a trigger for an Audit.
6
Accreditation / Scheme
Accreditation / Scheme
    • This entity can only be created by Heavy Vehicle Analysis, once created this may be linked as an involved party to the Audit.
7
Organisation / Person
Organisation / Person
    • The Organisation/Person involved (if applicable) must be linked to the Audit to identify the involved parties
8
Task
Task
    • PT Audits link to tasks in the same way cases do. The individual tasks record the actions taken and also hours of effort spent. Effort should be for all compliance officers involved in the task. E.g. if two TI's attend on Audit and the activity takes two hours - that is four hours of effort.
    • The correct tasks must be used to enable accurate reporting on hours of effort.

9
Education
Education
    • Education entity is to be used, when the outcome of the audit is to educate.
10
Infringement
Infringement
    • The Infringement entity should be used if the outcome of the audit results in an infringement being issued. The infringement entity can only be created by Heavy Vehicle Analysis. Officers should scan the infringement and forward to Compliance.Intelligence@tmr.qld.gov.au referencing the PT Audit Record ID.
