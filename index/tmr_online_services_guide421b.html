TMR Online Services Guide (5101)


What it isThe Department of Transport and Main Roads (TMR) offers customers a wide range of online services that enable them to complete transactions and obtain specific information regarding their licence/s, industry authorities and vehicle/vessel registration without attending a Customer Service Centre (CSC).
Terms and conditions of useBefore a customer can use any TMR online services, they must read and agree to the general terms and conditions of use. Some services may have further specific conditions which may also need to be agreed.
Online Services Screen Shots/ e-correspondence examplesSupporting screen shots of TMR's Online Services can be found on the Business Systems Support SharePoint Page. This page also contains examples of emails, eNotices and eReminders that are sent to customers.

Information specific to Direct Debit can be found on the Direct Debit Support Materials page.
Accessing TMR Online ServicesTo access online services, certain information must be entered by the customer. The amount of information required depends on the online service being accessed and the associated risk to the customer's online information.

The levels of access and requirements to use online services are outlined below.

NOAC RestrictionsA No Online Access Customer (NOAC) restriction is available to customer who wish to prevent access to TMR on-line services. The NOAC must be requested by the customer and will remain in place until the customer requests its removal.

For the application an removal process refer to:

Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Customer Details > System Information > Qualifications, Restrictions & Text > Customer Restrictions > NOAC

Account ManagementAll enquiries and processes (excluding booked hire services) relating to the payment component of online lodgement services including:
  • refund of full payment (less merchant fee)
  • missing payments
  • multiple payments

need to be directed to Cash and Banking in writing via email.

To arrange the generation of approved refunds in full, CSC/Hub must complete the email template, Refer: Customer > Admin > Administration > 3. Money > Account Management Guide > Refunds > Release Refunds

Online services for Booked Hire Service Licences and Booking Entity Authorisations will need to be directed to TransLink Regional Passenger Transport Office at Carseldine
ContactsWhere a customer experiences difficulty with an online service (excluding booked hire services), or wishes to provide feedback about an online service, they can do so:
  • via the online feedback function on the TMR Contact Us page
  • by phone to the TMR call centre on 13 23 80 (within Australia) or +61 7 3405 0985 (outside Australia); Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm (excluding public holidays)
  • in person at a TMR CSC

Where a customer experiences difficulty with a booked hire online service, or wishes to provide feedback about the booked hire online services, they can do so:
  • by phone to the TMR call centre on 13 23 80 (within Australia) or +61 7 3405 0985 (outside Australia); Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm (excluding public holidays)
