Reinstatement of Light Vehicle Practical Driving Tests (7561)


Approved ActionReinstatement of Light Vehicle Practical Driving Tests
When15 June 2020
BackgroundFrom Monday, 13 July at 10am practical driving test bookings will be opened again for all customers.

Customers can book their test through the online service or by calling 13 23 80.
Who will be impacted and how?
Customers
Customers
    Customers who previously had a light vehicle test booking were contacted by phone, email or SMS to advise that they can rebook their test by phoning 13 23 80.

    Other customers who may have been intending to book their practical test soon were also emailed to advise about the reinstatement of practical driving tests and the prioritisation process.

    These customers were identified as those who:
    • have lodged their learner logbook
    • have passed their learner logbook
    • have had a learner logbook and special need tenure exemption granted
    • hold an overseas licence and have recently passed a written test.
Operators
Operators
    Several measures have been put in place to protect the health and safety of CSC staff and customers, including: 
    • mandatory screening for customers 
    • the provision of alcohol-based hand sanitiser  
    • pre-test vehicle cleaning requirements 
    • reinforcement of general personal hygiene measures. 

    There will be no additional safety measures implemented with the reinstatement of light vehicle tests.
Booking the testsCustomers can book or reschedule their tests by booking online or calling 13 23 80.

The TMR Online Service for swapping test bookings isn't available.

No bulk bookings can be made by driving schools during the initial implementation period.  
Presenting for the testTo ensure that there is time for the additional safety measures to be met customers must arrive at the CSC as least 20 minutes prior to their test time.
