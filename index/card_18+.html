Card 18+ (455)


Cards 18+
What it isThe Card 18+ is a proof of age card which allows anyone (aged 18 years & over) to prove that they have reached a stated age.

The Department of Transport and Main Roads issued Card 18+ on behalf of the Queensland Department of Tourism, Fair Trading and Wine Industry Development.

The Card 18+ has not been issued by TMR since 2010. The alternate product is the Photo Identification card.
Transactions
Replacement
Replacing a Card 18+
Replacing a Card 18+
    If the holder of a Card 18+ attends an office they must be:
    • Advised that the Card 18+ cannot be replaced,
    • Given the opportunity to apply for Photo ID card,
    • Advised to complete the Photo Identification Application/Replacement form,
    • Advised that full Photo ID card requirements will need to be met.

    Refer: QT Shop > 1. Customer Products > Photo Identification Card Guide
Display on card
Updating the display on the Card 18+
Updating the display on the Card 18+
    If the holder of a Card 18+ attends an office and advises that they want an address added or the gender indicator removed from being displayed on their existing Card 18+ they must apply for an Photo ID card. They must be advised that full Photo ID card requirements will need to be met.

    Refer: QT Shop > 1. Customer Products > Photo Identification Card Guide
SurrenderMay be voluntarily surrendered:
  • By the holder of Card 18+
  • On behalf of a deceased person.
  • If found and returned by other than holder of Card 18+
