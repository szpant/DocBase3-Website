SAP Invoices
SAP Invoices
    Applies to
    Applies to
      Receipting a customer payment for invoice issued by TMR via the SAP to Accounts Receivable Suspense Account (432406000)
    1
    Before You Start
    Before You Start
      Customer must present invoice. Invoices issued from TMR via SAP include but not limited to:
      • State Boat Harbour Mooring Fees
      • MSQ Buoy Mooring Fees
      • Reinstate invoice for direct debit rejection of vehicle dealers

      A sample invoice has been included for your reference below. Customer number, Invoice number and Company Code are located on the invoice.

      Invoice Sample
    2
    		Receipt Payment (TICA)
    3
    Add Text Message
    Add Text Message
      Details required to be recorded in the text message can be obtained from the tax invoice provided by the customer.

      The following information must form part of the text:
      • SAP Invoice number (located on the invoice is required so that the payment can be cleared against the invoice in SAP)
      • SAP Customer number (located on the invoice)
      • SAP Company Code (MRCO, MRBO or MRWG) (located on the invoice)
    4
    Finalise Transaction
    Finalise Transaction
      Take a photocopy of the invoice and place documents with the operators daily filing for that day to be sent to Iron Mountain, and give customer Invoice presented for payment and receipt.


      Note:The following process is then completed once TICA has interfaced with SAP:
      • The payment is allocated to SAP GL account code 10363.
      • Cash and Banking Unit prepares a ‘Post with clearing’ to allocate the payment against the Customer Invoice.
      • The GL Account Code 10363 is monitored and reconciled by Cash and Banking Unit on a monthly basis to ensure that any SAP Customer related payments receipted in TICA are allocated to the correct invoice in a timely manner.
Staff Reimbursements
Staff Reimbursements
    Applies to
    Applies to
      Receipting a payment from a staff member who wishes to reimburse TMR for mobile phone calls, overpayments of allowances or reimbursement of Corporate Card Transactions.
    1
    Before You Start
    Before You Start
      Ensure the staff member has a General Ledger Receipt Request form (F4001) with details of reimbursement complete.
    2
    		Receipt Payment (TICA)
    3
    Add Text Message
    Add Text Message
      Details required to be recorded in the text message can be obtained from the forms provided by the staff member.

      The following information must form part of the text:
      • Employee Name (First Name, Middle initial and Last Name)
      • General Ledger Account No* (generally the account number will be the same as the expense account the original payment was made from)
      • Cost centre (same cost centre as the one the original payment was made from e.g. travel cost centre, pool cost centre etc)
      • GST Tax code**
      • Short description of what the payment is for

      *If you are unsure of which General Ledger account number to use, contact your Regional Advisor - Financial and Business Management (RA(FBM)).
      **GST Tax code details should be on the General Ledger Receipt Request form. If not, please contact your RA(FBM) to confirm the correct code to use. The following are known GST Tax Codes:
      • Reimbursement of mobile phone expenses - P1
      • Reimbursement of travel expenses - P5
      • Reimbursement of Corporate Card transactions – P1

      Note:All the information required to be included in the text (as outlined above) is on the General Ledger Receipt Request form (F4001)
    4
    Finalise Transaction
    Finalise Transaction
      • Take a photocopy of the General Ledger Receipt form (F4001) and place documents with the operators daily filing for that day to be sent to Iron Mountain.
      • Give customer the General Ledger Receipt form (F4001) presented for payment with receipt.

      Note:The following process is then completed once TICA has interfaced with SAP:
      • The payment is allocated to SAP GL account code 10363.
      • Cash and Banking Unit will have the payment allocated to the correct GL account.
      • The GL Account Code 10363 is monitored by Cash and Banking Unit on a monthly basis to ensure that any staff reimbursements receipted in TICA are allocated to the correct GL account in a timely manner.
