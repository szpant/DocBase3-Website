Transfer Interstate Restriction (TIST) (4696)

TIST Restriction
A TIST restriction exists on TICA when a vehicle or trailer is recorded on NEVDIS more than once as a result of a vehicle being registered in Queensland and in another state.

How the Restriction is Recorded
How the Restriction is Recorded
    The restriction will be automatically recorded on TICA as a result of a weekly advice from NEVDIS.

    If the vehicle has National Heavy Vehicle Number Plates attached the TIST restriction does not cancel the plates which are placed into the status of "Interstate" to allow their re-use if the vehicle registration returns to Queensland from a participating jurisdiction.
Transactions that can be Performed with the Restriction
Transactions that can be Performed with the Restriction
    If a TIST restriction exists, the only transaction that can be performed by operators on the vehicle is a cancellation. Once the restriction is removed normal processing can occur.


    Note:If the vehicle has NHVNP attached that are in the status of "Interstate" it is essential that the number plates are not surrendered unless physically presented.
Invalidating a TIST restriction
Invalidating a TIST restriction
    A TIST restriction applied to a vehicle with National Heavy Number Plates attached should never be invalidated. If a processing error is identified operators should contact Vehicle Identification Unit (VIU) for further advice.

    Invalidating a TIST will also invalidate the “Interstate” plate status resulting in issues if the customer applies to reuse the plates in Queensland.
Registered Operator will Receive
Registered Operator will Receive
    Instead of a vehicle registration notice being produced at the time of renewal, the registered operator will receive a NEVDIS Vehicle Notification Notice.

    Sample notice attached:
    F4677

    F4678
Requirements of the Registered Operator
Requirements of the Registered Operator
    The registered operator is required to sight the VIN on both the vehicle and the compliance plate (non-RAV vehicles only) and record the details on a Vehicle Details Notification (F4676) form and email Vehicle Identification Unit (VIU)

    Sample form F4676 attached:
    F4676
Contact Details for Further Assistance
Contact Details for Further Assistance
    Further assistance is available by contacting Vehicle Identification Unit (VIU)
