Cancel a Receipt (474)

Applies to
Applies to
    Cancelling a receipt that has been incorrectly processed.
1
Identify receipt to be cancelled
Identify receipt to be cancelled
    • Determine which transaction was processed incorrectly.
    • Determine that the cancellation is for the same system date the transaction was processed. If not the same the day the transaction must be invalidated.
    • Before an operator can cancel any receipt, approval must be sought from a SASM, SPECPRO or Manager. (The approver will confirm whether the receipt can be cancelled or unmatched).
    • Once approval is granted, follow the cancel receipt procedure below.

    List Customer Receipt (TICA)
2
Cancel Receipt (TICA)
Cancel Receipt (TICA)
    The approving officer is to check the text is sufficient before the text is printed.

    Text must be recorded when cancelling the receipt and include as a minimum:
    • CRN
    • Product/transaction the receipt relates to eg Renew DL, purchase of modification book
    • Reason for cancellation, Operator error is not sufficient on its own, for eg Operator Error - incorrect CRN **** selected correct CRN is 1234567890 transaction will be reprocessed
    • Userid of the operator performing the transaction
    • Userid and position of approving officer.
3
Record on Receipt
Record on Receipt
    If the customer requested a physical copy of the receipt at time of payment and it is subsequently cancelled, it must be physically marked as cancelled by either:
    • Writing cancelled across the face of the receipt
      OR
    • Stamping with a cancelled stamp
      AND
    • Clearly note new receipt number on the cancelled receipt. When new receipt is not issued reason for not issuing is to be clearly noted.
    • Text entered will be printed once cancellation is successful. Once the transaction has been reprocessed the new receipt number must be written on the cancelled receipt.
    There is no requirement to print a cancelled receipt if the original receipt was not physically generated. TICA will still automatically print the cancelled receipt text but this can be printed on scrap paper and destroyed.
4
Cancelled Receipt and Supporting Text Document
Cancelled Receipt and Supporting Text Document
    • Once completed the approver is to write their userid and sign the operators banking sheet in the cancelled receipt section and then store the cancelled receipt documents (if provided) in the banking room.
    • The cancelled receipt process for that operator is finalised for end of day banking.
