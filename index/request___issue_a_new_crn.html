Request / Issue a New CRN (2530)

Applies to
Applies to
    Processing customer application for a new Customer Reference Number (CRN) if their existing CRN has been used fraudulently by another individual
1
Check Documents
Check Documents
    Action
    Specifics
    Police Agency Incident Report Check report is from the correct agency and contains the required information.
    Refer to: Customer > 3. Change Customer Detail > Change Customer Detail Guide > Request for new CRN > Documents Required
    Statutory DeclarationCheck declaration is correctly completed.

    Note:A Statutory Declaration can also be signed and witnessed by a lawyer and/or a conveyancer.
2
Email request to IMU
Email request to IMU
    Confirm with Manager/SASM then email a copy of all documentation to the Identity Management Unit (IMU) for consideration.
    Retain documentation until the IMU advises the outcome of the request.
3
Request Granted
Request Granted
    If the request is granted:
    • On advice from IMU contact customer to attend CSC;
    • When customer in attendance - confirm identity
    • Any products held by the customer will need replacing to show the
      new number.
    • All existing card products (with the old CRN), are to be returned to the Customer Service Centre before the issue of any replacement products.
      Refer to: the relevant How To.
