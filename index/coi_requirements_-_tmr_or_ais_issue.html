      Emergency VehiclesSES vehicles are exempt the COI at New Business, Renewal and Transfer. Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Inspections > Certificate of Inspection > COI Exemptions > Rules > Exemptions > TICA Error for work around process.

      A COI is not required for buses that:
      • Are not used as Public Passenger Service Vehicles and;
      • Have a <4.5t GVM and;
      • Have a purpose of use of Private (PRIV) or Training (TRAI)
      Or
      • Are built to carry between 10 and 12 seated adults (including the driver) and are registered with a purpose of use:
        • Commercial (COML)
        • Produce Carrying (Farming) (FARM)
        • Dealer (DEAL)

      Definitions
      Definitions
        A bus means a motor vehicle built mainly to carry 10 or more seated adults including the driver.

      Note:Axle override is a term used to signify a vehicle with a manufacturer’s Gross Vehicle Mass (GVM) over 16t has a registered GVM under 16t.
