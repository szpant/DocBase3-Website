    Surrender of Number Plates
    Surrender of Number Plates
        If plates were not surrendered, a declaration must be completed explaining why the plates are not available. Acceptable reasons include:
        • Stolen, lost or destroyed

        Note: Vehicles no longer under a number plate confiscation notice - the plates must be obtained from the QPS before the vehicle's registration may be cancelled.

        If the plates were surrendered interstate and the plates are not recorded on TICA/NEVDIS as surrendered, evidence of surrender must be provided (eg plate receipt).

        Note:Where a customer is unable to access the vehicle to retrieve the number plates after an accident:
        • The letter from the insurer must state this; and
        • The customer must complete the declaration on the cancellation form stating that they are unable to access the vehicle to retrieve the number plates.
