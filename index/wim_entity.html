WiM Entity
WiM Entity
    This procedure will outline how to create a WiM Entity in CID

    Limited staff PA(I)'s, STI, FIO's and Heavy Vehicle Analysis have the ability to create this entity type in CID.

    Should you not be in the above group you are able to send an email to compliance.intelligence@tmr.qld.gov.au with the WiM notice/document requesting a record be created.

    Note:ANPR and WiM documents should be sent together.
