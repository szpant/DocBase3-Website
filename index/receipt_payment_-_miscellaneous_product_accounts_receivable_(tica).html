Receipt Payment - Miscellaneous Product Accounts Receivable (TICA) (5054)

1.From TICA homepage select Miscellaneous Product hub
The Miscellaneous Product Hub will display
2.Hover on the Non Customer Products menu item in the navigation bar and click the Administration sub menu item
3.In the Select Product drop down menu, select Accounts Receivable
4.Enter the quantity as "1" and the fee will be either total SAP invoice amount to be paid, or total staff reimbursement amount to be receipted in GL account
5.Click the Select pushbutton
6.Click the Submit pushbutton
The Make Payment task page will display
7.Take payment as per current procedure
Refer: Admin > 3. Money > Administration Money Guide > Payment Method
8.The Payment Note - Web Page Dialog box will display
9.Record required information* in the text field and click the OK pushbutton
The Pay Successful message will display.

*For SAP Invoices - Refer: QT Shop > 2. Non-Customer Products > The How To: Non-Customer Products > Non-Customer Product Procedures > Receipt Miscellaneous Product - Accounts Receivable > 3. Add text message for requirements

*For Staff Reimbursements - Refer: QT Shop > 2. Non-Customer Products > The How To: Non-Customer Products > Non-Customer Product Procedures > Receipt Miscellaneous Product - Accounts Receivable > 3. Add text message for requirements
