Standard Plates Issue/Replace Requirements (3089)

Customer cannot request a specific standard plate, plates must be issued in strict ascending numerical order.

            Issue Requirements
      Replacement
Normal
  • Currently registered vehicle
  • Written request if Govt vehicle requesting normal plates
  • Standard Number Plate Replacement Application (F3540)
  • Original plates or declaration explaining why plates unavailable for surrender if lost, stolen or destroyed (declaration completed on form)
  • SIVE plates can be requested in either a Standard or US Size
  • Taxi/Limousine/Special Purpose Limousine/Booked Hire Licence Amendment Application F2976 (BHSL vehicles only)

Note:Plate fee exemptions may apply when replacing standard plates. For exempted plate fees and requirements refer: Plate Fee Exemptions
Government
  • Govt Qual

    Note:The first registered operator must have a GOVT Qual to be eligible for Government plates.
Farm
  • Farm concession
SIVE
  • SIVE concession
Dealer/Trade Plates
  • Dealer/Trailer Trade Plate Application (F1276)
Conditional
  • Conditionally registered vehicle/trailer
Taxi/Limo/Special Purpose Limo
  • Taxi Registration (including Substitute Taxi)
  • Limo Registration
  • Special Purpose Limousine Registration

Note:Local TransLink Regional Office must authorise the issue of T (Taxi/Substitute Taxi) plates or L (Limo/SPL) plates
  • Attach/Remove/Replace Taxi/Limousine Plate Application (F4621)
  • Taxi/Limousine/Special Purpose Limousine/Booked Hire Licence Amendment Application (F2976)

Internal Form completed by local Translink Office:
  • Authority to Issue/Transfer Taxi/Limousine/Special Purpose Limousine Number Plates (F1701)

Note:Local TransLink Regional Office must authorise the issue of T (Taxi/Substitute Taxi) plates or L (Limo/SPL) plates
National Heavy Vehicle Number Plate (NHVNP)
  • Heavy vehicles participating in the National Heavy Vehicle Registration Scheme except:
    • SIVE Concession
    • Farm Concession
    • Conditional Registration
    • Fire Rescue and Ambulance Vehicles (FRAV)
  • Standard Number Plate Replacement Application (F3540)
  • Original plates or declaration explaining why the plates are unavailable for surrender if the have been lost, stolen or destroyed
Motorised Wheelchair
  • Registered motorised wheelchair
  • Standard Number Plate Replacement Application (F3540)
  • Original plate or declaration explaining why the plate is unavailable for surrender if lost, stolen or destroyed (declaration completed on form)
  • Only replace with a motorised wheelchair plate
