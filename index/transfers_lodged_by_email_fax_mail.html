Transfers Lodged by email/fax/mail (3079)

Transfer Lodged:
Queensland Resident (not in remote areas)Transfers received by email/fax/mail should not be processed.
Queensland Residents in REMOTE AREAS and
Interstate or Overseas Resident		Applies to:
  • Queensland residents in remote areas and interstate or overseas residents.
  • Executors/administrators/next of kin's who are located in remote areas, interstate or overseas.
  • Dealers participating in the Rego Easy Scheme – applies only if they are signing on behalf of Qld remote registered operators. Full requirements outlined in Dealer signing transfer applications on behalf of registered operators who are Qld residents in remote areas must be met.

List of Remote Areas

Refer: Vehicle Rego > 8. Transfer > The How To: Transfer > Registration Transfer Procedures

Vehicle Transfers
