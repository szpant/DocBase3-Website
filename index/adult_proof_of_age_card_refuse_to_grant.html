Photo Identification Card Refuse to grant (3372)

Photo Identification Card Refuse to grant
What it isThe chief executive must refuse to grant a Photo Identification (Photo ID) Card if:
  • The identity of the applicant has not been confirmed, e.g. 1:N facial image verification has failed.
Notification to ApplicantA written notice must be given to the applicant advising:
  • The decision;
  • The reason for the decision;
  • Information regarding a review of the decision.
Grounds to Refuse to Grant
  • The digital photo fails the 1:N image verification
Photo ID Card Act 2008 s11(2)
Transactions
Refuse to Grant Photo Identification (Photo ID) Notice
Refuse to Grant Photo Identification (Photo ID) Notice
    This notice is to be given when the decision is to not grant an application for a Photo Identification Card.
Photo ID Card Act 2008 s11

    Refuse to Grant - Photo ID 1 to Many image - April 2019


    If the person is not satisfied with the decision to refuse to grant a Photo Identification Card, the person may ask to have this decision reviewed by:
    • The chief executive Photo ID Card Act 2008 s39; or
    • The Queensland Civil and Administrative Tribunal (QCAT) QCAT Act 2009 s157(2)
Review decision by CE
Review decision by CE
    If the person decides to ask the chief executive for a review of the original decision, they may be required to give a written notice setting out the reasons for the review.

    The person must lodge their written notice:
    • with the Manager of the Department of Transport and Main Roads licence issuing office where the refusal notice was issued.
    • within 28 days of the original decision.
Review decision by QCAT
Review decision by QCAT
    If the person decides to lodge a review of the reviewed decision with QCAT, they may need to complete a written application setting out the full details of the circumstances of the refusal, and the reasons why they are taking this action.

    The person must lodge their written notice within 28 days of the reviewed decision.

    The person may get further assistance with their QCAT review and application by contacting QCAT on 1300 753 228 or visiting www.qcat.qld.gov.au
Review decision - CE confirms decision
Review decision - CE confirms decision
    This notice is given when the decision is to reaffirm the original decision to refuse to grant an application for a Photo Identification Card.
Photo ID Card Act 2008 s39

    The person may apply to QCAT for a further review.
    Review Decision - Photo ID CE confirms decision - April 2019
Review decision - CE to set aside
Review decision - CE to set aside
    This notice is to be given when the decision is to set aside the original decision to refuse to grant an application for a Photo Identification Card, the Photo ID Card can be granted.
Photo ID Card Act 2008 s39
