Recording and Actioning Medical Certificates (2826)

IMPORTANT: Recording of Medical Condition Notifications

S conditions:
  • Medical certificates that add or remove an S condition must be recorded in the MCR Hub.
  • Self declarations to add or remove an S condition, are not recorded in the MCR hub.
  • Formless: Self declarations to add or remove an S condition made as part of formless transaction does not require an Driver Licence Amendment (F4358) form. If the customer's advice differs from the medical certificate, the CSO must note on the Driver Licence Transaction History that the customer has declared they do not require spectacles to drive. The medical certificate must be recorded in MCR Hub.
  • MCR Unit: The MCR Unit may add or removed an S condition following advice from the customer given over the phone. To do this the customer must be authenticated to Level 2, using the Call Centre authentication process, and a completed note for file put with the operators filing.

For industry Authority transactions:
  • Record medical certificate in the MCR Hub, ONLY pass as a pre-requisite if the driver licence must be amended based on the medical certificate.
