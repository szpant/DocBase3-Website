Managing Operator Smartcard Error
Smartcard status
Definition and Action
Smartcard Status
Smartcard Status
    The smartcard TICA history screen shows the production status of the smartcard. The table below details the transactions that can be processed at each stage of production.

    Smartcard Status Definition Available Operator Actions Transactions performed / prevented
    Pending A transaction requiring a Smartcard fee has been processed, but the payment has not been receipted, or the payment has been unmatched/cancelled.
    		Pending status does not restrict processes. Transactions can be amended or invalidated and reprocessed.All transactions can be performed while in "Pending" status.
    Registered The application has been approved and is ready for a 1:N match, or, if a 1:N match is not required, the request is ready to be sent to the production house.

    		The Registered status restricts processes. Transactions that are prevented while the smartcard is in the "Registered" status:
    • Cancellation of all products
    • Surrender (DL, ML, IA, APA)
    • Amend Industry Authority
    Production RequestedThe Smartcard is being made by the production house.

    		The Production Requested status restricts processes. NOTE A change of address can be processed but will not change where the smartcard is sent.

    Transactions that are prevented while the smartcard is in the "Production Requested" status:
    • Correction of smartcard product details
    • Add or remove conditions (all products)
    • Downgrade of a driver licence
    • Surrender of a DL, ML, IA, APA
    • Replacement of the smartcard
    • Change of name
    • Change of customer details
    • Amend Industry Authority
    • Merging of customer details where one customer has a smartcard in this state.
    • Unmatch a smartcard payment
    • Invalidation of the transaction that registered the smartcard
    Active The Smartcard has been produced and is being/has been mailed to the customer. Active status does not restrict processes. Transactions can be amended or invalidated and reprocessed.All transactions can be performed while the smartcard is in the "Active" status.
    Note: Before reprocessing a transaction refer to the Smartcard Return policy to determine if the smartcard must be returned. For further information Refer: Smartcard Guide > Smartcard Return.

    For a comprehensive list of Smartcard Production Status please refer to: Smartcard > 1. Smartcard > The Smartcard Guide > Getting a Smartcard > Smartcard Status > Production Status or click here
When customer must attend CSC
When customer must attend CSC
When customer must attend CSC
    Most of the transactions applicable to this process (below) can be performed without the customer having to attend the CSC in person.

    The customer may only be required to attend the CSC if:
    • they do not have a valid image set, and/or
    • a new digital signature must be captured.
Time limitations on correcting driver licence errors
Errors identified less than 14 months after they occur
Errors identified less than 14 months after they occur
    Errors identified less than 14 months after they occur can be corrected by a SPECPRO profile holder.

    Contact Help Direct if the customer:
    • has been issued a Notice to Appear on an infringement
    • has been involved in an accident as the driver of a vehicle
Errors identified more than 14 months after they occur
Errors identified more than 14 months after they occur
    If the error is identified more than 14 months after the transaction occurred contact Help Direct. Help Direct will contact Policy Unit if required. Staff are reminded not to contact the policy units directly.

    Advise Help Direct the customer:
    • has been issued a Notice to Appear on an infringement
    • has been involved in an accident as the driver of a vehicle
