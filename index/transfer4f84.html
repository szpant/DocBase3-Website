Renewal Fees Due at Transfer
    If a transfer application is lodged when the registration is expired the acquirer can either:
    • pay the transfer costs and outstanding renewal fees
      or
    • cancel and re-register the vehicle

    If the acquirer is enrolled in direct debit and wants to pay a one-month term the pay term can be updated in TICA once the transfer is processed. Operators should confirm that the customer is signed up for eNotices and eReminders and has their mobile number recorded.
