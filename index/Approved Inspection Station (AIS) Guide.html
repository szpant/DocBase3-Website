Approved Inspection Station (AIS) Guide (4622)


What it isAn Approved Inspection Station (AIS) is a fixed establishment such as a service station, garage or workshop where motor vehicles are inspected and issued either a Safety Certificate / Certificate of Inspection under the provisions of the Transport Operations (Road Use Management) Act 1995.

An AIS may be approved to inspect one or more combinations of vehicle types, provided examiners approved by TMR are employed to carry out the inspections.
