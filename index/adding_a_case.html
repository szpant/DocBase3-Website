Adding a Case (7017)

This procedure will outline how to add a Case in CID.
1
Open Data Entry module
Open Data Entry module
2
Select the "Case" data entry form
Select the "Case" data entry form
    In the Detail pane select the Case form from the drop down.

3
Complete the data entry form
Complete the data entry form
    The data entry form has a number of fields that are required to be completed.



    There are several tabs on the form which all may require fields to be completed.
    Form tabs
    Form tabs


      Entity Details
      Entity Details
        1. At least one matter must be selected.
        2. A description must be included.

      When a matter is not selected the record will appear on the weekly non-compliance report.
      Entity Prosecution Details
      Entity Prosecution Details
        Enter the date and reference number when the case has been sent to prosecutions.
      Entity - Prosecution Details
      Entity - Prosecution Details
        Use this tab to identify the Principal Adviser Investigations who is leading the case.
      Entity - Administration
      Entity - Administration
        This tab is auto-filled - no action required


    All mandatory fields are marked with an indicator.
    Image: Mandatory indicator
    Image: Mandatory indicator

    If a mandatory filed is on a tab, the tab also has an indicator.
    Image: Mandatory indicator on a tab
    Image: Mandatory indicator on a tab

4
Save the record
Save the record
    Click Save to commit your entry to the CID.



    A save successful message will briefly appear.


    Note:The data entry form will return to a blank form. This is by design and does not mean your record did not save.

    To confirm your record saved, check the Recent items pane. The item you just saved should be listed.


5
Cancelling record entry
Cancelling record entry
    You can cancel your entry by clicking the Cancel button or by clicking the Reset button.
