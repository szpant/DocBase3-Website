Q-Flow Classification Codes (7359)

What it is A 'Classification' is the entry recorded to identify the action or group of actions that are performed whilst the customer is being served at the counter.
Why Enter Classifications?Entering the Classification code in line with the business rules helps to improve the consistency and validity of data used for service management. It provides the business with important information on how long a particular service takes to deliver.
Key PrinciplesA list of Classification Codes has been developed to standardise the capture of the elements of a customer's visit.

Note: Each Classification Code should only be entered once.
