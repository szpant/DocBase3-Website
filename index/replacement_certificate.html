Replacement Certificate (3114)

What is it:It is the replacement of a registration:
  • Certificate
  • Registration Notice
that is either:
  • Destroyed
  • Not received
  • Sent to incorrect address
  • Incorrectly printed
  • Stolen
  • Lost

Automatically Generated Replacement Certificate
Automatically Generated Replacement Certificate
    A replacement certificate is generated automatically for organisations without an ACN recorded in TICA if these details are changed:
    • Plate Number
    • Insurance Class
    • Pay Mode
    • Number of trailers to be towed
    • Registration Category
    Replacement registration certificates are not automatically posted for individuals or organisations with an ACN recorded in TICA. These customers can obtain their registration certificate from the Get Registration Certificate online service, or by requesting that a certificate be emailed to them or manually printed by an operator.

    Individuals who nominate to receive eNotices will be emailed a new certificate in the above listed circumstances.
Requirements
Evidence of Identity
Evidence of Identity
    If a customer requests a replacement:
    • Online (certificates only), they must verify their details and provide the registration number
    • In person, EOI is required.

    Customers should be encouraged to use the online service for obtaining their registration certificates. If the customer is unable to use the online service, an operator can email the customer a copy of their certificate. A certificate should only be printed by an operator if specifically requested by the customer.
Rules
Who Can Apply
Who Can Apply
    Registered Operator/s or their representatives can order replacements.

    If a representative is requesting the replacement be emailed to the registered operators email address, no EOI is required.

Online Requests
Online Requests
    Customers can obtain registration certificates using the Get Registration Certificate online service.

    As this online service is accessed using Customer Login Customer Login Services, certificates will not contain the customer's address. If a customer is unable to use the online service, an operator can request a replacement via TICA.

    Certificates can be saved as a PDF, printed or emailed immediately.
Operator Requests
Operator Requests
    A replacement processed by an operator will be sent via email or printed immediately. It is preferred that registration certificates are emailed to the customer, they should only be printed if specifically request.
