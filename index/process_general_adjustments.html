General Banking Adjustment Types and Processes (5046)

Misc-Credit
Misc-Credit
    This action creates a credit note for a miscellaneous non-customer product (MPRO), or credits a customer's account, which may be refunded if required.

    Miscellaneous Credit (TRAILS)
Revenue Account Adjustments
Revenue Account Adjustments
    Revenue Account adjustments transfer monies either from a revenue account to a customer account or from a customer account to a revenue account.

    Revenue Account to Customer Account (TICA)
    Customer Account to Revenue Account (TICA)
Liabilities Account Adjustments
Liabilities Account Adjustments
    Liability Adjustments are generally performed when a Tax / GST component needs to be adjusted to/from a customer account.

    These adjustments transfer monies either from a liabilities account to a customer account or from a customer account to a liabilities account.

    Liabilities Account to Customer Account (TICA)
    Customer Account to Liabilities Account (TICA)
Over Account Adjustments
Over Account Adjustments
    Operator Over Account to Identified Customer Account (TICA)
    Operator Over Account to Sundries Miscellaneous Account (TRAILS)
    Close Operator Cashbook before performing an OPOVER (TRAILS)
    Receipting Office Bank Deposit Over to Customer Account (TICA)
    Receipting Office Bank Deposit Over to Sundries Misc. (TRAILS)
    Transfer Receipting Office Bank Deposit to Receipting Office Overs (TRAILS)
    Adjust Receipting Office Overs Account to Operator Overs Account (TRAILS)
Under Account Adjustments
Under Account Adjustments
    Operator Under Account to Identified Customer Account (TICA)
    Operator Under Account to Receipting Office Operator losses Account (TRAILS)
    Close Operator Cashbook before performing OPUNDER (TRAILS)
    Receipting Office Bank Deposit Under to Customer Account (TICA)
    Operator Under Account to Operator Customer Account (TRAILS)
    Transfer Receipting Office Bank Deposit to Receipting Office Under (TRAILS)
    Adjust Receipting Office Unders to Operator Unders Account (TRAILS)
Operator Losses Account
Operator Losses Account
    This adjustment is used to transfer monies from the Receipting Office Operator Loss account to the Operator Loss General Ledger account to recognise the loss write-off as an expense to the department.

    Receipting Office Operator Losses to Operator Losses Account (TRAILS)
Amending Operator Over / Under Account
Amending Operator Over / Under Account
    This adjustment is used when an under/over occurs for the same operator following the Amend action on the Maintain Payment Item screen.

    Amend Operator Over/Under Account (TRAILS)
Reversing an Account Adjustment
Reversing an Account Adjustment
    An adjustment may be reversed if it was adjusted incorrectly.

    Create / Reverse / Copy an Adjustment
Offset Different Operators Under / Over
Offset Different Operators Under / Over
    This adjustment is used when an operator over is offset against another operator's under; for example, operators exchanging change during daily operations. The adjustment is only created with the manager's approval. The accounts affected must be for different operators.

    Offset Different Operators Under / Over (TRAILS)
Operator Floats
Operator Floats
    Where possible, all floats should be issued to a counter as office floats. A float may only be issued to an individual operator where there is a genuine business need.

    This adjustment will assign a float to an individual operator
    Assign Float to an Individual Operator

    This adjustment will return an individual operator float to a receipting office float
    Return of Individual Operator Float to Receipting Office Float
