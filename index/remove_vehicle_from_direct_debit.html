Remove Vehicle from Direct Debit (7504)

1. From the TICA Home page click on the Vehicle/Trailer registration hub
The Vehicle/Trailer Registration Hub will display
2. Select Manage Direct Debit from the Registration drop-down menu.
The Manage direct debit screen in the Customer Online Account Management portal will display.
3. Select Remove registrations from the left-hand menu
The Remove registrations screen will display
4. Select the vehicles that are to be removed from DDRR
5. Select Continue to remove the vehicle/s.


Note: Removing all registrations from DDRR will mean the customer is no longer eligible and they will be unenrolled from DDRR. If removing the vehicle to reprocess an error ensure that the vehicle is re-added if required.
