Tow Truck Immediate Suspend (7384)


What it isThe Chief Executive may immediately suspend a tow truck licence, tow truck driver certificate and tow truck assistant certificate for public safety reasons by giving the licence or certificate holder written notice - Tow Truck Act 1973, section 21B.

The basis for taking immediate suspend action may be due to the seriousness and nature of complaints received such as:
  • If a person complains to a police officer about the conduct of the licence or certificate holder and, having regard to the nature of the complaint, the chief executive believes, on reasonable grounds, the conduct complained of justifies taking immediate suspend action ; or
  • If after having regard to statements or other information about the conduct of the licence or certificate holder given to the chief executive, the chief executive believes, on reasonable grounds, the statements or other information justifies taking immediate suspend action.

The basis for taking immediate suspend action may also be due to the grounds under 21A and/or they are no longer an appropriate person under s4C due to significant adverse criminal and/or traffic history or any of the reasons mentioned under s4C and 21A.

Immediate suspend action may be combined with show cause to cancel a licence or certificate.

For assistance in deciding whether to initiate immediate suspend action, the ground/s that may apply or help with drafting letters - contact tow.trucks@tmr.qld.gov.au
GroundsIf the chief executive considers a ground exists to immediately suspend a tow truck licence, tow truck driver certificate or tow truck assistant certificate, the Chief Executive must give the licence or certificate holder written notice, stating that the licence or certificate will be immediately suspended from a specified date or until a specified action occurs such as criminal charges are decided by the court. Immediate suspend may be combined with show cause to cancel.

Ground 1
Ground 1
    Public safety has been endangered, or is likely to be endangered, because of the authority holder’s conduct and the authority should be immediately suspended 21B(1)(a)
Ground 2
Ground 2
    Having regard to the conduct of the authority holder, when performing activities under the authority or at any other time, the chief executive believes, on reasonable grounds:
    (i) the person is no longer an appropriate person to hold an authority and the authority should be immediately suspended; or
    (ii) it is in the public interest to immediately suspend the authority
    21B(1)(b)
Immediate Suspension
Suspension Period
Suspension Period
    The suspension period may be:
    • Up to 56 days after the notice is given, or
    • Until a specified action occurs (such as charges decided by a court) provided the action is within 56 days
    The immediate suspension continues from the specified date in the Notice until the earlier of:
    • a decision is made about show cause to cancel (if immediate suspend is being combined with show cause to cancel) or
    • the end of any extension to the suspension period
Extending the suspension period
Extending the suspension period
    The chief executive may extend the suspension period by up to 56 days.
    An immediate suspension may be extended more than once by periods of 56 days. Refer s21B(5)
    If the immediate suspension period is extended the customer must be issued a Suspension Extension Notice. Refer below: Notices > Suspension Extension Notice - September 2018
Outcome of internal review
Internal review
Internal review
    If requested by the licence or accreditation holder, an internal review must be conducted of the decision to suspend the tow truck licence, tow truck driver certificate or tow truck assistant certificate.
Internal review overturns the decision
Internal review overturns the decision

    If, after considering all written representations made within the stated time frame, the Chief Executive considers the internal review representation acceptable, the licence or certificate holder may continue unaffected.
    Refer below: Notices >Internal Review Decision Notice - decision overturned - September 2018
Internal review upholds the decision
Internal review upholds the decision
    If, after considering all written representations made within the stated time frame, the Chief Executive considers grounds still exist to uphold the suspension of the tow truck licence, tow truck driver certificate or tow truck assistant certificate, the Chief Executive must inform the licence or certificate holder of the decision by written notice.

    Refer below: Notices >Internal Review Decision Notice - decision upheld - September 2018

    The written notice contains information advising that the licence or certificate holder may apply through the QCAT process to have the decision reviewed and that they can also apply to QCAT for a Stay of operation.

    If the customer lodges an application for review of decision with QCAT, a TMR representative will attend and both parties will be informed of the outcome.
Giving of noticesNotices may be sent by mail and/or email and may be given in person.

Returned Notices
Returned Notices
    Sent by Email
    Sent by Email
      If a notice sent by email returns a non-delivery error it must be mailed through Australia Post (if it wasn’t already mailed)
    Sent by Mail
    Sent by Mail
      If a notice sent via Australia Post is returned unclaimed, this does not affect the process. The matter must proceed and a decision made based on the evidence mentioned in the notice.

      A text message should be placed on TICA to alert any CSCs transacting business with the licence or certificate holder to obtain a new address then contact the relevant office progressing the show cause or immediate suspend action.
Licence/certificate not returnedWhen a licence or certificate is suspended or cancelled the holder must return the licence or certificate to a CSC in person or by mail to the address listed on the notice.

The licence or certificate must be returned within the time stated in the notice. If the licence or certificate is not returned within the time specified in the notice the non-surrender of the licence or certificate should be referred to the local Compliance Area.
