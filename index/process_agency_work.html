Process Agency Work (889)

Applies to
Applies to
    Processing of work received from a TMR Agent where:

    Offline Agents: Transactions Receipted by Agent
    • No work is processed at agency
    • Money and documents sent to a CSC for processing

    Semi-Offline Agencies: Access 1300/1800 Dial in Number
    • Transactions are telephoned to a CSC and processed by CSC up to the payment
    • Debt for transaction sits in the agents account
    • Account reconciled when schedule and payment received

1

Receive Agency Work
    Document received should include:
    • Agency Disbursement Schedule (Cover Sheet)
    • Supporting Documents for transactions

    Note: Funds are disbursed to TMR by SSQ overnight.

    Some TMR Agents may provide a cheque, which must be receipted to the Agent's TRAILS account:

Processing Payment
Processing Payment
    • Check the TRAILS required amount on List Payment Request Screen with the amount recorded on the Schedule
    If amounts match:
    If amounts match:
      • Complete transaction:

    If amounts do not match:

      If amount does not match due to:

      Underpayment Action
      Underpayment Action

        Write to customer explaining that a short payment has occurred and request payment within 28 days of the date of the letter. A customer account statement will also be sent requesting payment.

        For letter templates for under payment of registration renewal, please refer to Admin > 3. Money > Account Management Guide > Under-Payments - Registration > Underpayments received by mail/off line Agency > Short Payment letters

        Note:A TRAILS overdue notice is generated and sent to the customer every 35 days in the event payment is not received. Once 2 overdue notices have been sent to the customer, Financial Operations will request the Centre to forward the written correspondence sent to the customer to begin the debt recovery process.

        Payment methods
        Payment methods
          The customer account statement specifies the acceptable payment methods for customers.

          Where the customer returns to the Agency the original transaction was completed at to settle the account, the Agency will receipt the money owed and the amount will be displayed on the Agency Disbursement Schedule with a description so the customer/transaction can be identified.

          The amount should then be adjusted to the customer's account.

          Create an Adjustment (TICA/TRAILS)


      Overpayment
      Overpayment
        For amounts greater than $5 authorise refund
        Refer: Admin > 3. Money > The How To: Money > Money and Banking Procedures > Refund by Cheque

        For amounts less than $5:
        • Do not refund unless requested by customer
          Refer:           Admin > 3. Money > The How To: Money > Money and Banking Procedures > Refund by Cheque

      Record text message fully outlining action taken
