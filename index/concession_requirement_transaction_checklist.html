Charitable
Charitable
    QualCHAR
    CodeCHAR
    FormF4508
    Proof RequiredMust be listed in DocBase as an approved organisation for a Charitable Concession. If the QUAL has been applied but the name is not listed, do not process, contact Help Direct.

    All assessments are made by the Registration Policy Unit. When an application is approved/reassessed the Registration Policy unit will apply the QUAL to the organisations' CRN.

    Note:Organisations on the approved list stated as having 'Mulitple CRNs' must be linked as parent, child organisations in TICA. If parent entity has CHAR Qual then all linked child organisations are automatically eligible for the concession. A CSO may ONLY add a QUAL to a child organisation that is linked to its Parent organisation. The Parent organisation MUST have a current CHAR QUAL.

    Any subsidiary/sub-branch organisation are separate entities and must be assessed by the Registration Policy Unit under a separate application. Subsidiary/sub-branch organisations have different IA numbers or ACN and must not be linked in a parent/child relationship in TICA.
    Additional DocumentsFor information on application requirements, Refer: Vehicle Rego > 03. Types of Registration > Concessional Registration > Other Concession Types > Charitable

Legend
* Replacement Cards
    A new or replacement concession card can take up to 10 days to be issued. A Confirmation of Concession Card Entitlement form is issued for this time. This form can be used as proof of entitlement provided if it:
    • is the original form
    • states that it is issued to cover a person waiting for a new/replacement pension concession card
    • is valid (the form is usually issued for 2 weeks)
