Request to Reissue Notice to Choose (4932)

Request to Reissue Notice to Choose
When a Notice to Choose may be reissued
Eligibility
Eligibility
    A notice to choose may only be reissued if:
    • The customer held a valid Queensland driver licence for at least a portion of the ‘choice period’ and the relevant suspension period has not ended.
    • The ‘choice period’ is from the notice issue date to the choice date.
    • The customer may not nominate a good driving behaviour period to commence on a date outside of the ‘choice period’ or if their driver licence was invalid for the entire choice period.
    And
    • Since the date the customer’s licence became suspended they have not been:
      • Involved in a motor vehicle crash as the driver of a vehicle (TMR will not intervene as there may be insurance implications); or
      • Intercepted by the Queensland Police Service and issued a notice to appear in court for unlicensed driving (TMR will not intervene as this may affect court proceedings or QPS investigations); or
      • Issued any infringement notices (TMR will not intervene as this may affect court proceedings or QPS investigations).

    If the customer does not meet these requirements, TMR is unable to intervene in the demerit point process and the suspension stands.
Rules
Rules
Rules
    • A customer is unable to nominate a good driving behaviour period if at the time of nomination their licence is suspended, cancelled or expired or they are disqualified from holding or obtaining a Queensland driver licence.
    • A notice to choose must not be reissued after the relevant suspension period has ended.
Reissuing a Notice to Choose
In Person
In Person
    To reissue a Notice to Choose the customer must complete a Customer Request to Reissue Accumulation of Demerit Points - Notice to Choose (F4964).

    Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > The How To: Enforcement > Enforcement Procedures > Reissue Notice to Choose > In Person
By Mail/Fax/Email
By Mail/Fax/Email
    Where a licence holder is genuinely unable to attend a CSC or a driver licence issuing agent, they may be provided the option to mail, fax or email their request to reissue the Accumulation of Demerit Points - Notice to Choose(F4964)

    Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > The How To: Enforcement > Enforcement Procedures > Reissue Notice to Choose> Unable to Attend CSC

Rules
Rules
Rules
    • The new Notice to Choose will be generated overnight and sent to the customer’s postal address or residential address if there is no postal address.
    • If the customer wishes to elect a good driving behaviour period immediately a Notification of Driver Licence Enforcement Option (F4012) may be printed for the customer to complete – the earliest day the customer may nominate is the following day, they cannot elect to start the good driving behaviour period on the same date that the notice to choose is reissued.
    • Under no circumstances can a good driving behaviour period be backdated. The Notice to Choose must be withdrawn, a new notice issued and a future start date nominated.
