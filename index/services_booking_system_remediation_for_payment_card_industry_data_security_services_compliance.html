Services Booking System Remediation for Payment Card Industry Data Security Services Compliance (7224)

Project NameServices Booking System Remediation for Payment Card Industry Data Security Services Compliance
When1 December 2017
BackgroundThe Department of Transport and Main Roads (TMR) is changing the way customers pay for vehicle inspections or driver assessments booked via phone, email or fax. This change is required to enable TMR to comply with a new level of financial standards. This new level of financial standards will ensure that our customers’ credit card details remain securely managed.

From 1 December 2017 customers who book an inspection or a driver assessment by phone/email/fax will be given an order number, they will use the order number to pay the inspection fee through a secure online service.

We will no longer accept credit card details over the phone, via email or fax. Customers retain the option to pay at a customer service centre.

For same day bookings customers must complete payment within 30 minutes or their booking will be cancelled. For bookings that are not same day, customers will have until midnight on the day they make the booking to complete the transaction.
Who will be impacted and how?
TMR Staff
TMR Staff
    Staff taking bookings for vehicle inspections and driver assessments will no longer be able to accept credit card details over the phone, by email or fax.
Customers
Customers
    Customers booking a vehicle inspection or a driver assessment will no longer be able to pay by phone, email or fax.

    From 1 December customers must either obtain an order number and pay online or pay at a CSC (an order number cannot be used to pay at a CSC). They can also prepay at a CSC.
Further informationThe way a customer books a vehicle inspection or a driver assessment is not changing. This change only applies to the payment method when an inspection is booked on the phone, by email or fax.

Information will be provided to the impacted customers from 18th September to advise them of the upcoming changes. This will include emails to customers making bookings, information in the Driver Trainer newsletter, and factsheets available at Truckie Toolbox talks.
