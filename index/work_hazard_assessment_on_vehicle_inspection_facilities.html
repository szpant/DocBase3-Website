Work Hazard Assessment on Vehicle Inspection Facilities (5280)

Vehicle Inspection Facility Work Hazard Assessment and Pre Start Check List
What it isAn assessment is conducted using the Vehicle Inspection Facility Work Hazard Assessment and Pre Start checklist at all TMR, leased and privately owned inspection facilities, to ensure the facility is safe for TMR staff to carry out inspections.

A hazard is anything that has the potential to harm people, property or the environment.
AssessmentAuthorised Officers (Safety Examiners, Transport Inspectors and Senior Transport Inspectors) must not conduct vehicle inspections at a vehicle inspection facility if a hazard is considered an unacceptable risk.
Vehicle Inspection Facility Work Hazard Assessment
Vehicle Inspection Facility Work Hazard Assessment
    • A Senior Transport Inspector (STI) or a TMR employee nominated by the STI must conduct a hazard assessment on each vehicle inspection facility being managed by the STI
    • TMR owned facilities must be conducted annually, or if there is a change to the work environment or the equipment being used
    • TMR leased and privately owned facilities must be conducted:
      • prior to initial use; and
      • if there are changes to the ownership of the premises, site layout or primary purpose of use; and
      • annually to coincide with renewal of the site lease agreement, or in the case of primary produce sites, every two years
    • The assessment will be conducted in consultation with the Authorised Officers and Regional Safety Advisor to:
      • identify hazards
      • assess risks
      • control risks
      • review control measures
Pre Start Vehicle Inspection Facility Hazard Assessment
Pre Start Vehicle Inspection Facility Hazard Assessment
    • TMR Owned Facilities
      • Authorised officers must complete a 'Pre-Start Vehicle Inspection Facility Hazard Assessment' monthly and submit it to the STI
    • TMR Leased and Privately Owned Facilities
      • Authorised Officers must:
        • obtain a copy of the 'Vehicle Inspection Facility Site Safety Plan' prior to performing vehicle inspections
        • complete a 'Pre-Start Vehicle Inspection Facility Hazard Assessment':
          • monthly at a leased site with a monthly or longer lease
          • the first day of inspections at a site with consecutive days booked
          • each day at a site with single day bookings
Identified Hazards
Identified Hazards
    If a hazard is identified the Authorised Officer completing the assessment must advise the STI prior to commencing any inspections at the site.

    The STI, in consultation with the Regional Safety Advisor, will determine the approach to resolving the identified issues. The resolution may include:
    • liaison with site owners to eliminate or reduce the identified hazards or
    • arranging a new site

    If use of a site must be terminated because hazards cannot be resolved the Manager (Compliance), with the approval of the Regional Director, will manage the termination.
Recording Assessments
Hazard Assessments
Hazard Assessments
    All hazard assessments must be filed in TMR's Document Management System (DMS) for each vehicle inspection facility.

    Completed Pre-Start Vehicle Inspection Facility Hazard Assessment forms must be returned to the STI within 7 days and added to the site DMS file.
Register
Register
    Each region must maintain a register of all vehicle inspection facility locations within their region. The register should record:
    • Location
    • Off-Road Y/N
    • Site owner TMR or Private
    • Private sites - owner and contact details
    • Equipment on site - Pit/Roller Brake Tester (RBT)/ Shaker Plates/Vehicle Inspection Trailer(VIT) required
    • Date of last assessment
MonitoringSenior Transport Inspectors will monitor Authorised Officer's adherence to TMR's Workplace Health and Safety obligations and are responsible for ensuring Authorised Officers complete a 'Pre-Start Vehicle Inspection Facility Hazard Assessment' prior using vehicle inspection facilities.
