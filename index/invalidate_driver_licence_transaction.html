Invalidate Driver Licence Transaction (1580)

Applies to
Applies to
    Invalidating a transaction including:
    • Licence issued to incorrect customer
    • Licence issued for incorrect period (if update term/yrs doesn't work)
    • Incorrect action processed (eg duplicate instead of renewal)
    • Licence issued for incorrect class
    • Licence surrendered in error
    • Licence status is Tran and should be Current
    • Licence status is pending due to internet renewal not completed
    • An immediate medical suspension and the Notice of Immediate Suspend and proposed cancellation has been returned

    Rules for Invalidating
    Rules for Invalidating
      Most actions taken from the Driver Licence Hub can be invalidated.
      • The selected transaction to be invalidated must be the most recent transaction for the Licence
      • If there are actions of PAY and UNPA together, the next transaction can be invalidated
      • If the selected transaction has a paid payment request associated then the Invalidate action is not allowed until the payment has been cancelled or unmatched
      • A fee free transaction (eg interstate transfer) can be Invalidated as there is no payment request on the Driver Licence History Summary
      • If a transaction is to be reprocessed and it was originally completed over 14 months ago the Business Systems team must be contacted to open the 14 month window
