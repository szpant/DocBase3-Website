Fleet Inspections (5221)


Statement
  • Compliance Officers will manage and conduct fleet inspections during or outside business hours at a vehicle inspection facility on a fleet of 15 or more TMR inspectable vehicles owned by the same operator.
  • TSD will only conduct fleet inspections outside business hours when vehicles are genuinely unavailable during normal business hours.
  • All costs associated with fleet inspections conducted outside business hours will be invoiced to the fleet operator using the Fleet Inspection Cost Recovery Model.
  • A Senior Transport Inspector must approve an exception to the number of vehicles in a fleet or the inclusion of AIS inspectable vehicles.
  • Defect Notices must be issued to the fleet operator at the time of the vehicle inspection ensuring all vehicles identified as having minor, major or dangerous defect are issued a Defect Notice on site. Authorised Officers must not allow operators the option to repair and reinspect immediately and not be issued with a Defect Notice.
  • Fleet operators are to be encouraged to take up the National Heavy Vehicle Accreditation Scheme - Maintenance Management Module.
Note:A half day, or part thereof, booking fee of $26.80 or full day booking fee $53.60 is to be applied irrespective of the number of vehicles inspected. A booking fee is not to be charged per vehicle when booking fleet inspections outside business hours.


Note:An exception to the number of vehicles in a fleet or the inclusion of Approved Inspection Station (AIS) inspectable vehicles must be approved by a Senior Transport Inspector.
Procedure
Senior Transport Inspectors
Senior Transport Inspectors
    Senior Transport Inspectors are responsible for:
    • Provide a Fleet Inspection Application to operators requesting a fleet inspection booking.
      Appendix 3 - Fleet Inspection Application
    • Assess and approve the information supplied on the Fleet Inspection Application including requested exceptions to the number of vehicles in a fleet or the inclusion of Approved Inspection Station (AIS) inspectable vehicles. Contact the fleet operator if further information is required to process the application.
    • Ensure the fleet inspection is booked into the Services Booking System (SBS) or the regional SBS administrator using TRAILS SBS vehicle inspection location instructions and SBS training materials available on DocBase.
      Appendix 4 - Services Booking System Hand Over Guide
    • Charge the operator a half day or part thereof booking fee of ($26.80) or full day booking fee
      ($53.60) irrespective of the number of vehicles inspected in SBS. Apply the booking fee to the first registration number that appears on the       Fleet Inspection Booking Form for a half day or the first two registration numbers for a full day.
      Note:a booking fee is not to be charged per vehicle when booking fleet inspections.
      Attachment 1 - Fleet Inspection Booking Form December 2017
    • Ensure all privately owned premises have a current Agreement for Licence to Use Private Facilities by checking the relevant vehicle inspection facility DMS file. If no Agreement exists, make arrangements to complete the Agreement with the operator.
      Attachment 3 - Agreement for Licence to Use Private Facilities
    • Ensure all leased work sites have a current Agreement for Licence to Use Facilities by checking the relevant vehicle inspection facility DMS file. If no Agreement exists, make arrangements to complete the Agreement with the operator.
    • Roster Authorised Officers to conduct fleet inspections in accordance with shift rostering principles.
    • Review submitted Vehicle Inspection Checklists and discuss any concerns with the Authorised Officer.
    • Review and forward the completed Costing for a Fleet Inspection Form and Fleet Inspection Report to the relevant regional finance area.
    • Invoice all costs associated with fleet inspections conducted outside business hours to the fleet operator using the cost recovery model.
      Appendix 8 - Fleet Inspection Report
      FLEET INSPECTION Cost Recovery Invoice Template 2020-2021
    • Attach all associated fleet documents to the vehicle inspection facility DMS file.
Authorised Officers
Authorised Officers
    Authorised Officers are responsible for:
    • Print from the SBS database the associated Daily Booking List and Vehicle Check List before conducting a fleet inspection.
    • Perform the vehicle inspections on the nominated fleet vehicles.
    • Complete a Vehicle Check List for each vehicle inspected for the purpose of a programmed vehicle safety audit.
      Appendix 6 - Vehicle Checklist
    • Issue any Defect Notices to the fleet operator at the time of the vehicle inspection ensuring:
      • All vehicles identified as having minor, major or dangerous defects are issued a Defect Notice on site (An option to repair and reinspect immediately and not be issued with a Defect Notice must not be provided).
      • Fleet operators are aware that Defect Notices cleared on site will attract the relevant reinspection fee which must be paid before issuing a Certificate of Inspection (COI).
      • Defect Notices cleared by a 3rd party or AIS follow the AIS exception policy.
    • Ensure results of vehicle inspections are entered into TRAILS.
    • Submit to a Senior Transport Inspector the following completed forms:
      • Costing for a Fleet Inspection Form ensuring the hourly overtime and kilometres travelled rates are applied as per the Fleet Inspection Cost Recovery Model.

      FLEET INSPECTION Cost Recovery Invoice Template Jul 21 - Dec 21
      FLEET INSPECTIONS Cost Recovery Invoice Templates Jan 22 - Jun 22

      • Fleet Inspection Report for all vehicles listed on the Daily Booking List.
      • Individual Vehicle Inspection Checklist.
      • Copies of completed Defect Notices and Certificate of Inspections.
    • Record outside business hours fleet inspection tasks in the Services Time Sheeting and Reporting System (STARS) under additional activities for overtime payment. The time captured is the time from the employee’s office premises to the operator’s site and return.

      This procedure is summarised in the Fleet Inspection Procedure Flow Chart.

      Appendix 11 - Fleet Inspection Procedure Flow Chart
Regional Finance area
Regional Finance area
    Regional Finance area is responsible for:
    • Preparing a Receivable Invoice/Credit memo using the information from the Costing for a Fleet Inspection Form and Fleet Inspection Report and forwarding this to the Shared Service Agency.
