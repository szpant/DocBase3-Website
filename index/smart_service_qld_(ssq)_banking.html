Smart Service Qld (SSQ) Banking (7558)

What it isSmart Service Qld (SSQ) provides a primary point of contact for customers to access government services through multiple delivery channels.

The SSQ site in Charlotte St, Brisbane City is now authorised to perform TMR business transactions processed in TICA.

At close of business each day, SSQ Operators will reconcile TMR takings before following the SSQ end of day banking procedure.

Note: The procedure for SSQ end of day banking is not recorded in DocBase.
Banking Procedures
TMR End of Day Banking Procedure for SSQ Staff
TMR End of Day Banking Procedure for SSQ Staff

    At the end of business day the following procedure must be followed:
    • Operator closes counter
    • Print cheque list (from TICA) if required
    • Follow TMR procedure for any cancelled receipts and/or signed EFTPOS receipts
    • Undertake TMR end of day banking with TMR Banking Officer
    • Request the TMR cash total from the TMR Banking Officer
    • Submit cash, cheques if applicable, and banking sheet (below) to the TMR Banking Officer
    • EFTPOS totals are not recorded on the banking sheet
    • Operator to follow their end of day process for SSQ business

    Note:The Banking Officer and Checking Officer roles will be undertaken by TMR staff. End of day banking documentation will be filed with TMR's daily filing.
    Note:At this time all SSQ operators in the TICA collection screen should reflect a NIL balance.

Problems
Discrepancies
Discrepancies

    As per the agreement that SSQ are responsible for all banking discrepancies, TMR cash will not be banked under.

    TMR will advise the Operator of the total TMR cash transactions for remittance. Operators must give the total TMR cash collections to the Banking Officer before performing the SSQ business end of day banking.

    SSQ should seek assistance from the TMR Banking Officer to identify payment recording errors.


    Note:If there are discrepancies over the value of $100, TMR will provide assistance with finding the payment/customer. The course of action from here will be negotiated between SSQ and TMR Managers.
