Special Purpose Vehicles
    Special Purpose Vehicles can be either:
    • Mobile Machinery over 4.5 tonnes that is not eligible for conditional registration:
    • Straddle carrier
    • crane
Special Purpose Vehicle Type P
Special Purpose Vehicle Type P
    Is a special purpose vehicle, other than a vehicle that may be conditionally registered, built or permanently modified for—
    (a) off-road use; or
    (b) use on a road related area; or
    (c) use on an area of road that is under construction.

    There is no applicable registration fee.
Special Purpose Vehicle (Type T)
Special Purpose Vehicle (Type T)
    A special purpose vehicle (type t) is a special purpose vehicle, other than a special purpose vehicle (type p), that—
    (a)is built, or permanently modified, primarily for use on roads; and
    (b)has no axle or axle group loaded above the mass limits stated in the Transport Operations (Road Use Management) - Mass Dimensions and Loading Regulation 2005 Schedule 2.

    The Special Purpose Vehicle concession rate is 25% of the Special Purpose Vehicle full fee.

    Note: No axle or axle group is loaded in excess of the regulation limit.

Special Purpose Vehicle (Type O)
Special Purpose Vehicle (Type O)
    A special purpose vehicle (type o) is a special purpose vehicle, other than a special purpose vehicle (type p), that—
    (a)is built, or permanently modified, primarily for use on roads; and
    (b)has at least 1 axle or axle group loaded above the mass limits stated in the Transport Operations (Road Use Management) - Mass Dimensions and Loading Regulation 2005 Schedule 2.

    The Special Purpose Vehicle concession rate is 25% of the Special Purpose Vehicle full fee.

    Note: One axle or axle group is loaded in excess of the regulation limits.
