Discrepancies and Illegible Identifiers (7100)

VIU VIN Problem Sheet
VIU VIN Problem Sheet
    VIN Problem Sheet (March 2013)

Error DescriptionAction Required after completing the VIN Problem Sheet
VIN does not DecodeFor all vehicles, CSO is to conduct a PRI, complete both the VIN Problem Sheet and Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529) and contact VIU for advice.

Additional requirements for imported vehicles:
  • Scan and email import papers, safety certificate and consumer information documents.
Duplicate VIN/ChassisContact VIU for advice.
Vehicle not Found or Incomplete in NEVDIS/TICAContact VIU for advice.
More than one character of the VIN/Chassis on the safety certificate or certificate of inspection. All other details must match.CSO to conduct PRI and either:
  • mark the correction on the inspection document;
    or
  • update TICA.
One character of the VIN/Chassis on the safety certificate or certificate of inspection. All other details must match.Use the information in TICA and mark the correction on the inspection document.
Minor Error VIN/Chassis on Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet different to TICA (e.g. more than one wrong character on VIN/Chassis) for a self assessed vehicle trailer.If the vehicle/trailer is:
  • With the customer, CSO to perform PRI.
  • Not with the customer, they must re-inspect and complete a new Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529).

Correct the character on the Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529) and continue with transaction.
Make, model or year on Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet do not match TICA/NEVDISThe customer must complete a new Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529) to confirm the vehicle details (including the VIN/Chassis).
Vehicle Details (excluding VIN/Chassis, make, model or year) on Safety Certificate, Certificate of Inspection or Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet do not match TICA/NEVDISConfirm correct details with customer and either:
  • Update TICA using the information on the Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet if required;
    or
  • Correct error on the inspection document.
Previous TMR errorUpdate TICA using the information on the inspection document.

If a vehicle is exempt a safety certificate, customer must complete a Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529).
