Scenarios - Garage Address (3195)

Registered Business operating from a Private ResidenceVehicle registered in the name of a registered business with an individual as the registered operator and garage address is for the individual
(eg someone who runs a business from their residence)

Documents to SignEstablishes
Business registration displaying the individual's name (registered operator) as the proprietor of the business Link between the individual (registered operator) and the registered business
Garage address document for the individual Establishes address exists + links individual to the garage address.
Registered to an Organisation but Garaged at Home of EmployeeVehicle registered to an organisation but is garaged at the home of an employee as there is no actual local depot (eg travelling salesman for a company and the base of operation is his residence as there is no local depot)

Documents to SignEstablishes
Documents to verify the person is an employee of the organisation (registered operator)Links between the organisation and the employee
Garage address document for the employeeEstablishes address exists and links employee to the garage address.
Registered to an Organisation but Garaged at Address of Sub-OrgVehicle registered to an organisation but is garaged at the address of a sub-organisation (eg parent organisation that operates under the name of a sub unit of the organisation)

Documents to SignEstablishes
Documents to verify the link between the parent organisation (registered operator) and sub-organisationLink between the parent organisation and the sub-organisation
Garage address documents for the sub-organisationEstablishes the address exists + links sub-organisation to the garage address.
Registered in Business Name with Company as Reg. OperatorVehicle registered in the name of a registered business with a company as the registered operator and garage address is for the director of the company
(eg company that runs a registered business from director's home)

Documents to SignEstablishes
Business registration displaying the company (registered operator) as the proprietor of the registered business

Document to verify the link between the company and the director		Links the business name and company

Links the company and the director
Garage address document for the directorEstablishes the address is real + links the director to the garage address

Acceptable document to verify the link between an employee/director and
an organisation are:
  • Person is in uniform
  • Business card or identification
  • Is a regular representative at the centre
    • Document from company/business on official letterhead
    • TMR can phone the organisation if necessary
    • Documentation from ASIC
Registered Operator an Overseas visitor on Travel Visa (Itinerant)
Documents to SignEstablishes
Garage address form F4408 Declaration confirms that applicant has no fixed address – nominates next known address as Queensland registered address.
E-contact and e-notices requiredEnsures receipt of renewal notices
Must provide postal address
