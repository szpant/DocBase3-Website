Queensland Electric Vehicle Strategy (7407)

Project NameQueensland Electric Vehicle Strategy
When1 December 2018 to 30 January 2019
BackgroundIn 2017, the Queensland Government launched the The Future is Electric: Queensland’s Electric Vehicle Strategy – a strategy to help Queensland shift to a cleaner, greener Electric Vehicle (EV) fleet.

The strategy outlines 16 initiatives that will:
  • empower the community to make informed choices
  • enable the transition to EVs through charging infrastructure
  • explore cost-effective programs to support uptake of EVs
  • envisage what future actions may be required.

A focus of the strategy is the Queensland Electric Super Highway, a network of fast charging stations allowing EVs to travel from Coolangatta to Cairns and Brisbane to Toowoomba.
Who will be impacted and how?Our Customer Service Delivery Product Leadership Team (CSDPLT) has endorsed the promotion of the Electric Vehicle Strategy through our customer service centres. From December 2018, a short video about the Electric Vehicle Strategy will be included in the customer service centre video loop. Selected centres along the Electric Super Highway will be provided with printed materials (e.g. banners, flyers) to display until 31 January 2019. These centres include Cairns (Kenny Street), Mackay, Brisbane (Elizabeth Street) and Southport.
