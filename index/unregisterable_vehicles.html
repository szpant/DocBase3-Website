Unregisterable Vehicles (3008)


What are they?They are vehicles that do not qualify for registration as they:
  • Do not comply with vehicle design requirements
    and
  • Do not have a compliance plate fitted or RAV entry
Examples
Tuk Tuks
Tuk Tuks
    Tuk Tuks imported from overseas prior to 01 July 1992 are considered unsafe for road use in Australia.

    Refer: Vehicle Rego > 02. Registerable Vehicles > Light Vehicles > Motorcycle > Three Wheeled (Trikes) for additional information on Tuk Tuks.
Display or Model Vehicle
Display or Model Vehicle
    A non-compliant vehicle built for display at shows or exhibitions can never be registered.

    Eg. Homemade vehicles built by members of a machinery club

    Sample Graphic
    DISPLAY VEHICLE
Miniature Vehicle
Miniature Vehicle
    A non-compliant vehicle built to resemble a full size vehicle.
    eg. 'Pocket Rockets'
    • Mini-motorcycles do not comply with Australian Design Rules, and cannot be registered
    • They are not permitted to access roads or road related areas such as footpaths or nature strips
Bicycle with an internal combustion engine attached
Bicycle with an internal combustion engine attached
    These vehicles cannot be registered or ridden on a road or road-related area under any circumstances as they are considered unsafe for road use.

    These vehicles are classed as non-compliant motorcycles.

    Refer: TMR website > Registration > Registering Vehicles > Wheelchairs and small devices > Vehicles and devices that are illegal on the road network
