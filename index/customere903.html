Seatbelt Exemption Certificates
Seatbelt Exemption Certificates

Customers who have been issued with a valid seatbelt exemption certificate can register their certificate with TMR, to avoid being issued with a camera-detected Warning Letter or Infringement Notice for failing to wear a seatbelt while driving a motor vehicle.

If a certificate is not recorded before a customer is issued a Warning Letter or Infringement Notice, they can lodge it after the fact. If it is lodged:

    • During the Warning Letter Phase, any further Warning Letters or Infringement Notices for driver seatbelt offences won't be issued for the remaining period the recorded certificate is valid for
    • After an Infringement Notice has been issued, their fine can be waived if the certificate is lodged within 28 days of the notice and it was valid on the date of the offence.

Important: Although the exemption is recorded on TICA, the customer must still carry it with them at all times when driving (as a passenger, the driver must be carrying your certificate). This is because the driver must show the certificate to the Police if requested.
