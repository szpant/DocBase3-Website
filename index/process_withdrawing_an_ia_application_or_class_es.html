Process Withdrawing an IA application or class/es (2888)

Applies to
Applies to
    Withdraw an IA application or specific classes on an application for:
    • Tow Truck Driver/Assistant
    • Dangerous Goods Driver
    • Pilot/Escort Vehicle Driver
    • Driver/Rider Trainer
    • Traffic Controller
1
Check the status of the application
Check the status of the application
    The application must be in a lodged status to enable any withdrawal of an authority or any class/es.
    • Check the status of the application in TICA
      List Application History (TICA)
    If the application is NOT in a lodged status:
    If the application is NOT in a lodged status:
      • The customer may not withdraw the apllication for the authority or any class/es
        End of procedure
2
Check documents and EOI
Check documents and EOI
    Item
    Details
    Application FormRetrieve the original application form
    • Check the application form has been completed correctly
    • For audit purposes, the customer must:
      • Date stamp the orginial application form
    • Complete Office Use Section of application form
    • Date stamp the current date on the form
    EOI Documents
    • Compare signatures on EOI and application form
    • Check EOI document authenticity (if required).
      Refer to: Evidence of Identity > Recording and Verifying Documents> Manually Verifying Authenticity of documents
3
Record EOI Documents
Record EOI Documents
    Record/Update/Exempt/Invalidate EOI, Name Change, Residency Doc or Exemption

    Note:All newly presented documents must be recorded, even if in excess of minimum EOI requirements.
4
Withdraw authority or class on application (in TICA)
Withdraw authority or class on application (in TICA)
    If the status of the application is still lodged:
    Withdraw IA Application or Class/es (TICA)
5
Refund fees (if applicable)
Refund fees (if applicable)
    If the customer requested that the whole application be withdrawn:
    • Refer to IA policy for refund requirements. The refundable fees will be automatically credited to the customers account.
      Refer: Product Policy Guide > Refunds
    • Refund correct amount
      Refund Payment (TICA)
6
Advise the customer
Advise the customer
    Advise customer
    • Advise the customer that the authority or class/es on the application has been withdrawn.
    • Advise them that a refund (if applicable) will be mailed to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address.
    • If the customer only withdrew class/es on the application, inform them that they may be contacted to come back into the CSC, otherwise;
    • If the application is approved, the smartcard will be mailed to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address (if applicable)
    • If the application is refused, that they will be notified.
