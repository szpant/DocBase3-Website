Criminal History Request (0.000021)


To Create a Criminal History Request
A criminal history must be requested in TICA from the Lodge IA Application Screen when processing a new application for the following products:
  • dangerous goods driver licence*
  • driver/rider trainer
  • tow truck driver/assistant
  • pilot/escort vehicle driver
  • traffic controller
  • driver authorisation

    Note*:Dangerous goods driver licences do not require a New Zealand criminal history check.

    Note:If the customer has declared an alias name or historic names on the Industry Licence application form, the customer record must be updated in TICA to include this information.

    Refer to: TICA help > Update Individual Name to add alias name
  • Check the customer's place of birth details are recorded; it is essential that the country is also recorded. This is a mandatory requirement of the QPS CrimTrac system and if missing, the automatic request will be rejected meaning manual follow up and further delays for the customer.
    For Example:
    • The country is always a mandatory field (however, this is not imposed through the system)
    • If the country is AUS then the State and Town/City fields are also mandatory
    • If the country is NZL then the Town/City field is mandatory
    • If the country is other than AUS or NZL then only the country is mandatory
  • Check the customer's record and confirm that gender details are recorded correctly as either male or female.
To Create a Criminal History Request:
  • Click on the Individual customer hub hyperlink on the TICA home page
  • Display customer by using Name Search or CRN
  • From the Additional Requirements menu in the navigation bar select the Criminal History Requests menu item
  • Select add (which will flow to the "Add Criminal History Requests" page)
  • Select the appropriate action in the drop down boxes for;
    • Product
    • Request Reason
    • Request Type (National check or New Zealand check or select both) and
    • Fee Exemption (if applicable) drop down boxes
  • Select Submit

Note:This process does not automatically flow to the payment screen. This is to remind you to collect the product application fees as well.
Criminal History Requests
After close of business all National requests are batched and forwarded to QPS and the status on the Criminal History Request Page is changed to "Submitted".

After close of business all NZ requests are batched and forwarded to the relevant policy unit who send the request to the NZ authorities. The status on the Criminal History Request Page is changed to "Submitted".

If a request is not automatically finalised the next day it may mean either:
  • the search time has been extended due to complexities such as alias's.
  • the customer has a criminal history

Outstanding applications should be monitored daily against the TRAILS report inbox. Customer should still be advised that it may take up to 6 weeks for the requests to be finalised. Once a renewal application lapses no further criminal history requests should be processed unless a new application is lodged.

Criminal histories are extremely sensitive documents and all efforts to protect a customers privacy should be made.

If a customer's criminal history has not been returned within 30 working days and the customer has presented at the Customer Service Centre to determine the delay please contact:
  • Passenger Transport Division (Criminal History Check) (for DA's only)
  • Industry Licensing (Criminal History Check)
