Procedure for CSCs and DTMR Receipting Sites to Order Quick Cash Envelopes (2993)

CSC/DTMR Receipting Site
Quick Cash Envelopes
The Customer Service Centre or DTMR receipting site is to email the Commonwealth Government Banking Centre (QGBC) at address: qgbc@cba.com.au

What to include in the email
What to include in the email
    The email must include the following information:
    • Name and address of the requesting DTMR receipting site
    • The number of quick cash envelopes required – a standard order is 200 for large sites and 100 for medium to small sites.
    • DTMR contact person and phone number
Email Template
Email Template
    An email template, which includes all the above information, has been provided below:
    Quick Cash Envelope Order for Insert CSC Name

Note:
  • The quick cash envelopes will be delivered directly to the CSC/DTMR physical address provided in the email request.
  • Receipting site officers do not have financial delegation to authorise fees to debit TMRs bank account, therefore quick cash envelopes must only be ordered via email to the QGBC.
Deposit Books
The Customer Service Centre or DTMR receipting site is to email the Cash and Banking unit at address: cashandbanking@tmr.qld.gov.au

The email must include the following information:
  • Customer Service Centre or TMR Receipting Office
  • Agent Number
  • Postal Address
  • TMR Bank Account
  • Number of Books required (Maximum of 5 books can be ordered at one time)

An email template, which includes all the above information, has been provided below:
Deposit Book Order for Insert CSC Name

Delivery
Quick Cash Envelopes
Upon receipt of the email, QGBC will process the order and send the envelopes by courier directly to the requested CBA branch for collection. In more remote areas this may take a few days.

Under no circumstances should quick cash envelopes be ordered direct from the CBA branch as a substantial charge will be applied to the individual DTMR receipting site for the envelopes if not ordered via QGBC.

Important Note:
The use of the brown or black night safe wallets has been phased out. Any CSC or DTMR receipting site that has retained or still use these wallets should surrender them to their closest CBA bank branch and obtain a receipt for their surrender. The receipt must be forwarded to Cash & Banking Unit.
Deposit Books
Upon receipt of the email request, Cash and Banking will place an order with CBA. Once the deposit books are received, Cash and Banking will post to the CSC or TMR Receipting Office.

Please allow up to 4 weeks for delivery.
