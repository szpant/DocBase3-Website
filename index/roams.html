ROAMS (7518)


ROAMS
What it isRoad Operations Asset Management System (ROAMS) is an asset management and maintenance system which holds data for intelligent transport systems and electrical (ITS&E), Traffic Survey Data Management (TSDM), busway and tunnel assets.

It is a central repository for asset data and is used to manage work orders for incident and fault rectification works.

The system is owned by the Engineering and Technology Unit within the Department of Transport and Main Roads (TMR).
Benefits
Benefits
    • Streamlined maintenance and performance monitoring of assets
    • Electronic recording of information
    • Whole of life asset management
    • Improved operational and management efficiencies
    • Consistent state-wide maintenance and management practices
    • Improved visibility of inspection and replacement schedules, asset condition and maintenance history
    • A foundation to manage safety risks, investment and remediation of the targeted assets
    • Better system = stakeholder and user acceptance
    • Compliance with ISO55000 Asset Management standards
Use of ROAMS
Use of ROAMS
Compliance use of ROAMS
Compliance use of ROAMS
    CSB Compliance will utilise ROAMS to centrally record site information relating to TMR Interception Sites. This includes recording of initial information, maintenance of those records and requesting repairs utilising system generated work orders.

    The benefits for Compliance are:
    • Centralised recording of asset and IT system information pertaining to on road compliance interception sites within a single state-wide database
    • Ability to maintain the interception site information recorded in the system
    • A process to request and track required repair and maintenance work through an IT system
Responsibilities
Manager (Compliance)
Manager (Compliance)
    It is the responsibility of the Manager (Compliance) in each region to ensure that all Interception Sites are registered in ROAMS based on the state-wide interception site lists.
    Northern Region on road Interception site list - Attachment 1.1
    Central Region on road Interception site list - Attachment 1.2
    Southern Region on road Interception site list - Attachement 1.3
    SEQN Region on road Interception site list - Attachment 1.4
    SEQS Region on road Interception site list - Attachment 1.5

Senior Transport Inspector
Senior Transport Inspector
    Senior Transport Inspectors are to ensure each Interception Site is reviewed at least every 12 months to ensure the accuracy of ROAMS. Senior Transport Inspectors are to add text, including who reviewed the site and the date of the review, in the comments section for the site.
    ROAMS must be updated if changes to an Interception Site have been identified or when repairs or maintenance are required.
Additional Information
Additional Infromation
Additional Infromation
    The following documents are stored in the Interception sharepoint site:
    • Site Safety Plan
    • Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)
    • Weighbridge/axle platform calibration certificate
    • Site assessment for Cat 3
    • Site survey results
    • Photos of the site and all signage.

Access
Access
Access
    Access to ROAMS is made via Service Centre Online request.

    Enter ROAMS Internal User Access Request in search function

    Transport Inspectors and Compliance Administration staff should request access to Access / Update Sitesso they can edit details recorded about Interception Sites.

    Managers and Senior Transport Inspectors should request access to Access / Update Site and Work Orders.

    All users should also request access to Traffic Survey and Data Management (TSDM) if they don't currently have access.

    Approval is by next level manager:
    • Transport Inspector and Admin staff approved by STI
    • STI approved by Manager
    • Manager approved by RD
    For assistance with ROAMS email tsdm@tmr.qld.gov.au

Work Orders
Work Orders
Work Orders
    Creating a work order to request repairs or maintenance for an Interception Site is restricted to Manger (Compliance) and STI.

    Site issues
    Site issues
      If an issue is identified at an Interception Site add details to ROAMS and advise the STI.
      • follow the update site details procedure
      • add details of the issue/s in the comments section
      • if the site is not safe to use the status of the site must be changed to Non-Operational and text added to Non-Operational Reason.
      • the STI will update ROAMS when the issue is rectified or Work Order completed.
