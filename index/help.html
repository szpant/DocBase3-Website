Customer Services Branch Staff - How to change policy and procedure
Customer Services Branch Staff - How to change policy and procedure
    We endeavour to present a flawless product to the Service Delivery Network, it's Agents and Representatives. You are part of a quality control process and if you notice any of the following issues noted below, please raise a Policy or Procedure BIID:
    • Policy or procedure that contradicts legislation,
    • Policy that references old legislation, is out of date or incorrectly referenced,
    • Procedure that is no longer used or is substantially incorrect to current widespread practice,
    • A policy or procedure document does not make sense (you should have consensus that a group of people could not understand it),
    • A policy or procedure document that requires more comprehensive information to clarify a subject,
    • A document that would not stand up to public/parliamentary/media scrutiny,
    • A document that contradicts the Code of Conduct or is illegal.

    Your feedback helps us to present a professional product for you and your co-workers.

    We thank you for your time.

    The Database Management Team

      We will:
      • Review your BiiD and check the content, time frame and it's validity.
      • Check spelling, punctuation and grammar. You should try to have this already done as it will delay your issue being completed.
      • Complete the work within the nominated time frame, subject to staffing, workload, projects and urgent priorities.

    How to obtain authorisation to the BiiD
    How to obtain authorisation to the BiiD
      Most DTMR officers can access the BiiD system in read only mode but only certain officers may create new issues in the BiiD.

      To obtain access to create new issues you must:
      • be in one of the following or equivalent positions;
        • Advisor
        • Senior Advisor
        • Manager of a CSC/QGAP
        • Manager of a work unit related to Policy/Procedure
        • Policy Officer (or similar to)
        • Technical specialist
        • Business Advisor
        • Project Officer
        • Senior Project Officer
        • Member of the State BiiD Reference Group
        • Delegates may be considered by the admin group
      • send an e-mail to DB2 Assist explaining your request.

      Access to the DocBase category is restricted to a limited number of TRB/CSB/Project Group personnel.
