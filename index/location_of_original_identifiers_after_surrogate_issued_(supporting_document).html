Location of Original Identifiers After Surrogate Issued (Supporting Document) (5326)

Location of Original Identifiers After Surrogate IssuedBefore this process is followed operators must ensure that VIU have provided the following:
  • cancelled the issue of the surrogate
  • updated the text restriction with this advice
Procedure
(1) Vehicle not stamped and not registered with surrogate
(1) Vehicle not stamped and not registered with surrogate
    In instances where the original identifiers match the compliance plate, the new business should be processed using the original identifiers.
(2) Vehicle stamped and not registered with surrogate
(2) Vehicle stamped and not registered with surrogate
    In instances where the original identifiers match the compliance plate, the new business should be processed using the original identifiers and the stamped surrogate number is required to be overstamped with the letter 'X' .
(3) Vehicle stamped and registered with surrogate
(3) Vehicle stamped and registered with surrogate
    In instances where the original identifiers match the compliance plate, the record on the system should be updated to reflect the original identifier and the stamped surrogate numbers overstamped with the letter 'X' .

    To ensure an accurate history of the vehicles identification is maintained, the registration transaction must not be invalidated.

Note:In instances where the original identifiers do not match the compliance plate/RAV entry, and the restriction text does not provide advice on the correct identifier to be used, operator to contact the VIU for assistance.
