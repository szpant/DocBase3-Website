Checklist of Documents by Vehicle Type (1101)

Customers may be entitled to exemptions and concessions. Please refer to the relevant document for the entitlements, rules and requirements.

Light Motor Vehicle
Light Motor Vehicle
    • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
    • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (F5225) (if applicable) or a Letter of Authority and/or any other evidence that satisfies the operator
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Concession documents (if applicable)
    • Proof of Origin
    • Original (or a Certified copy by CTP Provider) CTP Certificate/Nominate on Application Form
    • Safety Certificate (unless exempt) - For electronically issued certificates, only the certificate number is required
    • Written Off Vehicle Inspection (if applicable)
    • Completed Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529) - for vehicles exempt safety certificate
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Proof of Duty Exemption (if applicable)
    • If entered on the RAV, modification documents for any mods completed after RAV entry
    • If not entered on the RAV, Import documents and modification documents (if applicable)
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
Heavy Motor Vehicle
Heavy Motor Vehicle
    • Application Form
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Concession documents (if applicable)
    • Proof of Origin
    • Original (or a Certified copy by CTP Provider) CTP Certificate/Nominate on Application Form
    • Proof of tare weight (Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Weights/Measurements > Proof of Tare Weight)
    • Certificate of Inspection AIS or TMR (unless exempt) - For electronically issued certificates, only the certificate number is required
    • Written Off Vehicle Inspection (if applicable)
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Proof of Duty Exemption (if applicable)
    • Excess Dimension/Mass Permits (if applicable)
    • If entered on the RAV, modification documents for any mods completed after RAV entry
    • If not entered on the RAV, Import documents and modification documents (if applicable)
    • Appropriate Inspection Sheets
    • Vehicle Details Inspection Sheets (F3529) PRI conducted by HVRAS officer
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
    Note:Any bus that has a specified service route must have a road usage = route service and have a customer qualification RSER.
Trailers
Trailers
    Light Trailer <.75
    Light Trailer <.75
      • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
      • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (if applicable)
      • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
      • Proof of Origin
      • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
      • Self Assessment of Single Axle Trailers (F3970)
      • Written Off Vehicle Inspection (if applicable)
      • If entered on the RAV, modification documents for any mods completed after RAV entry
      • If not entered on the RAV, Import documents and modification documents (if applicable)

    Light Trailer >.75 <= 3.5t
    Light Trailer >.75 <= 3.5t
      • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
      • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (if applicable)
      • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
      • Proof of Origin
      • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
      • Safety Certificate (unless exempt) - For electronically issued certificates, only the certificate number is required
      • Written Off Vehicle Inspection (if applicable)
      • If entered on the RAV, modification documents for any mods completed after RAV entry
      • If not entered on the RAV, Import documents and modification documents (if applicable)
      • HVRAS Inspection document (if applicable)

    Heavy Trailer >3.5t
    Heavy Trailer >3.5t
      • Application Form (when processed at a formless centre not required for trailers over 3.5t but less than 4.5t)
      • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (if applicable)
      • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
      • Proof of Origin
      • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
      • Proof of tare weight (applicable to Special Purpose Vehicle Trailers only) Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Weights/Measurements > Proof of Tare Weight)
      • Excess Dimensions Permit (if applicable for a specially constructed trailer - Refer: Excess Dimensions Permit)
      • Certificate of Inspection AIS or TMR (Unless Exempt) - For electronically issued certificates, only the certificate number is required
      • Written Off Vehicle Inspection (if applicable)
      • Modification documents (if applicable)
      • Inspection Sheets (if PRI already conducted by HVRAS officer)
      • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
      • HVRAS Inspection document (if applicable)

Conditional
Conditional
    • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
    • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (if applicable)
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Proof of Origin
    • Original (or a Certified copy by CTP Provider) CTP Certificate/Nominate on Application Form
    • Proof of tare weight (if applicable) (Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Weights/Measurements > Proof of Tare Weight)
    • Permit issued by Police (if applicable)
    • Completed Pre Registration Inspection Sheets
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Excess Dimension/Mass Permit (if applicable)
    • Modification documents (if applicable)
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
Rally Vehicles
Rally Vehicles
    • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
    • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (F5225) (if applicable) or a Letter of Authority and/or any other evidence that satisfies the operator
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Proof of Import (If imported and first registration)
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Original (or a Certified copy by CTP Provider) CTP Certificate/Nominate on Application Form
    • Confederation of Australian Motorsports (CAMS) Queensland Rally Vehicle Registration Scheme (QRVRS) Authority to Operate (ATO)
    Processing Instructions
    Processing Instructions
      • All Rally Vehicles are to be processed using a chassis number (regardless of their age or if a 17 digit identifier is present)
      Note: Please contact Vehicle Standards for assistance.

Motorised Wheelchair
Motorised Wheelchair
    • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
    • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (F5225) (if applicable) or a Letter of Authority and/or any other evidence that satisfies the operator
    • Proof of Origin
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Completed Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529)
    • Completed Motorised Wheelchair Statement (F4414)
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
Evaluation Vehicles
Evaluation Vehicles
    • Original Import Approval (if applicable)
    • Application Form, unless processed at a formless centre
    • Authority to Represent Registered operator Vehicle Registration (F5225) (if applicable) or a Letter of Authority and/or any other evidence that satisfies the operator
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Proof of Origin
    • Original (or a Certified copy by CTP Provider) CTP Certificate/Nominate on Application Form
    • Proof of tare weight (if applicable) (Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Weights/Measurements > Proof of Tare Weight)
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Compliance Plate Exemption Permit
    • Proof of Duty Exemption (if applicable)
    • Exemption of Fitment of Compliance Plate
    • Excess Dimension/Mass Permits (if applicable)
    • Modification documents (if applicable)

    There are 3 types of evaluation vehicle:
    • Pre-release evaluation
    • Market evaluation
    • Engineering evaluation

    These vehicles are all for evaluation purposes only.
    They can be either imported or Australian built.

    Compliance Condition type T is required upon registration. Type T code is not to be altered.

    Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Imported Vehicles > Registration > Registerable Commercial Import Vehicles > Conditional > Evaluation Vehicles for additional information.
Special Purpose Vehicles
Special Purpose Vehicles
    • Application Form
    • Proof of Garage Address (if applicable)
    • Concession documents (if applicable)
    • Proof of Origin
    • Original (or a Certified copy by CTP Provider) CTP Certificate/Nominate on Application Form
    • Proof of supervised weigh (if applicable) (Refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > Weights & Measurements > Tare > Proof of Weight)
    • Certificate of Inspection AIS or TMR/HVRAS (unless exempt) - For electronically issued certificates, only the certificate number is required
    • Gas Certificate (if applicable)
    • Proof of Duty Exemption (if applicable)
    • Excess Dimension/Mass Permits (if applicable)
    • Modification documents (if applicable)
    • Appropriate Inspection Sheets
    • Vehicle Details Inspection Sheet (F3529) PRI conducted by HVRAS officer
    • Personalised Plates Application or Remove/Attach Personalised Plates Form (if applicable)
