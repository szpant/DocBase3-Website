Process the surrender/return of IA Class/es and/or IA smartcard (2870)

Applies to
Applies to
    Processing the surrender of an Industry Authority product or class which may include:
    • Dangerous Goods Driver
    • Driver Authorisation
    • Driver/Rider Trainer
    • Pilot/Escort Vehicle Driver
    • Tow Truck Driver/Assistant
    • Traffic Controller
    Note:This action may effect a multiple IA licence/accreditation/certificate holder. If any authorities remain current to the customer, the smartcard will need to be replaced. Refer to the relevant policy product guide for advice.
    For further details:
    Refer: 1. Industry Authority > Multiple IA and IA Smartcards
