Transport and Main Roads Customer Services Support and Customer Contact Centre Services Support List (7431)


The Department of Transport and Main Roads offers a wide range of services for customers by alternate methods. Below is a list of the available services, that can be delivered by the Customer Contact Centre Operators.

Services where a payment is required to be made, and not able to be processed by the Customer Contact Centre, email templates are to be used by the operator to send to the applicant to allow the service to be processed.

Please note: turnaround time is (ideally) within 3 working days. All requests are treated as 'urgent'.

Note: Any with 'Processed by CSS' will be processed by Customer Service Support. If a customer is following up on one that would have gone to CSS, call an SCCC for assistance.
