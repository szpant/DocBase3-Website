Issue Court Certificate (2769)

Applies to
Applies to
    Producing a Court Certificate containing registration or licensing information, to be used in legal proceedings.
1
Before You Start
Before You Start
    Ensure requestee is authorised to receive the certificate
2
Check Documents
Check Documents
    • Check request is on letterhead from an allowable organisation and includes all required information

    Refer: Admin > 1. Administration > Court Certificates > Format of Request
3
Forward to your regional court certificate central processing hub (SEQ only)
Forward to your regional court certificate central processing hub (SEQ only)
    Where a request is made to a CSC in a region with a court certificate central processing hub, the request should be forwarded to the central processing hub for completion.

    The central processing hub will then make arrangements with the requesting party for the collection/postage of the completed certificate/s.

    For central processing hub contact details, Refer: Offence Management > 1. Court Cases >Court Certificates > Where to request a court certificate
4
Collect the information
Collect the information
    Collate the required information from the relevant source:
    • TICA (Registration & Licensing)
    • SILAS (Industry Licensing)
    • Iron Mountain (Certified copy of a document)

    If collecting traffic history from TICA, print 5 years of history unless otherwise requested.
5
Process ROI in TICA
6
Prepare Certificate
Prepare Certificate
    Enter the relevant information into the sample certificate.
    Refer: Admin > 1. Administration > Court Certificates > Certificate Templates

    Print an original on TMR letterhead, and 1 copy of each certificate:
    Original is given to the requesting officer/organisation
    • 2nd copy is to be filed in a secure location at the centre

    If Traffic History requested:

    Note:If no history recorded over the past 5 years attach copies of the "No Recorded Traffic History"

    • Write a page number in the top right corner of each traffic history page (eg write (1) if not printed on page)
    • Attach each page of history to the relevant certificate copy:
    • Original traffic history pages to original certificate (Staple in three places down left hand margin of page - like a booklet)
    • Copies of history to other certificate copies(Staple top left hand corner)
7
Arrange for delivery/collection of completed certificate
Arrange for delivery/collection of completed certificate
    Completed court certificates may be:
    • collected in person by the requesting officer or an authorised representative, or
    • mailed (posted) to the requesting officer/organisation

    If certificate/s is being collected, store in a secure area until collection.

    For EOI requirements for collection, Refer: Admin > 1. Administration > Court Certificates > Method of delivery of completed court certificates > EOI Required for collection of completed certificates

    If delivery is requested by post, mail the certificate to the requesting party.
8
Complete Court Certificate Register
Complete Court Certificate Register
    If being collected, have organisation's representative sign the Register on receipt of Court Certificate
9
Filing
