Tenure
Tenure
    An applicant must hold, and have held for a continuous period of at least 1 year (from date of application) a valid open licence for the class of vehicle for which the applicant wishes to provide training.

    Valid tenure is any day within the 12 month period that the licence is not:
    • Expired
    • Cancelled
    • Suspended (excluding SPER suspensions)
Licence Status
Licence Status
    The licence must be current at the time of lodgement. Any SPER matters must be finalised, but the time spent on the SPER suspension is counted as valid tenure.
##If applicant is a multiple IA holder and wants to voluntarily surrender their Driver/Rider Trainer Accreditation but retain other IAs
**A pre-renewal criminal history check is performed automatically by TMR prior to the renewal being sent out. If the holder declares any criminal history within two (2) months of applying for the renewal, an adhoc check must be performed to exclude any missed history. If a criminal history has not been passed on TICA, the transaction cannot proceed.
⌂If replacing a card that was not received in the mail, the customer does not need to complete an application form if the request is made within 90 days of the card being issued.
