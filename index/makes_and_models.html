Makes and Models (1471)

What it isThe capture of makes and models information assists the Queensland Police service in:
  • Combating theft
  • Investigations and operations

It also aids TMR enforcement teams in performing their on-water surveillance.
Who can Add
Who can add Makes and Models
Who can add Makes and Models
    Creation of makes and models access is restricted to those who have a suitable history in ship makes and models.

    Access is never given to an operator for business convenience.

    Only SPECPRO Profile holders are able to perform this action.
Create
Creating Makes and Models
Creating Makes and Models
    The following point should be noted when creating makes and models:
    • Any make less than two (2) years must have one of the following before creation:
      • Information from the manufacture establishing that the ship will be manufactured on a commercial basis
      • Brochure showing evidence of the make and model of the ship
      • Evidence of Company or Business registration

    Performance Monitoring
    Performance Monitoring
      1.A report is generated for Data Management to check accuracy of creation and maintenance actions.
      2.If an error is found it is passed back to the creator for correction.
      3.Corrections are monitored by Data Management
      4.Failure to comply with guide lines will be reported to management and action taken.
