Combination or hyphenated version of surnames
    A marriage certificate issued by the Registrar of the relevant Birth, Deaths and Marriages (BDM) is acceptable EOI for a customer wishing to change their surname to a combination or hyphenated version of their spouse's surname and their own surname.

Truncation (shortening) of Customer Names
Truncation (shortening) of Customer Names
    TICA has the following character limits for customer names:
    • family name 28 characters
    • first name 16 characters
    • middle name/s 16 characters.

    Note:It is possible to type a customer name into TICA that exceeds the above characters limits, however, TICA will not save any characters beyond these limits.

    Customer names that exceed the character limits must be recorded as follows:
    Family name
    Family name
      • Where a customer's family name exceeds the 28 character limit, their full family name must be recorded in the TEXT field of TICA.
      • A customer cannot choose how their family name is truncated (shortened). All customer family names (including hyphenated names) must be entered correctly and will be truncated (shortened) automatically by TICA after the first 28 characters.
      • The customer must be advised of the 28 character limit and that their family name on their physical driver licence will only appear up to the 28 character limit.
    First name
    First name
      • Where a customer's first name exceeds the 16 character limit, their full first name must be recorded in the TEXT field of TICA.
      • A customer cannot choose how their first name is truncated (shortened). All customer first names (including hyphenated names) must be entered correctly and will be truncated (shortened) automatically by TICA after the first 16 characters.
      • The customer must be advised of the 16 character limit and that their first name on their physical driver licence will only appear up to the 16 character limit.
    Middle name/s
    Middle name/s
      • Where a customer's middle name/s will exceed the 16 character limit, the customer can choose one of the below options:
        1.middle name/s are truncated (shortened) after the first 16 characters
        2.only have their initial/s for their middle name/s recorded
        3.only have their first middle name and then initial/s for any other middle name/s recorded
      • Where a customer's middle name/s exceeds the 16 character limit, their full middle name/s must be recorded in the TEXT field of TICA.
Customers with one name
Customers with one name
    A customer can be recorded in TICA with only one name (either surname or given name only). This name can consist of two names, please refer to the samples below.

    Name on EOI (example)Record in surname field in TICA
    SinghSingh,=
    Manpreet Singh (all in given or surname field)Manpreet Singh,=

    No details are required to be entered in the first and middle name fields.

    To search for a customer with one name in TICA after they have been created, operators should enter the customer name followed by ,=. For example, if the customer's name is Sandeep, operators should search in TICA using Sandeep,=.

    A customer whose two-part surname has been wrongly recorded in TICA as a surname and given name can get their record amended to reflect what is recorded on their Category A document. This is done on a case by case basis and only if:
    • their record indicates this occurred in the past when EOI procedures were not as strict,
    • they produce standard EOI documents with the two-part surname, and
    • the possibility of fraud is considered and ruled out.
OrganisationAn organisation’s name should be the full name that it is recognised or traded under. Abbreviations can be used based on common usage/acceptance.
Attention to a Specific Person
Attention to a Specific Person
    This is applicable to organisations when a specific person is required to receive mail. The person's name is entered on the Cust Organisation Maintenance Screen (Accessed through the LORG screen) in the contact name field.
Division/Branch of an Organisation
Division/Branch of an Organisation
    Many business and Govt departments may choose to manage their vehicle fleets within a division or branch of that organisation.

    This information will be shown on renewal notices and certificates of registration.

    This information is recorded in the Contact Ph field of the Customer Organisation Maintenance screen.
