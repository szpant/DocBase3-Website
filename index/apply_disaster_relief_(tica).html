Apply Disaster Relief (TICA) (100)

Reduced Pay Terms

Process Update Rego 3 or 6 mths exempt Surcharge
Process Update Rego 3 or 6 mths exempt Surcharge
    1.Click on the appropriate hub on the TICA Home page e.g. Vehicle hub, Trailer hub hyperlink

    The ‘selected Hub’ will display
    2.Display the customer using Plate, CRN or Name and Date of Birth and click the Display push-button

    Note:
    If the Plate is displayed or the customer has only one registration record, the details will automatically display on the Registration Hub

    If more than one record exists, the List Customer Registrations will display

    a. Highlight the required registration and click the Select push-button

    The Display Successful message will display
    3.Click the Update renewal details hyperlink

    The selected Update Registration Renewal task page will display
    4.Select the required term (3 or 6 months) from the Pay Term (months) drop-down box
    5.Select DISASTER from the Relief Type drop-down box
    6.Click the Update push-button

    The Business Exception - Web page Dialog box will display
    7.Highlight DISASTER RELIEF in the list box
    8.Input the following text in the Additional Information field:

    ‘As per Disaster Relief Package’
    9.Click the Confirm push-button

    The Update Successful message will display
    10.Select the Payment hyperlink from the left bottom of the context area

    The Make Payment task page will display
    11.Take payment as per current procedure


Exemption from RRRF

Process Update Rego 3 or 6 mths exempt Surcharge with RRRF exemption
Process Update Rego 3 or 6 mths exempt Surcharge with RRRF exemption
    1.Click on the appropriate hub on the TICA Home page e.g. Vehicle hub, Trailer hub hyperlink

    The ‘selected Hub’ will display
    2.Display the customer using Plate, CRN or Name and Date of Birth and click on the Display push-button

    Note:
    If the Plate is displayed or the customer has only one registration record, the details will automatically display on the Registration Hub

    If more than one record exists, the List Customer Registrations will display

    a. Highlight the required registration and click the Select push-button

    The Display Successful message will display
    3.Click the Update renewal details hyperlink

    The selected Update Registration Renewal task page will display
    4.Select the required term (3 or 6 months) from the Pay Term (months) drop-down box

    Note:
    Steps #4 to #9 are only required if the customer wishes to take the 3 or 6 month payment option.
    5.Select DISASTER from the Relief Type drop-down box
    6.Click the Update push-button

    The Business Exception - Web page Dialog box will display
    7.Highlight DISASTER RELIEF in the list box
    8.Input the following text in the Additional Information field:

    ‘As per Disaster Relief Package’
    9.Click the Confirm push-button

    The Update Successful message will display
    10.Select the Payment hyperlink from the left bottom of the context area

    The Make Payment task page will display
    11.Click the Update push-button

    The Update Successful message will display
    12.Once your SASM has made the adjustment for RRRF into the account , take payment as follows:
    a.Highlight registration to be paid in Select items to pay
    b.Click in the Pay From field group and enter the amount to be paid from the account

    The Net to pay will re-display
    13.Take the remaining payment as per current procedures
