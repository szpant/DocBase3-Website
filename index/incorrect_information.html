Incorrect Information (3352)


Incorrect Information - Driver licence
What it isAccuracy of information on the Driver Licence
A written notice may be given to the holder of a Qld driver licence if:
  • The information on the licence or returning driver certificate is incorrect.
  • There is a reasonable belief that an error was made when issuing the licence or returning driver certificate s127 TO(RUM-DL) Reg 2010
NotificationThe written notice will:
  • Require the licensee to return the driver licence/driver licence receipt/returning driver certificate
  • Identify the incorrect information
  • Identify the correct information.

A replacement driver licence/certificate with the correct information must be issued. (No fee applies.)

Notice
The following notice has been structured to comply with the s127(1)(b) TO(RUM-DL) Reg 2010
Incorrect info on DL - CE error

Note: For steps on how to Manage Operator Error involving smartcards:
