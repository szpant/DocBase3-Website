Enter bus frame inspection details in TICA (7646)

Applies to
Applies to
    entering bus frame inspection details in TICA
1
Log into TICA
2
Click on the appropriate Vehicle Hub hyperlink on the TICA Home page.
The Vehicle Hub will display
3
Choose VIN/Chassis/Plate/Engine from the drop-down list
4
Enter the Identification number or plate in the field provided and click on the Display pushbutton.
The Search Vehicle task page will display.
Highlight the correct vehicle and click the Select pushbutton
The Vehicle Hub will display
5
If the highlighted vehicle description has a current restriction recorded against it, the Restrictions - WebPage Dialog box will display. When viewing is completed, click the OK pushbutton.
6
Hover on the Vehicle menu in the navigation bar and select the Restrictions menu item.
The Vehicle Restrictions task page will display.
7
Click on the Add... pusbutton
The Add Restriction Webpage Dialog box will display
8
Choose the Text Information from the type drop-down list
9
Enter the Effective Date (todays date)
10
Enter the required information in the Additional Note field
This information should include the following:
  • The Name and Authority number of the TMR Authorised Officer who carried out the Frame Inspection;
  • The date that the Frame Inspection was carried out;
  • Where the Frame Inspection took place;
  • The odometer reading of the bus inspected;
  • Details of what panels were removed;
  • Any additional comments/ conditions applicable to the inspection.
11
Click OK pushbutton
