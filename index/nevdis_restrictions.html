IMPR

IMPO
IMPO
    • Vehicle can be registered
    Message Details:
      "Import Restriction contact 4000 to F10 to Continue, F12 to Cancel"
    • Press OK or Cancel to the restriction message displayed

    Reason for Restriction
    Reason for Restriction
      The following imported vehicle will have the NEVDIS restriction:
      • Personal Imports
      • Trailer Import Condition
      • Import Conditional of Full Compliance
      • Conditional SOFA
      • Conditional Import - Visitor
      • Evaluation Vehicles
      • Imported as a Rally vehicle
      • Exempt Import landed prior to the Act
      • Import Restriction
      • Import subject to full letter of compliance

    Registration Process
    • Conduct PRI on trailers and heavy vehicles and personally imported motorcycles (light motor vehicles 4.5t GVM or less are exempt PRI)
    • If PRI is required:
      • Advise the customer that this is a random inspection not a result of the restriction
      • Complete
        Vehicle Identity Verification Checklist
      • Contact the VIU and email relevant documentation toVIUnit@tmr.qld.gov.au
      • VIU will advise if vehicle can be registered proceed with transaction
    Vehicle Cannot be Registered
    Vehicle Cannot be Registered
      Vehicle cannot be registered and will require a Police Inspection.

      Advise customer:
      • The VIU will contact them to organise Police Inspection
      • They will need an unregistered vehicle permit to drive the vehicle home
      • Add text message against customer and vehicle stating:
        'This vehicle has a NEVDIS Import Restriction in (State) and has been referred to the VIU for investigation. There is doubt regarding the true identity of the vehicle. The vehicle will require a police inspection. Contact the VIU prior to registering this vehicle.'
        After the police inspection the:
        • Customer will re-present the vehicle for registration
        • CSO is to:
          • Perform PRI (if required)
          • Contact the VIU to confirm successful police inspection
          • Register the vehicle provided all other requirements are met
          • DO not expire text as it is required at a later stage
Stolen
Stolen Process
Stolen Process
    If a vehicle is recorded as stolen in NEVDIS CSC is to:
    • Note vehicle details and restriction type
    • Contact Help Direct
    • Advise customer to take a seat (do not advise that QPS are being contacted)
    • Retain paperwork

    Help Direct will:
    • Contact QPS to determine the status of the vehicle

    If vehicle remains stolen on the QPS system:
    • Transaction cannot be processed
    • CSC to contact local QPS station to have Officers come and investigate

    If vehicle restriction no longer applies:
    • Help Direct will remove the TICA restriction
    • QPS will have the NEVDIS restriction removed
    • CSC can process transaction when restrictions removed

    If applicants vehicle has the same identifiers as the vehicle listed as stolen but is not the vehicle listed as stolen a surrogate identifier is to be issued.

    For the process to issue a surrogate identifier, please refer: Vehicle Rego > 05. Vehicle Requirements > The How To: Vehicle Requirements > Vehicle Requirements Procedures > Issue a Surrogate Vehicle Identifier

QLD WOVI
QLD WOVI
    If:
    • The vehicle is identified as a repairable write-off
      and
    • There is a WOVI pass result recorded in TICA (check the List Inspection History Screen
      and
      • The written off vehicle restriction has been cleared in NEVDIS
    Process the New Business application as per normal procedure.

    If vehicle has had a WOVI completed more than 24 hours previously but it is not on TICA contact the VIU.

    Please note that QIS advises WOVI customers that a pass entered into the system by a QIS Inspector does not update in NEVDIS until overnight, therefore the vehicle will not be able to registered until the following working day.

Offensive Advertising
OFAD (Transfer)
OFAD (Transfer)
    Vehicle cannot be transferred and will be cancelled (at least 14 days after the registration cancellation notice has been given to the registered operator) unless TMR receives advice from the Advertising Standards Bureau (ASB) that it has withdrawn its advertising breach notice.

    Message Details:
    RG046E ACTION CANNOT BE PROCESSED DUE TO VEHICLE/SHIP RESTRICTION
    • TICA will be unable to continue.
    • Advise customer of the text restriction details which will outline the Offensive Advertising Case Reference number and the contact details for the Advertising Standards Bureau (ASB).
    • Refuse Transaction.
OFAD (New Business)
OFAD (New Business)
    Vehicle cannot be registered.

    Message Details:
    RG046E ACTION CANNOT BE PROCESSED DUE TO VEHICLE/SHIP RESTRICTION
    • TICA will be unable to continue.
    • Advise customer of the text restriction details which will outline the Offensive Advertising Case Reference number.
    • The customer must provide a statutory declaration stating that the advertising subject to the Australian Standards Bureau (ASB) case, has been removed from the vehicle before the vehicle can be registered.
    • To expire the OFAD restriction, contact Customer Service Support
