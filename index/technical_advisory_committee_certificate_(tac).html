Technical Advisory Committee Certificate (TAC) (7140)

What is it?A Technical Advisory Committee Certificate (TAC) is an inspection certificate that ensures the vehicle complies with set construction and safety standards.
Requirements
New BusinessTransfer
  • Original green copy of TAC issued to registered operator
  • Approval letter signed by the State Director of the Australian Street Rod Federation (Qld)
  • Original green copy of TAC issued to new registered operator
  • Approval letter signed by the State Director of the Australia Street Rod Federation (Qld)
  • Safety Certificate
Rules
  • Only issued by the Aust Street Rod Federation (ASRF) is the only body authorised to issue TAC's
  • Cost is determined by the ASRF
  • A TAC is valid for the life of the Street Rod unless there is a change of ownership
  • New owner must obtain a TAC issued in their name prior to transfer/new business
  • Certificate cannot be accepted if altered
  • Always return the TAC to the customer
  • A copy is to be filed with documentation

Australian Street Rod Federation Contact can be found at:
http://www.asrf.org.au/tac/
