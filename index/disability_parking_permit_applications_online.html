Disability Parking Permit Applications Online (7112)

Project NameDisability Parking Permit Applications Online
WhenJune 23, 2017
BackgroundAs part of TMR’s commitment to provide more online services, customers will soon be able to apply for disability parking permits online.

The Disability Parking Permit Applications online service will provide customers with an additional method to:
  • submit an application for a disability parking permit;
  • renew a permit;
  • replace an existing permit;
  • attach a medical certificate (where applicable)
  • make payment (where applicable)

No changes have been made to the existing processes for reviewing applications by the Disability Parking Permit Processing Unit. All other methods of accepting applications will still apply.

This service will be available to both individuals and organisations who have an existing customer reference number.

Further information will be available closer to implementation.
Who will be impacted and how?Frontline staff will be able to direct customers to the online service rather than advising them to submit their application by mail.
