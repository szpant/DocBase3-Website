Court Results (2960)


What it isA court result is the outcome of a court hearing, after the issue of a Complaint and Summons for a matter to be dealt with by a court. A Complaint and Summons may be issued as a result of a Breach Report or Infringement Notice.
Role of TMRCourt results for certain offences are entered onto TRAILS/TICA and may form part of a persons traffic record. TMR is not involved with the administration of court results (e.g. payment of fines) and any enquiries should be directed to the relevant Magistrates Court.
Driver Offence Notification (DON)Once a court result is entered for a traffic offence that is intercepted roadside, a DON may be emailed to the registered operator of the vheicle the offence was committed in (if the offender was not the registered oeprator). The DON will include the offender’s name and details of the offence.TMR is authorised to provide these details to the registered operator under Section 77AAA of the Transport Operations Road Use Management Act 1995.

At this stage most breaches do not contain vehicle registration details therefore DONs will not be issued. Most offences which are dealt with through the courts (for example, drink or drug driving, unlicensed driving) will not be notified at this stage.

Refer: Driver Offence Notification

No Conviction RecordedIf a court decides not to record a conviction (NCR) for an infringement the demerit points will still be allocated and the infringement will appear on the person's traffic history.

Schedule 4 of the Transport Operation (Road Use Management) Act 1995 (TORUM Act) provides that a person who is found guilty of an offence under this legislation is taken to be convicted of the offence despite the court’s decision to not record the conviction.
