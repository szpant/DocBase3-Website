New Business - Document Validity Checklist (428)

The documents listed below are possibly required, customers may be entitled to exemptions and concessions please refer to the relevant document for the entitlements, rules and requirements.
Safety Certificate
Safety Certificate
    Check the Safety Certificate
    Check the Safety Certificate
      • Is the correct type
      • Is current
      • Is fully completed
      • Is the original certificate
      • Contains NO alterations
      • Is signed

    Unacceptable Safety Certificate
    Unacceptable Safety Certificate
      • Is incorrect type for vehicle
      • Is not current
      • Is not fully complete
      • Is not the original certificate
      • Contains alterations
      • Is not signed


    Never alter or amend a Safety Certificate.

    At New Business the original Safety Certificate must be returned to the customer, unless discrepancies exist.

    Refer: COI (unless exempt) > SC and COI Discrepancies (below)

    At Transfer the original Safety Certificate must be retained by TMR.

Check Proof of Origin

Check CTP
Check CTP
    Determining ITCE
    Determining ITCE
      The first requirement is to check the ITCE question on the TMR application form. This determines the ITCE as yes or no.

      If the question is not on the application form or not answered, the operator should check with the customer if present, otherwise check the ITCE question on the CTP certificate if supplied.

      If the question is not on the CTP certificate or not answered, the operator should determine if the premium paid on the certificate is for ITCE yes or no from the laminated CTP rate charts or in DocBase.

    CTP Certificate
    CTP Certificate
      • Have original certificate
      • Was not issued more than 30 days ago
      • Current for same period as registration application
      • All alterations are initialled

    Nomination Notice
    Nomination Notice
      • Is fully completed
      • Signed by at least one (1) applicant
      • Correct class selected
      • All alterations are initialled

    Refer: 04. Registration > CTP
