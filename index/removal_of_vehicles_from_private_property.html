Tow Truck Reform - Removal of vehicles from private property (7301)


Project NameRemoval of vehicles from private property
When16 April 2018
BackgroundAn investigation into the practice of removing vehicles from private property was conducted and made 22 recommendations which were accepted by the Queensland Government. Changes to the Tow Truck Act 1973 and Tow Truck Regulation 2009 adopt the recommendations, requiring the removal of vehicles from private property to be conducted by an accredited tow truck driver and assistants using licensed tow trucks.
Who will be impacted and how?Impacts will include changes to existing policy and procedures relating to tow truck licence, tow truck driver and assistant accreditation. System changes include TICA/TRAILS, Compliance Intelligence Database and SILAS. Staff at Customer Service Centres and their supporting areas (ie Help Direct), Transport Inspectors, Call Centre and Tow Truck Hotline, will need to be aware of the changes.
Further informationUpdates are being made to the TMR website, letters are being sent to stakeholders, registered operators of vehicles recorded with a body shape of tow truck, existing tow truck licence holders and information provided with tow truck licence and driver and assistant accreditation renewals.
