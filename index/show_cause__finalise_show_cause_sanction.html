Show Cause/ Finalise Show Cause Sanction (662)

Show Cause
Show Cause
    Applies to
    Applies to
      Performing a Show Cause on a Qld licence holder as a result of advice/documentation received that grounds may exist to:
      • Amend, suspend or cancel a:
        • Qld driver licence
        • HPV or LNDR exemption
        • Alcohol Ignition Interlock Program exemption
      • Extend an interlock driver's prescribed period.
    1
    		Advice Received
    2
    Decide if Grounds exist for Show Cause action
    Decide if Grounds exist for Show Cause action
      • Check the grounds for show cause for the relevant process:
        Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > Licensing Show Cause
        • If satisfied that reasonable grounds for show cause action exists, commence show cause proceedings.
        • If not satisfied:
          • Take no further action
            or
          • Communicate further with the informant/requesting officer
    3
    Invitation to Show Cause
    Invitation to Show Cause
      If you believe grounds for show cause exist, you may issue the licensee a notice stating the action to be taken and the reason for the action

      For a copy of the Notices Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > Show Cause > Driver Licence Show Cause > Show Cause Notices
    4
    Record Text Restriction (TRAILS) (TICA)
    Record Text Restriction (TRAILS) (TICA)
      Record a text message on TRAILS stating:
      • The date the Show Cause Notice was sent
      • The proposed action
      • Reason for proposed action
      • Your name, office & contact details
      • Details of documents required for licence to be re-issued
      (TICA) Add (use text type option)
    5
    Licensee Response to Invitation to Show Cause Notice
    Licensee Response to Invitation to Show Cause Notice
      Licensee surrenders Driver Licence
      Licensee surrenders Driver Licence
        • If licence has been immediately suspended on medical grounds, remove the suspension before recording the surrender:
          Finalise Show Cause Sanction
        • Record Surrender
          Surrender Driver Licence
        • Update Text to include licence surrendered & date
          (TICA) Edit (use text type option)
        • File all documentation. End of process.

      Licensee requests an extension
      Licensee requests an extension
        If the licensee requests an extension of time or the Chief Executive requires an extension to be made (eg. licensee applied for reconsideration of immediate suspend or amend action) to the show cause hearing date:
        • Chief Executive decides whether to grant the extension
        • Written notification must be sent to advise the licensee of the decision

        For a copy of the Notices Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > Show Cause > Driver Licence Show Cause > Show Cause Notices

      Does not respond to the Notice
      Does not respond to the Notice
        If the licensee did not respond to the Show Cause Notice:
        • Take the proposed action from the date specified in the Notice
        • Send written notification to the licence holder advising them of the final outcome

        For a copy of the Notices Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > Show Cause > Driver Licence Show Cause > Show Cause Notices
    6
    Show Cause Hearing
    Show Cause Hearing
      At the hearing the Chief Executive considers personal or written representations made by the driver licence holder
    7
    Chief Executive's Decision
    Chief Executive's Decision
      After considering representations the Chief Executive must decide whether or not to take the proposed action stated in the Invitation to show cause notice, or to take a different action.
    8
    Notice to Licensee - decision of Show Cause Hearing
    Notice to Licensee - decision of Show Cause Hearing
      The Chief Executive sends written notification to the holder advising them of the outcome of the Show Cause Hearing.

      For a copy of the Notices Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > Show Cause > Driver Licence Show Cause > Show Cause Notices
      • Update the text restriction
      (TICA) Edit (use text type option)
    9
    		Add Show Cause Sanction
    Note: HPV/LNDR exemption show cause outcome (processed by CPU):
    • Update the HPV/LNDR exemption certificate screen in TICA per below:
      • Amend the expiry date of the exemption on TICA to the current date
      • Update the restriction text with "Application expired due to show cause outcome"
    10
    		File Documents
    11
    Further Assistance
    Further Assistance
      For further assistance with the Show Cause process, please contact Help Direct.
Finalise Show Cause Sanction
Finalise Show Cause Sanction
    Applies to
    Applies to
      • Removing a Show Cause sanction to enable a driver licence to be issued
      • Removing an immediate suspension on medical grounds, that was processed using the Show Cause action in TICA
    Before you Start
    Before you Start
      • Ensure the show cause sanction period has ended
      or
      • Ensure the person has provided the relevant documentation stating they meet the requirements to be reissued with a Qld driver licence
      or
      • Ensure the person is eligible for the removal of the immediate medical suspension
    1
    		Finalise Show Cause Sanction
    2
    		File Documents
