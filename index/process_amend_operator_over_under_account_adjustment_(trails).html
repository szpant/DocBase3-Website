Amend Operator Over/Under Account (TRAILS) (5031)

1.Fastpath to ADJU in the Command Line press ENTER
Flows to Account Adjustment screen
2.'C’ for Create
3.TAB to ‘Type?’ field and insert OPAMEND
4.TAB to ‘Control Total’ and enter the cash amount
5.TAB to ‘SEL’ field and ‘S’ to select the line
6.Insert the Operator’s OPUNDER Account Number (this appears on the ‘List of Operator Account Balances’ report)
7.TAB to ‘DR / CR’ field and insert the CR (credit)
8.In the ‘Amount’ field, enter the Cash Amount (the amount should be the same as the control total) press ENTER
9.TAB to the next line
10.TAB to ‘SEL’ field and ‘S’ to select the line
11.Insert the Operator’s OPOVER’s Account Number (this appears on the ‘List of Operator Account Balances’ report)
12.TAB to ‘DR / CR’ field and insert the DR (debit)
13.In the ‘Amount’ field, enter the Cash Amount (the amount should be the same as the control total ensuring DR must equal CR) press ENTER
Flows to Text Editor screen
14.TAB to the text line field
15.Insert the Text shown below:

Operators User ID had an operator under and an operator over of (for example, $10.50) refer to adjustment numbers (for example, 15200000 and 64520000). This adjustment is to offset the operator accounts.

Note:The adjustment numbers you will need to quote are the adjustments that created the under or over in the operator accounts at the time of banking
16.Go HOME and enter ‘U’ for Update press ENTER
Message displays ‘Update Successful’
17.F12
Flows to Account Adjustment screen
Message displays Create Successful
