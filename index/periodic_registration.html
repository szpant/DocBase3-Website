Periodic Registration (3019)


What is it?Periodic Registration is an alternative registration option for industry group members who cannot operate for periods of time due to road closures resulting from extended wet weather conditions.
Features and Benefits
Features and Benefits
    • Allows for the registration to become dormant during inactive times and re-active when conditions are better
    • Registration is cancelled when dormant and re-registered when re-activated.

    The benefits allow:
    • Greater flexibility to choose a period of registration to suit operators needs
    • Ability to suspend and reactivate registration to coincide with road closures

    Policy Rationale
    Policy Rationale
      TMR was approached by the Livestock Transporters Association of Queensland (LTAQ) to obtain "seasonal registration" for their members. LTAQ members transport livestock and are grounded for weeks or months each year due to wet weather causing road closures in their area of operation.

      These vehicles did not meet seasonal registration guidelines as they were used to transport livestock on behalf of primary producers between paddocks for grazing.

      Periodic registration was created to meet the specific needs of these operators and has since expanded to other industry groups.
Requirements
  • Application can be made by Industry Groups only
  • Registered operators must be accredited with alternative compliance
  • Registered operator must have an ALTC qual

Alternative Compliance Accreditation
Alternative Compliance Accreditation
    • Vehicles must be recorded with alternate compliance under the National Heavy Vehicle Accreditation Scheme (NHVAS), Maintenance Management Module. Approved vehicles are recorded in TICA as having 'Road Use approval' for 'ALT Compliance NHVAS Maintenance'.
    • Removes the requirement for COI's when registration re-activated.

    Note:The National Heavy Vehicle Regulator is responsible for the management of the NHVAS. Information about the NHVAS can be found at www.nhvr.gov.au
Eligible Vehicles
Eligible Vehicles
    • Prime Movers and Trucks with GVM>6t
    • Trailers with ATM >4.5t
Rates
