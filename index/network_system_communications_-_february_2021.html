Vehicle Identification Unit Team Meetings and Workshops
Vehicle Identification Unit Team Meetings and Workshops

    The Vehicle Identification Unit will be unavailable to answer your phone calls between the hours of 08:30am until 09:30am every Wednesday due to Unit Staff Meetings.

    The VIU meetings are scheduled in line with CSC customer free time. All non-urgent enquiries can be emailed to viunit@tmr.qld.gov.au and a VIU staff member will respond as quickly as possible.

    The VIU are focused on improving current processes and educating DTMR staff, as a result the VIU hold a number of workshops. In the event that you contact the VIU and your call is not answered, please send an email to the above email address and a VIU team member will follow-up as quickly as possible.
