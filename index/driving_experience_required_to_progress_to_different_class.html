Driving Experience Required to Progress to Different Class (451)

The person must hold a current P/P1/P2 Type (unless otherwise indicated) or Open licence for the required period specified in the table below.

Note:Returning drivers are eligible to progress to class previously held without meeting minimum tenure. For more information on returning drivers, Refer: 1. Qld Driver licence > Returning Driver

What can make up the required period
What can make up the required period
    Can include
    • Periods that a lower class of licence was held

        Customer is applying for HR class:
        • Held C class licence for 2 years
        • Has now upgraded to MR class and has held MR for 6 months
        • Is eligible to upgrade to HR using the:
          • C Class Required period of 2 years (ie Does not need to meet the MR required period for progressing from MR to HR of 1 year as they are eligible using the lower C Class)
    • Any period that the person held a valid licence
    • Periods broken by expiry, suspension or cancellation
    • (ie. does not need to be continuous)
    • Only periods completed within 5 years before application for higher class
    • Time spent on an Interstate (inc. external territories/NZ/Foreign/Defence Force/Restricted) licence
      Non-Qld licence for tenure period
      Non-Qld licence for tenure period
        Proof is required if the person is using time spent on a non-Qld licence towards the required learner licence or experience tenure period. The details will need to be recorded in the Non-Queensland product hub in TICA whenever Interstate/Overseas Licence information is presented.

        Proof may be obtained by:
        • The customer presenting a non-Qld licence
        • The customer presenting confirmation of licence details from the relevant non-Qld licence issuing authority
        • Faxing the relevant interstate/NZ authority to obtain the licence details
    • Can include time spent serving a period of good driving behaviour (GDB)

    • Periods that the licence was suspended, cancelled or expired

Q-Ride Pre-learner Course
    From 1 October 2016 applicants for a class RE learner licence are required to complete a practical Q-Ride pre-learner course if they live within a 100km radius of a Q-Ride training area. If they live outside a 100km radius they may obtain an exemption from completing the course.

    Returning drivers are not required to complete the pre-learner course to reobtain their motorcycle licence.

    Customers who hold an interstate or foreign motorcycle learner licence are not required to complete the pre-learner course to obtain a Qld class RE learner licence.
