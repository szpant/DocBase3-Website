Waiving a Penalty Infringement Notice (PIN) (TRAILS) (7364)

Applies to
Applies to
    This procedure will outline how to waive a PIN in TRAILS.

    Note:Only STI may waive a PIN in TRAILS.
1
Fastpath MADJ
Fastpath MADJ
    • Fastpath MADJ on Command line
    • Press <Enter>

    “Maintain Adjudication Details” page will display
2
Display Customer
Display Customer
    • <Tab> to [Name]
    • Enter in Surname, First Name
    • Press <Tab>
    • Enter in Birthdate in [Dt of Birth]
    • Type M or F (Male/Female)
    • Press <Enter>
    • Press F8 or F7 (Page Up/Page Down) to navigate list (if required)
    • <Tab> to and Type S in [Sel] beside customer
    • Press <Home>
    • Type M
    • Press <Enter>

    Search Tips
    Search Tips
      • If there is too many references listed Type N in [Phonetic (Y/N)]
      • Type in customers [Suburb/Town] if multiple same names are listed (e.g. smith)
      • If certain details are not known, leave field blank
3
Display Infringement
Display Infringement
    • Press Home "D"
    • Press <Enter>

    “List Infringements for Customer” page will display
    • Select Infringement to be waived
    • Press <Enter>

    Infringement will be populated on “Maintain Adjudication Details” page
4
Waive Infringement
Waive Infringement
    • Enter WAIV in the Decision field
    • Press F4 in the Reason..? field to select reason
    • Enter details for Authorised by
    • Press Home "U" to update
