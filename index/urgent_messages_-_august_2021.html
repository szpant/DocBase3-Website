20/08/2021 - TIPPS (Transport Information, Policy and Procedures System) Launches Monday 23 August 2021
20/08/2021 - TIPPS (Transport Information, Policy and Procedures System) Launches Monday 23 August 2021

    TIPPS (Transport Information, Policy and Procedures System), our new knowledge base for customer service delivery, launches Monday, 23 August 2021.

    TIPPS has a better search capability, enhanced functionality (such as adding articles to favourites and subscribing to them), and improved support.

    TIPPS can be accessed by visiting https://knowledgebase.tmr.qld.gov.au. Alternatively, you can find TIPPS on our intranet under 'Systems & Resources > Business tools and systems > TIPPS'.

    For those who still have not yet completed training, you should complete the online training course/s available through Accelerate (search 'TIPPS'). Once you've completed the online course, you can familiarise yourself with TIPPS in the practice environment.

    Important note: There will be test data in the TIPPS training environment​, and you shouldn't use this information in your role.​

    DocBase and PTDoc will remain available to use until close of business, Friday, 20 August 2021. From this date onwards, you will only be able to access TIPPS.

    Please also note that TIPPS is best viewed using either Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge browsers. Internet Explorer is not a supported browser.

    All support requests, including access requests, enhancements and incidents, should be requested/reported through Service Centre Online.

    All data contained with DocBase and PTDoc will be migrated to TIPPS. You will still find the same information you are used to, but it will be easier to find and presented in a more user-friendly manner.

    For more information, check out the SKDB intranet site or email the project team.


    Kaleb Ball
    Change Manager
    SDKB Project

16/08/2021 - HPT Tenure Requirements - 16 August 2021
16/08/2021 - HPT Tenure Requirements - 16 August 2021

    TICA has been updated to recognise overseas Provisional or Open licences when calculating tenure requirements for the Hazard Perception Test.

    If a customer has been issued a Queensland Learner Licence and has a Provisional or Open overseas licence recorded in their non-Queensland product screen, they will meet tenure requirements, a Special Need Tenure Exemption is no longer required to be recorded.

    TIPPS will be updated to reflect this after the freeze is completed.

    Peta Berry
    A/Principal Advisor (System Support)
    Service Innovation and Systems


13/08/2021 - Takata Airbag Recall – Cancellation of Registrations - Friday 13 August 2021
13/08/2021 - Takata Airbag Recall – Cancellation of Registrations - Friday 13 August 2021

    As part of the Takata Airbag Recall process, TMR has now commenced action to cancel vehicle registrations for those customers who have not complied with requests to have the faulty airbags replaced.

    The cancellation of these registrations commenced on Monday 10 August 2021. In addition to cancelling the registration, a RCAL restriction is being recorded against each of the relevant vehicles which prevents re-registration.

    These vehicles cannot be re-registered without evidence that the airbag has been replaced. Standard processes for removing the RCAL restriction will apply.

    For any customer enquiries regarding these cancellations, please advise customers to email: qldtakatarecall@tmr.qld.gov.au

    Jessica Crawford
    A/Principal Advisor
    Capability, Engagement and Learning

11/08/2021 - Issues with PrepL/HPT payments made at CSC - Resolved - Wednesday 11 August 2021
11/08/2021 - Issues with PrepL/HPT payments made at CSC - Resolved - Wednesday 11 August 2021

    Please be advised that there was an issue identified with PrepL/HPT payments made at a CSC. Payments were being accepted through the payment screen in TICA but were not correctly applied to the product. This resulted in the course remaining in a pending status and customers unable to complete their HPT/PrepL.

    This is has now been rectified and all affected customers have been contacted. However, can all CSC operators please monitor these types of transactions for the rest of today.

    If a customer presents to pay for their HPT/PrepL at your counter please accept payment and then check the Course Enrolments to check that the current status is not “Pending.” Course Enrolments are accessed through the Driver Licence Hub > Links > Manage Course Enrolments.

    If an issue is identified please contact Online Support Services for further instructions.

    Peta Berry
    A/Principal Advisor (System Support)
    Service Innovation and Systems

19/08/2021 - QCIS and iPad Operating System - Update
19/08/2021 - QCIS and iPad Operating System - Update

    Please update your iPad operating system to iOS14.7.1 and QCIS version 2.1.5.

    To check the iOS version:
    • Go to Settings > General > Software Update



    To update:
    • Plug your device into power and connect to the Internet with Wi-Fi.
    • Tap Download and Install.

    Once the version has been updated please ensure the Automatic Update feature for your iPad is turned off. Note: The Automatic Update feature must be turned off so that iOS15 isn’t installed on the device before it can be properly tested.



    To check QCIS version:
    • Open QCIS > Go to Settings > Version

    The app should update automatically, but if not, please download it from the TMR app catalogue.

    Errors:
    If you are experiencing issues with QCIS, if possible take a screen shot of the issue/error message and forward it with the Issue Template for QCIS Users to Compliance_Support email address.

    Peta Berry
    A/Principal Advisor
    Service Innovation and Systems
