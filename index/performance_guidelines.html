Performance Guidelines (3113)

Form 4
Form 4
    NHVR Notice:Applies to:
    Form 4 (Guideline for Excess Dimension - Vehicles Carrying Indivisible Articles, Special Purpose Vehicles, Vehicles that require a Pilot or Escort in Queensland) has been transitioned to the NHVR. There are four Notices that cover all the vehicles and combinations that were covered under Form 4. See NHVR Notices below.
    Note:
    • Restricted travel time/day information can be accessed via the Qld Government Publications portal - Queensland Access Conditions Guide; and
    • Where pilot and/or escort vehicles are required for operation under a NHVR Notice or Permit, pilot and/or escort vehicle operators and drivers should refer to the Guideline for Pilot and Escort Operations in Queensland on the Qld Government Publications portal.
    Multi-State Class 1 Load Carrying Vehicle Dimension Exemption Notice 2016 (Amendement Notice 2019 includes the Qld Schedule).Class 1 load carrying vehicle comprised of:
    a) a truck; or
    b) a truck and trailer combination; or
    c) a prime mover towing—
          • a low loader; or
          • a low loader dolly and a low loader; or
          • a house removal trailer; or
          • a load platform; or
          • a low loader dolly and a platform; or
          • a semitrailer; or
          • a jinker trailer; or
          • a low loader dolly and a jinker trailer
    National Class 1 Special Purpose Vehicle NoticeSPVs to 40 tonnes operating in Queensland excluding all terrain mobile cranes with 3 or more axles.
    Queensland Class 1 Special Purpose Combination Notice 2019Special Purpose Combination up to 40 tonnes - (prime mover towing a special purpose trailer).
    Queensland Class 1 Heavy Vehicle (Indivisible Item) Dimension Exemption Notice 2019B-double and road train combinations carrying indivisible items.
