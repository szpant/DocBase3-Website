Dog Trailer
Dog Trailer
    MRC - Mass Rate for Charging
    Administration fee - None required

    To Determine Fees
    To Determine Fees
      For the purpose of determining fees the following rules apply:
      • 2 axles less than 1 metre apart are taken as 1 axle
      • 3 axles 2 metres or less apart are taken as 2 axles
      • 4 axles 3.2 metres or less apart are taken as 3 axles

      Note:The registration label will display the number of axle groups for a dog trailer for example:
      • TD11 is one axle at the front and one axle at the back ie TD2 is the charge code
      • TD23 is two axles at the front and 3 axles at the back ie TD5 is the charge code
