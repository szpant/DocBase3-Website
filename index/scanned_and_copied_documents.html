Scanned and Copied Documents (7282)


Copied Documents
What it isTMR, TMR Agents and Rego Easy Dealers may accept and provide copied documents, that is, scanned, pdf, faxed or photocopied documents in certain circumstances.
Customers may provide copied documents in certain circumstances.
Accepting copied documentsTMR, TMR Agents and Rego Easy Dealers may accept and provide copied documents, that is, scanned, pdf, faxed or photocopied documents in the following circumstances:
  • policy specifically states that a copy or certified copy of a document is acceptable; or
  • policy specifically states the document can be provided electronically, that is by email or facsimile; or
  • the relevant TMR form states it may be given to TMR by email or facsimile; or
  • there is no EOI requirement for the action; or
  • the application is a copy (e.g. it has been pre-filled or scanned and then printed) but the signature is original.

Copied documents may also be used:
  • to resolve errors in accordance with the Filing and BE error correction procedures.
  • when a customer has requested TMR to provide a document to them electronically and;
    • their identity has been established, and
    • transaction requirements have been met.
When a copied document is deemed acceptable it may be provided by email, fax, mail or in person.
Rules for copied documents
  • If the transaction requires supporting documentation, policy must state that copied documents can be accepted.
  • The integrity of the information must be maintained, that is, the copy must be complete and of good quality.
  • Copied forms must be correctly completed.
