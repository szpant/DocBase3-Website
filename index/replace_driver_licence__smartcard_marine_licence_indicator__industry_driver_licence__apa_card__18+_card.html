Applies to
    Replacing a:
    • Driver Licence
    • Marine Licence Indicator card including:
      • issuing a Marine Licence Indicator card when the driver licence is cancelled or transferred
    • Photo Identification (Photo ID) Card including:
      • Removal of gender displayed on a Photo ID card
    • 18+ Card

      Note: Replacing a Card 18+

      If the holder of a Card 18+ attends an office they must be:
      • Advised that the Card 18+ can not be replaced,
      • Given the opportunity to apply for a Photo ID card,
      • Advised to complete the Photo Identification Card Application/Replacement form,
      • Advised that full Photo ID card requirements will need to be met.

      Refer to: Photo Identification Card Guide
    Industry Authority
    Industry Authority

      • Dangerous Goods Driver
      • Tow Truck Driver/Assistant Driver/Rider Trainer

    Capturing facial image
    Capturing facial image
      If TICA prompts to capture the customer's facial image, direct the customer to the seat for the default camera and request them to:

      • Sit on the seat provided and to stay within the markings

        Note: If customer uses a mobility aid, retract or remove the chair and have the customer position themselves within the designated markings of the photo area
      • Look straight ahead at the camera (a frontal image of their face is required)
      • Remove any head attire or sunglasses (unless there are valid religious, cultural or medical reasons for not doing so, then refer to Manager)
      The customer must remain in your line of vision during the entire transaction.

      Once the facial image is successfully captured, request the customer to return to your counter.
    1:1 image match fails
    1:1 image match fails
      If the 1:1 image match fails contact the Identity Management Unit for an assessment of the image

      If IMU advises that the image match is unacceptable:
      • IMU will advise what action is to be taken
      Refer to:
      Smartcard Facial Image Recognition

      End of procedure

      If IMU advises that the image match is acceptable:
      • Continue with procedure

    Verify Receipt/Interim Authority/Confirmation Slip details
    Verify Receipt/Interim Authority/Confirmation Slip details
      • If driver licence replacement is a formless transaction the operator must:
        • ask the customer to verbally confirm that the information they have supplied is true and correct before signing the confirmation slip. Key information should be highlighted such as:
          • contact details
          • licence type and class
          • effective and expiry date
          • transaction details
        • take a copy of the driver licence receipt and retain with the licence filing
      • The customer must confirm that the Receipt/Interim Authority/Confirmation Slip details are correct and sign in the space provided
      • If any corrections are required, amend the relevant information and reprint the relevant Receipt/Interim Authority/Confirmation Slip
      • Obtain and destroy incorrect receipt/confirmation slip

Give/Advise customer
Give/Advise customer
    Give customer
    • Customer portion of Medical Certificate (where applicable)
    • Their original EOI/residency documents
    • Receipt/Interim Authority/Confirmation Slip
    • Financial Receipt (if applicable)

    Advise customer
    Driver Licence
    Driver Licence
      • That the Driver Licence Receipt authorises them to drive until the smartcard is received.
      • The Driver Licence will be posted by standard mail in an unmarked envelope to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address.

      If Licence has an LNDR Restriction
      If Licence has an LNDR Restriction
        If the licence is subject to a late night driving restriction inform the holder of the applicable driving restrictions. Refer: Driver Licence > 3. Enforcement > Late Night Driving Restriction
    Photo Identification Card
    Photo Identification Card
      • That their application for a Photo ID Card has been lodged pending approval
      • If approved the Photo ID Card will be posted by standard mail in an unmarked envelope to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address.
    Marine Licence
    Marine Licence
      • That the Marine Licence Indicator Receipt is confirmation that they have been granted a Recreational Marine Driver Licence or Personal Watercraft Licence until the smartcard is received.
      • The smartcard will be posted by standard mail in an unmarked envelope to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address.
    Industry Authority
    Industry Authority
      • That the Interim Industry Authority authorises them to operate until the smartcard is received.
      • The smartcard will be posted by standard mail in an unmarked envelope to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address.
