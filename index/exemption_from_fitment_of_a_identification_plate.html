Exemption from Fitting a Compliance Plate (3050)

What is itAn Exemption Permit issued under To(RUM - Vehicle Standards and Safety) Regulation 2010 (for light vehicles) or the Heavy Vehicle National Law (for heavy vehicles) can exempt a vehicle from having a compliance plate fitted
Vehicles
Requiring an Exemption Permit
  • Rally Vehicles (part of a Rally Permit)
  • Individually Constructed Vehicles
  • Test and Evaluation vehicles
  • Other special case vehicles as determined by the TMR Vehicle Standards Unit (for light vehicles) or NHVR (for heavy vehicles).
Requirements
Heavy Vehicles
Heavy Vehicles
    For information about how to apply for a identification plate exemption permit for a heavy vehicle, the registered operator should contact the NHVR on 1300 MY NHVR (696 487) or www.nhvr.gov.au

Light Vehicles
Light Vehicles
    Individually Constructed Vehicles (ICVs)
    • ICV exemption permit
    • If the customer does not have an ICV permit Refer: Specific Modification Requirements

    Test and Evaluation vehicle requirements may include:
    • F5050 form should be filled in and emailed to Vehicle Standards
    • Original Authority to Import issued by the Department of Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development and Communications (DITRDC).

    Rally Vehicles
    Rally Vehicles
      • A copy of a confederation of Australian Motor Sport Queensland Rally vehicle registration scheme Authority to Operate.
      • CAMS Open National Class Rally Licence or Higher

    Where to Apply
    Application should be made to Vehicle Standards
