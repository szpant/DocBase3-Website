Process Industry Authority application amendment (73)

Applies to
Applies to
    Amending a lodged application for a:
    • Tow Truck Driver/Assistant
    • Dangerous Goods Driver
    • Pilot/Escort Vehicle Driver
    • Driver/Rider Trainer
    • Traffic Controller
1
Determine Industry Authority Application Status
Determine Industry Authority Application Status
    • Check TICA to determine the current status of the Industry Authority application
    • The application must be in lodged status to enable amendment

    If the application is in a status other than lodged...
    • If application is finalised (approved, refused, lapsed or withdrawn) it cannot be amended.
    • Advise customer of situation if the amendment cannot continue

      End of procedure
2
Check Transaction Requirements & Eligibility
Check Transaction Requirements & Eligibility
    Dangerous Goods Driver Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Driver Rider Trainer Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Pilot Escort Driver Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Tow Truck Driver/Transaction Requirements Checklist
    Traffic Controller Transaction Requirements Checklist
      If eligibility or requirements are not met, refer below to Refuse to Consider Application
3
Check documents and EOI
Check documents and EOI
    Item
    Details
    Application Form
    • Retrieve and check the original application form and documentation
    • Ask the customer to fill out another application form (if any details are different from the original application form)
    • Check that the the new application form has been completed correctly
    • Ask the customer to sign & date the new application form in your presence
      • If the application form is not completed, signed or dated, either:
        Have the customer complete it, otherwise refer below to Refuse to Consider Application.
    • Complete Office Use Section of the new application form
    • Date stamp the new application form 11

      Note: Do not stamp or mark any original EOI/qualification/competency documents.
    • Check that the customer has supplied their contact details (Telephone/Fax and/or Email)
    • Attach the new application to the original application
    EOI Documents
    • Compare signatures on EOI and the new application form
    • Check EOI document authenticity (if required).

    Refer: Evidence of Identity > Recording and Verifying Documents > Manually Verifying Authenticity of documents
    Competency documents
    • Check documents meet requirements
      Note: The original competency documents supplied with the original application may still be valid, check the policy guide to determine if the document/s is still valid.
    • Photocopy or scan (if required)
    Medical
    • Check Medical Certificate (if required)

      Note: The original medical supplied with the original application may still be valid, check the policy guide to determine if the medical is still valid.
4
Refuse to Consider Application (if required)
Refuse to Consider Application (if required)
    As this is a new application, you can refuse this application and the original can stay the same if the customer fails to meet the new requirements. Prepare Refuse to Consider notice (if required).

    Refer to: Product policy guide > Transactions Details > Refuse to Consider > Written Notices

    Give customer
    • EOI / other documents presented
    • Application form (Keep the application form if a 1:1 image match is performed)
    • Notice requesting further documents or information relevant to the application (if applicable) refer above
    Advise customer
    • That the application cannot be considered at this stage
    • The reason the application cannot be considered
    • The application may be considered when the required eligibility is met, or requested documents provided

    File Documents (if applicable)

    End of procedure
5
Record EOI Documents
Record EOI Documents
    Record/Update/Exempt/Invalidate EOI, Name Change, Residency Doc or Exemption

    Note:All newly presented documents must be recorded, even if in excess of minimum EOI requirements.
6
Record the Amended Industry Authority Application
Record the Amended Industry Authority Application
    • Update the amended details (TICA)
    • Check TICA, relevant policy guides and the Transaction checklist to identify outstanding requirements Check Outstanding Business Requirements (TICA)
7
If the amendment is refused
If the amendment is refused
    • Prepare refusal letter and send to applicant
      Note:Refer to Individual Policy documents (ie: Dangerous Goods Licence Guide etc) for refusal letters and guidelines
      Additional actions required for Dangerous Goods Licences
      Additional actions required for Dangerous Goods Licences
        • Include a copy of 'Reviews and Appeals Information Bulletin'
        • Record text message on TICA outlining details
        • Notify DGU (Dangerous Goods Unit) by email when refusal processed(address:dgu@tmr.qld.gov.au) advising:
          • CRN
          • Applicant's full name
          • Applicant's date of birth
          • Refusal date & eligible reapply date

    • Refund fees in customers account
    • Include with filing documentation:
      • A copy of the refusal letter in the filing documentation;
      • Relevant documentation that supported the decision to refuse (e.g. Traffic printout and/or Criminal history documents).
    • Retain filing documentation for 28 days to allow time for the appeal process to expire
    • File as per office procedure
    • Once the review period has been exhausted, forward a copy of the refusal letter and the application for filing to Iron Mountain.

      Continue with step 10
8
If the amendment is accepted
If the amendment is accepted
    Continue to process the application using the amended application.
    > Process New Issue IA application > Begin at Approve/refuse process
    > Process Add/Upgrade IA application > Begin at Conduct Searches
    Note:You are not required to complete steps that have been covered in this procedure.
9
Give/advise customer
Give/advise customer
    Give customer
    • Their original EOI/competency documents (if applicable)
    • Customer portion of Medical Certificate (if applicable)
    Advise customer
    • If additional searches are required, advise the customer that:
      • A criminal history and/or interstate traffic history search is required and their application cannot be finalised until the search results are received.
    • They may be contacted to come back into the CSC, otherwise;
      • the smartcard will be mailed to their postal address, or if none recorded, their residential address.
10
Continue to process application using the original application
Continue to process application using the original application
    Continue to process the application using the original application.
    > Process New Issue IA application > Begin at Approve/refuse process
    > Process Add/Upgrade IA application > Begin at Conduct Searches
    Note:You are not required to complete steps that have been covered in this procedure.
