#
If Declared an adhoc criminal history request must be made
*
Must be presented if one exists
$
Not required at time of lodgment. It must however, be provided within 3 months of lodgment of the application
^
Only required if conditions are added or removed during the currency of the TCAS
##
If applicant is multiple IA holder and want to voluntarily surrender their TCA but retain other industry authority/ies
+
Has held an "open or provisional" Australian or foreign drivers licence in the last 5 years prior to this application
~
Refer Smartcard > 1. Smartcard > The Smartcard Guide > Replacement Smartcards to ascertain if a fee is required
%
If the applicant answers "Yes" to questions in section 9.1 or 9.2 on form F4115, they must have question 10 completed by a doctor or optometrist.
If the applicant answers "Yes" to question 9.3,9.4 or 9.5 then question 10 must be completed by a doctor.
