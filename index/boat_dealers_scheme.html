Boat Dealers Scheme (319)


What it isThis scheme is similar to the Approved Dealers Scheme for vehicles.

It allows CSC’s to keep track of authorised signatories for dealerships.

The named people will be allowed to sign the Applications for Registration.
RequirementsCSC’s must maintain a file for each Dealer.

Marine dealers (New Ships) are not issued with a licence. It is the Office Manager's responsibility to ensure the validity of the application.

    TransactionsAll transactions (including acquisitions) must be processed in sequence.

    Dealer Acquisitions
    Dealer Acquisitions
      • An acquisition is when a dealer acquires a vessel from another dealership, an auction or as a trade in vessel.
      • Dealers can only acquire a vessel through TMR if it is currently registered in Queensland.
      • A cancelled vessel or interstate registered vessel cannot be acquired by a dealer through TMR; a new business application is required.
      • Dealers must lodge their dealer acquisition form within 14 days of acquiring the vessel and before disposal of the vessel to another dealer/auction house or private sale.
      • A vessel can only be transferred out of a dealer’s name once a check has been completed to ensure that the dealer has acquired the vehicle. If the acquisition has not been processed, the dealer must be contacted and the acquisition processed before the transfer out can proceed.
      • If an acquisition cannot be processed, evidence must be obtained from the dealer showing the disposer and the transfer processed using the different seller function. Once the transfer has been processed, the dealer's processing CSC must be notified so they can deal with the outstanding matter.
    Registration Cancellation
    Registration Cancellation
      • When processing the cancellation, operators must ensure that the vessel is registered to the correct dealer. If not, an acquisition may have been processed out of sequence. Some transactions may require previous transactions to be invalidated to allow the cancellation to be processed on the correct date.
      • Once a cancellation has been processed, any monies can be refunded to the dealer.
    New Business
    New Business
      New business transactions, where the vessel is being registered to a customer who has purchased the vessel must meet all standard requirements.

      When selling a new vessel if the dealer would like to provide the new owner with registration at the time of delivery, the dealer is required to attend to this registration prior to the delivery date for the vessel.

      The dealer and customer can choose one of the following options to register the vessel:
      • Dealer registers as agent for the owner,
      • Owner registers the vessel themselves,
      or
      • Dealer registers for transfer to the customer.

      Please refer to the fact sheet for further information:
      Boat Dealers - Registration options with boat sales


      Note: If the customer does not have a CRN, the dealer must supply a New Customer Application (Individual/Organisation) form (F3503), along with a copy of the customer’s EOI.
    Transfer
    Transfer
      All dealer transfers (from the dealer to an individual/organisation) are processed the same as standard registration transfers. Dealer transfers, however, are processed under the agent reference (dealer qual) on the date that the transfer is processed (the transfer date must not be changed).
    Renew
    Renew
      • All renewals for dealers are processed the same as standard registration renewals.
      • The vessel should be registered in the dealers name, unless it has been sold and renewal of the registration was agreed as part of the sale.
      • Where a dealer does not have a renewal notice for the vessel and it is not in their name (i.e. they are renewing for a customer as an agreed part of the sale), they must follow the same process as Renew Registration - In Person, not registered operator and without renewal notice
      • If the request is made via fax or email, the written request must state that they have permission from the registered operator to renew the registration.
