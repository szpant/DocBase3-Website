    Restricted LGAs
    Restricted LGAs
      To align with the Queensland Health Directive, from Monday 9 August 2021, TMR CSC and QGAP team members in a lockdown area (Refer: Restrictions for Locked Down Areas – South East Queensland | Health and wellbeing | Queensland Government (www.qld.gov.au), will have the authority to refuse service to a customer for a transaction that is not essential.

      Essential services are defined as transactions which cannot be completed via another channel (such as online or phone) and meet at least ONE of the following conditions:
      • are required within the next week;
      • will have financial or legal implications if not completed;
      • are required to support ongoing employment.

      Customer Service staff can consider the circumstances of each customer and apply discretion to serve a customer who doesn’t necessarily meet the essential service requirements.
