Booking a COI Inspection (3040)

Where to Book
Where to Book
    Customers may book an inspection by either:
    Using the courtesy phone located in a CSCCSC:
    • Pays money into customers account prior to the call being made
    • Issues customer with a receipt
    Off Line Agency
    • Off-Line Agent issues customer with a receipt
    • Customer faxes the receipt to SBS on 07 3839 0673 then contacts SBS
    • Customer contact SBS to make a booking
    Phoning the SBS on 132 390 Acceptable forms of payment include:
    Prior to making booking
      • Funds in customer account
      • Cheque/Mail order sent by mail
    After making booking
      • Pay online with a credit card (excluding AMEX), time limits apply.
        Refer:         Paying for Booking > Insufficient funds available in account> Pay by credit card below
      • SBS can use customers credit limit
      • Pay to customer account at CSC/Agent. As time limits apply once paid the CSO is to immediately email HelpSBS (email template) with details of the customer and payment. Refer below: Paying for Booking > Insufficient funds available in account > Other option
    Accessing the internet site at https://www.tmr.qld.gov.au/
    • Credit card is the only acceptable payment method. A credit card surcharge will apply.
    Online via the My Account portal
    • Credit card is the only acceptable payment method. A credit card surcharge will apply.
Bulk BookingsTo be eligible to make bulk inspection bookings through selected Vehicle Inspection Centres the customer must:
  • Be created in TRAILS as an involved party
  • Have a Vehicle Inspection Booking Agent Qual (VIBA)

Booking ReminderIf a customer is signed up to receive e-reminders, a reminder will be emailed to them 5 days prior to the inspection booking date. The reminder will include the following details:
  • Customer name
  • Registration number
  • Vehicle inspection booking date
  • Link to reschedule a vehicle inspection online service
  • Link to swap 2 vehicle inspections online service
Changing a BookingCustomer must:
  • Notify TMR 2 business days prior to the booked inspection
    and
  • Supply:
    • The booking reference number
      OR
    • Meet standard evidence of identification requirements (Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Standard Evidence of Identity > Step 2 - Check Standard EOI Requirements > By Phone)

Failure to notify 2 business days prior will result in the loss of the inspection fees paid.
