Deceased
Updating a CRN as deceased
    Manual Notification
    Fact of Death Notification


    Note:Where the next of kin is present, the deceased customer's CRN should be checked for other TMR products that have been issued, eg driver licence, industry authority or APA/Photo ID card. If necessary, advise the representative of the deceased that these products should be surrendered to TMR.

    Once a customer is updated as deceased (either manually or automatically):
    • A NORG and NOAC restriction will be automatically applied to their record to restrict registration and plate transactions. Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Customer Details > System Information > Customer Restrictions
    • A Registration Notice Individual Restriction letter (F5094) will be issued if the deceased individual has any current/expired registrations/plate products.
    • Any refund cheques will be automatically issued to the 'Estate of <deceased>'.
