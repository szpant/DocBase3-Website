About Show Cause Notices
Notices
These notices have been structured to meet the requirements of sections 126(3), 125(2) and (6) of the Transport Operations (Road Use Management - Driver Licensing) Regulation 2010.

Note:Please ensure that the instructions and any other information not required in the notice has been deleted before printing.

    4a - Show Cause Notice – Amend - No longer has a special need for a class HC licence

    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received indicating that the person no longer meets the requirements for a special need HC licence therefore the HC Learners should be removed.

    4a - Show Cause - HC Learner Licence (Amend)

    4b - Show Cause Notice – Cancel - Now holds Non-Qld driver licence

    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received that a Qld licence holder now holds a licence in another State or Territory.

    4b - Show Cause - Granted Non-Queenlsand Driver Licence (Cancel)

    4c - Show Cause Notice – Cancel - No longer residing in Qld

    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received that a Qld licence holder who now resides in another State or Territory.

    4c - Show Cause - No Longer Queensland Resident (Cancel)

    4d - Show Cause Notice – Cancel - False or Misleading information
    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received that a Qld licence holder gave false or misleading information when applying for a Qld driver licence

    4d - Show Cause - False or Misleading Information (Cancel)

    4e - Show Cause Notice - person is disqualified in another country
    The Show Cause notice is to be issued (in person or by MAIL) when i nformation has been received from an overseas authority that the Qld licence holder has been disqualified from driving in that country since the grant of the Qld licence.
    4e - Show Cause - Disqualified in Another Country (Cancel)
    Cancellation notice - person is disqualified in another Australian State or Territory
    The Cancellation notice is to be issued (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received from an interstate authority that the Qld licence holder has been disqualified from driving in that state or territory since the grant of the Qld licence.
    Cancellation notice - QLD licence holder disqualified in another Australian State or Territory
    Cancellation notice – person is disqualified in NSW and subject to the NSW Mandatory Alcohol Interlock Program (MAIP)
    The Cancellation notice is to be issues (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received from an interstate authority that the Qld licence has been disqualified from driving in NSW and is subject to the MAIP.
    Cancellation notice - QLD licence holder subject to NSW MAIP

    4f - Show Cause Notice – Contravened a condition of licence
    This notice should only be given after consultation with the Senior Manager (Transport Policy), Licensing and Identity, Road Safety and System Management.

    4f - Show Cause - Contravene Condition of Licence

    4g - Show cause notice - ineligible to renew licence by electronic communication
    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received that the licence holder was ineligible to renew their open licence by electronic communication.

    4g - Show Cause - Ineligible to Renew Under Part 7 (Cancel)

    4h - Show cause notice - ineligible for the class or type of licence
    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received that the licence holder was ineligible for the class or type of licence.

    4h - Show Cause - Not Eligible for Class or Type of Licence (Amend)

    4i - Show cause notice - Cancel - ineligible for the class or type of licence - Queensland disqualification
    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) when information has been received that the licence holder was ineligible for the class or type of licence as a result of Queensland court disqualification.

    4i - Show Cause Not Eligible for Licence - Qld Disqualification (Cancel)

Extension of Time
    If a decision to show cause will take longer than the date shown in the original show cause letter, an extension of time to show cause should be issued to the customer.

    Delegation starts at SASM or Manager levels.

    This notice is to be given (in person or by MAIL) confirming/declining a request by a licence holder for an extension of time to show cause.
