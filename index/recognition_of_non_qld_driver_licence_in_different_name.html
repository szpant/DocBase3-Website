Recognition of non Qld driver licence in different name (2578)

What it isIt may occur that a customer's non Qld driver licence is still in a previous family name, and their evidence of identity (EOI) documents are in their current name. All other personal details must be the same eg. date of birth, first and middle names.

Example
Customer applies to transfer their NZ licence to Qld:
New Zealand Driver Licence Name = Mary Jane Smith Category A & B documents = Mary Jane Jones

To enable recognition of the non Qld driver licence for transfer or driving experience, a supporting document must be shown in addition to the normal EOI requirements. This document may be used only to link the licence to the person.

If the person cannot produce one of the below listed documents the driver licence will not be recognised and the person will be treated as one who has never held a driver licence.
Supporting Documents
  • Any EOI name change document showing both family names.
    Refer:     Evidence of Identity -> List of documents that can be used for EOI -> Name Change
  • Non Australian Marriage certificate showing both family names
  • Passport showing both family names that is current or expired <2yrs
  • Photo driver licence in previous family name (if photo matches customer), valid at any time in the last 5 years
  • The non Qld driver licence details are already on TICA under the current name (eg. previous transfer)
