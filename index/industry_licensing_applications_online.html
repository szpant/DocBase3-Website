Industry Licensing Applications Online (5321)

Project NameIndustry Licensing Applications Online
WhenFriday, 26 June 2015
BackgroundFrom Friday, 26 June 2015, customers will have the option to lodge Traffic Controller Accreditation applications and Dangerous Goods Driver Accreditation applications online at www.tmr.qld.gov.au

Customers previously needed to attend a Customer Service Centre in person to lodge their application. Or alternatively customers in remote areas needed to complete applications using the Industry Authority Remote Area Kit.

Customers will be able to answer the required questions via the online application, upload the relevant documentation to support their application and pay the associated fee via the online services webpage.

The planned implementation of new and upgraded online services aligns with TMR’s strategic goals and objectives by providing innovative and secure online services and making these services easily accessible and customer friendly for all users.
Who will be impacted and how?The implementation of this project will deliver significant benefits for TMR staff. It is expected implementation of these services will include:
  • Increase in customer online transactions leading to a reduction in face to face customer transactions.
  • Provide a consistent lodgement process and increase efficiencies.
  • The online service will include built in features to ensure more accurate information is passed through to the processing area, therefore enabling faster lodgement and assessment of applications.
  • As this service is solely for the lodgement of new applications, a new mechanism for processing online payments unique to these new online services has been developed.

How this will impact customers

The implementation of this project will deliver significant benefits to customers. It is expected that the project will deliver:
  • Improved customer satisfaction by being able to lodge applications online at a time convenient to the customer.
  • Improved accessibility to TMR services on mobile devices.
  • Consistent customer experience with Queensland Government online services .
  • A user friendly interface in line with technological advances to improve customer service and deliver transport services.
  • Potentially faster processing with higher quality information being captured resulting in a faster turn-around.

To access the Industry Licensing Applications online service, customers will need to provide:
1.suitable identification (in the form of unique document identifiers) (see attached Customer Authentication Samples) or
2.customer e-Contact Details and obtain an Online Access Code (OAC)
