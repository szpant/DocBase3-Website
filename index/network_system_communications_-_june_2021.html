22/06/2021 - Doomadgee QPS - Unavailable - 21 June to 25 July 2021

    The Doomadgee QPS will be unavailable to process TMR work and restricted area permits between 21 June to 25 July 2021.

    As Doomadgee is a restricted area, customers will need to obtain unregistered vehicle permits through the website prior to taking the vehicle outside of Doomadgee for registration purposes. The surrounding QPS stations have been notified and once vehicles are registered, the customer will be required to be issued with the “removal from restricted area” letter in order to return to Doomadgee.

    Customers can access a range of Transport services online at www.tmr.qld.gov.au or by visiting the closest QPS processing station at Burketown (97kms)

29/06/2021 - Reference to DORIS - Removed
29/06/2021 - Reference to DORIS - Removed

    From 7 July 2021, due to technical issues, the archived copy of DORIS licensing records will no longer be accessible by the Help Desk for validation of TRAILS/TICA content for purposes such as assessing Returning Driver applications.

    The TRAILS/TICA database, which includes licensing records converted from the previous driver licensing system DORIS, continues to be TMR’s definitive source of laminate and smartcard driver licence histories.

    If you have any questions in relation to this advice please contact Kelly Simon on 3066 2509 or email SysSec@tmr.qld.gov.au.

Vehicle Identification Unit Team Meetings and Workshops

    The Vehicle Identification Unit will be unavailable to answer your phone calls between the hours of 08:30am until 09:30am every Wednesday due to Unit Staff Meetings.

    The VIU meetings are scheduled in line with CSC customer free time. All non-urgent enquiries can be emailed to viunit@tmr.qld.gov.au and a VIU staff member will respond as quickly as possible.

    The VIU are focused on improving current processes and educating DTMR staff, as a result the VIU hold a number of workshops. In the event that you contact the VIU and your call is not answered, please send an email to the above email address and a VIU team member will follow-up as quickly as possible.
