Exemptions from Logbook Requirements (3161)

What it isA person may apply for an exemption from meeting the logbook requirements.

Note:Delegated officers within the Indigenous Driver Licensing Unit may waive the requirements for a learner licence holder who resides within a remote indigenous community to apply for, and pay the fee associated with lodging an exemption from the learner logbook requirements.
Refer: Indigenous Driver Licensing Program
Who may ApplyA person who is ordinarily required to complete the logbook requirements

Note:This includes non-Queensland licence holders who are required to meet the learner logbook requirements.
Eligibility
Eligibility Criteria
Eligibility Criteria
    A person who is not able to meet the logbook requirements due to either of the following:
    • A car or a supervisor is not reasonably available to them
    or
    • The person lives in an area with a limited road network
    and
    • There is no significant benefit for the person to satisfy the logbook requirements by driving a car on the road network
    and
    • There is no reasonable likelihood of the person moving from the area or having an opportunity to drive a car in an area with a diverse road network
TO(RUM-DL) Reg. 2010 s34(1)&(2)
Information Sheet
Information Sheet
    An Information Sheet - Application for exemption from the logbook requirements (S4480) - is available on the TMR website for interested applicants.
Application RequirementsThe applicant must:
  • Complete an online exemption application or an Exemption from Logbook Requirements Application (F4469)*.
  • Pay the non-refundable application fee.
  • Provide a statement^ from a supporting person (e.g. immediate family member, carer or guardian) confirming the circumstances that prevent them completing the 100 hours supervised driving.

* Forms are available at CSCs or on the TMR website
^ Only required when car or supervisor is not available to applicant

Assessment of ApplicationIn deciding whether to grant the application to the person, the chief executive must:
  • Confirm eligibility requirements are met
  • Consider the person’s traffic history

Application GrantedIf the application is granted, it is valid indefinitely and a written notice is forwarded to the applicant.
Tenure Requirements if Exemption granted
Tenure Requirements if Exemption granted
    The person must hold their learner licence for a minimum cumulative period of 2 years within the 3 years prior to applying for the provisional driver licence.

    The 2 year period is overridden by presenting a completed learner logbook with 100 hours of driving experience including 10 hours of night driving to TMR. In this instance the original 1 year learner licence tenure period would still apply.
Application RefusedIf the application is refused the person will receive a written notice including:
  • The decision
  • The reasons for the decision
  • That the person may apply for reconsideration of the decision
