Photo Identification Card Release of Information (3357)


Release of Photo Identification Card Information
What it is
What it is
What it is
    Information from the following can be requested and released from the register:
    • Photo Identification (Photo ID) card details includes address or full name history (includes aliases)
    • Photo ID card history
    Note:A release of information does not include the Photo ID Card holder's digital photo and digitised signature.

    Refer: Release of Information > 5. Smartcard > Smartcard Release of Information > Release of Digital Photo and Digitised Signature
Request made by individuals
Request must be made by Individual
Request must be made by Individual
    Applications for release of Card details/Card history information can only be made by a person who:
    • is requesting their own information
    • has been authorised by the Photo ID Card holder to obtain their information
    • has a proven legal authority to obtain the information e.g. Police officer
How to apply
Verbal request for information
Verbal request for information
    Staff may verbally advise a person of information about their Photo ID card ONLY if satisfied that the person enquiring is the person to whom the information relates (ie their own information).

    The customer must either present standard EOI documents or answer a series of questions to satisfy staff of their identity. Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Standard Evidence of Identity > Step 2 - Check Standard EOI Requirements.
Written request for information
Written request for information
    Applications may be lodged-
    In person by:
    • completing the application form (F2121)
    • presenting the EOI documents as detailed on the application form
    • paying the fee

    By mail by:
    • completing the application form (F2121)
    • providing certified copies of the EOI documents as detailed on the application form
    • providing a cheque or money order in Australian Dollars (AUD) for the fee
    • mailing all of the above to:
      Department of Transport and Main Roads
      Customer Service Direct
      GPO Box 1412
      Brisbane, Qld 4001
    Note: The fee may be paid at a driver licence issuing centre, however if this option is chosen the fee must be paid prior to the completed application being sent to the department.

    By fax by:
    • paying the fee at a driver licence issuing centre or by sending a cheque or money order before lodging the application
    • completing the application form (F2121)
    • providing certified copies if the EOI documents as detailed on the application form
    • faxing the above documents to +61 7 3066 7845
    Note: An application may only be made by fax if the fee has been paid prior to the completed application being sent to the department.

    By Email (QPS Only):
    • Request must be made from a QPS email address (i.e. ...@police.qld.gov.au) and have the appropriate signature block that clearly identifies the person requesting the information.
    • Copies of documents will be sent via the post.
When representing
When representing Photo Identification card holder
When representing Photo Identification card holder
    If a Photo ID Card holder is unable to present the application themselves they can have another person apply on their behalf.
    • This also applies to legal entities or organisations. A representative of the entity or organisation is required to transact the business.
    • When applying for information about another person's record, the Photo ID card holder's written authorisation is required.

      Note:There are further EOI documents, authorities and signatures required for persons who are transacting the business on the card holder's behalf.
    .
    The Release of Information Application (F2121) provides an area for the Photo ID Card holder’s written authorisation.
      • The Photo ID Card holder must be contacted to verify the release of the information unless the applicant holds a power of attorney for the Photo ID Card holder.
        Refer: Power of Attorney

      Note:The Photo ID Card holder’s written authorisation is not required, if requested by a person with proven legal authority.

      Refer: Transaction Requirements below for full requirements.
Proven legal authority
Requests from persons with proven legal authority

Release Copy of Documents (Photo ID Card Application Forms)
What it is
What it is
What it is
    Under Photo ID legislation, individuals are entitled to copies of documents that were given at the time of an application for a Photo ID Card:
    They are entitled to copies of documents:
    • That they have provided to TMR and
    • Which contain their personal information.

    This applies to the Photo Identification Application form and accompanying documents (photocopied or scanned) eg EOI documents.
    • Photocopied documents are requested from Iron Mountain.
    • Scanned documents are requested from IMU.

    Note:QPS officers may request copies of documents for enforcement purposes free of charge.

Release Original Documents (Photo ID Card Application Forms)
    Original documents may only be released by:

    Customer Service Support - Unpaid Searches Unit
    Email: searches @TMR.qld.gov.au
    Phone: 3066 0133

    Original documents may only be released to the following persons when a Letter of Authority is supplied:
    • The Superintendent of Police
    • Inspector of Police
    • Crown Law Officer (acting on behalf of TMR)
    • Courts (if requested by subpoena)
      Refer: Offence Management > 1. Court Cases > Court Certificates

    When accepting the original document the authorised person must sign & complete a TMR letter indicating:
    • The particulars of the document released
    • Their name
    • Rank and serial number (If applicable)
