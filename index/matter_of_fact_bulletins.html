Matter of Fact Bulletins (7057)

Matter of Fact
The Matter of Fact is a periodic update of court outcomes and how they impact upon enforcement of Transport Laws. Matter of Fact is intended to provide insight into legal issues and the implications of court decisions for Transport Inspectors.
Contact
For assistance or questions relating to the Matter of Fact periodicals contact your Principal Advisor (Investigations).
