How to Access ICO (7152)

Applies to
Applies to
    This procedure will outline how to access and view information in ICO.
1
The login screen to access ICO
The login screen to access ICO
    ICO is accessed through the CIP Portal or alternatively https://ico.tmr.qld.gov.au/

    You will be required to read, accept and abide by the Terms and Conditions.



    Enter your email address and password

2
Search for an Approved Inspection Station
Search for an Approved Inspection Station
    All AIS's are listed in ICO, even if they haven't enrolled in ICO. If the status of the AIS in RUMIIS is cancelled, suspended or expired the AIS still appears in ICO, even if no inspection certificates have been issued in ICO.

    RUMIIS should still be checked to confirm the status of the AIS.



    Search Results Returned

3
AIS Details Page
AIS Details Page
4
List of Inspections carried out at the AIS
List of Inspections carried out at the AIS
    This information is able to be sorted by clicking on any of the column headings. The data can be sorted in ascending or descending order by clicking on the relevant heading a number of times.

4.1
View Inspection Details
View Inspection Details
    • Click on the individual SC / COI Report blue button to view the Certificate / Report.
    • Click on the individual result for all details recorded (e.g. Faults, photos).

5
Defect Inspections
Defect Inspections
    The navigation is the same as in section 4.

    Click on the Defect Notice number to access the details of the defect inspection carried out at that AIS.

6
AIS Examiner Details
AIS Examiner Details
    An Examiner can only be associated with an AIS if those details are recorded in RUMIIS.



    Click on the Inactive Examiners tab to access the list of examiners that the Approval Holder does not want using ICO at their AIS.

7
AIS Reporting
AIS Reporting
    This report can be very useful for a Transport Inspector as it provides a snapshot of inspections performed and the associated fail rates.

7.1
Sorting Report Data
Sorting Report Data
    The data in the report can be sorted to provide different views.

    This is done by selecting the options in the 2 drop down boxes in the top left of the reporting screen.



8
ICO Library
ICO Library
    The documents contained in the ICO library are all electronically linked and include all AIS forms as well as links to relevant legislation.
