IA55 Report (3364)

IA 55 Report
If your office receives an email from Industry.Licensing.CrimTrac@tmr.qld.gov.au or DGIL@tmr.qld.gov.au in relation to an IA55 report, you must take the action relevant to the industry authority. The IA55 report details changes in a person’s driver licence status and will be used to establish the action that needs to be taken on Industry Authority (IA) where the holders driver licence has been suspended, cancelled or expired.

Some (IA's) are dependent on the holder having a current driver licence, therefore if the holder's driver licence is suspended/cancelled/expired the IA is automatically suspended / cancelled / invalid and the holder can no longer perform in the IA role.

Industry Licensing policy areas will monitor the IA55 report from TICA.

An email will be sent from industry licensing crimtrac or DG&IL mailbox to the Manager of the Customer Service Centre (CSC) that is closest to the applicant (Relevant Customer Service Centre) with the details of the driver licence status. The manager will need to refer to the action column in below tables to determine the action required.

If the CSC has any questions in relation to the IA55 report or information contained in an email received from Industry Licensing Policy please email Industry.Licensing.CrimTrac@tmr.qld.gov.au or DGIL@tmr.qld.gov.au.
