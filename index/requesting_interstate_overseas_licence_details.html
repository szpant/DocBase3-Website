Requesting Interstate/Overseas Licence Details (565)


Requesting InformationRequests must be lodged via the interstate/overseas authority's preferred method of contact. The only requests that can made over the telephone are for clarification or confirmation of a written response.

For details on when to confirm interstate information, Refer: Confirming Non-Qld Details

Confirmation of an expired licence is not required if the licence details are on NEVDIS.

Requests will take 5 business days to be processed - the customer must be advised of this timeframe.
Requirements
  • Customer to provide:
    • EOI (at least 1 Cat A document);
      and
    • An email address (preferred but not mandatory) so that they can be informed once the interstate results are received.
  • CRN must be created before ROI request is sent.
  • Customer must be given a CRN card with the 5th business day written on it.
  • Customer's email address must be added to e-contact details.
    Note:It must be reiterated to customers that they should not contact the call centre to check on the progress of their request until after the 5th business day.

Returned results
    When the ROI results are returned:
      • The email must be filed in a relevant folder per local arrangement (e.g. "Returned ROI Requests") so that the NQPR screen can be updated once the customer returns;
        and
      • the customer must be notified to return to the CSC. Refer: Customer notification.

    The emailed results can be deleted from outlook once updated into TICA or after 1 month.
Customer notificationWhen the interstate ROI is returned, check the history and ensure the interstate licence can be transferred to Qld. The customer can then be notified via an automated email to return to the centre for their transfer to be processed. Refer: Interstate Licence Customer Contact SharePoint page - https://intranet.tmr.qld.gov.au/sites/csb-reg/Pages/InterstateContact.aspx

If the customer's history indicates disqualifications, suspensions, interlocks etc, contact the customer by phone or email to discuss. Refer: Email Template Interstate licence transfer unable to be completed - April 2019
