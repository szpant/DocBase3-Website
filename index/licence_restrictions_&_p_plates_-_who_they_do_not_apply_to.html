Licence Restrictions & P Plates - who they do not apply to (3168)

Does not apply toRestriction/ RequirementWhen driving in the course of their duties a:
An Emergency Service Worker*
or
An Exempted Police Driver^		Mobile Phone usageMotor Vehicle
Peer Passengers
High Powered Vehicles
Display P PlatesCar

*An Emergency Service Worker means:
  • A fire officer under the Fire and Emergency Service Act 1990

    Note:Fire officer does not include members of the Rural Fire Brigade or State Emergency Service (SES)
  • A service officer under the Ambulance Service Act 1991
^An Exempted Police Driver means:
  • A police officer
  • A watch-house officer within the meaning of the Police Service Administration Act 1990
  • A special constable within the meaning of the Police Service Administration Act 1990
  • A community police officer within the meaning of the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Communities (Justice, Land and Other Matters) Act 1984
  • A person appointed by the commissioner under the Public Service Act 1996, part 9, as a police liaison officer
  • A member of a police service of another State
