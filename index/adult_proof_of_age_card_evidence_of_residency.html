Photo Identification Card Evidence of Residency (3335)

Photo Identification Card - Evidence of residency
What it isEvidence of Residency provides proof to TMR that the person is a resident of Qld.
Why requiredTo ensure Photo Identification Cards that are administered by TMR are issued to persons who ordinarily reside in Queensland at the time of the application.
When RequiredOnce a customer has EOR recorded in TICA they will not need to supply further EOR documentation unless it is determined that they have moved away from Queensland and returned (eg. Interstate or Overseas licence transfer.)

To determine if EOR is required, check the EOR column in the Transaction Requirements Checklist.

Address on Document differs to TICA - Replacement Photo ID Card
Address on Document differs to TICA - Replacement Photo ID Card
    If the supplied residency address differs to the address on TICA the customer must be able to tell the CSC operator the address recorded on TICA.

    If they can confirm the address on TICA then the new address should be updated on TICA and the replacement PIC issued.

    Note:Evidence of residency is not required when applying for a replacement APA card.
Features documents must contain
Documents must:
  • Be originals only*
  • Identify the issuing organisation
  • Clearly display customer's name
  • Contain a current Qld residential address which forms part of the document eg. pension card
Notes:
  • Address added to another part of the document are not acceptable e.g. address on back of birth certificate
  • *Printed internet/electronic bank statements, bills and B-Pay View documents (for electricity, gas, telephone and local government rates) are considered originals for this purpose.
