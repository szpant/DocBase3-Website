Cancellation and Re-registration - COVID-19 (7550)

What it isCancellation of Registration during COVID-19
Re-registration after being cancelled due to COVID-19 (up to 6 months after the COVID-19 emergency ends)

The following is a summary of the changes being implemented for COVID-19 with respect to cancellation and re-registration of vehicles:
  • Waiving of the admin fee upon cancellation (applies to all types where customer is cancelling as a result of COVID-19 emergency)
  • Removing requirement to surrender standard plates
  • Customisation of Eligible Plates free of charge for those who intend to re-register in the same name
  • Standard Plate fee waiver at time of re-registration in the same name/CRN of the same vehicle (if plates were not customised and cancellation occurred from 29 January 2020 onwards)
  • Safety Certificate exemptions for re-registering in the same name/CRN and where no vehicle details have changed
  • COI exemptions at re-registration for re-registering in the same name, where no vehicle details have changed and within 12 months of the issue date of their previous COI. The COI will be due for renewal on the same date that it was due if the vehicle registration had not been cancelled. Public Passenger Transport Vehicles which have 6 monthly COIs will have the due date extended for 12 months from the effective date of their previous COI.
  • A one-off 3 month pay term at new business for all light vehicles (excluding trailers). An 'on/off' system parameter will determine when this option is available. All customers can access this pay term whilst the parameter is 'on' if they are:
    • Re-registering following previous cancellation due to COVID-19; or
    • Experiencing financial hardship (not explicitly due to COVID-19)

There is no limit on the number of times that customers can access the cancellation and re-registration relief measures providing they declare that they are/were impacted by COVID-19 at the time of the relevant transaction.

Note:Eligible primary producers should be encouraged to make their registration/s dormant rather than cancelling and re-registering.

Cancellation of registration
Cancellation of registration
    • The administrative fee will be included in the refund cancellation amount by manual adjustment
    • The requirement to surrender standard numbers plates has been removed - customers must securely dispose of them (e.g. bend/make plates unable to be attached to a vehicle)
    • New tick boxes have been added to the Vehicle/Queensland Regulated Ship Cancellation of Registration Application form F3517 for:
      • Cancelling due to COVID-19
      • Customisation of Eligible Plates - this will be free of charge and has been added so that the customer can retain the plates if intending to re-register
      • Securely self-disposing of standard/taxi plates

    If a customer attends to cancel and:
    • Hasn’t yet filled in a form, ensure that they are given the new COVID-19 cancellation form.
    • Brought the plates to surrender, the operator can accept/destroy them.
    • Has a previous cancellation form and their plates are to be customised, the operator must add a note “plates customised due to COVID-19” which the customer must initial.
    • they are cancelling due to COVID-19, the operator must add a note "COVID-19" in the reason for cancellation which the customer must initial.

    Operator cancellation actions:
    Operator cancellation actions:
      1. Customise the plates (if required) and waive the customisation fees by completing an adjustment as follows:
      • Adjustment type: REVCUST
      • Debit the REVCUST account:420035351 for the relevant Customised Plate Fees
      • Credit the Customer Account for the same amount
      • Add text: COVID-19 cancellation and the registration number for vehicle/s


      Operators must ensure that the correct account code is selected as waived fee amounts are included in departmental reporting.

      Note:If adjusting for multiple vehicles, do 1 adjustment for the total amount.
      For example: 10 plates: $53.90 x 10 = adjustment would be $539.00 to customer account then take payment from the account.

      2. Cancel Registration

      Note:If the customer has self-destroyed/disposed the surrender reason is "Destroyed".

      3. Manually adjust the admin fee before processing the refund. For current fees, Refer: Fees and Forms > 01. Fees > Vehicle Registration > Admin/Surcharge

      For example:
      • Adjustment type: REVCUST
      • Debit the REVCUST account: 420005828 for $19.40
      • Credit the Customer Account for $19.40
      • Add text: COVID-19 cancellation and the registration number for vehicle/s

      If there is:
      • ≥ $19.40 pro-rata refund payable, adjust the full $19.40
      • < $19.40 pro-rata refund payable, only adjust the pro-rata amount

      For example:
      • Pro-rata refund $15
      • Pro-rata admin cancellation fee $15
      • Net refund before adjustment = $0
      • Adjust $15 to customer and refund them $15

      Operators must ensure that the correct account code is selected as waived fee amounts are included in departmental reporting

      Note:If adjusting for multiple vehicles, do 1 adjustment for the total amount.
      For example: 10 cancellations: $19.40 x 10 = adjustment would be $194.00 to customer account. This amount will then be included in the refund following cancellation of the vehicles.

      4. If intending to re-register and the vehicle has a COI, check the COI expiry date and refer to the table below to request a EOT (if required). The purpose of requesting an EOT is so that the vehicle can retain its' COI so the customer doesn't have to get one when they re-register the vehicle.

      Note:If being processed at Longreach, the EOT will be processed in house. Only transactions processed at CSC's should be referred to SBS for the EOT.

      SCHA vehicle
      • Send email to SBS COVID-19 EOT Request - Rego to request an EOT for 12 months from the previous COI effective date.
      Alternative Compliance
      • Check CRN to make sure customer has ALTC qualification.
      • Upon cancellation, the ALTC qual will be removed. Operator will need to request it be re-added at time of re-registration. Refer to Re-registration section below.
      FARM vehicle with primary Primary producers registration concession
      • Promote seasonal registration in the first instance, if the customer refuses, send an email to SBS COVID-19 EOT Request - Rego to request an EOT for 12 months + 2 weeks from today's date (can't be more than 2 years from original COI effective date).
      All other COI vehicles
      • If more than 2 months until the COI expiry date, send an email to SBS COVID-19 EOT Request - Rego to request an EOT for 12 months from the previous COI effective date.
      • If less than 2 months until the COI expiry date, no action is required as it is unlikely the customer will re-register before the COI expiry date. In the event that they do, an EOT can be completed at the time of re-registration.

      5. Add text restriction/s to the vehicle with a 12 month expiry date:
      • Cancelled due to COVID-19
      • Plate customised due to COVID-19 (if plate being customised)
      • EOT requested due to COVID-19
Re-registration (within 6 months of COVID-19 emergency ends)
Re-registration (within 6 months of COVID-19 emergency ends)
    • Additional relief has been provided for customers who re-register the same vehicle in exactly the same name, providing no changes have been made to the vehicle.
    • The re-registration relief measures only apply to cancellations that occurred on or after 29 January 2020 and the cancellation was:
      • voluntary and the registration has a text restriction that indicates the cancellation was due to COVID-19; or
      • voluntary and the customer declares it was cancelled due to COVID-19 and the cancellation was processed before the relief measures were announced (25/4); or
      • automatic and the customer declares it was auto cancelled because they were affected by COVID-19.
    • If the customer did not customise their plates, the new plate fee for re-registration will be waived. This can be done via manual adjustment:
      • Adjustment type: REVCUST
      • Debit the REVCUST account: 420015828 for the relevant Customised Plate Fees
      • Credit the Customer Account for the same amount
      • Add text: COVID-19 re-registration and the registration number for vehicle/s

    Operators must ensure that the correct account code is selected as waived fee amounts are included in departmental reporting.

    Note:If adjusting for multiple vehicles, do 1 adjustment for the total amount e.g. 10 re-registrations: $31.45 x 10 = adjustment would be $314.50 to customer account then take payment from the account along with additional fees paid by the customer to re-register.

    • An individual form is required for each vehicle being re-registered
    • The vehicle cannot be re-registered if the vehicle had a defect notice that was not cleared prior to registration being cancelled.
    • Advise the operator to contact Compliance regarding clearing the defect notice before the vehicle can be re-registration.

    Safety Certificate vehicles
    Safety Certificate vehicles
      • Will be exempt from SC upon re-registration
      • Operators will need to select "SAME REG OPER" to exempt the SC requirement
      • The text that was recorded at time of registration cancellation will need to be manually expired
    COI vehicles
    COI vehicles
      • The original COI expiry date (at time of cancellation) will apply (excluding buses >12 seats/PPSE/SCHA vehicles as they are entitled to 12 months from their original COI effective date)
      • COI vehicles that did not have the EOT applied at time of cancellation and are re-registering prior to the expiry date of the original COI (or within 12 months of their COI effective date for buses >12 seats/PPSE/SCHA) will need to be referred to SBS COVID-19 EOT Request - Rego
      • If the original COI expiry date has passed (or it's been more than 12 months since the COI effective date for buses >12 seats/PPSE/SCHA), they will need to obtain a new COI from an AIS.
      • The text that was recorded at time of registration cancellation will need to be manually expired.
      • Heavy vehicles will not require a HVRAS inspection or CSC measure/weigh if none of the details have changed
      • Alternative Compliance vehicles - send an urgent email to NHVAS@tmr.qld.gov.au requesting that the ALTC qual be re-added due to COVID-19. Also phone 3066 4666 to have the request actioned immediately.
    Customer Re-registration process
    Customer Re-registration process
      Individuals
      Individuals
        • Customer must attend a CSC/processing centre
        • At formless centres no application is required
        • If not a formless centre the Vehicle Registration Application (F3518) must be completed. This form is being updated to outline to COVID-19 arrangements.
        • If previous plates were customised as part of the cancellation process there is no requirement to complete Remove/Attach Personalised/Customised Number Plate/s Application (F2964)
        • If previous plates were surrendered, they be issued with free standard plates
        • Safety Certificate is exempt.
        • For COI Vehicles - refer to rules above
        • Full EOI requirements/formless process/standard CSC payment methods apply
        • Customer must declare (either verbally or on the form, as a notation, if one was completed) that no vehicle details have changed since cancellation
      Organisations
      Organisations
        The below applies to all organisation customers who either lodge by email (more than 50 vehicles) or in a CSC (less than 50 vehicles).
        All customers to:
          • Complete one Vehicle Registration Application(F3518) if all registration details (other than the vehicle description) are the same for all vehicles
          • Provide a document (like sample below) with all of the vehicle details:
          • VIN
          • Make/Model
          • Plate number (if previously customised)

            Note:If registration details are different they will need to provide a separate form for each vehicle.

          • Safety Certificate is exempt
          • For COI Vehicles - refer to rules above. If an inspection is required, the customer can still utilise all other re-registration COVID provisions (e.g. using an attachment).
          • Customer must declare on form/s (as a notation), or attachment that no vehicle details have changed since cancellation

            If the previous plates were:
            • Customised:
              • There is no requirement to complete Remove/Attach Personalised/Customised Number Plate/s Application (F2964)

                Note:If the customer does not wish to sign up for customer account direct debit they will need to attend a CSC to pay.

            • Surrendered:
              • Customer will need to lodge their application in a CSC and will be issued with free standard plates
              • Full EOI requirements/standard CSC payment methods apply

          Less than 50 vehicles
          Less than 50 vehicles
            • Customer must present to a CSC for transaction to be processed
            • Full EOI requirements/standard CSC payment methods apply
            • Formless process or form/with attachment can be used
            • If the previous plates were:
            • customised, there is no requirement to complete Remove/Attach Personalised/Customised Number Plate/s Application (F2964)
            • surrendered, they will be issued with free standard plates
