Retention & Filing of Documents (2662)


The following is not an exhaustive list of the documents required to be retained.

If an application was received and processed at a formless centre an Application form is not required. For further requirements regarding application forms Refer: Fees and Forms > Formless > 3. Formless Customer Applications.

If the document is not listed, please refer to the Disposal Schedule available on the Information Management Intranet, Retention and Disposal Schedules. Alternatively, please refer to the Electronic Storage of Document procedure where a list of approved documents for electronic storage is available. Refer: Admin > 5. Filing and Business Exceptions > Electronic Storage of Documents

All references to the address for Iron Mountain can be found in the DocBase Address Book: Iron Mountain

TRAILS filing is to be sent to Iron Mountain DIRECT either by using TOLL or Messenger Post.

The option of using Iron Mountain's reply paid service should be used only by agencies and by centres in rural/remote areas of the state who are unable to access Toll or Messenger Post.

Note:Payment card details must be blacked out with permanent marker if provided in writing on any documentation prior to being filed.
