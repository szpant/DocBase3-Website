About Evidence of Identity (EOI)
What it isEvidence of identity documents prove:
Individuals
  • The legal existence of the person (Category A documents)
  • Their name and date of birth (Category A documents)
  • The use of their name in the community (Cat B documents)
Organisations - The existence of the organisation

      Call transferred from SSQ
      If an SSQ consultant has verified EOI requirements and then transfers the call to a TMR consultant, they will record a text restriction against the customer that includes the date and time that the EOI was verified.

      When a TMR consultant receives the call, they must do the following before answering the enquiry:
      • Check that the EOI was verified within the last 30 minutes
      • Confirm knowledge based questions
      • Expire the text restriction

      If more than 30 minutes have lapsed, the customer will have to satisfy EOI requirements again.
