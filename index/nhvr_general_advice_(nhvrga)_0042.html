NHVR General Advice (NHVRGA) 0042
The NHVR has released NHVRGA 0042

This NHVR General Advice is to inform Authorised Officers and other relevant staff about changes to NHVR Access permits. Additional route information will be added to the contents of NHVR permits issued under Chapter 4 of the Heavy Vehicle National Law (HVNL). This General Advice does not apply to Vehicle Standard permits and delegated Road Managers Permits.

As part of the NHVR’s commitment to improve our customer experience the NHVR has made some changes to the access permitting process with the online NHVR Portal (the Portal). The Portal was launched in August 2016.

The Portal takes the existing traditional paper-based permits application environment to a complete, integrated online digital services platform. Over 20,000 permits have now been processed online using the Portal and the Portal is now the Industry preferred permits application lodgement platform. Recent changes to the Portal system will now allow additional route information improving the textual description of routes included in permits.

The additional route information will be included in permits where the application has been lodged on or after 4pm AEST Friday 19 of August 2017. Permit applications lodged prior to this time and date will not be impacted.
