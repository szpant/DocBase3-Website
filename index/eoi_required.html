EOI required - The standards
Four optionsThe EOI required will be shown in the table below as one of the following 4 options:
  • None required
  • Name of document/s required
  • Standard EOI
  • Standard EOI + Name of additional document/s required
Levels of EOI
About the EOI Levels
About the EOI Levels
    As most transactions or products share the same EOI requirements, a Standard EOI group has been created as the benchmark.
    If LESS than Standard EOI is...If MORE than Standard EOI is...
    ...required, the table will state either None required or name the required document/s....required, the table will state Standard EOI and name the additional required document/s.
Standard EOI
What makes up Standard EOI
What makes up Standard EOI
    Standard EOI may vary according to the customer type (e.g. individual, company) and the method of contact with TMR (e.g. in person, by phone).
