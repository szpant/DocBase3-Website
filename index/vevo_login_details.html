ImmiAccount Administration
For the purpose of undertaking a work entitlement check for driver authorisation applicants, the Passenger Transport Authorisation Unit (PTA) is the account administrator for all TMR ImmiAccounts.

The allocated ImmiAccount password will be updated by the PTA.

In the event that your region’s allocated ImmiAccount username becomes locked, please consider using your regions alternate login details or contact PTA on 3338 4994.
