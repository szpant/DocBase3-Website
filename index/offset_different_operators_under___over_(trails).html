Offset Different Operators Under / Over (TRAILS) (5038)

1.Fastpath to ADJU in the Command Line press ENTER
Flows to Account Adjustment screen
2.'C’ for Create
3.TAB to ‘Type?’ field and insert OPOFFSET
4.TAB to ‘Control Total’ and enter the cash amount
5.TAB to ‘SEL’ field and ‘S’ to select the line
6.Insert the Operator Overs Account Number (this appears on the ‘List of Operator Account Balances’ report OPOVER)
7.TAB to ‘DR / CR’ field and insert the DR (debit)
8.In the ‘Amount’ field, enter the Cash Amount (the amount should be the same as the control total) press ENTER
9.TAB to the next line
10.TAB to ‘SEL’ field and ‘S’ to select the line
11.Insert the Operator Unders Account Number (this appears on the ‘List of Operator Account Balances’ report – OPUNDER)
12.TAB to ‘DR / CR’ field and insert the CR (credit)
13.In the ‘Amount’ field, enter the Cash Amount (the amount should be the same as the control total ensuring DR must equal CR)
press ENTER
Flows to Text Editor screen
14. TAB to the text line field
15.Insert the text shown below:

An over occurred for operator an under occurred for operator (for example, BEFLANA) due to incorrect change being given to customer. The customer demanded a cash refund of the money. Insert adjustment numbers from previous OPOVER & OPUNDER adjustments. Include your name, CSC, phone number and date.
16.Go ‘HOME’ enter ‘U’ for Update press ENTER
Message displays Update Successful
