Extension of Time COVID-19 (7531)

    Extension of Time - COVID-19
    What it isTo assist customers and protect staff, SBS and Compliance Authorised Officers can issue an Extension Of Time (EOT) for 6 months from the expiry of the last or current Certificate Of Inspection (COI) if the customer is unable to obtain a COI due to infection or self-isolation because of COVID-19.

    This applies to both TMR and AIS inspectable vehicles.
    COVID 19 process
    SBS Booking
    SBS Booking
      An EOT can be issued without an inspection booking in SBS.
    Application
    Application
      If the customer applies over the phone an Extension of Time Application (F2483) is not required.

      If the customer applies over the phone, complete the email template, process the application in TRAILS, add text in TICA, send and save the email in DMS.

      Refer to DMS procedure for saving emails.

      For the Extension of Time COVID-19 Template, Refer: How to apply > By Phone section below.
    Medical Certificate
    Medical Certificate
      The customer is not required to supply a medical certificate if they declare infection or self-isolation because of COVID-19.
    SBS
    SBS
      SBS can issue an Extension Of Time (EOT) for 6 months from the expiry of the last or current Certificate Of Inspection (COI) if the customer is unable to obtain a COI due to infection or self-isolation because of COVID-19.

      The EOT can be issued without an inspection booking in SBS.

      If the customer requests a further extension, they must be referred to Compliance for assessment.
    Compliance
    Compliance
      Compliance Authorised Officers can issue an Extension Of Time (EOT) for 6 months from the expiry of the last or current Certificate Of Inspection (COI) if the customer is unable to obtain a COI due to infection or self-isolation because of COVID-19.

      The EOT can be issued without an inspection booking in SBS.
    Text Restrictions
    Text Restrictions
      When an EOT is issued, record the following text in TICA against the registration:

      EOT issued <date> COVID-19 approval <Name > <ID number>

      Refer: Inspections > The How To: Inspections > Inspection Procedures > COVID-19 EOT > Adding a Text Restriction in TICA
    How to applyA customer can apply for an EOT by submitting an Extension of Time Application (F2483) form or over the phone.
    By Phone
    By Phone
      If the customer applies for an EOT because of COVID-19 complete Extension of Time COVID-19 Template

      Refer: Inspections > The How To: Inspections > Inspection Procedures > COVID-19 EOT > Completing the Extension of Time Email Template
    TRAILS
    		An Extension of Time can only be issued via TRAILS.
    Access to TRAILS is made via Service Centre Online.

    Note: An EOT is not required to be printed unless requested by the customer. To stop an EOT from printing in TRAILS:
    • type SPAR in the command line at the bottom of the screen
    • remove the front office printer details and update


    Refer: Inspections > The How To: Inspections > Inspection Procedures > COVID-19 EOT > Issuing an Extension of Time in TRAILS
    TMR site closure
    Site Closure
    Site Closure
      If TMR are unable to provide inspections at a location due to closure:
      • Region to advise SBS (HELPSBS@tmr.qld.gov.au)
      • Region to manage the current bookings for the site.
      • Regional staff to contact customers,
      • offer an alternative site if available,
      • customer will have to contact SBS to rebook if an alternative site is available.
      • EOT may have to be issued to booking date.
      • If no alternative site is available, issue an EOT for 6 months from expiry of last COI.
      • Customer to contact SBS for refund.
      • Future bookings.
      • SBS offer alternative site if available,
      • If no alternative site is available, issue EOT for 6 months from expiry of last COI and refund fees.
    AIS closures
    Defect Notice Clearance
    Defect Notice Clearance
      If a customer is not able to get a TMR issued Defect Notice cleared due to AIS closure advise the customer to contact the local Compliance office for assistance.
