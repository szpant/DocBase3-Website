To Determine Fees
    For the purpose of determining fees the following rules apply:
    • 2 axles less than 1 metre apart are taken as 1 axle
    • 3 axles 2 metres or less apart are taken as 2 axles
    • 4 axles 3.2 metres or less apart are taken as 3 axles

Heavy Caravan and Non-load Carrying Trailers
Heavy Caravan and Non-load Carrying Trailers
    For example Caravan, Mobile billboard, mobile laboratory, mobile library and mobile workshop)
    Registration Category: HCVN

    Body Shapes: CVAN & CST only

    The concession fee is calculated by applying the percentage (below) X the Heavy Trailer Axle
    fee (axles X Heavy Trailer per axle fee) X 0.35 (35%) = Registration fee.
    The concession rate is;
    • for a trailer with 1 axle—50%;
    • for a trailer with 2 axles—35%;
    • for a trailer with 3 or more axles—30%.

    The equation is;
    (Number of heavy axles X Heavy Trailer (per axle) fee) X (Concession percentage (find
    appropriate percentage above)) X 0.35 (35%) = Registration fee
