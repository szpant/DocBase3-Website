Withdraw Authority to Drive in Qld (Failed Practical Driving Test) - Foreign Licence (5068)

Applies to
Applies to
    Withdrawing the authority to drive in Qld due to a failed practical driving test for the holder of a foreign driver licence.
    (This applies to a person identified as holding a WA heavy vehicle licence under investigation).
Before you Start
Before you Start
    Ensure there are grounds to withdraw the authority to drive.
1
Customer fails practical Driving Test
2
Notify customer of Withdrawal of Authority to Drive
Notify customer of Withdrawal of Authority to Drive

    During debrief, the customer must be notified verbally and in writing of the withdrawal of their authority to drive on their foreign licence:
    • Give the customer the Notice of withdrawal of Authority to Drive - Failed Practical Driving Test (F5044) add notation on Driver Assessment Report -F5044 issued to customer -/-/-.
    • As an option Notice of withdrawal of Authority to Drive - Failed Practical Driving Test (F5044) can be emailed (if EContact details completed) when adding restriction.

    Note: For QPS offices unable to access the TMR Corporate Forms database, a copy of this form is available on the QPS SharePoint site.

    There is no requirement to insert the customer's details on the form.

    • Advise the customer - "As a foreign licence holder who has failed a practical driving assessment, you are no longer able to drive on Queensland roads using your foreign licence. If you wish to continue to learn how to drive you will need to apply for a Queensland Learner Licence and obey all the conditions of this licence. You may only use your foreign licence to take another practical driving assessment."
3
Create Individual Customer (TICA) (if required)
4
Record failed practical driving test in TICA
5
Add customer restriction - Withdrawal of Privilege (WRPV) (TICA)
Add customer restriction - Withdrawal of Privilege (WRPV) (TICA)

    Add the customer restriction WRPV to record the withdrawal of authority action.

    Add customer restriction (TICA)

    Note:The restriction reason "Failed Prac Test - Overseas Licence" and corrective action "Information Only" must be used when adding the restriction. The effective date of the restriction is the date the restriction is being added - this date cannot be backdated.
6
Record Notation of Driver Assessment Report
Record Notation of Driver Assessment Report
    • Note on the Driver Assessment Report that "Notice Given, WRPV Restriction Added"
