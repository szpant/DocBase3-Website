28/08/2020 - New Forms for Disability Parking Permit Applications Available COB, Friday 28 August 2020
28/08/2020 - New Forms for Disability Parking Permit Applications Available COB, Friday 28 August 2020

    From COB today, the new and amended forms will be released to support the expansion of eligibility criteria for a disability parking permit. The forms include applicants diagnosed as legally blind reflecting the changes which come into effect Monday 31 August 2020.

    The new and amended forms are:
    • F5280 Australian Disability Parking Permit - Legally Blind Application (Individual)
    • F4814 Australian Disability Parking Permit - Mobility Impairment Application (Individual)
    • F4881 Australian Disability Parking Permit Application (Organisations)

    Important: after COB today please destroy all printed copies of:
    • F4814 Australian Disability Parking Permit Application (Individual)
    • F4881 Australian Disability Parking Permit Application (Organisations)

21/08/2020 - Changes to the Disability Parkikng Permit Scheme - Updated
21/08/2020 - Changes to the Disability Parkikng Permit Scheme - Updated

    The eligibility criteria for the disability parking permit scheme will be expanded to include people who are diagnosed as legally blind from Monday, 31 August 2020. People who have been assessed as legally blind should not apply for the scheme until this date.

    However, if an application using the current form is received at a CSC for a legally blind person from Monday, 24 August it can be accepted and forwarded to the Disability Parking Unit. The customer should be advised that the application will be held and not processed until 31 August 2020 or after.

    Additionally, a new application form Australia Disability Parking Permit - Legally Blind Application F5280 will be made available from Friday, 28 August.

    Further information can be found in the Project Update.

    Peta Berry
    A/Senior Procedures Officer
