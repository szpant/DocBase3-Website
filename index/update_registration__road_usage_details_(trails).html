    Change to Drought Registration (TRAILS)
      • <Tab> to [Pay Mode]
      • Enter DRHT (or F4 and select from list)
      • <Tab> to [Pay Term(Mnths)]
      • Enter 3 or 6
      • Press <Home>
      • Type U for Update in Action Field
      Message Displays: RG434-W: Pay Mode of Drought selected. F10 to CONFIRM, F12 to CANCEL
      • F10 to confirm and change the status of the record
      Accesses Record Business Exception
      • Select the appropriate reason and <Enter>
      Message displays: SS089-I: Exception Request successfully recorded
      • F12 to exit
      Message Displays: GB012-I: Update SUCCESSFUL

      Note:The drought update, like CTP, become effective on the next payment term, not the current one.

          (Receipt will automatically print)
      CHEQUE
      CHEQUE
        • <Tab> to [Method?] below [Cash]
        • Press Press F4
        Accesses List Payment Item Methods (AP11)
        • Type S beside Cheque type
        • Press <Enter>
        Accesses Payment (AP01)
        • Enter the following details
        • Amount recieved in [Amount]
        • Number in [Reference Number]
        • Cheque Drawer name in [Drawer]
        • Bank initials/branch in [Bank/Branch]
        • Press <Enter>
          (Receipt will automatically print)
