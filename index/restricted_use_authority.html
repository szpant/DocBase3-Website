Restricted Use Authority (7517)


What it isA Restricted Use Authority allows for a vessel to operate unregistered when being used for a specific purpose, for one or more of the following:
  • Trial, test or demonstration of seaworthiness
  • Demonstration or display associated with sale
  • Building, disposing of, fitting out
  • Use of ship pending finalising registration
  • Relocating, removing or repairing
  • Other operational aspect of ship or equipment

RequirementsDocuments required:
  • Completed Restricted Use Authority Application (F1572) to be lodged at a local Maritime Regional Office.
EOI Required
EOI Required

    Individual

      • Current Qld driver licence (or one that has been current at any time within the last 2 years)
      • 1 Category A + 2 Category B documents

    Representing

Rules
Period of Issue
Period of Issue
    • 1 month or less
    • 1 month or greater
Display of Authority
Display of Authority
    • The holder of the authority must carry the document on the ship, and;
    • If practical, the authority must be visibly displayed on the ship, and;
    • The holder of the authority must tell other crew or masters of the ship the conditions of the authority and how the ship can be operated
