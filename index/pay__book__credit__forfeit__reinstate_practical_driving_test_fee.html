Pay/ Book/ Credit/ Forfeit/ Reinstate Practical Driving Test Fee (311)

QPS
Pay only/Book and Pay
Pay only/Book and Pay
    Applies to
    Applies to
      Any of the following:
      Pay only
      • Paying for a test without making a booking
      Pay & Book
      • Paying and booking a test at the same time
      Book only
      • Booking a test previously paid
    Before you Start
    Before you Start
      • Check that the customer has the correct payment method (if paying)
      • Identify customer for whom practical driving test is being paid and/or booked (Check CRN in TICA)
    1
    Check Applicant's Eligibility to Book Test
    Check Applicant's Eligibility to Book Test
      Tenure
      • Verify applicant has met the required tenure period ie. has held the relevant Learner, P/P1/P2 Type or Open for the required period
      Location
      • For Class R and RE booking ensure the customer residential address is outside the 100km Q-Ride training area
      Pre-requisites
      • Hazard Perception test has been passed
      • Learner licence holders required to complete the logbook requirement have a passed a logbook and that pass will be valid at the time of the test.

        Note:If the logbook has expired:
        • Reprocess the supervised driving application in TICA
        • Select the same reason as the original application
        • There is no requirement for the customer to complete a physical application
        • The lodge/decision date is the date the application is being re-processed (current date).
        • A note can be placed against the transaction on daily filing "reprocessed learner logbook".
    2
    Process on TICA
    Process on TICA
      Select the relevant TICA Procedure:

      Pay only
      or
      Pay & Book      		Book Practical Driving Test (TICA)
      Book onlyBook Practical Driving Test (TICA)
    3
    Complete Driving Test Appointment Form
    Complete Driving Test Appointment Form
      Complete a Driving Test Appointment form including:
      • Date of Test
      • Place of test
      • Time to attend
    4
    Give/Advise Customer
    Give/Advise Customer
      Give Customer
      • Receipt (if required)
      • Driving Test Appointment Sheet (F3910) (if booking)
      Advise Customer
      • Of written test requirements if not yet met
      • Of where information on Q-Ride providers is available
    5
    Record details in Driving Examiner's Test Book
    Record details in Driving Examiner's Test Book
      The following details should be recorded:
      • Date of Test
      • Time of Test
      • Class of Licence being tested
      • Applicant's Name & CRN
      • Applicant's Contact details
