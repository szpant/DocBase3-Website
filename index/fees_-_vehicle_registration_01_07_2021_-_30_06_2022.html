Duty
    Duty Rates
    Duty Rates
      The Dutiable Value of a vehicle is first rounded up to the nearest $100 (e.g. A vehicle with a dutiable value of $15010 would be rounded up to $15100). Duty payable for a vehicle is then calculated at a variable percentage rate of the Dutiable Value using the number of cylinders/rotors or fuel type.
      All Motor Vehicles that have:
      1, 2, 3, 4 cylinders, 2 rotors and steam vehicles 3% or $3 per $100
      5 and 6 cylinders and 3 rotors 3.5% or $3.50 per $100
      7 or more cylinders 4% or $4 per $100
      Electric, Petrol-electric and diesel-electric vehicles 2% or $2 per $100
      Conditional registration vehicles and tractor based mobile machinery $25 flat rate
    Duty rate for light motor vehicles (not more than 4.5t) with a dutiable value over $100,000
    Duty rate for light motor vehicles (not more than 4.5t) with a dutiable value over $100,000
      The Dutiable Value of a vehicle is first rounded up to the nearest $100 (e.g. A vehicle with a dutiable value of $100010.00 would be rounded up to $100100.00). Duty payable for a vehicle is then calculated at a variable percentage rate of the Dutiable Value using the number of cylinders/rotors or fuel type.
      Light Motor Vehicles (not more than 4.5t) with dutiable value over $100,000.00 rates:
      4 cylinders and 2 rotors 5% or $5 per $100
      6 cylinders and 3 rotors 5.5% or $5.50 per $100
      7 or more cylinders 6% or $6 per $100
      Electric vehicles 4% or $4 per $100
