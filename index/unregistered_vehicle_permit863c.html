Unregistered Vehicle Permit (3098)

What is it?An unregistered vehicle permit allows for an unregistered vehicle to drive on the roads to and from specified destinations for specific purposes, provided the vehicle meets the requirements of:
  • the Transport Operations (Road Use Management-Vehicle Standards and Safety) Regulation 2010; and
  • the Transport Operations (Road Use Management-Mass Dimension and Loading) Regulation 2005.
RequirementsDocuments required:
  • Completed Unregistered Vehicle Permit Application (F3523)
  • Where a CTP insurer has not been nominated on (F3523), the customer must present a class 22 CTP insurance certificate for the required time.

    Note:While an unregistered vehicle permit is required for a caravan/trailer, CTP is not required for a caravan/trailer towed by:
    • Queensland registered vehicle
    • Unregistered vehicle with a Qld Unregistered Vehicle Permit

    Note:The Class 22 CTP insurance certificate does not have to be in the same name as the customer applying for an unregistered vehicle permit.
  • Any permit issued under the Transport Operations (Road Use Management) Act 1995 (if applicable) for example:-
    • Police Permit
    • Excess Mass Permit
    • A Permit issued for an exemption from a Vehicle Standard

    Note:Unregistered Vehicle Permit Application (F3523) indicates when the above permits may be required.

EOI Required
Rules
  • If a vehicle, which includes a trailer, does not have an identifier (VIN/Chassis number), an unregistered vehicle permit cannot be issued. The vehicle must be towed or transported. However, if the vehicle/trailer was manufactured prior to 1930, an engine number may be used.

  • Return trips can be made as long as they are:
      • Completed within the allowed time
      • Within Queensland

Purchase of Permit and CTP online
    An online service for the purchase of a UVP (including nomination and purchase of Class 22 CTP insurance) is available to eligible customers.

    The UVP cannot be purchased online where:
    • An excess mass permit is required
    • A police permit is required
    • Class 22 CTP insurance has already been purchased (CTP must be nominated as part of the online transaction)

    For more information regarding customer eligibility for online services, please refer to the TMR website: http://www.tmr.qld.gov.au/Online-services.aspx
