Consular Plates (3085)

What is it?Consular plates are Business/Corporate plates issued to approved Foreign
Consuls.
Requirements
  • CRN must have CONS qual
  • Consular Corp Number Plates - Issue/Replace/Remake Application (F5007)
  • Standard Number Plate/s Replacement Application (F3540)
Processing Consular Plate Application
Processing Consular Plate Application
    Action PartyActions
    CSC Role
    • Obtain approval of CSC Office Manager to send to Brisbane City CSC
    • Scan and email all documentation to Brisbane City CSC at consular_plates@tmr.qld.gov.au with subject line 'TMR Consular order request'
    • Contact SA(SM) from Brisbane City CSC (3305 8302) to issue a plate number
    • Process Registration application with number provided by Brisbane City CSC
    Refer: Vehicle Rego > 11. Plates > The How To: Plates > Plates Procedures > Standard Issue Plates > Process Application for Consular Concession
    Brisbane City CSC's Role
    • Issue plate number
    • Record details in Consular spreadsheet
    • Process plate application
    • Advise CSC when complete
    • Notify Principal Visits and Protocol Coordinator Department of the Premier and Cabinet
    Refer: 03. Types of Registration > Concessional Registration > Other Concession Types > Consular
Rules
  • First time orders will be for a pair of plates unless and accessory is also ordered
  • Plates must be surrendered and cancelled when the Consulate returns to their home country. Scan and email a copy of the application to Brisbane City CSC at consular_plates@tmr.qld.gov.au with subject line 'TMR Consular Cancellation'
  • Plates can be removed and attached to another vehicle in the same Consuls name by making an application to TMR. Scan and email a copy of the application to Brisbane City CSC at consular_plates@tmr.qld.gov.au with subject line 'TMR Consular Remove/Attach Plates'
