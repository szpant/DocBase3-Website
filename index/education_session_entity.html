Education Session Entity (7149)

Education Session Entity
Education Session Entity
    The Education Session entity captures instances where transport compliance officers provide education to industry or other groups on a wide range of matters.

    Sessions delivered may be the result of an investigation or Audit where education is determined as the best outcome, or where compliance officers attend at the request of a company to speak to a particular topic, or where transport compliance officers run an information session for a group on a topic of interest to them – for example caravan and towing safety.

    All staff have the ability to create this new entity type via CID. Simply log into CID

    What are the fields on the entity? Below is a description of each field and how it should be utilised
    1
    Record ID
    Record ID
      • System generated, cannot be changed
    2
    Session Date
    Session Date
      • Date session held/conducted.
      • Single date.
      • If multiple sessions were held on different dates, create one Education Session entity for each date.
    3
    Session Subject
    Session Subject
      • Free text field.
      • Be succinct but descriptive.
      • This can be thought of as the ‘title’ of the CID entry – someone who reads this should know in a line or two, that an education session was delivered to whom, when and what it was about.
      • This field should contain (at a minimum);
        • The name of the group or company the session was delivered to
        • The topic being presented
      • Example;
        • Toolbox talk, Toll Logistics Gladstone - completing heavy vehicle work diaries.
        • Trailer and caravan safety presentation, pensioners association of Queensland at Ekka Showgrounds.
      • Do not capture one-on-one-roadside driver education in this entity.
    4
    TMR Lead
    TMR Lead
      • This is a list of compliance officers only. If the TI is assisting another area (e.g. TRB) select the name of the lead TI involved.
      • Select TI from list.
    5
    Assigned Region
    Assigned Region
      • Select compliance region from list.
    6
    Number of attendees
    Number of attendees
      • Select the range that reflects the number of participants involved.
      • The aim is not to count specifically the number of people in total who have attended education sessions. It is to give an idea of how many people have attended sessions where TMR content has been presented.
      • Zero can be recorded for sessions where they were organised but no one attended.
      • These are important to capture as effort was made to prepare for and attend – if no one attended that is a reflection of the company not the TI.
      Range:
      • 0
      • 1-5
      • 6-10
      • 11-25
      • 26-50
      • 50+
    7
    Topic
    Topic
      Select one from list:
      • Chain of Responsibility
      • Fatigue
      • General Compliance
      • GHMS
      • IAP
      • Load Restraint
      • Mass/Dimension/Loading
      • Passenger Transport (incl. OA/DA)
      • Road Safety (non-HVNL)
      • Speeding
      • Toolbox Talk
      • Tow Truck
      • Vehicle Standards
      • Select topic most relevant to the session.
      • If multiple topics were presented, select the one with the most content.
      • If session was designed for one topic, but covered multiple topics select the one it was designed/intended to cover.
    8
    Delivery partner
    Delivery partner
      • This is a mandatory field and should be completed whether there was a delivery partner or not.
      • Only one can be selected from the list so if there are multiple partners, select the main partner or the partner who organised the session in the event compliance are assisting.
      • Select from the list;
        • No partner (TI/compliance delivered only)
        • TMR Product (CSB product area e.g. Road Safety)
        • AustRoads
        • Industry
        • NHVR
        • Police
        • Representative Body
        • TransLink
        • Other
    9
    Comments
    Comments
      • Free text field.
      • Only add if information is not captured elsewhere on the entity.
      • For example;
        • Topic
        • Delivery partner – if a presentation was made in conjunction with RACQ – nominate Other in the delivery partner list and then record RACQ as a delivery partner in comments.
    10
    Attachments
    Attachments
      • Use this section to attach a document relating to the education session. E.g. Attendance list.

What the Education Session entity links to in CID
What the Education Session entity links to in CID
    An Education Session entity can be linked in CID as:
    Outcomes
    • AIS Audit
    • Case - Where a TI has completed an investigation and delivered an education session, they should create an Education Session entity then link it to the existing Case ID.
    • Intercept
    • IAP NCR - (Only being used by IAP - Heavy Vehicle Analysis)
    • PT Audit
    • TTL Audit

    Linked to:
    • ANPR
    • IAP Enquiry - (Only being used by IAP - Heavy Vehicle Analysis)
    • Object
    • Organisation
    • Person
    • Place / Location
    • WiM
