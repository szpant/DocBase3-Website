    Notification (F3527) for Ship Category and Purpose of Use change    		Vessels with a POU of SCHO or SURF or Other Vessel may have restrictions of use applied.

    The restrictions will need to be removed if being transferred to a POU of Private. To remove the restriction:
    · Email F3521 to Maritime.Operations@msq.qld.gov.au; and
    · Confirm in the email the vessel will be used for private purposes.
