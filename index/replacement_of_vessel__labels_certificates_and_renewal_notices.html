Replacement of Ship Certificates and Renewal Notices (1953)


What it isCertificates can be replaced online, by email or by printing locally.

Individuals and organisations with an ACN recorded in TICA will no longer receive registration certificates by mail.

Registration periods varied at renewal, or organisations without an ACN recorded in TICA will continue to receive their registration certificates in the post.

Note:Replacements for Certificates and Renewals are issued free of charge.
