Refuse to consider (3347)


Refusing to consider
Refuse to consider applicationRefuse to Consider
Means the application has not been accepted on certain grounds and will therefore not be considered. A Refuse to Consider decision cannot be appealed.

An application for a product must not be considered if:
  • The applicant does not allow the chief executive to take and keep a digital photo and a digitised signature of the person, or
  • The applicant fails the 1:1 facial image comparison, or
    Refer: Smartcard > 1. Smartcard > Smartcard Facial Image Comparison > 1:1 Image Comparison > 1:1 Not Verified
  • The application is not:
    • In the approved form, or
    • Supported by the required information or documents, or
    • Accompanied by the prescribed fee.
Roles and responsibilitiesThe operator (CSO):
  • May refuse to consider an application that does not satisfy the application requirements.
  • Must advise the customer what is required to satisfy the application requirements (the majority of decisions will be verbal).
  • May give the customer one of the following notices if required.
  • Must retain the original application form (if provided) when a 1:1 facial image comparison has been performed and filed with the operator's daily filing.
  • Must hand back the original application form (if provided) to the customer for all other refuse to consider reasons.
