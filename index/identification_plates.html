Identification Plates (3056)


Prior to 1/7/2021, it was mandatory for Identification (Compliance) plate to be fitted under the Australian Design Rules (ADR) to ensure vehicles meet structural and safety standards for road use in Australia. The identification plate was either:
  • Constructed of metal
  • An adhesive label

From 1/7/2021, applicable vehicles may be recorded on the Register of Approved Vehicles (RAV) instead of having a compliance plate fitted.

Missing Identification Plates
Missing Identification Plates
    • Applies to vehicles that previously had a compliance plate only.
    • A replacement adhesive label or a letter of compliance can be provided by manufacturer where a lost or accidentally damaged label can be proven.
    • Proof of previous registration in Queensland or interstate can be accepted if the manufacturer's VIN or chassis number is on the vehicle.
    • If the vehicle is an Individually Constructed Vehicle, email Vehicle Standards with the VIN to confirm the vehicle identity for the issuing of a safety certificate.

TRAILS/TICA Compliance Type Codes
TRAILS/TICA Compliance Type Codes
    A - AUTHORISED IMPORT
    A - AUTHORISED IMPORT
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes an registration exempt controlled object)
      • Compliance month/year could be blank in TICA
      • If motorbike, it must be manufactured between 1976 and 1989 inclusive
      • Automatically generated a business exception
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    C - COMMERCIAL IMPORT
    C - COMMERCIAL IMPORT
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes a registration exempt controlled object)
      • Compliance month/year must be provided in TICA
      • Compliance year must be < 15 years from manufacture
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    E - EXEMPT
    E - EXEMPT
      • Must be one of the following:
      • Registration exempt controlled object
      • Exempt semi trailer
      • Exempt other trailer
      • Motorbike manufactured before 1976
      • Vehicle manufactured before 1972
      • All tractors
      • Compliance month/year must be blank in TICA
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    I - OLD IMPORT
    I - OLD IMPORT
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes a registration exempt controlled object)
      • Compliance month/year can be blank in TICA
      • Motorbike must be:
      • Manufactured on or after 1976
      • More than 15 years old
      • Vehicle must be:
      • Manufactured on or after 1972
      • More than 15 years old
      • A business exception is automatically created
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    O - APPROVED OPERATION
    O - APPROVED OPERATION
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes a registration exempt controlled object)
      • Can be an imported motorbike with an exemption authority
      • Compliance month/year must be blank in TICA
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    P - PERSONAL IMPORT
    P - PERSONAL IMPORT
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes a registration exempt controlled object)
      • Compliance month/year must be provided in TICA
      • Compliance year must be < 15 years from year of manufacture
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    R - ENGINEERS REPORT
    R - ENGINEERS REPORT
      Only to be used on:
      • Individually Constructed Vehicles
      • Special case vehicles approved by Vehicle Standards
      • Compliance month/year must be blank in TICA
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    T - PERMIT/LETTER
    T - PERMIT/LETTER
      • Only to be used for Rally Vehicles and Test and Evaluation vehicles
      • Do not use this code on any other vehicle without approval from Vehicle Standards (Ph (07) 3114 5844)
      • Compliance month/year must be blank in TICA
    X - LOST OR DESTROYED
    X - LOST OR DESTROYED
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes a registration exempt controlled object)
      • The vehicle was manufactured with an identification plate
      • Refer missing identification plates above
      • Compliance month/year must be blank in TICA
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    Y - IDENTIFICATION PLATE EXISTS
    Y - IDENTIFICATION PLATE EXISTS
      • Can be any vehicle or trailer (excludes a registration exempt controlled object)
      • Plate is attached to vehicle/trailer
      • Compliance month/year must be provided in TICA
      • Any current NONC or TEMP restriction must be expired
    Z - RAV ENTRY EXISTS
    Z - RAV ENTRY EXISTS
      • Vehicle is recorded on the RAV
