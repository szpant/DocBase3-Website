3 month pay term for Registration New Business (7597)

Enhancement Name3 month pay term for Registration New Business
OverviewAs part of TMR's COVID-19 registration relief measures, a system change will come into effect to allow all light vehicles (excluding trailers) to have a one-off 3 month pay term at new business. The 3 month pay term will be available to anyone who is:
  • Re-registering following previous cancellation due to COVID-19; or
  • Experiencing financial hardship (not explicitly due to COVID-19)

At the end of the 3 months, the customer's rego renewal will default to 12 months and they can opt to pay 6 months or enrol in Direct Debit for a 3 or one-month term.

The system change will include an 'on/off' parameter which will enable this option to be turned off and back on in the future if needed.

Existing light vehicles that are already entitled to a 3 month pay term at new business will not be affected by this change.
Who will be impacted and how?
Customers:
Customers:
    • Will now be able to request a one-off 3 month pay term when completing a registration new business transaction.
    • Will have their pay term defaulted to 12 months at renewal and can opt to pay 6 months or enrol in Direct Debit for a 3 or one-month term.
CSC operators:
CSC operators:
    • Will be able to apply a 3 month pay term to customers completing new business transactions.
    Note: The new business form (for non-formless centres) is currently being updated to reflect the new pay term.
    • Must make customers aware that it is a one-off 3 month pay term and that their renewal notice will default to 12 months.
    • Should encourage direct debit sign up for future renewals and provide interested customers with the Enrol in direct debit and add vehicle email template.
