Devices (7331)


Devices
What it isCompliance Officers use a range of devices to assist them in their official duties including iPad minis and ANPR Cameras.
Appropriate use of ICT servicesTMR mobile devices such as iPads minis, iPhones and laptops should not be taken home, they should be stored securely in the office unless the officer is not rostered to return to the office daily.

Relevant ICT policies include:

TMR use of ICT Services, Facilities and Devices Policy
TMR use of ICT Services, Facilities and Devices Standard
Authorised and unauthorised use of ICT Services, Facilities and Devices Guideline
iPad miniTMR Security procedures do not permit sharing iPads with other staff members.

Refer: TMR’s Information Policies and Standards: https://inside.tmr.qld.gov.au/sites/IPS/Pages/Home.aspx

Ensure the Find my iPad function is enabled when the iPad mini is set-up to assist locating the device if it is lost or stolen.

For information on how to set up an iPad mini, Refer: Systems > How To > iPad mini

Using your iPad mini
    You will be supplied an iPad mini with rugged case and straps to use while on the road.

    You have the option to order a Stylus Pen through your local stationery ordering process to enable customers to sign for notices on the iPad mini.
