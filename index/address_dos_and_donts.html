Address Do's and Don'ts (2994)

Do's and Don'ts
Care of Post Office
Do not use C/PO
Use CARE PO
Punctuation
Do not use punctuation marks (, . " ' : ;) in any field
Brackets
Do not use ( ) or { } or [ ] in any field
Abbreviation
Only use the allowable abbreviations in DocBase
Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Customer > Customer Name & Address Standards > Address Standards > Address formats
Postal Address
Do not use only a postal address in place of a Business or Residential address
Do not use a space between P and O
Corners
Do not use corners
Care of
Do not use C/
Mail Service (MS)
Do not place MS with residential address
Suburbs
Do not place Estate name or Suburb in the Street Name Field
Garaging Address
Do not add garaging address if same a Business or Residential address
Do not add Garaging address if not connected to a vehicle
No Place of Abode or NFPA
Do not use 'NFPA' or 'No Place of Abode'
NFPA Exception
NFPA Exception
    NFPA (No Fixed Place of Abode) can only be used if the customer is an itinerant. This will need to be entered in the street name field with a valid suburb and postcode combination (the CSC suburb and postcode).
