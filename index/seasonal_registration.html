Seasonal Registration (3017)


What is it?Seasonal Registration is an alternative option that allows vehicle registration periods to coincide with rural activities such as harvesting, without having to cancel at the end of a season and re-register when the season begins again.
Eligibility
Vehicle Usage
Vehicle Usage
    Vehicle and trailers are either:
    • Owned by Primary Producers and used only in registered operator's business of primary production
      or
    • Used by someone other than a primary producer (eg, contract harvester) to transport primary produce from a farm or fishing waters to where the primary produce is either:
      • Loaded onto a train, vessel or another vehicle
      • Stored before being processed
      • Subjected to the first processing step
      • Processed

    Seasonal registration is not available for vehicles used in the secondary transport of primary produce (eg , transport of raw sugar from a mill or refinery)
Vehicle Types
Vehicle Types
    Eligible Vehicles/Trailers include:
    • Prime mover or truck with GVM >=6t
    • Trailer with MRC >4.5t

Documents Required
  • Seasonal Registration Application from (F2336) for each vehicle
Applying Seasonal Registration
Applying Seasonal Registration
Applying Seasonal Registration
    • An application for Seasonal registration can be made at any time during, or at the end of, the currency of a registration term by completing Form F2336.
    • The duration of a season (the time that the registration is active) can be for 3, 6 or 12 months which means that a vehicle's registration can be active during the time when primary produce is harvested and transported.
    • Seasonal registration allows the customer to choose to put their vehicle/s registration term "on hold" if it is not intended to use the vehicle for a period of time, for example, when the harvesting period has ended.
      This "on hold" period is known as a dormant period.       For example the vehicle is registered in January for 12 months. The vehicle is only being used from June to September. Therefore the registration is:
    • Dormant January to the end of May
    • Active June to end of September
    • Dormant October to January

    Activating Dormant Period
    Activating Dormant Period
      • Vehicle can be made dormant when not in use during a seasonal registration period. To make application for a dormant period the customer is required to complete form F2336.
      • To activate a dormant period, the Pay term must be changed to 0.
      • A vehicle may remain dormant for 12 months before being cancelled
        (Refer: Vehicle Rego > 09. Cancellation > Automatic Cancellation)
      • Registration automatically becomes dormant at the end of an active period

    Activating the Registration
    Activating the Registration
      A seasonally registered vehicle that is dormant can be reactivated during a dormant period to either a seasonal or standard registration term.

      For a seasonal registration period:
      • Customer notifies TMR using the Seasonal Registration Application form (F2336)
      • The Pay term of 3, 6 or 12 is entered
      • Fees are then raised and payment can be made

      For a standard registration period:
      • The Pay mode is updated from Seasonal to Standard
      • The Pay term of 3,6 or 12 is entered
      • Fees are then raised and payment can be made
Rules
Account Management of Seasonal Registration
Account Management of Seasonal Registration
    Once registration is dormant:
    • A credit for the unexpired portion of the registration and CTP is placed in customers account
    • The registered operator can either
      • Have the money refunded
        or
      • Retain as a credit for future periods of registration

    Advance Payments
    Advance Payments
      • TICA does not allow a future payment to be made until the current season expires
      • Advance payments can be accepted by paying the money to the customers account with appropriate text
Registration Renewal Options
Registration Renewal Options
    When the vehicle's registration term or dormant period is due to expire, the options for vehicle registration renewal are as follows:

    If the vehicle is currently registered with seasonal registration either;
    • Register the vehicle with standard or seasonal registration for a registration term of 3, 6 or 12 months; or
    • Cancel the registration if it is no longer required (If the vehicle registration notice is disregarded the vehicle's registration will be automatically cancelled).

    If the vehicle's registration is currently dormant either;
    • Register the vehicle with a standard or seasonal registration term of 3, 6 or 12 months; or
    • Cancel the registration if it is no longer required (The vehicles' registration will be automatically cancelled on the expiry date of the dormancy period if a new registration term is not requested).
