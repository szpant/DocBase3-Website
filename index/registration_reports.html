Incorrectly Issued Heavy Vehicle Number Plates (W363)
Incorrectly Issued Heavy Vehicle Number Plates (W363)
    Incorrectly Issued Heavy Vehicle Number Plates (W363)
    DescriptionThis report monitors the issue of National Heavy Vehicle Plates to heavy vehicles that undergo a plate replacement or are registered as a New Business from 1 July 2018 onwards. Conditional registrations, SIVE, FRAV and farm plate vehicles are excluded.

    The report identifies relevant transactions and identifies heavy vehicles which have been issued the incorrect plate format.

    Note: Customised or Personalised plates may incorrectly appear on this report. No action is to be taken if they are incorrectly included.

    The report is generated and sent via emailed from the CSB Data Analysis Team.
    Details Produced
    • Region
    • Location Name
    • User Code
    • Customer ID
    • Processing Date
    • Transaction Type
    • Registration Plate
    • Plate Type Code
    FrequencyThe report:
    • Is generated daily
    • Distributed to the CSC Manager and PCM
    Steps to takeThe following steps need to be taken to rectify the error:
    • The CSC Manager is to advise the operator of their error and the requirement to issue a National Heavy Vehicle Number Plate (NHVNP.)
    • The operator must record a text restriction against the customer advising that a standard plate was incorrectly issued instead of a NHVNP.
    • The customer is entitled to a free NHVNP, should they wish to obtain one, the next time they present at a CSC.
