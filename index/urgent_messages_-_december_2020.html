21/12/2020 - Redbank Select Service Centre Closure
21/12/2020 - Redbank Select Service Centre Closure

    The Redbank Select Service Centre will close permanently at 4.30pm Thursday 24 December, to ensure the safety and longer-term satisfaction of customers.

    As more customers have been returning to the centre since COVID-19, it’s become increasingly difficult to keep people safe and enforce social distancing due to the centre’s location within a shopping complex. The location and limited service offering makes it hard to meet customer expectations and results in customer frustration.

    Customers will be notified of the closure on the TMR website and by signage on-site and will be encouraged to access our online services for a range of transport and motoring transactions. Alternatively, those needing to visit us in person will be redirected to the Ipswich or Sherwood Customer Service Centres, which are about a 15-minute drive from Redbank.

    Customer service staff and Contact Centre consultants can refer to the attached scripting and FAQs to assist in responding to enquiries.

07/12/2020 - Updating number plate details for BHSL vehicles
07/12/2020 - Updating number plate details for BHSL vehicles

    If the number plates on a BHSL vehicle are replaced a Taxi/Limousine/Special Purpose Limousine/Booked Hire Licence Amendment Application (F2976) must be also completed. After updating the plate details in TICA, operators must update the registration number recorded against the BHSL in the Managed booked hire staff UI.

    Due to system changes over the weekend, some customers may have issues with their BHSL if they have previously replaced their number plate. In order to rectify the issue:
    • CSC operators should have the customer complete Taxi/Limousine/Special Purpose Limousine/Booked Hire Licence Amendment Application (F2976) and then update the registration details in the Managed booked hire staff UI
    • All other operators should advise the customers to complete Taxi/Limousine/Special Purpose Limousine/Booked Hire Licence Amendment Application (F2976) and email it through to bhslhub@translink.com.au for processing. The BHSL hub will respond by email once the registration number has been updated.

    Peta Berry

07/12/2020 - Procedure to Follow Before Signing into TICA on Monday 07 December 2020
07/12/2020 - Procedure to Follow Before Signing into TICA on Monday 07 December 2020

    Registration and Licensing Systems are conducting an application release this evening 25/9/2020. From time to time, changes impacting TICA have triggered intermittent issues with TICA post deployment.

    To avoid these issues, we advise you carry out the following procedure before logging into TICA on 28/9/2020.

    It is recommended to clear your cache (in Internet Explorer).

    To clear your cache please follow the below process:

    1. Open Internet Explorer (If you are using Windows 10, please note that it must be Internet Explorer and not Edge. To find Internet Explorer, click on the windows button in the start bar and type in Internet Explorer)

    2. Select Tools from the menu line (or alt X on keyboard when you are in IE) and then Internet options



    3. Choose to Delete Browsing History



    4. Please uncheck ‘Preserve Favourites website data’ box and then check the boxes as per below in the example. Once completed click ‘delete’ and this will return you to the Internet Option page and click ‘OK’.



    5. Repeat step 2 and select Tools from the menu line (or alt X on keyboard when you are in IE) and then Internet options



    6. Click Settings button



    7. Click View files button as below



    8. Upon clicking View files the windows explorer will launch with directory location on your local machine containing the temporarily cached files. Please verify the directory location looks like the below screenshot " <<logged in username>>\AppData\Local\Microsoft\Windows\INetCache"



    9. Select All temporary files within the above directory location. You can use your mouse to select all files or you can select one file on the right and click "Ctrl +A" combination to select all files. Once all files within the directory are selected, please click Delete key on your keyboard to delete the files. You will be prompted with a Yes/No dialogue box to confirm, please verify the message looks similar to below screenshot and click Yes to delete the temporary files.



    10. Close ALL IE windows, then only launch TICA

    If, after following these instructions, you are still unable to access TICA, please contact the Service Centre on 3066 2000.

    Kat Tristram
    Senior Advisor Release Management
    Information Technology Branch

21/12/2020 - QCIS and iPad Operating System
21/12/2020 - QCIS and iPad Operating System

    Please ensure you have updated your iPad operating system to iOS14.3 and QCIS is version 2.1.3(101)

    To check the iOS version:
    • Go to Settings > General > Software Update

    To update:
    • Plug your device into power and connect to the Internet with Wi-Fi.
    • Tap Download and Install.

    To check QCIS version:
    • Open QCIS > Go to Settings > Version

    The app should update automatically, but if not, please download it from the TMR app catalogue.
