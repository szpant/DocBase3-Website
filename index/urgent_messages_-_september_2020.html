25/09/2020 - Procedure to Follow Before Signing into TICA on Monday 28 September 2020
25/09/2020 - Procedure to Follow Before Signing into TICA on Monday 28 September 2020

    Registration and Licensing Systems are conducting an application release this evening 25/9/2020. From time to time, changes impacting TICA have triggered intermittent issues with TICA post deployment.

    To avoid these issues, we advise you carry out the following procedure before logging into TICA on 28/9/2020.

    It is recommended to clear your cache (in Internet Explorer).

    To clear your cache please follow the below process:

    1. Open Internet Explorer (If you are using Windows 10, please note that it must be Internet Explorer and not Edge. To find Internet Explorer, click on the windows button in the start bar and type in Internet Explorer)

    2. Select Tools from the menu line (or alt X on keyboard when you are in IE) and then Internet options



    3. Choose to Delete Browsing History



    4. Please uncheck ‘Preserve Favourites website data’ box and then check the boxes as per below in the example. Once completed click ‘delete’ and this will return you to the Internet Option page and click ‘OK’.



    5. Close ALL IE windows, then only launch TICA

    If, after following these instructions, you are still unable to access TICA, please contact the Service Centre on 3066 2000.

    Kat Tristram
    Senior Advisor Release Management

14/09/2020 - Disability Parking Permit application for a person who is legally blind
14/09/2020 - Disability Parking Permit application for a person who is legally blind

    A customer may apply for a disability parking permit if they are legally blind provided that:
    • the individual holds a specified concession/benefit card; OR
    • the individual has been diagnosed as legally blind by a doctor, optometrist or ophthalmologist.

    These concession/benefit cards are accepted as evidence of legal blindness:
    • current TransLink Vision Impairment Travel Pass
    • current Department of Human Services Disability Support Pension card displaying the code 'BLIND' (Centrelink BLIND)
    • current Department of Veteran's Affairs Pensioner Concession Card showing the code 'BLIND'

    Staff are reminded that when a customer presents an applicable concession/benefit card the operator must copy both front and back of the concession card when accepting a Disability Parking Permit application for a person who is legally blind. The copy of the card as well as the application form must then be sent to the Disability Parking Unit as per current practice.

09/09/2020 - Managing Blue Card Applicants
09/09/2020 - Managing Blue Card Applicants

    The introduction of an online service on the Blue Card Services (BCS) website, allows customers to apply for and renew their Working with Children Card (Blue Card), using their TMR customer details.

    Please ensure you have read and continue to refer to the DocBase pages developed for these customers.

    Important reminders:
    • Like our own services, we must be asking and recording e-contact details for every blue card customer– without these details on their TMR record, the customer cannot proceed through the Blue Card application online.
    • The Blue Card - New Customer Notification is to be emailed to all new TMR customers who have attended a counter to obtain a CRN for the purpose of applying for a Blue Card. TMRs standard CRN card is not to be used for these customers.
    • Customers with a NOAC restriction, cannot apply for a Blue Card online without first attending a CSC to provide written consent to temporarily lift this NOAC. Changes have been implemented to assist customers who have a NOAC restriction – please ensure you are following the documented DocBase process.

01/09/2020 - Expiring the Recall (RCAL) Vehicle Restriction
01/09/2020 - Expiring the Recall (RCAL) Vehicle Restriction
    Staff are reminded the Recall (RCAL) vehicle restriction may be expired once the customer provides evidence from the manufacturer, or approved repairer, that the fault has been rectified. At present, this restriction has only been applied to those vehicles affected by the Takata Airbag Recall.

    Operators with a SPECPRO, CUSTPRO, IA CSC MANAGER (DAMANGR), SENIOR TRANSPORT INSPECTOR (STRPTINS), or DATAMAN profile are able to manually apply/edit/remove the RCAL restriction.

    Please refer: Vehicle Rego > 04. Registration > Vehicle Restrictions or Text > Vehicle Restrictions > RCAL > Manually Applying/Removing the RCAL Restriction
