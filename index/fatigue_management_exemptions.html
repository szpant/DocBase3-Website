Fatigue Management Exemptions (2331)

What is an ExemptionThe Heavy Vehicle National Law Act 2012 provides for exemptions from the requirement to fully comply with certain provisions.

Exemptions may be granted to:
  • individuals
  • groups of people engaged in a similar activity
  • a class of people who drive a similar type of vehicle for a specified period of time or for a specific activity

Literacy exemptionsPersons who have insufficient reading or writing skills to accurately complete a work diary may apply for a work diary literacy exemption which allows them to use other means to record their work and rest hours.

Eligibility requirements
Eligibility requirements
    • Applications must contact NHVR by phone for further details on requirements
    • The applicant needs a report on their literacy level from a qualified person
    • Applicant must nominate a 'nominee/scribe' to complete the work diary on their behalf
    • The scribe must agree to make accurate records for the applicant
    • Applications may be sent by post or email to:
      Post: Fatigue Exemption Applications
      Email:NHVR
