  • Be created in TRAILS as an involved party
  • Have a Vehicle Inspection Booking Agent Qual (VIBA)
Paying for Booking
Paying for a Booking
Paying for a Booking
    If a customers account is in CR with the inspection fee amount or the customer has a pre-approved credit limit on their customer account SBS pays from the account of the customer.
    Insufficient funds available in account
    Insufficient funds available in account
      Pay by credit card
      Pay by credit card
        If the customer does not have sufficient credit in their account and no pre-approved credit limit, they may pay by credit card. SBS will process the booking and provide the customer with an order number. The order number will be emailed to the customer if they provided an email address at the time of booking, it can also be provided to the customer verbally. The customer can then use the Pay an order online service to pay the booking fee.

        For same day bookings, the booking must be paid within 30 minutes of the booking being made. All other bookings must be paid for by midnight on the day the booking is made. Any bookings not paid for by the due time will be automatically cancelled. The customer will need to re-book.
      Other options
      Other options
        The customer may make a booking with SBS, then pay at a customer service centre. For same day bookings, the booking must be paid within 30 minutes of the booking being made. All other bookings must be paid for by midnight on the day the booking is made. Any bookings not paid for by the due time will be automatically cancelled. The customer will need to re-book.

        A CSC operator can only use the option to pay to a customer account. Once paid the customer is to call SBS to advise and confirm the booking. The CSC operator is not to pay any pending payments as the system does not recognise these payments and does not confirm the inspection booking.
    ExpensesOvertime and travelling expenses are payable in addition to the inspection fee, when an inspection is completed by TMR outside normal hours at the request of the vehicle's owner.
    TMR Inspection Fee Exemptions
    Charitable Organisations
    Charitable Organisations
      Charitable Organisations are exempt from paying the fee for a TMR COI Inspection (inc. booking fee).

      No exemptions are given to Charitable Organisations for any other TMR inspection fees, such as a defect re-inspection fee.
    Drought affected primary producers
    Drought affected primary producers
      Drought affected primary producers may be eligible for an exemption from the fee charged for a TMR COI Inspection (inc. booking fee) if they are located in a drought declared area.

      Refer: Vehicle Rego > 03. Types of Registration > Drought Assistance Package > Waiving of COI fees
    Process to adjust accounts
    Process to adjust accounts
      Step 1
      Use adjustment type REVCUST to adjust fees from Post GST Motor Vehicle Inspection Fee and Inspection Booking Fee for Vehicles accounts.

      Step 2
      Use adjustment type LIABCUST to adjust fees from GST Motor Vehicle Inspection Fee AND GST Inspection Booking Fee accounts.

      Note:Refer: Admin > 3. Money > The How To: Money > Money and Banking Procedures > Create/Reverse/Copy and Adjustment
    Booking ReminderIf a customer is signed up to receive e-reminders, a reminder will be emailed to them 5 days prior to the inspection booking date. The reminder will include the following details:
    • Customer name
    • Registration number
    • Vehicle inspection booking date
    • Link to reschedule a vehicle inspection online service
    • Link to swap 2 vehicle inspections online service
    Changing a BookingCustomer must:
    • Notify TMR 2 business days prior to the booked inspection
      and
    • Supply:
      • The booking reference number
        OR
      • Meet standard evidence of identification requirements (Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Standard Evidence of Identity > Step 2 - Check Standard EOI Requirements > By Phone)

    Failure to notify 2 business days prior will result in the loss of the inspection fees paid.
    Cancelling a Booking
    Cancel a Booking
    Cancel a Booking
      Customer must:
      • Notify TMR 2 business days prior to the booked inspection
      and
      • Supply:
        • The booking reference number
        OR
        • Meet standard evidence of identification requirements (Refer: Customer > 1. Customer > Standard Evidence of Identity > Step 2 - Check Standard EOI Requirements > By Phone)

      Failure to notify within 2 business days will result in the loss of the inspection fees paid. If the customer does not wish to re-book the fees will need to be refunded


      Note:If original payment was by credit card the credit card surcharge is not refunded.
