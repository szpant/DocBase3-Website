Special Plate Types -Storage Locations (3230)


Most CSC's will not have all plate types in stock. If your centre does not have the plate type required contact a CSC from the list below to arrange the release of the correct plate type for processing.

The plates must be sent to the customer’s address the same day as requested.

If you cannot locate a centre holding a plate type please contact Plates Administration for assistance.
