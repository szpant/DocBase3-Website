Where to request a Court Certificate

    Court certificate requests can be made at all TMR customer service centres, via email or fax.

    SEQ North and SEQ South regions have a central processing hub and CSCs that sit within these regions should forward the request to the relevant central processing hub for completion.

    Once the certificate/s has been prepared, the central processing hub will make arrangements with the requesting party for the collection/postage of the completed certificate/s.

    Please refer below for central processing hub contact details.
