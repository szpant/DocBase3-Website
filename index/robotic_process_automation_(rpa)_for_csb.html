Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for CSB (7502)

Project NameRobotic Process Automation (RPA) for CSB
WhenMid 2020 - mid 2021
BackgroundAs part of CSB's Mobility Strategy, CSB approached the TMR Digital Capability Unit in 2018 to partner on an opportunity to explore the application of Robotics Process Automation (RPA) in our business. RPA is an advanced form of business process automation that allows anyone to define a set of rules based instructions performed by a human on their computer, then have a 'bot' perform those same instructions with minimal human interaction. This enables the robot to rapidly automate repeatable, mundane daily processes.

RPA has been successfully tested within CSB by two Proof of Concepts (PoCs) for both the Learner Logbook Assessment process and the HR Reporting process. The Learner Logbook Assessment process has also successfully progressed to pilot using real customer data in the TRAILS production environment and is currently assessing Logbooks submitted through recognised logbook mobile phone applications (apps).

During the Learner Logbook Assessment pilot, a discovery process was completed across three CSB business units (Customer Service Operations, Customer Contact Centre and Service, Innovation and Systems) to identify potential RPA opportunities. Using the outcomes of this activity, the project team intends to investigate, validate and apply business process improvement to the top 10 potential RPA opportunities to work on with selected vendor/s.

The project has been approved to commence a procurement process to evaluate and select a vendor/s to work with CSB on the identified RPA opportunities and provide RPA software and capability.
Who will be impacted and how?In the early stages, the project team will be approaching teams from CSO, CCC and SIS to further investigate potential RPA processes. This investigation is intended to be minimally disruptive, but may take some time from nominated staff.
Further informationFurther information will be provided as the project progresses.
