Sell a Work Diary (1225)

Applies to
    The sale of National Driver Work Diary (work diary) to current driver licence holders. It includes the sale of a:
    • Work diary for the first time issue
    • Replacement of a previously issued work diary (including lost, stolen or destroyed)
    • Replacement of a seized work diary
