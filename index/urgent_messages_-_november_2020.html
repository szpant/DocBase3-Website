27/11/2020 - Christmas Closures for Plate Manufacturers
27/11/2020 - Christmas Closures for Plate Manufacturers

    To have personalised plates delivered in time for Christmas, orders must be placed by COB Tuesday 8 December 2020. Customers can continue to order personalised plates after this date; however, delivery prior to Christmas is not guaranteed.

    Standard issue number plates will be despatched up to Monday 21 December 2020. The manufacturer will dispatch plates where the re-order level has been reached or will be reached over the Christmas break.

    If you require extra standard issue number plates during the closure period, please submit a manual Plate request to PlatesAdministration@tmr.qld.gov.au.

    For pre-Christmas delivery, manual order requests must be submitted by 12 noon, Wednesday 16 December 2020.

    The manufacturer will be closed from Friday 18 December 2020 and will reopen Monday 4 January 2021.

    Plates Administration

13/11/2020 - Update - TICA issue impacting some users – Unable to open PDFs in TICA
13/11/2020 - Update - TICA issue impacting some users – Unable to open PDFs in TICA
    There are new instructions to resolve this issue. If you are still experiencing issues with TICA opening PDFs that appear blank, please follow the steps below rather than reinstalling TICA. There have been some reports of issues following the TICA reinstall.

    Before commencing, close TICA and Adobe Acrobat if open.

    1. Click the start button and open Software Centre


    2. Click on Installation Status and then “Adobe Acrobat Reader DC 20.012.20041”


    3. Click Uninstall


    4. Initially, this may give an error when removing. If so, click Retry.


    5. Once completed, Click the Applications tab and then select the Acrobat Reader DC 20.009.20063 version.


    6. Click Install


    7. Once this installation is complete, the process is finished. Close the Software Centre window and launch TICA as normal.

    If you still experience issues after following these steps please contact the Service Centre.

    Lindsay Abdi
    Principal Advisor (Systems Support)
    Customer Services Branch

03/11/2020 - Blue Card and HRWL Applicants - Evidence of Identity
03/11/2020 - Blue Card and HRWL Applicants - Evidence of Identity

    Staff are reminded to please ensure that all customers attending a TMR CSC for the purpose of obtaining a Blue Card or HRWL, are presenting all EOI requirements to obtain a CRN.

    Operators must ensure that they capture:
    • an image set
    • proof of residential address
    • eContact details.

    As the customer does not need to be a Queensland resident to apply for a Blue Card or HRWL, an interstate proof of address document can be provided and recorded in TICA.

    DocBase has been updated to advise when this occurs, the following text is applied to the customer's record:

      'An interstate residency document has been recorded. If the customer presents to TMR to obtain a TMR product then they must provide a Queensland proof of residency document and this must be updated in TICA'

    If an address document is not recorded in TICA, the customer will not be able to validate successfully online and will be required to return to TMR. Please ensure these requirements are met.

    Please refer to DocBase updates:
    High Risk Work Licence
    Working with Children Card (Blue Card)

    Nicole Clues
    Project Manager, Identity and Card Management Services
    Strategy and Engagement

30/11/2020 - QCIS update was released this afternoon, Monday 30th November
30/11/2020 - QCIS update was released this afternoon, Monday 30th November

    This release resolves compatibility issues with iPadOS14 and also contains some minor bug fixes.

    This afternoon you will be able to update all QCIS iPad devices to the newest iOS version.

    If a message says that an update is available, tap Install Now. You can also follow these steps:
    1. Plug your device into power and connect to the Internet with Wi-Fi.
    2. Go to Settings > General, then tap Software Update.
    3. Tap Download and Install.

    You will also be able to turn automatic updates back on in Settings > General > Software Update > Automatic Updates > On.

    The app should update automatically, but if not please download it from the TMR app catalogue.
