ROI Verbal Requests (3072)

What is it?A request for current/historical registration records made verbally.
Who Can Apply?
  • Registered Operator
  • Interstate Registration Authorities
  • Qld Police Service (QPS)
  • Individuals and Organisations
Requirements for
Applicants
Requested by:Rules:Allowable Information:
Registered Operator
  • Standard EOI
    or
  • Registration Number or VIN if requesting:
    • information that is available on the TMR Check Registration Status on-line service
    • a vehicle's current CTP insurer. Note: this information is also publicly available at https://maic.qld.gov.au.
    or
  • Registration number and registered operator name if:
    • confirming registration is currently enrolled in Direct Debit
  • Current details only that would appear on a ROI statement
  • Registration fees
  • Outstanding Registration fees
  • Transfer date (disposer or acquirer only)
  • Axle configuration (heavy vehicles)
  • COI/Extension of Time (EOT) expiry date
  • Amount of vehicle registration duty paid
  • Purpose of use
  • Payment Reference details

Note:The above information may be given to persons/organisations with proven legal authority to act on behalf of the registered operator. For example:
  • General or Enduring Power of Attorney (The TMR operator must be satisfied that the evidence provided clearly indicates the authority of the person/organisation to manage the financial affairs or pay the registration on behalf of the registered operator)
  • Public Trustee is listed as the paying agent/second registered operator or a TEXT is applied advising they are managing the registered operator's financial affairs (The TMR operator must conduct a search of Govnet for the member of the Public Trustee and confirm all their information displayed. If the caller is not listed on Govnet, then written confirmation of their authority may be emailed through on Public Trustee letterhead)

Refer: Release of Information > 6. Vehicle Registration > Vehicle Rego Release of Information > ROI Statement Information
Interstate Registration Authority
  • Standard EOI
  • Current/historical registration details that would appear on a ROI statement

Refer: Release of Information > 6. Vehicle Registration > Vehicle Rego Release of Information > ROI Statement Information
QPS
  • Standard EOI
  • Current/historical details that would appear on a ROI statement
  • Information to assist the interpretation of TICA

Refer: Release of Information > 6. Vehicle Registration > Vehicle Rego Release of Information > ROI Statement Information
Other Individuals/ Organisations
  • Applicant must provide a Registration Number or VIN.
Information that can be released:
  • Information that is available on the TMR Registration Status online service at Check Registration Status
  • A vehicles current CTP insurer. Note: this information is also publicly available online at https://maic.qld.gov.au

Refer: Services > 1. Online Services > TMR Online Services Guide > TMR Online Services List (4985)

Information not provided by the TMR Registration Status online service that can only be confirmed/denied:
Note:*Answers are Yes or No only
Current Records
Current Records
    Current details that would appear on a ROI statement can be confirmed or denied*, excluding:
    • Postal address
    • Residential Address
    • Garage address

    Refer: Release of Information > 6. Vehicle Registration > Vehicle Rego Release of Information > ROI Statement Information
Expired/Cancelled Records
Expired/Cancelled Records
    The following information about a vehicle record can be confirmed or denied*:
    • auto cancellation date
    • registration status "NOT REGISTERED"
    • purpose of use
    • learner approved motorcycle (LAM) status (if applicable)
    • registration expiry date (expired registration only)
    • plate number
    • VIN
    • Chassis
    • vehicle make, model, body shape and year
    • COI/EOT expiry date

    Under no circumstances can any other details be confirmed or denied.

    If customer requires any other specific vehicle related information, they must request this information in person or in writing and pay the appropriate fee.

    Refer: Fees and Forms > 1. Fees > Fees > Vehicle Registration > Release of Information (ROI) > Current ROI Fees

What is it?A request for a List of Current Registrations
Who Can Apply?
  • Individual Registered Operators
  • ACN Organisations Registered Operators
Requirements for
Applicants
Requested by:Rules:Allowable Information:
Registered Operators at CSC – print list of vehicles/plates recorded to registered operator in TICA at CSC
    • Standard EOI is required.
    • All Registered Operators can obtain a printed list of vehicles/plates recorded to the Registered Operator in TICA.
    • List is to be printed from the List Registrations screen in TICA.
    • The vehicle/plate list provided must not contain cancelled registrations or plates.
    • No fee or form is applicable
  • Information provided in the report printed from the List Registrations screen in TICA.
  • For process to print list, Refer:Release of Information > Vehicle Registration > The How To: Release of Information > List of Registration Request

Note:The vehicle/plate list provided must not contain cancelled registrations or plates
Registered Operator by telephone – view list of vehicles recorded to Registered Operator in TICA via TMR Online Services
  • Where possible (and before advising customer to attend a CSC) refer Individuals and ACN Organisations Registered Operators to TMR’s Online Services to view vehicles recorded to the Registered Operator in TICA.
  • The list of vehicles can be viewed via customer’s account using TMR’s Online Services, My Registrations
  • EOI required – Customer login with extra details
  • Information provided via customer’s account using TMR’s Online Services, My Registrations

Note:There is currently no print option in the online service, therefore if the customer requests a printed copy they will need to attend a CSC and provide EOI for the release of the document.
