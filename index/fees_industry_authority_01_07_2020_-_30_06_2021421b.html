CrimTrac FeeA national criminal history check is part of the approval process for a number of different licences, authorities and accreditations issued by the Department of Transport and Main Roads to obtain employment or operate in certain occupations and industries.

Any person who applies to be the holder of a relevant licence, authority or accreditation is subject to a criminal history check.

The CrimTrac fee is a one-off fee paid by the applicant at the time of a new application.
