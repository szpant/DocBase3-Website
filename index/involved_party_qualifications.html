Involved Party Qualifications (1612)

AGNT
AGNT
    It specifies involved parties who do TMR business as agents. This includes Authorised Dealers, QPS Police Stations, QGAP Agents and Clerks of the Court who process transactions via the 1300 and 1800 telephone networks. This qualification enables them to carry out transactions in TRAILS via electronic interfaces.
DAIS
DAIS
    This is assigned to dealers and Agents who deal with TMR through the Dealer and Agency Interface System (DAIS). This qual helps enforce system security and allows authorised dealers carry out certain transactions in TRAILS via electronic interfaces.
DAB
DAB
    This is assigned to involved parties who exist in TRAILS to allow Direct Debit of monies from their bank accounts. These moneys are only direct debited when the involved party owes money to TMR as a result of business transactions. At the end of each day any outstanding amounts are rolled up to FIMS and the amount debited from their bank account. This includes those with the DAIS system.
EDIC
EDIC
    An individual/organisation authorised to have electronic interface to TRAILS as a client. It includes dealers through DAIS and solicitors using APS.
EDID
EDID
    An individual/organisation who provides an electronic interface to TRAILS as a distributor. It includes CITEC and DAIS developers.
FILE
FILE
    Used to redirect filing report to a supporting CSC. Redirected reports include:
    • RG61 Document Filing Summary
    • AA62 Daily Customer Account Report
    • AA92 List of Operational Account Balances
    • AA93 List of Manual Adjustments
    • AA97 Exception Report of Payment Requests Acquitted by accounts

    This qualification can be added and updated by the following profiles used with CSC's:
    • DAMANGR
    • DATAMAN
    • EUO
    • INSPCORR
    • MANAGER (Dalby)
    • SBSMANGR
    • SPECPRO
    • VIU
QPSR
QPSR
    Used to redirect the following reports to a different location.
    • PD81 Practical Driving test bookings
    • IP55 Identity Document verification failure report
    • RG61 Daily filing reports
    It is not recommended that this Qual is applied as it will result in the QPS location not receiving their daily filing reports.

    This qualification can be added and updated by the following profiles used with CSC's:
    • DAMANGR
    • DATAMAN
    • EUO
    • INSPCORR
    • MANAGER (Dalby)
    • SBSMANGR
    • SPECPRO
    • VIU
