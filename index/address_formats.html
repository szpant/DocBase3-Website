Attention Specific Person
Attention Specific Person
    Field:Contact Name
    Format:<Contact Name>
    Example:G R Jensen-Smith

    This is only necessary when an organisation specifically requests that their nominated officer be the contact for all registration related correspondence.

    The name of the contact officer is recorded in the Update Organisation Details hub.

    Record the initials of the nominated officer & full surname. Dual family names should be separated by a hyphen (eg Jensen-Smith)

Lot/Section Number
Lot/Section Number
    Field:Street Number
    Format:<lot> <lot number>
    Example:Lot 25

    A lot/section reference is allocated to a property by a Government Department during the sub-division of a particular parcel of land.

    Lot or Section numbers should only be used prior to the allocation of street numbers by the Government Department.

    Customers should be instructed to advise TMR when they have been allocated a street number.

Postcodes
Postcodes
    Field:Postcode
    Format:<postcode>
    Example:4051

    If you choose to select the Suburb/Town? and there is only one valid postcode for that suburb TICA will automatically preset the postcode. The postcode can also be manually keyed in.

    Note:TICA has a system edit that will prevent the wrong postcode from being entered for a listed suburb in the TICA reference table. The exceptions to the rule is if the [State?] field is blank (for overseas addresses) & the [Cntry?] field is not AUS:
    • The ZIP code can be keyed in; or
    • The [State] field can be listed as "OVR", the [Postcode] field can be listed as 0000 and the ZIP Code can be listed in the [Suburb/Town] field.

    TICA Example
    TICA Example
      If a postcode only is entered, and prompt on the suburb field, the system will allow a suburb only with the corresponding postcode to be selected from the List State Suburb Postcode screen.

      Postal Definitions
      Postal Definitions
        Postal Definitions
        Post Office (PO)
        • Has post office boxes
        • Offers Counter services
        • Sells retail product
        • Does not process mail
        Business Centre (BC)These centres provide for the larger business customer.
        • Has post office boxes
        • Has Counter service
        • Sells retail product
        • Has pick-up and delivery services
        • Processes mail to a preparation stage
        Delivery Centre (DC)
        • Has post office boxes
        The prime objective of the delivery centre is to deliver mails and parcels. This is achieved by Postal Workers and Contractors.
        Mail Centre (MC)
        • Can have post office boxes
        The main objective is to process mail to feed the delivery system. This is processed in a factory environment.
        Mail Service (MS)
        • Australia Post has advised that any supplementary address information for rural Queensland address is helpful. Property name should be recorded in the (Property Id) field.

    Street Number
    Street Number
      Field:Street Number
      Format:<street number/s>
      Example:17 or 17-19 or 17B

      Generally only one house/property number is used (eg 17).

      If the house/property covers a range of numbers this range should be included using a hyphen (-) with no spaces (eg 17-19).

      If the number includes an alpha character, there must be no spaces between numerals & the alpha character (eg 17B).

    Villa
    Villa
      Field:Property Id, Unit / Flat, Street Number
      Format:Property Id: <Complex Name>
      Unit / Flat: <VLLA> <number>
      Street Number: <street number>
      Example:Eastlakes Retirement Village
      VLLA 2

      Many customers reside in villas as part of body corporate complexes such as retirement villages. It is important for Australia Post to clearly identify the respective villa within a complex. This information is recorded in the [Unit / Flat] field.

      The abbreviation for Villa is VLLA.
